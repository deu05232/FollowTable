# Deer Browse Pressure by Season for Common Landscape Plant Groups (Behavior-Focused)

| Season | Most Browsed Plant Group | Typical Browse Intensity | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Early Spring | Tender perennials | High | New shoots are targeted first |
| Late Spring | Annual bedding plants | Medium–High | Soft foliage is preferred |
| Summer | Fruits & soft leaves | Medium | Browse shifts with moisture availability |
| Early Fall | Shrubs (new growth) | Medium | Deer prepare for rut/winter |
| Late Fall | Evergreens & twigs | Medium–High | Limited forage increases browsing |
| Winter | Woody stems | High | Food scarcity drives heavy browsing |
| Drought Periods | Irrigated gardens | Very High | Deer concentrate near water |
| Post-Rut | Mixed garden plants | Medium | Recovery feeding increases |
