# Top Gear test track lap-time adjustment guide (conditions and seconds applied)

| Track condition label | What the surface was like | Seconds added/removed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hot | Heat/humidity affecting grip | −2 seconds |
| Mildly moist | Slight damp with dry patches | −2 seconds |
| Moist | Light rain leaving a wet surface | −3 seconds |
| Wet | Wet surface after rain/snow melt | −4 seconds |
