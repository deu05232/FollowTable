# Cranial Nerves III, IV, VI  General Properties (Not Muscle-by-Muscle)

| Cranial nerve | Number | Primary modality | Parasympathetic component | Brainstem emergence | Major targets (broad) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Oculomotor | III | Mainly motor | Yes (Edinger–Westphal) | Ventral midbrain | Most extraocular muscles; levator palpebrae; pupil constriction |
| Trochlear | IV | Motor | No | Dorsal midbrain | Superior oblique |
| Abducens | VI | Motor | No | Pontomedullary junction | Lateral rectus |
| Trigeminal (ophthalmic) | V1 | Sensory | No | Pons | Cornea, upper eyelid, forehead |
| Facial | VII | Mixed | Yes | Pons | Facial expression; lacrimation |
| Optic | II | Sensory | No | Diencephalon | Vision |
