# Brake Fluid DOT Ratings and Boiling Points (Reference Only)

| Fluid Spec | Dry Boiling Point (°C) | Wet Boiling Point (°C) | Compatibility Notes | Typical Vehicles |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| DOT 3 | 205 | 140 | Glycol-based; common OEM fill | Many compact sedans |
| DOT 4 | 230 | 155 | Higher temp than DOT 3; glycol-based | Performance trims, European cars |
| DOT 5 | 260 | 180 | Silicone-based; not mixable with DOT 3/4 | Classic cars (some conversions) |
| DOT 5.1 | 260 | 180 | Glycol-based; not silicone; ABS-friendly | Modern cars with ABS/ESC |
| DOT 4 LV | 240 | 160 | Low viscosity for faster ABS response | Late-model vehicles |
| DOT 3 Synthetic | 210 | 145 | Marketing term; still glycol-based | Mixed applications |
| Mineral Oil (non-DOT) | N/A | N/A | Used in some hydraulic systems; not DOT | Select motorcycles/bikes |
