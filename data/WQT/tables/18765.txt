# Polyatomic Ion Names and Formulas (Formulas Missing Explicit Charge Signs)

| Ion name | Formula (no charge shown) | Common in | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Nitrate | NO3 | Fertilizers | Conjugate base of nitric acid |
| Nitrite | NO2 | Food preservatives | Oxidation state differs from nitrate |
| Sulfate | SO4 | Minerals | Often forms insoluble BaSO4 |
| Sulfite | SO3 | Wine additives | Can be oxidized to sulfate |
| Carbonate | CO3 | Limestone | CO2/HCO3− buffer system |
| Phosphate | PO4 | Biology | Multiple protonation states |
| Ammonium | NH4 | Salts | Only common polyatomic cation |
