# U.S. Child Restraint Recommendations by Age (Not State-Specific)

| Child Age/Size Group | Recommended Restraint | Typical Seating Position | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Birth–12 months | Rear-facing infant seat | Rear seat | Keep rear-facing as long as possible |
| 1–3 years | Rear-facing convertible seat | Rear seat | Transition only when limits reached |
| 4–7 years | Forward-facing seat with harness | Rear seat | Use harness to max limit |
| 8–12 years | Belt-positioning booster | Rear seat | Booster until seat belt fits properly |
| 13–15 years | Adult seat belt | Any (rear preferred) | Lap belt low on hips |
| 16+ years | Adult seat belt | Any seat | Always buckle up |
| All ages | Correct belt fit guidance | Any seat | Shoulder belt across chest, not neck |
