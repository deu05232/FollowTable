# Autonomic Neurotransmitters by Synapse Location (Preganglionic vs Postganglionic)

| ANS division | Pathway step (handoff point) | Neuron / fiber type | Primary transmitter released | Main receptor at next cell | Typical target of release |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sympathetic | Preganglionic → ganglion | Preganglionic sympathetic neuron | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) | Postganglionic neuron in sympathetic chain or prevertebral ganglion |
| Sympathetic | Postganglionic → effector | Postganglionic sympathetic neuron (typical) | Norepinephrine (NE) | Adrenergic (α/β) | Smooth muscle, cardiac muscle, glands |
| Sympathetic (exception) | Postganglionic → effector | Postganglionic sympathetic neuron to sweat glands | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Muscarinic (M3) | Eccrine sweat gland |
| Sympathetic (exception) | Postganglionic → effector | Postganglionic sympathetic neuron to renal vasculature | Dopamine (DA) | Dopaminergic (D1) | Renal vascular smooth muscle |
| Sympathetic (neuroendocrine) | Preganglionic → adrenal medulla | Preganglionic sympathetic neuron | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) on chromaffin cells | Chromaffin cell secretion into blood |
| Sympathetic (neuroendocrine) | Adrenal medulla → body (circulation) | Chromaffin cell (modified postganglionic) | Epinephrine (Epi) ± Norepinephrine (NE) | Adrenergic (α/β) | Distant effector organs via bloodstream |
| Parasympathetic | Preganglionic → ganglion | Preganglionic parasympathetic neuron | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) | Postganglionic neuron in terminal/intramural ganglion |
| Parasympathetic | Postganglionic → effector | Postganglionic parasympathetic neuron | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Muscarinic (M2/M3) | Smooth muscle, cardiac muscle, glands |
