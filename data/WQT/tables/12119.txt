# U.S. Passport Application Fees (Routine vs Expedited) — Common Services

| Passport service (main item) | Applicant category / notes | Routine fee (USD) | Expedited fee (USD) |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| New passport book (Form DS-11) | Age 16+ (first-time or replacement) | 130.00 | 190.00 |
| New passport book (Form DS-11) | Under 16 | 100.00 | 160.00 |
| Passport renewal book (Form DS-82) | Eligible adults | 130.00 | 190.00 |
| Replacement passport (lost/stolen) | Age 16+ | 130.00 | 190.00 |
| Replacement passport (lost/stolen) | Under 16 | 100.00 | 160.00 |
| Passport card (first-time, DS-11) | Age 16+ | 30.00 | 90.00 |
| Passport card (first-time, DS-11) | Under 16 | 15.00 | 75.00 |
| Add passport card with renewal (DS-82 add-on) | Adult renewing book & adding card | 30.00 | 90.00 |
