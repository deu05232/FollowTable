# Time zone offsets by country (standard vs daylight)

| Country/Territory | Standard time (UTC offset) | Daylight time (UTC offset) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| United States | UTC-05:00 to UTC-08:00 | UTC-04:00 to UTC-07:00 |
| Mexico | UTC-06:00 to UTC-08:00 | UTC-05:00 to UTC-07:00 |
| Jamaica | UTC-05:00 | - |
| Japan | UTC+09:00 | - |
