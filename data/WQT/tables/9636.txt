# Yellow Fever Guidance by Country (Includes Exemptions and Other Vaccine Notes)

| Country | Yellow Fever Status (Required/Recommended) | Exemptions/Notes | Other Travel Vaccine Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kenya | Required for travelers arriving from risk areas | Certificate may be checked at entry | Consider typhoid for long stays |
| Uganda | Recommended for most travelers | Some entry checks reported | Hepatitis A commonly advised |
| Nigeria | Required for entry | Proof required for ≥ 9 months | Meningitis vaccine advised in dry season |
| Brazil | Recommended for many regions | Risk varies by state | Ensure tetanus booster |
| Thailand | Not required (unless arriving from risk countries) | Proof required if coming from endemic areas | Rabies for animal exposure |
| South Africa | Not required (unless arriving from risk countries) | Applies to certain transit histories | Consider routine vaccines |
