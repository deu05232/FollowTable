# Egyptian Gods and Goddesses  Specific Functions and Spheres

| Deity | God/Goddess of |
|---|---|
| Aten | Solar disk; divine aspect of the sun |
| Atum | Creator god; first emergence and creation |
| Bes | Household protection, childbirth, and warding off evil |
| Hapi | Annual Nile inundation and fertility |
| Heqet | Childbirth and fertility |
| Khepri | Rising sun; rebirth and renewal |
| Montu | War and martial strength |
| Neith | Weaving and war |
| Serqet (Selket) | Protection (especially against venom) and funerary guardianship |
| Set (Seth) | Storms, desert, disorder (and protector of Ra in some myths) |
| Sobek | Crocodiles; Nile waters and military power |
| Tefnut | Moisture, rain, and humidity |
| Wadjet | Protection of Lower Egypt; royal protection |
| Nekhbet | Protection of Upper Egypt; royal protection |
| Anuket | The Nile and its cataracts (southern river regions) |
