# Common Garden Pests and Suggested Companion Plants (Pest-Centered Guide)

| Pest | Plants Reported to Deter It | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mosquitoes | Lemon thyme, lime basil, citronella grass | Often cited for strong citrus-like scent |
| Aphids | Chives, nasturtium, garlic | Also managed with beneficial insects |
| Japanese beetles | Chives, geranium, tansy | Beetles may still appear during peak season |
| Carrot fly | Lettuce, chives, rosemary | Physical barriers also recommended |
| Cabbage moth (cabbage white) | Thyme, sage, dill | Interplanting sometimes used |
| Slugs | Rosemary, fennel, wormwood | Best combined with traps and barriers |
| Ants | Chrysanthemums, mint, pennyroyal | Ant control depends on colony location |
| Ticks | Chrysanthemums, lavender, rosemary | Yard management is usually required |
| Whiteflies | Basil, marigold, nasturtium | Use with sticky traps for monitoring |
| Root-knot nematodes | French marigold, mustard greens | Soil solarization often mentioned |
| Fleas | Chrysanthemums, lavender, mint | Also used in dried sachets |
