# Iran — Childbirth-Related Leave Entitlements (National Overview)

| Leave type | Statutory duration | Salary replacement during leave | Payment basis/notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Maternity leave (standard) | 26 weeks (6 months) | 100% of covered wage | Typically paid via social insurance for eligible insured workers |
| Maternity leave (multiple birth) | 26 weeks (6 months) | 100% of covered wage | Same pay rule; applies for twins+ where recognized |
| Prenatal leave (within maternity) | Up to 8 weeks before birth (counts toward total) | 100% of covered wage | Part of the 26-week maternity entitlement, not additional |
| Postnatal leave (within maternity) | Remainder after birth (to total 26 weeks) | 100% of covered wage | Used after delivery up to the cap |
| Paternity leave | 0 weeks (no general statutory paid paternity leave) | 0% | If available, may depend on employer/sector rather than a universal rule |
| Parental leave (non-maternity) | 0 weeks (no general statutory parental leave) | 0% | No nationwide entitlement separate from maternity leave |
| Adoption leave | Not specified as a universal national entitlement | Not specified | May exist in specific regulations/collective agreements rather than a single nationwide rule |
| Nursing/breastfeeding breaks (time off at work) | 1 hour per workday (commonly until 24 months) | 100% (paid time) | Paid break during working hours; not counted as full-day leave |
| Job protection during maternity | Applies during maternity leave period | N/A (job protection, not wage rate) | Intended to protect return-to-work rights during approved maternity leave |
