# Oilseed Crops — Oil Yield and Extraction Efficiency (Not a Fat Profile Table)

| Crop / source | Typical oil content of seed (%) | Mechanical press yield (kg oil/ha) | Solvent extraction yield (kg oil/ha) | Meal byproduct (kg/ha) | Notes |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Soybean | 18–20 | 350–550 | 500–700 | 1,500–2,500 | Major global protein meal source |
| Sunflower | 38–45 | 800–1,100 | 1,000–1,400 | 900–1,300 | High variability by cultivar |
| Canola (rapeseed) | 40–44 | 900–1,200 | 1,100–1,600 | 1,000–1,500 | Widely grown in temperate zones |
| Peanut | 45–50 | 700–1,100 | 900–1,400 | 600–1,000 | Food and oil crop |
| Sesame | 45–55 | 400–700 | 500–900 | 300–600 | Often smallholder-produced |
| Coconut (copra) | 60–65 | 900–1,400 | 1,100–1,700 | 800–1,200 | Oil derived from dried kernel |
| Palm fruit | 20–23 (fresh fruit) | 3,000–4,000 | 3,500–4,500 | 1,000–2,000 | Includes palm oil + palm kernel streams |
| Rice bran | 15–22 (bran) | 200–400 | 300–600 | 800–1,500 | Requires stabilization to prevent rancidity |
