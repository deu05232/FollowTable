| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Inbound (from EXO) | TLS certificate name | mail.contoso.com | EAC / PowerShell | Enforce TLS identity |
| Inbound (from EXO) | Require TLS | true | EAC / PowerShell | Secure transport |
| Outbound (to EXO) | Smart host | contoso-com.mail.protection.outlook.com | EAC / PowerShell | Route to EXO |

| Outbound (to EXO) | Address spaces | contoso.com | EAC / PowerShell | Determines routing |
