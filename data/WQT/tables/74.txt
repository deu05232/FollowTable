| 1970 | 25 Jul 2005 | Ena Sharples and Minnie Caldwell ask Cyril Turpin to help get Albert Tatlock out of his house but they did not realise that he would do it by breaking into the place. Bill Gregory tells Elsie Tanner that Phyllis is dead and he is buying a bar in Portugal. He asks her to marry him. Elsie marries Alan Howard . Ray Langton has an affair with Audrey Fleming . Joe Donnelli confesses his role in Steve Tanner 's murder to Irma Barlow , and his downward spiral leads to a dramatic Christmas showdown with her father Stan Ogden and Minnie. |

Ena Sharples refuses to go to Blackpool with Minnie Caldwell and Dave, causing Minnie to call Ena a bigot. When Annie leaves for a day, Lucille Hewitt and Bet Lynch go-go dance on top of the bar for the regulars. |
| 1972 | 19 Sep 2005 | Emily Nugent and Ernest Bishop 's wedding; Billy attempts to sell The Rovers behind his mother Annie 's back; Len Fairclough , Elsie Tanner and Rita Sullivan enjoy a night on the town; Stan Ogden is accused of peeping on ladies undressing; Alf Roberts ' feelings for Maggie Cooke are complicated after the death of his wife; Stan drinks in the pub Olympics despite having alcohol poisoning ; Rita gets into a fight at the Capricorn after a woman calls her a bitch; Elsie realises that Alan is developing an addiction to alcohol as the residents perform a Christmas show in the Select. |

| 1976 | 16 Jan 2006 | A man attempts to keep Gail Potter hostage in her flat until Emily Bishop and Tricia Hopkins call the police and have the man arrested; Stan Ogden attempts to hide his unlicensed TV and breaks it, just as Hilda returns home with the licence; Stan and Albert Tatlock are locked in The Rovers cellar and have free drinks; The gossip on the Street is that Deirdre Langton is pregnant — until she sets the busybodies straight; Ernest and Emily's financial troubles are exacerbated when he is refused a loan by the bank; Ernest agrees to play musical accompaniment to a stripper and Emily walks in whilst she is practising her routine; Len Fairclough threatens to give Rita Sullivan the sack and she hits him, knocking him unconscious; Handel returns to the Street and informs Ena Sharples that Minnie Caldwell will be living at Whaley Bridge permanently, whilst Hilda curses her husband and their miserable lives after finding that Stan stole part of the Christmas money. |
