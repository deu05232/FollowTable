# Adult Blood Pressure Thresholds by Age Band (Age-Adjusted Targets Rather Than Standard Categories)

| Age band | Suggested SBP target (mm Hg) | Suggested DBP target (mm Hg) | Label used in this table | Comment |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| 18–39 | < 120 | < 80 | "Target" | prevention focus |
| 40–49 | < 125 | < 80 | "Target" | allow slightly higher SBP |
| 50–59 | < 130 | < 80 | "Target" | comorbidity-dependent |
| 60–69 | < 135 | < 85 | "Target" | age-related arterial stiffness |
| 70–79 | < 140 | < 85 | "Target" | individualized |
| ≥ 80 | < 150 | < 90 | "Target" | frailty considerations |
| Any age | ≥ 160 | ≥ 100 | "Very high" | consider urgent review |
