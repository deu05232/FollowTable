# Pesticide Types and What They Control (Classification Table)

| Control Category | Example Products/Substances | Target Organisms | Typical Use Sites |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Insecticides | Pyrethrins, Neem oil | Insects (aphids, beetles) | Vegetable gardens, ornamentals |
| Repellents | DEET, Picaridin | Mosquitoes, ticks | Human skin/clothing |
| Fungicides | Copper hydroxide, Sulfur | Fungi (mildews, blights) | Fruit/veg crops |
| Herbicides | Glyphosate, Pelargonic acid | Weeds | Paths, beds (spot use) |
| Rodenticides | Bromadiolone | Rodents | Buildings, outbuildings |
| Molluscicides | Iron phosphate | Slugs/snails | Beds, borders |
| Nematicides | Fluensulfone | Nematodes | Soil in intensive production |
| Miticides | Abamectin | Mites | Greenhouses, fruit trees |
| Attractants | Pheromone lures | Specific insects | Traps/monitoring |
