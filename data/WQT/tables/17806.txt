# # Military Retirement Pay Plans — Calculation Basis (Quick View)

| Retirement / Disability Plan | Basis |
| --- | --- |
| Final Pay | Uses final basic pay at retirement |
| High-36 | Uses the average basic pay of the highest 36 months |
| CBS/REDUX | Uses High-36 average with a reduced multiplier under the redux option |
| BRS (Blended Retirement System) | Uses High-36 average with a smaller pension multiplier plus TSP component |
| Temporary Disability Retired List (TDRL) | Uses disability rating method or years-of-service method, whichever is more favorable |
| Permanent Disability Retirement | Uses disability formula or years-of-service formula as applicable |
