# Syndromes with Known Cytogenetic Findings Listed Only as Abnormality Type (No Band-Level Locus)

| Condition | Reported chromosomal change (coarse) | Cytogenetic class | Common clinical findings |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cri-du-chat syndrome | Deletion of chromosome 5 | Terminal deletion | High-pitched cry, microcephaly |
| Williams syndrome | Microdeletion on chromosome 7 | Microdeletion syndrome | Elfin facies, supravalvular stenosis |
| DiGeorge syndrome | Microdeletion on chromosome 22 | Microdeletion syndrome | Conotruncal defects, immunodeficiency |
| Prader–Willi syndrome | Imprinting defect on chromosome 15 | Imprinting/UPD | Hyperphagia, hypotonia |
| Angelman syndrome | Imprinting defect on chromosome 15 | Imprinting/UPD | Ataxia, seizures, happy demeanor |
| Wolf–Hirschhorn syndrome | Deletion of chromosome 4 | Terminal deletion | Growth delay, “Greek warrior helmet” facies |
| Smith–Magenis syndrome | Deletion on chromosome 17 | Interstitial deletion | Sleep disturbance, self-injury |
| Miller–Dieker syndrome | Deletion on chromosome 17 | Contiguous gene deletion | Lissencephaly, seizures |
