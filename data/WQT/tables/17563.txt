# Zinc-Friendly Eating Rules (Allowed vs. Avoided Foods)

| Rule category | Allowed foods | Avoided foods | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shellfish | Oysters, crab, shrimp | Breaded/fried shellfish | Processing may reduce nutrient density |
| Red meat | Beef, lamb | Processed meats | Sodium and additives |
| Dairy | Cheese, yogurt | Sweetened yogurt | Added sugar |
| Legumes | Chickpeas, lentils | Raw beans | Antinutrients/food safety |
| Nuts & seeds | Pumpkin seeds, cashews | Candy-coated nuts | Added sugar/fats |
| Whole grains | Oats, whole wheat | Refined white bread | Lower micronutrient density |
| Supplements | Zinc gluconate (if prescribed) | Mega-dose zinc | Risk of copper deficiency |
| Beverages | Water, unsweetened tea | Alcohol excess | May impair absorption |
