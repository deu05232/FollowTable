# Opened Condiments and Sauces  Fridge Life (Not Cooked Leftovers)

| Condiment/Sauce | Typical Package Status | Refrigerator (After Opening) | Freezer | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ketchup | Opened bottle | 6–12 months | Not recommended | Quality-focused |
| Mustard | Opened jar | 12 months | Not recommended | Flavor fades over time |
| Mayonnaise | Opened jar | 2–3 months | Not recommended | Discard if separated/odor |
| Soy sauce | Opened bottle | 1–2 years | Not needed | High salt preserves |
| BBQ sauce | Opened bottle | 4–6 months | 2–3 months | Sugar may crystallize |
| Salad dressing | Opened bottle | 1–3 months | Not recommended | Emulsions can break |
| Hot sauce | Opened bottle | 1–3 years | Not needed | Vinegar-based |
| Jam/jelly | Opened jar | 6–12 months | 6 months | Mold risk once contaminated |
