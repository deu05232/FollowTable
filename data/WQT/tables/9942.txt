# Egyptian Mythological Realms and Their Associated Deities

| Realm/Place | Description | Deities commonly associated | Typical texts/contexts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Duat | Underworld/night journey realm | Osiris, Anubis, Ra | Book of the Dead; Amduat |
| Aaru (Field of Reeds) | Ideal afterlife landscape | Osiris, Ma'at | Funerary spells; judgment scenes |
| Nun | Primordial waters | Nun, Naunet | Creation accounts |
| Benben mound | Primordial emergence point | Atum, Ra | Heliopolitan theology |
| Heliopolis | Major cult city | Ra, Atum, Ennead | Solar cult traditions |
| Thebes | Political-religious center | Amun, Mut, Khonsu | New Kingdom state religion |
| Abydos | Funerary pilgrimage hub | Osiris, Isis | Mysteries of Osiris |
| Kom Ombo | River temple town | Sobek, Horus | Ptolemaic temple reliefs |
