# Egyptian deities and their domains (quick reference)

| Deity | Domain / role |
|---|---|
| Thoth | Writing, knowledge, wisdom, the moon |
| Set | Chaos, storms, disorder, deserts |
| Sobek | Crocodiles, the Nile, military power |
| Taweret | Childbirth, fertility, protection of mothers |
| Shu | Air, wind, separation of sky and earth |
| Tefnut | Moisture, rain, dew |
| Wadjet | Protection of Lower Egypt, cobra goddess |
| Nekhbet | Protection of Upper Egypt, vulture goddess |
