# Cross-reference  Common Yoga Pose Names (English) with Sanskrit Transliteration

| English Pose Name | Sanskrit (Transliteration) | Notes / Common Variations |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Downward-Facing Dog | Adho Mukha Svanasana | Often shortened to “Down Dog” |
| Upward-Facing Dog | Urdhva Mukha Svanasana | Distinct from Cobra (Bhujangasana) |
| Cobra Pose | Bhujangasana | Low backbend; elbows may stay bent |
| Child's Pose | Balasana | Sometimes called “Extended Child’s Pose” |
| Mountain Pose | Tadasana | Foundational standing posture |
| Triangle Pose | Trikonasana | Commonly practiced as “Utthita Trikonasana” |
| Extended Side Angle Pose | Utthita Parsvakonasana | “Utthita” = extended |
| Warrior I | Virabhadrasana I | One of three classic Warrior forms |
| Warrior II | Virabhadrasana II | Front knee stacked over ankle |
| Warrior III | Virabhadrasana III | Balance posture; torso parallel to floor |
| Tree Pose | Vrksasana | Balancing; foot on inner thigh/calf |
| Bridge Pose | Setu Bandha Sarvangasana | Sometimes practiced dynamically |
| Plank Pose | Phalakasana | Foundation for vinyasa transitions |
| Corpse Pose | Savasana | Final relaxation posture |
