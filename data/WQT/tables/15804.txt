# Child Restraint and Booster Seat Recommendations by Age Group (General Guidance)

| Child Age/Size Group | Recommended Restraint Type | Typical Seating Position | Transition Trigger | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Newborn–12 months | Rear-facing infant seat | Rear seat | Outgrows height/weight limit | Keep rear-facing as long as possible |
| 1–3 years | Rear-facing convertible seat | Rear seat | Reaches seat limit | Many children remain rear-facing to age 2+ |
| 4–7 years | Forward-facing harness seat | Rear seat | Outgrows harness | Use tether if available |
| 8–12 years | Belt-positioning booster | Rear seat | Fits adult belt properly | Lap belt low on hips, shoulder belt on chest |
| 13–15 years | Adult seat belt | Rear seat preferred | Proper belt fit | Avoid shoulder belt behind back |
| 16+ years | Adult seat belt | Any seat | — | Consistent belt use recommended |
| Special needs | Specialized restraint | As directed | Clinical guidance | Consult medical professional |
