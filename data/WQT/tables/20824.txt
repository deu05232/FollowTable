# Concerta (methylphenidate ER, OROS) — Age-Stratified Dosing Guide (Initiation, Titration, Maximums)

| Population (Age) | Initial Dose (Once Daily, AM) | Titration / Adjustment | Typical Effective Range | Stated Maximum / Upper Limit | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Children (6–12 years) | 18 mg/day | Increase by 18 mg/day at ~weekly intervals based on response/tolerability | 18–54 mg/day | 54 mg/day | Swallow whole; do not crush/chew/split |
| Adolescents (13–17 years) | 18 mg/day | Increase by 18 mg/day at ~weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | 72 mg/day (and do not exceed 2 mg/kg/day) | Monitor weight, appetite, sleep |
| Adults (18–65 years) | 18 mg/day or 36 mg/day | Increase by 18 mg/day at ~weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | 72 mg/day | Consider lower start if sensitive to stimulants |
| Children (under 6 years) | Not established | — | — | — | Safety/efficacy not established; use not recommended |
| Older adults (over 65 years) | Not established | — | — | — | Limited data; if used, consider cautious titration and monitoring |
| Any age (renal/hepatic impairment) | No specific adjustment provided | Individualize | — | — | Use clinical judgment; monitor for adverse effects |
