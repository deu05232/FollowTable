# Wine Varietal Body & Flavor Chart (Whites & Aromatics)

| Grape Variety | Typical Flavor Notes | Body (Descriptive) | Common Secondary Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pinot Grigio (Pinot Gris, light style) | lemon, green apple, pear | light | minerality, white flowers |
| Albariño | lime, peach, grapefruit | light–medium | saline, stone, floral tones |
| Sauvignon Blanc | gooseberry, lime, passion fruit | light–medium | grass, herbal notes, flinty mineral |
| Riesling (dry) | green apple, citrus, white peach | light | jasmine, slate/mineral, high acidity |
| Chenin Blanc | quince, apple, honey (subtle) | medium | wool, chamomile, mineral |
| Viognier | apricot, peach, orange blossom | medium–full | oily texture, ginger, spice |
| Chardonnay (unoaked) | lemon, apple, melon | medium | chalky mineral, fresh cream |
| Chardonnay (oaked) | baked apple, pineapple, vanilla | full | butter, toast, caramelized oak |
