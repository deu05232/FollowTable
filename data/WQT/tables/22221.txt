# Compact Reference  Differentiation Under Algebraic Operations (Including Multi-term Extensions)

| Operation type | Expression form | Differentiation rule | Scope / extension |
|---|---|---|---|
| Linearity (two terms) | \(a f(x) + b g(x)\) | \(\frac{d}{dx}[a f + b g]=a f' + b g'\) | \(a,b\) constants; combines constant multiple + sum rule |
| Linearity (many terms) | \(\sum_{k=1}^n a_k f_k(x)\) | \(\left(\sum_{k=1}^n a_k f_k\right)'=\sum_{k=1}^n a_k f_k'\) | Works term-by-term |
| Negation | \(-f(x)\) | \(\frac{d}{dx}[-f]= -f'\) | Useful for simplifying differences |
| Product (two factors) | \(f(x)g(x)\) | \((fg)'=f'g+fg'\) | Core product rule |
| Product (constant times function) | \(c\,f(x)\) | \((cf)'=c f'\) | Viewed as product where \(c' = 0\) |
| Product (three factors) | \(f(x)g(x)h(x)\) | \((fgh)'=f'gh+fg'h+fgh'\) | Extension by repeated product rule |
| Quotient | \(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\) | \(\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)'=\frac{f'g-fg'}{g^2}\) | Requires \(g\neq 0\) |
| Quotient as product | \(f(x)\cdot[g(x)]^{-1}\) | \((f g^{-1})' = f' g^{-1} + f (g^{-1})'\) | Often simplifies work with chain rule |
| Composition (chain) | \(y=f(u),\ u=g(x)\) | \(\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{dy}{du}\frac{du}{dx}\) | Same chain rule expressed via intermediates |
| Ratio of compositions | \(\frac{f(g(x))}{h(x)}\) | \(\left(\frac{f(g)}{h}\right)'=\frac{f'(g)g'\,h - f(g)h'}{h^2}\) | Illustrates combining chain + quotient (still a rule pattern) |
