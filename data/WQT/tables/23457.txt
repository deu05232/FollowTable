# Limousine Hourly Pricing by Occasion and Limo Style (With Gratuity and Overtime Notes)

| Limo style | Typical hourly rate (USD) | Prom/School event typical rate (USD/hr) | Wedding typical rate (USD/hr) | Night-out / club typical rate (USD/hr) | Notes (gratuity, overtime, extras) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Standard stretch limo | $100–$170 | $120–$190 | $110–$180 | $100–$170 | 15–20% gratuity commonly added; 3–5 hr minimum on prom dates |
| Stretch SUV limo | $130–$240 | $150–$260 | $140–$250 | $130–$240 | overtime often billed per 30 min; premium on Saturdays/holidays |
| Stretch Hummer limo | $180–$330 | $200–$360 | $190–$340 | $180–$330 | cleaning fee may apply if food/drinks allowed; deposit sometimes required |
| Party bus (medium) | $150–$280 | $170–$320 | $160–$300 | $150–$280 | per-stop fees sometimes charged; gratuity added to base + fuel surcharge |
| Party bus (large) | $200–$380 | $220–$420 | $210–$400 | $200–$380 | higher minimum hours (often 4–6); driver tip standard |
| Sprinter limo van | $120–$220 | $140–$240 | $130–$230 | $120–$220 | good for smaller groups; tolls/parking billed separately |
| Luxury sedan / black car | $70–$130 | $80–$140 | $75–$135 | $70–$130 | airport fees may apply; typically 2–3 hr minimum |
