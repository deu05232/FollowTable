|  | Maclachlan hunting tartan, first published in 1893. Maclachlan hunting , or Old MacLachlan . [32] [34] This sett appears in the Collection of the Highland Society, 1812 . Although one of the oldest tartans this sett has never been very popular with the clan. [32] |
|  | Old Maclachlan tartan. Small MacLachlan , or Old Maclachlan , also known as Moncreiffe . [32] This tartan was in the Wilsons of Bannockburn pattern book of 1790, listed as # 66. Over time the tartan had become associated with the Maclachlans. In 1974 the chief of Clan Moncreiffe , Sir Iain Moncreiffe of that Ilk , asked the 24th chief of Clan Maclachlan, Madam Maclachlan of Maclachlan, to be assigned the right to use this tartan, as the colours of it matched the colours in his Coat of Arms . Today it is known as a Moncreiffe tartan. [35] [36] |
