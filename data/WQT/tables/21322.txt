# Daily Calorie Target Options for Weight Loss (with BMI Eligibility)

| Daily Calories | Suggested Deficit | BMI Range Eligible | Notes |
|---:|---:|---|---|
| 1,200 | Aggressive | 30.0+ | Often used for rapid loss under supervision |
| 1,400 | High | 27.0–29.9 | May be too low for active individuals |
| 1,600 | Moderate | 25.0–26.9 | Common starting target |
| 1,800 | Mild | 23.0–24.9 | For small deficit or maintenance transition |
| 2,000 | Minimal | 18.5–22.9 | Typically not a weight-loss level for many |
| 2,200 | Maintenance+ | <18.5 | Not recommended for weight loss |
| 2,400 | Surplus | Any | Generally supports gain unless very active |
