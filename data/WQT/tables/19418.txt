# Resolution Standards Used in Cameras  Photo Output vs Video Scan Modes

| Mode | Label | Pixel Dimensions (W × H) | Scan / Frame Rate | Typical Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Photo | 12 MP still | 4000 × 3000 | — | Stills |
| Photo | 24 MP still | 6000 × 4000 | — | Stills |
| Video | 720p HD | 1280 × 720 | 60p / 30p | Web video |
| Video | 1080p FHD | 1920 × 1080 | 60i / 60p / 30p | General video |
| Video | 4K UHD | 3840 × 2160 | 60p / 30p | High detail video |
| Video | DCI 4K | 4096 × 2160 | 24p / 30p | Cinema workflows |
| Video | 8K UHD | 7680 × 4320 | 30p | High-end capture |
| Video | Slow motion | 1920 × 1080 | 120p / 240p | Action analysis |
