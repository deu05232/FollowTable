# III IV VI Mapping for Extraocular Muscles (Muscle-by-Muscle) Including Functional Action Cue

| Extraocular muscle | Main eye movement action (cue) | Motor cranial nerve | CN III division (if applicable) | Mnemonic anchor |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Medial rectus | Adduction | CN III (Oculomotor) | Inferior division | “All but LR & SO” |
| Lateral rectus | Abduction | CN VI (Abducens) | — | LR = 6 |
| Superior rectus | Elevation (esp. abducted), intorsion | CN III (Oculomotor) | Superior division | Part of “rest = III” |
| Inferior rectus | Depression (esp. abducted), extorsion | CN III (Oculomotor) | Inferior division | Part of “rest = III” |
| Superior oblique | Intorsion + depression (esp. adducted) | CN IV (Trochlear) | — | SO = 4 |
| Inferior oblique | Extorsion + elevation (esp. adducted) | CN III (Oculomotor) | Inferior division | Part of “rest = III” |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Eyelid elevation | CN III (Oculomotor) | Superior division | Ptosis with CN III palsy |
