# # Military Retirement Plans — Eligibility Notes

| Retirement Plan | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Final Pay | Applies to members who entered service before the High-36 system |
| High-36 | Retirement pay is computed from the average of the highest 36 months of basic pay |
| CBS/REDUX | Includes a reduced multiplier with a bonus option at mid-career |
| Disability | Retirement may be based on disability rating or length of service |
