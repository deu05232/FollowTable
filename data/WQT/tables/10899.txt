# EITC Eligibility Rules by Family Situation (Non-Income Requirements Emphasized)

| Family situation | Minimum age (no qualifying children) | Residency requirement | Valid SSN required | Investment income limit | Disallowed if filing status is | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| No qualifying children | 25–64 | U.S. resident alien or citizen for full year | Yes | Must be below annual cap | Married Filing Separately | Must live in U.S. more than half the year |
| 1 qualifying child | Any age | Child must live with taxpayer > 6 months | Yes | Must be below annual cap | Married Filing Separately | Child must meet relationship test |
| 2 qualifying children | Any age | Child(ren) must have valid SSNs | Yes | Must be below annual cap | Married Filing Separately | Tie-breaker rules may apply |
| 3+ qualifying children | Any age | Child(ren) must be under age limit unless disabled | Yes | Must be below annual cap | Married Filing Separately | Additional documentation recommended |
| Foster child case | Any age | Placement by authorized agency required | Yes | Must be below annual cap | Married Filing Separately | Must be treated as qualifying child |
| Separated spouses | 25–64 if no children | Special rules may allow non-joint filing in some cases | Yes | Must be below annual cap | — | Consider “considered unmarried” tests |
