# MPEG-4 Part 10 (H.264) levels and constraints

| Level | Max resolution (typical) | Max bitrate (approx.) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 3.0 | 720x480 @ 30fps | ~10 Mbps | Older devices often target this |
| 4.0 | 1920x1080 @ 30fps | ~20 Mbps | Common for Full HD |
| 4.1 | 1920x1080 @ 60fps | ~50 Mbps | Higher-performance decoders |
