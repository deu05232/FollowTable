# 3D Point-Group Rotation Orders for Common Solids

| Solid (3D) | Schoenflies symbol | Hermann–Mauguin | Rotational symmetry order (proper rotations) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Regular tetrahedron | T | 23 | 12 | Pure rotations only (no mirrors) |
| Cube / regular octahedron | O | 432 | 24 | Same rotation group for cube and octahedron |
| Regular icosahedron / dodecahedron | I | 532 | 60 | Highest-order Platonic rotation group |
| Square pyramid | C4v | 4mm | 4 | Includes vertical mirror planes in full group |
| Triangular prism | D3h | \-6m2 | 6 | Has 3-fold axis plus 2-fold axes |
| Cylinder | C∞v | ∞m | ∞ | Continuous rotational symmetry |
| Cone | C∞v | ∞m | ∞ | Continuous around axis |
| Rectangular box (cuboid) | D2h | mmm | 4 | Three perpendicular 2-fold axes (proper rotations) |
