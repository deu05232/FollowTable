# A Level Subject Choices (Sixth Form) — side-by-side by subject area

| Subject area | Common A Level subjects (choices) | Typical related combinations |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sciences | Biology; Chemistry; Physics | Bio+Chem; Phys+Math |
| Mathematics | Mathematics; Further Mathematics; Statistics (where available) | Math+FM; Math+Physics |
| Computing | Computer Science | CS+Math |
| Humanities | History; Geography; Politics; Religious Studies | History+Politics; Geography+Biology |
| Social Sciences | Psychology; Sociology; Economics | Psych+Bio; Econ+Math |
| Languages | French; German; Spanish; Chinese | Language + another humanity |
| English | English Literature; English Language; English Language & Literature | English + History |
| Arts | Fine Art; Photography; Graphic Design; Textiles | Art + Media or English |
| Performing Arts | Music; Drama & Theatre; Dance | Music + Math/Physics (for some pathways) |
| Business | Business; Economics | Business+Econ (often discouraged but possible) |
| Design & Technology | Design & Technology: Product Design | DT + Math/Physics |
| Sport & Health | Physical Education; Health & Social Care | PE + Biology |
| Classical Subjects | Classical Civilisation; Latin; Greek | Classics + History/English |
