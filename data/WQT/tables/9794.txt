# Vital Signs Quick Reference (Includes Blood Pressure Alongside Other Measures)

| Context | Heart rate (bpm) | Respiratory rate (breaths/min) | Temperature (°C) | Oxygen saturation (%) | Blood pressure (mm Hg) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Resting adult (general) | 60–100 | 12–20 | 36.1–37.2 | 95–100 | ~90–120 / 60–80 |
| Mild exertion | 90–140 | 20–30 | 36.5–37.8 | 93–100 | Variable; may rise |
| Fever | 80–120 | 16–24 | 38.0–40.0 | 92–100 | Often unchanged |
| Dehydration | 90–130 | 14–24 | 36.0–37.5 | 94–100 | May drop on standing |
| Pregnancy (2nd–3rd trimester) | 70–110 | 12–20 | 36.1–37.2 | 95–100 | Often lower mid-pregnancy |
| Older adult baseline | 60–100 | 12–20 | 36.0–37.2 | 94–99 | ~110–140 / 60–90 |
| Emergency flag (example) | >130 | >30 | <35 or >40 | <90 | SBP <90 or >180 |
| Note | — | — | — | — | This table is not a BP category cutoff chart |
