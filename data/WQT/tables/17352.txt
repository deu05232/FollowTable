# Alt codes for punctuation and keyboard symbols

| Symbol | Description | Alt code | Category |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| § | Section sign | Alt 0167 | Legal |
| ¶ | Pilcrow sign | Alt 0182 | Typography |
| • | Bullet | Alt 0149 | Typography |
| … | Ellipsis | Alt 0133 | Typography |
| “ | Left double quotation mark | Alt 0147 | Quotes |
| ” | Right double quotation mark | Alt 0148 | Quotes |
| — | Em dash | Alt 0151 | Dashes |
| – | En dash | Alt 0150 | Dashes |
