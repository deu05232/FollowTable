# Canon Order Overview  Major Sections and Book Counts by Tradition

| Tradition | Old Testament Sections (Order) | OT Book Count | Deuterocanon Included | New Testament Sections | NT Book Count |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---:|
| Jewish Tanakh | Torah → Nevi'im → Ketuvim | 24 | No | N/A | 0 |
| Protestant | Pentateuch → History → Poetry/Wisdom → Prophets | 39 | No | Gospels → Acts → Epistles → Revelation | 27 |
| Roman Catholic | Pentateuch → History (+Deuterocanon) → Wisdom → Prophets | 46 | Yes | Gospels → Acts → Epistles → Revelation | 27 |
| Eastern Orthodox | Pentateuch → History (+Anagignoskomena) → Wisdom → Prophets | 49 | Yes (varies) | Gospels → Acts → Epistles → Revelation | 27 |
| Ethiopian Orthodox | Broader OT collection (varies) | 50+ | Yes (expanded) | Broader NT collection (varies) | 27+ |
| Syriac Tradition | OT with Peshitta tradition variants | 39–46 | Varies | NT sometimes excludes certain books historically | 22–27 |
| Coptic Tradition | OT with deuterocanonical usage | 46+ | Yes | Standard NT set | 27 |
