# Career Paths for Economics Majors  Entry-Level Job Titles, Core Duties, and Typical Work Location

| Category | Job Title | What the Role Does (Practical Summary) | Typical Work Base / Location Context |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Data & Research | Economic Research Assistant | Supports economists by cleaning datasets, running regressions, and summarizing findings in briefs/slides | Often based in government agencies, think tanks, universities; usually major metro areas; sometimes hybrid |
| Data & Research | Junior Data Analyst (Economics/Policy) | Builds dashboards, analyzes trends (inflation, employment, prices), and answers ad-hoc questions for stakeholders | Common in cities with analytics hubs; hybrid/remote more common in private sector |
| Finance & Markets | Financial Analyst (FP&A) | Forecasts revenue/expense, builds budgets, and explains variances to business leaders | Typically office-based at corporate HQs; available in most large cities; hybrid common |
| Finance & Markets | Credit Analyst | Evaluates borrower financials, sets risk ratings, and recommends credit limits/loan terms | Banks and credit unions; roles concentrated in regional banking centers; mostly in-office |
| Public Sector | Policy Analyst (Economic Policy) | Reviews proposed policies, estimates impacts, writes memos, and helps design program evaluation | Usually capital cities or state hubs; some travel for stakeholder meetings |
| Public Sector | Budget Analyst | Tracks agency spending, builds budget proposals, and monitors compliance with funding rules | Government offices (city/county/state/federal); generally on-site in administrative centers |
| Consulting | Economic Consulting Analyst | Works on damages/market analysis, compiles evidence, runs models, and drafts expert-report materials | Often in large metros; project-based hours; hybrid possible but client deadlines drive location |
| Business Ops | Operations Analyst | Identifies process bottlenecks, analyzes KPI drivers, and recommends efficiency improvements | Large employers across industries; mix of HQ and operations sites; may require site visits |
| Real Estate & Housing | Housing/Real Estate Market Analyst | Tracks rents, vacancies, pricing, and prepares market reports for investment/development decisions | Concentrated in real estate-heavy metros; often office-based with local market focus |
| International & Trade | Trade Compliance / Trade Analyst (Junior) | Helps classify goods, supports customs documentation, and analyzes tariff/landed-cost impacts | Port cities/logistics hubs; may be based near distribution centers or corporate HQ |
| Social Impact | Program Evaluation Assistant | Designs surveys, cleans outcomes data, and summarizes whether programs meet goals | Nonprofits/NGOs and public agencies; location varies; field data collection can require travel |
| Sales & Commercial | Pricing Analyst | Analyzes price elasticity, monitors competitors, and supports discount strategy | Common in retail, SaaS, manufacturing; HQ-centric; hybrid common |
| Banking & Client | Relationship Banking Associate (Commercial) | Preps client materials, monitors accounts, and supports loan/treasury product onboarding | Branch or commercial banking offices; tied to local business markets; mostly in-person |
| Academia (Optional) | Economics Teaching Assistant (Graduate/Undergrad Programs) | Leads recitations, grades, holds office hours, and supports course instruction | Campus-based in college towns/metros; typically on-site during term |
