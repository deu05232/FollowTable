# Florida Felony Degree Penalty Schedule  Maximum Imprisonment and Maximum Fines

| Felony level (Florida) | Typical label / scope | Maximum term of imprisonment | Maximum fine |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| Capital felony | Highest level felony | Death or life imprisonment | N/A (fine not typically specified for capital level) |
| Life felony | “Life” level offenses | Life imprisonment | $15,000 |
| First-degree felony | Standard 1st degree | Up to 30 years | $10,000 |
| First-degree felony (punishable by life) | Enhanced 1st degree category | Life imprisonment | $10,000 |
| Second-degree felony | Standard 2nd degree | Up to 15 years | $10,000 |
| Third-degree felony | Standard 3rd degree | Up to 5 years | $5,000 |
| Third-degree felony (habitual felony offender enhancement) | If sentenced as HFO (overview category) | Up to 10 years | $5,000 |
| Third-degree felony (habitual violent felony offender enhancement) | If sentenced as HVFO (overview category) | Up to 10 years | $5,000 |
