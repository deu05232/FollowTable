# Bird Terminology  Male, Female, Young, and Nesting Context

| Bird | Male Term | Female Term | Young Term | Nesting/Young Stage Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Swan | Cob | Pen | Cygnet | Downy chick in first weeks |
| Goose | Gander | Goose | Gosling | Often precocial |
| Duck | Drake | Hen | Duckling | Brooded by hen |
| Turkey | Tom | Hen | Poult | Ground-nesting |
| Chicken | Rooster | Hen | Chick | Brooder hen or incubator |
| Pheasant | Cock | Hen | Chick | Precocial |
| Peafowl | Peacock | Peahen | Peachick | Follows hen shortly after hatch |
