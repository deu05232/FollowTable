| Surface Pro 4 | 2015-10 | 2015-10 | New 12.3-inch display | Windows 10 Pro |
| Surface Pro (5th Gen) | 2017-05 | 2017-06 | Refinement, fanless options | Windows 10 Pro |
| Surface Pro 6 | 2018-10 | 2018-10 | Quad-core CPUs | Windows 10 Pro |

| Surface Pro 8 | 2021-09 | 2021-10 | 13-inch, Thunderbolt 4 | Windows 11 |
| Surface Pro 9 | 2022-10 | 2022-10 | Intel + ARM options | Windows 11 |
| Surface Pro 10 | 2024-03 | 2024-04 | Business-focused refresh | Windows 11 |
