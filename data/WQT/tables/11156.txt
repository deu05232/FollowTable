# Financial Aid Package Components (Typical Annual Student Funding Mix)

| Aid Component | Description | Typical Amount | Eligibility Basis |
| --- | --- | ---:| --- |
| Federal Pell Grant | Need-based federal grant | $4,200 | FAFSA EFC/SAI |
| State Grant | Need-based state program | $2,000 | Residency + FAFSA |
| Institutional Grant | University-funded grant | $6,500 | Need-based |
| Merit Scholarship | Academic achievement | $8,000 | GPA/Test scores |
| Work-Study | Part-time campus job | $2,500 | Need-based |
| Federal Direct Loan | Student loan (subsidized/unsubsidized) | $5,500 | FAFSA |
| Parent PLUS Loan | Parent borrowing option | $10,000 | Credit-based |
