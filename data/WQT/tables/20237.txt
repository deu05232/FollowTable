# Aneuploidy Types and Karyotype Patterns (Selected Examples)

| Aneuploidy type | Karyotype example | Common label | Typical viability/notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trisomy | 47,XX,+21 | Trisomy 21 | Most common viable autosomal trisomy |
| Trisomy | 47,XY,+18 | Trisomy 18 | High infant mortality |
| Trisomy | 47,XX,+13 | Trisomy 13 | Severe congenital anomalies |
| Monosomy | 45,X | Monosomy X | Turner syndrome (sex chromosome monosomy) |
| Sex chromosome trisomy | 47,XXY | XXY | Klinefelter syndrome |
| Sex chromosome trisomy | 47,XXX | XXX | Often mild phenotype |
| Mosaic aneuploidy | 45,X/46,XX | Mosaic X | Variable clinical presentation |
