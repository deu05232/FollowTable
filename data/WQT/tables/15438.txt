# Yoga Sequence Cheat Sheet  Multi-Pose Rows Instead of One Pose Per Row

| Sequence Step | English Name(s) | Sanskrit Name(s) | Category |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Mountain → Upward Salute | Tadasana → Urdhva Hastasana | Warm-up |
| 2 | Forward Fold → Half Lift | Uttanasana → Ardha Uttanasana | Standing |
| 3 | Plank → Four-Limbed Staff Pose | Phalakasana → Chaturanga Dandasana | Vinyasa |
| 4 | Upward-Facing Dog → Downward-Facing Dog | Urdhva Mukha Svanasana → Adho Mukha Svanasana | Backbend/Inversion |
| 5 | Warrior I → Warrior II → Reverse Warrior | Virabhadrasana I → Virabhadrasana II → Viparita Virabhadrasana | Standing |
| 6 | Triangle → Extended Side Angle | Trikonasana → Utthita Parsvakonasana | Standing |
| 7 | Seated Forward Bend → Bound Angle | Paschimottanasana → Baddha Konasana | Seated |
