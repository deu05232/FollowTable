# # Alt-code entry for currency and legal symbols (Windows)

| What you type on the keypad | Symbol you get | Name/description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Alt+0128 | € | Euro sign |
| Alt+0163 | £ | Pound sign |
| Alt+0165 | ¥ | Yen sign |
| Alt+0167 | § | Section sign |
| Alt+0182 | ¶ | Pilcrow/paragraph sign |
