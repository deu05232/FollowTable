# Angina Treatments Organized by Clinical Use Case (Not by Drug Class)

| Clinical Scenario | Recommended Medication (Brand) | Generic | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Acute chest pain relief | Nitrostat | nitroglycerin | sublingual, rapid onset |
| Chronic stable angina prevention | Imdur | isosorbide mononitrate | once daily long-acting nitrate |
| Persistent angina despite beta blocker | Ranexa | ranolazine | add-on therapy |
| Variant (Prinzmetal) angina | Cardizem | diltiazem | calcium channel blocker often used |
| Post-MI with angina | Toprol XL | metoprolol | cardioselective beta blocker |
| Angina with hyperlipidemia | Lipitor | atorvastatin | risk reduction rather than symptom relief |
| Angina with thrombosis risk | Plavix | clopidogrel | antiplatelet adjunct |
