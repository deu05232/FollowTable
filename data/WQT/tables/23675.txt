# Genetic Disorders Grouped by Inheritance Category (Condition Classification Only)

| Category | Disorders (examples) |
| --- | --- |
| Autosomal dominant | Marfan syndrome; Huntington disease; Achondroplasia; Neurofibromatosis type 1 |
| Autosomal recessive | Phenylketonuria; Tay–Sachs disease; Sickle cell disease; Cystic fibrosis |
| X-linked recessive | Hemophilia A; Duchenne muscular dystrophy; G6PD deficiency; Fabry disease |
| Mitochondrial | Leber hereditary optic neuropathy; MELAS; MERRF; NARP |
| Chromosomal aneuploidy | Down syndrome (Trisomy 21); Edwards syndrome (Trisomy 18); Patau syndrome (Trisomy 13); Turner syndrome (45,X) |
| Genomic imprinting | Prader–Willi syndrome; Angelman syndrome; Beckwith–Wiedemann syndrome; Silver–Russell syndrome |
