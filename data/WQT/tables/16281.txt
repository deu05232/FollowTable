# Battery Internal Resistance and Output Behavior Under Load (Typical)

| Battery type | Nominal voltage (V) | Typical internal resistance (mΩ) | Recommended continuous current (A) | Voltage sag at 1 A (V) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Alkaline AA (fresh) | 1.5 | 120–250 | 0.5–1 | 0.12–0.25 |
| NiMH AA (charged) | 1.2 | 20–40 | 5–10 | 0.02–0.04 |
| Lithium primary AA | 1.5 | 60–120 | 1–2 | 0.06–0.12 |
| CR2032 coin cell | 3.0 | 300–800 | 0.01–0.02 | 0.30–0.80 |
| 9V alkaline (PP3) | 9.0 | 1000–2000 | 0.1–0.2 | 1.0–2.0 |
| Li-ion 18650 (high-drain) | 3.6 | 15–30 | 10–20 | 0.015–0.030 |
| Lead–acid (12 V, small) | 12.0 | 10–20 | 20–50 | 0.01–0.02 |
| LiPo pouch (RC) | 3.7 | 5–15 | 20–60 | 0.005–0.015 |
