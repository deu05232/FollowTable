# Oxygen-Containing Functional Groups  Recognition Motif and IUPAC Naming Elements

| Functional group family (O-based) | Defining motif (generic) | Typical IUPAC suffix (as principal group) | Typical IUPAC prefix (as substituent) | Common shorthand / note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Alcohol (hydroxyl) | R–OH | -ol | hydroxy- | Includes primary/secondary/tertiary alcohols |
| Phenol (aryl hydroxyl) | Ar–OH | -ol (often as phenol name) | hydroxy- | Aromatic hydroxyl; distinct reactivity vs aliphatic |
| Ether | R–O–R' | (no standard suffix; named as alkoxyalkane) | alkoxy- (e.g., methoxy-) | Oxygen linking two carbon groups |
| Epoxide (oxirane ring) | 3-membered cyclic ether | epoxy- / oxirane (parent) | epoxy- | Cyclic ether subclass; very recognizable motif |
| Aldehyde | R–C(=O)H | -al | formyl- / oxo- | Terminal carbonyl with H |
| Ketone | R–C(=O)–R' | -one | oxo- | Internal carbonyl |
| Carboxylic acid | R–C(=O)OH | -oic acid | carboxy- | Contains both carbonyl and hydroxyl in same group |
| Ester | R–C(=O)OR' | -oate | alkoxycarbonyl- | Carboxylic acid derivative with –OR' |
| Acid anhydride | (R–C(=O))–O–(C(=O)–R') | -oic anhydride | (rarely used) | Two acyl groups linked by oxygen |
| Acyl (carboxylic) acid halide | R–C(=O)X | -oyl halide | halocarbonyl- / chloroformyl- etc. | Oxygen-containing due to carbonyl; X is halogen but group is acyl-oxygen motif |
| Peroxide | R–O–O–R' | peroxide (name class) | peroxy- | O–O bond is defining feature |
| Hydroperoxide | R–O–OH | hydroperoxide | hydroperoxy- | Peroxide with terminal –OOH |
| Carbonate (ester of carbonic acid) | RO–C(=O)–OR' | carbonate | (alkoxycarbonyl-) | Di-ester motif around carbonyl oxygen |
| Carboxylate (anion) | R–C(=O)O− | -oate (salt) | carboxylate- (contextual) | Conjugate base of carboxylic acid |
