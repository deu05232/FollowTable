# Meal-prep cooked dishes  fridge and freezer holding times

| Prepared dish (cooked) | Refrigerator time | Freezer time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked rice (leftover) | 4–6 days | up to 6 months |
| Soup (cooked) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Stew or chili (cooked) | 3–4 days | 4–6 months |
| Casserole (baked, cooked) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked curry (leftovers) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked pizza slices | 3–4 days | 1–2 months |
