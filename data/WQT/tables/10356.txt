# Notable Canadian shipwreck incidents (selected records)

| Vessel | Incident date | What happened |
| --- | --- | --- |
| SS Atlantic | 1873-04-01 | Ran aground in thick fog off Nova Scotia and broke apart.
| RMS Empress of Ireland | 1914-05-29 | Collided in the St. Lawrence River and sank rapidly.
| SS Caribou | 1942-10-14 | Torpedoed in the Cabot Strait and sank after an explosion.
| Barge Irving Whale | 1970-09-07 | Sank during towing in the Gulf of St. Lawrence during heavy seas. |
