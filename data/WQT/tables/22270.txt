# Raster Graphics Editors — Comparison Including Adobe and Non-Adobe Products

| Software | Vendor | Platform | License | Typical cost | Strengths | Weaknesses |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adobe Photoshop | Adobe | Windows/macOS | Proprietary | Subscription | Deep ecosystem, advanced retouching | Cost, learning curve |
| Adobe Photoshop Elements | Adobe | Windows/macOS | Proprietary | One-time purchase | Consumer-friendly tools | Less pro features |
| GIMP | Community | Windows/macOS/Linux | GPL | Free | Powerful, extensible | UX inconsistency |
| Affinity Photo | Serif | Windows/macOS/iPad | Proprietary | One-time purchase | Fast, modern UI | Smaller plugin ecosystem |
| Corel PHOTO-PAINT | Corel | Windows | Proprietary | Suite pricing | Integration with CorelDRAW | Less industry standard |
| Paint.NET | dotPDN | Windows | Freeware | Free | Lightweight, simple | Limited advanced tools |
| Krita | Krita Foundation | Windows/macOS/Linux | GPL | Free | Great for painting | Not focused on photo editing |
| Pixelmator Pro | Pixelmator Team | macOS | Proprietary | One-time purchase | macOS-native, ML tools | macOS only |
