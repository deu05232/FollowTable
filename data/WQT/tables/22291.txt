# # Autonomic neurotransmitters by division and synapse type

| Signaling location in the pathway | Main messenger used | Typical receptor family on the next cell | Notes on how signaling is terminated |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Preganglionic autonomic synapse (both divisions) | Acetylcholine | Nicotinic (neuronal) | Enzymatic breakdown in synaptic region |
| Parasympathetic postganglionic synapse at the effector | Acetylcholine | Muscarinic | Enzymatic breakdown near the effector |
| Sympathetic postganglionic synapse at most effectors | Norepinephrine | Adrenergic | Reuptake into nerve terminal and metabolism |
| Sympathetic signaling to adrenal medulla | Acetylcholine | Nicotinic (chromaffin) | Triggers release of circulating catecholamines |
