# # Differentiation reference with classification columns (continuity & monotonicity focus)

| Function | Derivative | Continuity category | Monotonicity category | Notes on shape |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| x^3 | 3x^2 | continuous everywhere | increasing everywhere | odd symmetry |
| sin(x) | cos(x) | continuous everywhere | neither | periodic |
| e^x | e^x | continuous everywhere | increasing everywhere | convex |
| 1/(1+x^2) | −2x/(1+x^2)^2 | continuous everywhere | decreases then increases | bell-like |
