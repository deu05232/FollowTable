# U.S. Social Security Full Retirement Age (FRA) by Birth-Year Bracket

| Birth year range | Full retirement age (years, months) | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| 1937 or earlier | 65y 0m | Legacy rule |
| 1938–1939 | 65y 2m–65y 4m | Steps up by 2 months |
| 1940–1941 | 65y 6m–65y 8m | Gradual increase |
| 1942 | 65y 10m | Near completion of first phase |
| 1943–1954 | 66y 0m | Flat period |
| 1955–1959 | 66y 2m–66y 10m | Increases by 2 months |
| 1960 or later | 67y 0m | Final FRA |
| Unknown / mixed | Varies | Use SSA charts for specifics |
