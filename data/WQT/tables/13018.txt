# Common Clinical Note Abbreviations (Assessment, Diagnoses, Body Systems)

| Category | Abbreviation | Expanded meaning (plain language) | Typical clinical context |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cardiovascular | HTN | Hypertension (high blood pressure) | Problem list, vitals review |
| Cardiovascular | CHF | Congestive heart failure | Assessment/plan, past medical history |
| Cardiovascular | MI | Myocardial infarction (heart attack) | History, discharge summaries |
| Respiratory | COPD | Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease | Problem list, pulmonary assessment |
| Respiratory | SOB | Shortness of breath | Symptoms/ROS, triage notes |
| Endocrine/Metabolic | DM | Diabetes mellitus | Problem list, A1c follow-up |
| Renal | CKD | Chronic kidney disease | Problem list, nephrology notes |
| Gastrointestinal | GERD | Gastroesophageal reflux disease (acid reflux) | HPI, medication rationale |
| Neurology | CVA | Cerebrovascular accident (stroke) | History, neuro exam |
| Infectious disease | UTI | Urinary tract infection | Assessment/plan, urinalysis review |
| Oncology | CA | Cancer / carcinoma (often requires context) | Imaging reports, history |
| Musculoskeletal | OA | Osteoarthritis | Problem list, joint pain evaluation |
