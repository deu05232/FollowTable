# Annual Pay by Social Work Role and Setting (Annual Only, US)

| Role | Common Work Setting | Typical Annual Salary (USD) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Case Manager (Social Services) | Nonprofit / Community agency | $42,000 - $58,000 | Often varies by grant funding and caseload size |
| Child Welfare Social Worker | State / County government | $50,000 - $72,000 | Government roles may include step increases |
| School Social Worker | Public school district | $54,000 - $82,000 | Frequently aligned to teacher salary schedules |
| Medical Social Worker | Hospital / Health system | $58,000 - $85,000 | Higher pay in acute-care and specialty hospitals |
| Behavioral Health Clinician (MSW) | Community mental health clinic | $52,000 - $74,000 | May differ by billable-hour expectations |
| Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) | Private practice / Group practice | $70,000 - $110,000 | Compensation depends on payer mix and panel size |
| Psychiatric Social Worker | Hospital psychiatry / County DMH | $66,000 - $98,000 | Often higher in high-cost metro areas |
| Substance Use Disorder Counselor (MSW track) | Treatment center | $48,000 - $70,000 | Pay varies with certifications and setting |
| Social Work Supervisor | Public agency / Large nonprofit | $65,000 - $95,000 | Typically requires experience + license |
| Program Director (Social Services) | Nonprofit / Public health | $75,000 - $115,000 | Budget and staff size strongly affect pay |
