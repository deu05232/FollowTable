| Kenya | Nairobi (capital) | National capital and largest city |
| Ethiopia | Addis Ababa (capital) | Also hosts African Union HQ |
| Tanzania | Dodoma (official/administrative) | Dar es Salaam remains a major commercial center |
| Morocco | Rabat (capital) | National capital |
| Nigeria | Abuja (capital) | Federal capital territory |
| Senegal | Dakar (capital) | National capital |
| South Africa | Pretoria (administrative) | Part of a three-capital arrangement |
| South Africa | Cape Town (legislative) | Parliament seat |
| South Africa | Bloemfontein (judicial) | Judiciary seat |
| Eswatini | Mbabane (administrative) | Lobamba is the legislative/royal seat |
| Eswatini | Lobamba (legislative/royal) | Parliament and monarchy locations |
