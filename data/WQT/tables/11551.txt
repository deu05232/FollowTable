# Pet Food for Small Pets (Rabbits, Guinea Pigs, Hamsters) — Feeding Guide

| Pet Species | Recommended Food | Form | Feeding Frequency | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Rabbit | Timothy hay + rabbit pellets | Hay + Pellets | Daily | Hay should be primary diet |
| Guinea Pig | Vitamin-C fortified pellets + hay | Pellets + Hay | Daily | Needs dietary vitamin C |
| Hamster | Seed mix + lab blocks | Mix + Blocks | Daily | Avoid sugary treats |
| Ferret | High-protein ferret kibble | Dry | Daily | Obligate carnivore |
| Parakeet | Pellet diet + limited seed | Pellets | Daily | Provide fresh water |
| Turtle | Aquatic turtle pellets | Pellets | 3–5x/week | Supplement with greens |
| Fish | Tropical flakes/pellets | Flakes/Pellets | 1–2x/day | Avoid overfeeding |
