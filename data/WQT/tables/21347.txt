# Glycemic Index Categories with Example Foods (Mostly Labels, Not Measured Item Values)

| GI category | GI range | Typical effect label | Example foods (non-exhaustive) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Very low | 0–35 | Minimal rise | nuts, seeds, leafy greens |
| Low | 36–55 | Gentle rise | most beans, many fruits |
| Medium | 56–69 | Moderate rise | basmati rice, table sugar |
| High | 70–85 | Sharp rise | white bread, many breakfast cereals |
| Very high | 86+ | Very sharp rise | glucose, some mashed potatoes |
| Mixed meals | varies | Depends on composition | pizza, sandwiches, burritos |
