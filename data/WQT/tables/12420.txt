# State-by-state limitation periods for contract and debt claims (oral vs written)

| State | Oral agreement / verbal contract | Written agreement / written contract | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| California | 2 years | 4 years | General contract time limits |
| Texas | 4 years | 4 years | Same limit for many contract actions |
| Illinois | 5 years | 10 years | Written contracts often longer |
| Florida | 4 years | 5 years | Distinguishes verbal vs written |
