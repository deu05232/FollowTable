# Entry-Level Roles for Economics Majors  What You’d Do and What Skills to Highlight

| Role (Economics-Focused) | Typical Entry Level Title Variants | Core Tasks (Economics-Driven) | Key Skills/Tools to Highlight | Common Next Step Roles |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Economic Research Assistant | Research Assistant (Economics); RA (Macro); Research Associate | Literature review, data cleaning, regression analysis, drafting memos for economists | Statistics, econometrics, Stata/R/Python, academic writing | Economist; Policy Analyst; PhD/masters track |
| Policy Analyst (Economic Policy) | Policy Analyst; Economic Policy Associate; Policy Research Analyst | Policy impact analysis, cost–benefit analysis, briefing notes, stakeholder analysis | Micro/macro, CBA, Excel, writing, presentation | Senior Policy Analyst; Program Manager; Economist |
| Government/Regulatory Analyst | Regulatory Analyst; Rate Analyst; Market Surveillance Analyst | Market monitoring, rule impact assessment, forecasting, compliance economics | Industrial organization basics, data analysis, SQL/Excel | Competition/utility economist; regulatory economist |
| Economic Consulting Analyst | Economic Analyst; Litigation Support Analyst; Associate Economist | Damages estimation, merger analysis support, expert report analysis, econometrics for cases | Econometrics, IO, causal inference, Stata/R, attention to detail | Consultant; Senior Analyst; Antitrust Economist |
| Forecasting/Nowcasting Analyst | Economic Forecast Analyst; Macroeconomic Analyst | Build dashboards, forecast inflation/GDP/unemployment, scenario analysis | Time-series, ARIMA/VAR, Python/R, visualization | Senior Forecast Analyst; Macro Strategist |
| Development & Impact Evaluation Analyst | Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) Analyst; Impact Analyst | RCT/quasi-experimental evaluation, survey design, theory of change, cost-effectiveness | Causal inference, survey methods, R/Stata, reporting | Evaluation Specialist; Development Economist |
| Health Economics/HEOR Analyst | HEOR Analyst; Outcomes Research Analyst | Cost-effectiveness models, claims data analysis, pricing/reimbursement evidence | Health econ, decision models, Excel/R, evidence synthesis | Senior HEOR Analyst; Health Economist |
| Market Design/Platform Economics Analyst | Marketplace Analyst; Growth Analyst (Pricing); Experimentation Analyst | Price tests, elasticity estimation, A/B testing, demand modeling | Microeconomics, experiments, causal inference, SQL/Python | Pricing Economist; Product Economist |
| Public Finance/Budget Analyst | Budget Analyst; Fiscal Analyst; Revenue Analyst | Revenue forecasting, expenditure analysis, fiscal notes for legislation | Public finance, Excel, forecasting, writing | Senior Budget Analyst; Tax Policy Analyst |
| Urban/Housing Data Analyst | Housing Analyst; Regional Analyst | Housing affordability metrics, spatial analysis, policy evaluation for zoning/transit | Urban econ, GIS basics, regression, visualization | Urban Economist; Planning economist; policy lead |
