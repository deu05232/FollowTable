# New Testament Book Naming Across Major English Bible Editions (Selected Books)

| Bible translation / edition | Canon | Gospels | Acts | Pauline letters (examples) | Catholic letters (examples) | Revelation | Notable naming / ordering notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| King James Version (KJV, 1611/1769) | Protestant NT | The Gospel According to **Saint** Matthew; … Mark; … Luke; … John | The Acts of the **Apostles** | **The Epistle of Paul the Apostle to the Romans**; … to the Corinthians; … to the Galatians; … to Philemon | **The General Epistle of James**; … of Peter; … of John; … of Jude | The Revelation of **Saint John the Divine** | Uses extended formal titles (“Epistle of Paul…”, “General Epistle…”); “Saint” appears in many headings.
| New International Version (NIV, 2011) | Protestant NT | Matthew; Mark; Luke; John | Acts | Romans; 1 Corinthians; 2 Corinthians; Galatians; Ephesians; Philippians; Colossians; 1–2 Thessalonians; 1–2 Timothy; Titus; Philemon | James; 1–2 Peter; 1–3 John; Jude | Revelation | Short modern titles; typically “Revelation” (not “Revelations”).
| English Standard Version (ESV) | Protestant NT | Matthew; Mark; Luke; John | Acts | Romans; 1 Corinthians; 2 Corinthians; … | James; 1 Peter; 2 Peter; 1 John; 2 John; 3 John; Jude | Revelation | Similar short titles; consistent numeric prefixes (1/2/3).
| New Revised Standard Version (NRSV, mainline edition) | Protestant NT (often with Apocrypha in full Bible) | Matthew; Mark; Luke; John | Acts | Romans; 1 Corinthians; 2 Corinthians; … | James; 1 Peter; 2 Peter; 1 John; 2 John; 3 John; Jude | Revelation | Often published as “NRSV with Apocrypha” (OT extras), but NT book set remains standard 27.
| New American Bible, Revised Edition (NABRE) | Catholic NT | Matthew; Mark; Luke; John | Acts of the Apostles | Romans; 1 Corinthians; 2 Corinthians; … | James; 1 Peter; 2 Peter; 1 John; 2 John; 3 John; Jude | Revelation | Catholic edition; NT book names are largely same as Protestant modern editions; differences are primarily in OT/deuterocanon.
| Douay-Rheims (Challoner revision) | Catholic NT | The Gospel According to **St.** Matthew; … | The Acts of the **Apostles** | The Epistle of **St. Paul** to the Romans; … | The **Catholic Epistle** of St. James; … | The Apocalypse of **St. John** | Often uses **Apocalypse** for Revelation; more traditional/Latin-influenced headings.
