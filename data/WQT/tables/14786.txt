# Red Wine Varietals  Flavor Notes and Body Range for Quick Pairing Decisions

| Varietal | Common flavor descriptors | Body range |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Gamay | Red cherry, cranberry, violet, low tannin | Light |
| Sangiovese | Sour cherry, tomato leaf, dried herbs | Medium |
| Syrah/Shiraz | Blackberry, pepper, smoked meat, dark chocolate | Medium to full |
| Malbec | Plum, black cherry, cocoa, sweet spice | Full |
| Nebbiolo | Rose, tar, cherry, firm tannin | Full |
