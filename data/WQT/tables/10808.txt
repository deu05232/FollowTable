# Symptom-Relief Medications Used Alongside Prostatitis Treatment (Non-antibiotics)

| Medication | Category | Symptom targeted | Typical use note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ibuprofen | NSAID analgesic | Pelvic pain/inflammation | As needed for pain |
| Naproxen | NSAID analgesic | Pelvic pain/inflammation | As needed for pain |
| Acetaminophen | Analgesic/antipyretic | Pain/fever | Alternative to NSAIDs |
| Tamsulosin | Alpha-1 blocker | Urinary hesitancy/weak stream | Often used for LUTS |
| Alfuzosin | Alpha-1 blocker | Urinary symptoms | Alternative alpha blocker |
| Phenazopyridine | Urinary analgesic | Dysuria/burning | Short-term symptom relief |
| Sitz baths | Non-drug measure | Pelvic discomfort | Supportive care |
| Stool softener (docusate) | GI supportive | Constipation/straining | Supportive care |
