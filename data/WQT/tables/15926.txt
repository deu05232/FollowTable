# Rotation-Center Notation in Planar Patterns (International Symbols)

| Symbol used in diagrams | Meaning | Rotation order | Typical location in a pattern | Related symmetry context |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| 2 | half-turn center | 2 | midpoint of repeating segment | often in p2, p4, p6 |
| 3 | third-turn center | 3 | vertex of triangular motif | triangular/hexagonal lattices |
| 4 | quarter-turn center | 4 | intersection of square grid | common in square tilings |
| 6 | sixth-turn center | 6 | center of hexagon | hexagonal tilings |
| 2² | two distinct 2-fold centers | 2 | alternating points in lattice | used in pattern annotations |
| 3² | two distinct 3-fold centers | 3 | two orbits of triangle vertices | crystallographic diagrams |
| 4/2 | combined 4-fold with implied 2-fold | 4 and 2 | same point at different subgroups | subgroup relations in p4m/p4g |
