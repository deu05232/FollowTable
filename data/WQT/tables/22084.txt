# Venomous snakes recorded in Arizona and where they occur within the state

| Species (scientific) | Common name | Where in the state it is typically found |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Crotalus atrox | Western diamondback rattlesnake | Mostly southern and central desert regions; often in low-elevation scrub |
| Crotalus scutulatus | Mojave rattlesnake | Widely in desert areas, especially western and central parts |
| Crotalus tigris | Tiger rattlesnake | South-central desert and foothills, often in rocky slopes |
| Micruroides euryxanthus | Arizona coral snake | Primarily southern parts, especially near desert washes and thornscrub |
| Crotalus cerberus | Arizona black rattlesnake | Central highlands and adjacent mountain ranges |
| Crotalus pricei | Twin-spotted rattlesnake | Higher-elevation “sky island” mountain areas in the southeast |
