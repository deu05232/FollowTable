# Selected U.S. State Seat Belt Rules and First-Offense Monetary Penalties

| State | Coverage summary (who the rule applies to) | Where the requirement applies in the vehicle | Monetary penalty summary for a first violation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arizona | Drivers and front-seat occupants; certain ages required in all seating positions | Front seat for adults; broader coverage for younger occupants | Fine amount listed for a first citation |
| New Jersey | Drivers and applicable passengers based on age | Applies to required seating positions under the rule | Maximum fine stated for a first offense |
| Oregon | Drivers and passengers above the specified age threshold | All seating positions under the statute | Base fine noted for the initial violation |
