# Military Retirement and Disability Compensation Options (Overview)

| Benefit / Retirement Type | Who Typically Qualifies | Payout Determination Method | Key Compensation Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Regular Active-Duty Longevity Retirement | Active component members with sufficient years of service | Monthly retired pay = (Retired pay base) × (Multiplier based on years of service) | Retired pay base is commonly based on final basic pay or an average of highest months; multiplier differs by retirement system |
| Reserve (Non-Regular) Retirement | Guard/Reserve with enough qualifying years; generally paid at retirement age | Monthly retired pay derived from retirement points converted to equivalent years, then applied to a pay base × multiplier | Often called “points-based” retirement; payment start is typically later than active-duty retirement |
| Medical Retirement (Disability Retirement) | Members found unfit for duty due to service-connected condition | Monthly retired pay = max( (Pay base × disability %), (Pay base × longevity multiplier) ) subject to applicable caps | May interact with other disability programs; computation compares disability formula vs longevity formula |
| Disability Separation with Severance Pay | Members separated for disability but not medically retired | One-time severance = (Years of service) × (Basic pay) × (Severance factor) | Lump-sum rather than monthly retirement; may affect later VA compensation offsets depending on circumstances |
| Temporary Disability Retired List (TDRL) | Unfit with condition not yet stable for final rating | Monthly pay based on pay base × disability % (with periodic re-evaluations) | Status is temporary; final outcome may convert to permanent medical retirement or separation |
| Concurrent Receipt (e.g., Retirement + Disability) | Certain military retirees who also have qualifying disability ratings | Allows receipt of military retired pay plus disability compensation under qualifying rules | Eligibility depends on retirement type/years and disability rating thresholds; intended to reduce/avoid offsets |
| Combat-Related Special Compensation (CRSC) | Retirees with combat-related disabilities | Special compensation calculated based on approved combat-related disabilities; designed to restore retired pay otherwise offset | Amount and approval depend on service determination of combat-related nexus; separate application process |
| Survivor Benefit Plan (SBP) Annuity | Eligible beneficiaries of a retiree who elected coverage | Beneficiary annuity = elected base amount × SBP percentage; retiree pays premium from retired pay | Ongoing survivor income stream; reduces retiree’s monthly pay due to premiums while enrolled |
