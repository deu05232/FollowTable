# Peer-to-Peer Lending Platforms  Minimum Credit Score Requirements and Eligibility Notes

| Platform | Platform Type | Minimum Credit Score (stated) | Typical Loan Amount (USD) | Availability | Notes / Basic Eligibility |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| LendingClub | P2P marketplace / personal loans | 660 | 1,000–40,000 | U.S. | Often requires sufficient income and acceptable DTI; not available in all states |
| Prosper | P2P marketplace / personal loans | 640 | 2,000–50,000 | U.S. | May require a minimum number of credit lines; applicants with recent bankruptcies typically ineligible |
| Upstart | Online lending platform (partner banks) | 620 | 1,000–50,000 | U.S. | Considers education/employment factors in addition to score |
| Peerform | Online consumer lending | 600 | 4,000–25,000 | U.S. | Minimum score can vary by product/state; income verification common |
| Funding Circle | Marketplace for small business loans | 660 | 25,000–500,000 | U.S./U.K. (varies) | Business-focused; time-in-business and revenue requirements apply |
| Kiva | Crowdfunded microloans | No minimum score | 0–15,000 | Global | Community-based underwriting; may require trustee/endorsement rather than credit score |
| SoFi | Online lender (not pure P2P) | 680 | 5,000–100,000 | U.S. | Strong income/employment profile expected; score threshold often higher for best rates |
| Upgrade | Online lender | 600 | 1,000–50,000 | U.S. | Minimum score varies; also evaluates credit history depth and delinquencies |
