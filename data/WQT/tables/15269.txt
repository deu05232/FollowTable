# Typical ABV by Beverage Style and Category

| Category/Style | Typical ABV (%) | Examples (category-level) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Low-alcohol beer | 0.5–1.2 | "low-alcohol lager" | Not specific brands |
| Session IPA | 3.5–5.0 | "session IPA" | Wide variability |
| Pale ale | 4.5–6.2 | "American pale ale" | Depends on brewery |
| Stout | 4.0–8.5 | "dry stout", "imperial stout" | Strength varies strongly |
| Bock | 6.0–7.5 | "bock lager" | Traditional German style |
| Barleywine | 8.0–12.0 | "English barleywine" | Often bottle-conditioned |
| Kvass | 0.05–1.5 | "bread kvass" | Fermented soft drink |
| Kefir | 0.2–2.0 | "milk kefir" | Fermented dairy beverage |
