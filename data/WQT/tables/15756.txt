# Mechanical Properties of AISI 304 and 310 Stainless Steel at Room Temperature

| Property | 304 | 310 | Unit | Test Condition |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Tensile Strength (UTS) | 515–750 | 520–760 | MPa | Annealed |
| Yield Strength (0.2%) | 205–310 | 205–320 | MPa | Annealed |
| Elongation | 40–70 | 35–60 | % | 50 mm gauge |
| Hardness (Brinell) | 150–190 | 160–200 | HB | Typical |
| Modulus of Elasticity | 193 | 200 | GPa | RT |
| Thermal Conductivity | 16.2 | 14.2 | W/m·K | ~100°C |
| Specific Heat | 500 | 480 | J/kg·K | RT |
