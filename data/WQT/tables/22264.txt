# Adobe Fonts — Optical Sizes and Recommended Point Ranges (Typography Guide)

| Font family | Optical size | Intended point size range | Typical usage | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adobe Jenson Pro | Caption | 6–9 pt | Footnotes, dense text | Designed for small sizes |
| Adobe Jenson Pro | Regular | 9–13 pt | Body text | General reading |
| Adobe Jenson Pro | Subhead | 13–22 pt | Section headers | Better at mid sizes |
| Adobe Jenson Pro | Display | 22–72 pt | Posters, titles | For large typography |
| Minion Pro | Caption | 6–9 pt | Legal text | High readability |
| Minion Pro | Regular | 9–13 pt | Books | Standard sizes |
| Myriad Pro | Regular | 9–14 pt | UI text | Neutral sans |
| Source Serif | Display | 24–72 pt | Hero headings | Strong contrast |
