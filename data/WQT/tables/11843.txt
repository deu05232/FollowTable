# Drug interaction examples involving antibiotics sometimes used in prostatitis

| Trigger substance or co-med | Interaction mechanism description | Example affected medicine |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Grapefruit products | Inhibits certain metabolic enzymes, raising levels of some drugs | Buspirone |
| Metronidazole | Alters metabolism of some anticoagulants | Warfarin |
| Rifampin | Induces metabolic enzymes, lowering levels of some drugs | Oral contraceptives |
