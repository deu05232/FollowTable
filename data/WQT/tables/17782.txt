# Cooked breakfast items  how long they keep when chilled or frozen

| Cooked item | Refrigerator time | Freezer time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked omelet | 3–4 days | 1–2 months |
| Scrambled eggs (cooked) | 3–4 days | 1 month |
| Cooked pancakes | 3–5 days | 2 months |
| Cooked waffles | 3–5 days | 2 months |
| Cooked bacon | 4–5 days | 1 month |
| Cooked breakfast burrito | 3–4 days | 1–3 months |
