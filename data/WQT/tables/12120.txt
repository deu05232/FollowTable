# U.S. Passport Application Fees by Service (Routine Only)

| Passport Service | Routine Fee | Who Pays | Form | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| New passport book (age 16+) | $130 | Applicant | DS-11 | Does not include acceptance fee |
| New passport book (under 16) | $100 | Parent/guardian | DS-11 | Does not include acceptance fee |
| Passport card (age 16+) | $30 | Applicant | DS-11 | Card only |
| Passport card (under 16) | $15 | Parent/guardian | DS-11 | Card only |
| Renewal (book) | $130 | Applicant | DS-82 | Eligible applicants only |
| Replacement (lost/stolen) | $130 | Applicant | DS-11 | Standard processing |
| Name change with DS-82 | $130 | Applicant | DS-82 | If eligible |
| Passport acceptance (execution) fee | $35 | Applicant | DS-11 | Paid separately |
