# Frog Life Cycle Stages (From Egg to Adult)

| Species | Life Stage | Also Called | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Frog | Egg | Spawn | Laid in clusters in water |
| Frog | Hatchling | Embryo | Developing inside egg mass |
| Frog | Larva | Tadpole | Gills and tail, fully aquatic |
| Frog | Late larva | Polliwog | Growing legs, tail shrinking |
| Frog | Metamorph | Froglet | Small frog with remnant tail |
| Frog | Juvenile | Young frog | Terrestrial transition |
| Frog | Adult | Frog | Reproductive stage |
