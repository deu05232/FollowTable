# Iran  Family-Related Leave Policy Overview (Types of Leave and Eligibility)

| Leave type | Who is eligible | Maximum duration | Pay status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Maternity leave | Mothers (employees) | 6 months | Paid | Conditions may vary by sector |
| Paternity leave | Fathers (employees) | 2 weeks | Paid | Typically around childbirth |
| Parental leave (additional) | Parents | Case-by-case | Varies | Depends on employer policy |
| Adoption leave | Adoptive parents | Not specified | Not specified | May require documentation |
| Childcare leave | Parents | Not specified | Unpaid/varies | Often discretionary |
| Nursing breaks | Mothers | Up to 24 months | Paid breaks | Reduced working hours in practice |
