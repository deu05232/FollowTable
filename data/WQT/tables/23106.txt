# Wine Varietals (White) – Typical Flavors and Body

| White Wine Varietal | Typical aromas & flavors | Body |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sauvignon Blanc | Citrus, gooseberry, green herbs, grapefruit | Light |
| Pinot Grigio | Lemon, pear, green apple | Light–medium |
| Riesling | Lime, apricot, floral notes | Light |
| Chardonnay | Apple, citrus, butter, vanilla (often oaky) | Medium–full |
