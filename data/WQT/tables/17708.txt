# Extraocular Muscles Mapped to Brainstem Nuclei (Not to Named Cranial Nerves)

| Extraocular muscle | Brainstem nucleus emphasized | Brainstem level | Common lesion note (nucleus-level) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Medial rectus | Oculomotor nucleus (subnuclei) | Midbrain | Subnuclear lesions may spare some functions |
| Superior rectus | Oculomotor nucleus (subnuclei) | Midbrain | Contralateral subnuclear organization often highlighted |
| Inferior rectus | Oculomotor nucleus (subnuclei) | Midbrain | May be affected with midbrain infarcts |
| Inferior oblique | Oculomotor nucleus (subnuclei) | Midbrain | Often grouped with other CN III subnuclei |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Central caudal nucleus | Midbrain | Bilateral ptosis with midline involvement |
| Superior oblique | Trochlear nucleus | Midbrain (caudal) | Dorsal midbrain lesions can involve decussation |
| Lateral rectus | Abducens nucleus | Pons | PPRF/abducens nucleus lesions cause gaze palsy |
| Medial rectus (internuclear link) | MLF connection from abducens internuclear neurons | Pons → Midbrain | INO pattern described at pathway level |
