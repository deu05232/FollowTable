# Guidelines - DLNA

| Media Formats | Required Formats Set | Optional Formats Set |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Imaging | JPEG | GIF, PNG |
| Audio | LPCM, MPEG-1 L3 (MP3), MPEG-4 AAC LC | WMA9, AC-3, AAC, ATRAC3plus |
| Video | MPEG2, MPEG-4 Part 10 (AVC) with MPEG-4 AAC LC associated audio | MPEG1, MPEG4, WMV9, HEVC H.265 |
