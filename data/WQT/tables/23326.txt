# Sympathetic vs Parasympathetic  Organ-Level Receptor and Effect Summary

| Organ | Sympathetic receptor(s) highlighted | Sympathetic effect | Parasympathetic receptor(s) highlighted | Parasympathetic effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Eye | α1 | Mydriasis (pupil dilation) | M3 | Miosis (pupil constriction) |
| Heart | β1 | ↑ HR and contractility | M2 | ↓ HR |
| Bronchi | β2 | Bronchodilation | M3 | Bronchoconstriction |
| GI tract | α, β2 | ↓ Motility and secretion | M3 | ↑ Motility and secretion |
| Bladder | β3, α1 | Detrusor relaxation, sphincter contraction | M3 | Detrusor contraction |
| Blood vessels | α1, β2 | Vasoconstriction (most), vasodilation (skeletal muscle) | — | Minimal direct effect |
| Liver | β2 | ↑ Glycogenolysis/gluconeogenesis | — | Minimal direct effect |
| Sweat glands | — | ↑ Sweating | — | — |
