# BMI to Daily Calorie Deficit Recommendations (Aggressiveness Guide)

| BMI | Weight-Status Label | Recommended Daily Deficit | Expected Weekly Loss | Safety Caveat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 17.0–18.4 | Underweight | 0 kcal | 0 lb/week | Do not pursue weight loss |
| 18.5–22.9 | Normal weight | 250 kcal | ~0.5 lb/week | Monitor energy and mood |
| 23.0–24.9 | High-normal | 250–400 kcal | ~0.5–0.8 lb/week | Prefer activity-based deficit |
| 25.0–27.4 | Overweight (mild) | 400–500 kcal | ~0.8–1.0 lb/week | Emphasize protein/fiber |
| 27.5–29.9 | Overweight | 500–650 kcal | ~1.0–1.3 lb/week | Avoid very-low-calorie diets |
| 30.0–34.9 | Obesity Class I | 600–750 kcal | ~1.2–1.5 lb/week | Consider clinician guidance |
| ≥ 35.0 | Obesity Class II+ | 750–900 kcal | ~1.5–1.8 lb/week | Medical supervision recommended |
