# Bottled Water pH by Source Type (Typical Ranges and Examples)

| Source Type | Typical pH Range | Example Brands | Why It Varies |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Reverse Osmosis / Purified | 5.0–7.0 | Aquafina, Dasani | CO2 absorption, remineralization |
| Spring Water | 6.5–8.0 | Evian, Poland Spring | geology, dissolved minerals |
| Artesian | 7.0–8.5 | Fiji, Eternal | aquifer mineral profile |
| Mineral Water (still) | 6.0–8.5 | Voss, Acqua Panna | bicarbonate vs sulfate balance |
| Sparkling Mineral Water | 4.0–6.0 | Perrier, S. Pellegrino | carbonic acid from CO2 |
| Alkaline Enhanced (additives) | 8.5–10.0 | Essentia, Evamor | electrolytes, processing |
| Glacial | 7.5–8.5 | Icelandic Glacial | source rock and filtration |
| Electrolyte Sports Water | 3.0–5.0 | Propel | flavor acids, additives |
