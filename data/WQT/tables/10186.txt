# Vitamin and Mineral Recommendations with Food Sources for Teens (Mixed Content)

| Nutrient | Recommended daily intake for ages 14–18 | Example food source | Amount per serving |
|---|---|---|---|
| Calcium | 1300 mg/day | Milk (1 cup) | 300 mg |
| Iron | 11–15 mg/day | Lentils (1 cup cooked) | 6.6 mg |
| Vitamin C | 65–75 mg/day | Orange (1 medium) | 70 mg |
| Potassium | 4700 mg/day | Banana (1 medium) | 420 mg |
| Zinc | 9–11 mg/day | Beef (3 oz) | 5 mg |
| Magnesium | 360–410 mg/day | Almonds (1 oz) | 80 mg |
