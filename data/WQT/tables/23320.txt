# Autonomic Fiber Classes and Ganglionic Anatomy (with examples)

| Pathway element | Typical fiber type | Myelination | Approx. conduction velocity | Typical origin/route | Example structure mentioned |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sympathetic preganglionic | B fiber | Myelinated | 3–15 m/s | T1–L2 intermediolateral cell column | White rami communicantes |
| Sympathetic postganglionic | C fiber | Unmyelinated | 0.5–2 m/s | Sympathetic chain to target | Gray rami communicantes |
| Parasympathetic preganglionic | B fiber | Myelinated | 3–15 m/s | Brainstem (CN III, VII, IX, X) or S2–S4 | Vagus nerve |
| Parasympathetic postganglionic | C fiber | Unmyelinated | 0.5–2 m/s | Near/within target organ | Intramural ganglia |
| Splanchnic nerves (thoracic) | Mixed (mostly preganglionic) | Mostly myelinated | Variable | Thoracic sympathetic outflow | Greater splanchnic nerve |
| Enteric plexus connections | Mixed | Mixed | Variable | Local GI plexuses | Myenteric plexus |
| Adrenal medulla input | Preganglionic-like | Myelinated | 3–15 m/s | Direct to adrenal medulla | Chromaffin cells |
| Pelvic splanchnic nerves | Preganglionic | Myelinated | 3–15 m/s | S2–S4 | Pelvic plexus |
