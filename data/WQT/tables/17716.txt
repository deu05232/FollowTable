# Rule-Based Summary  Extraocular Muscles and Their Cranial Nerve Innervation

| Mnemonic / rule | Specific extraocular muscle (row-by-row) | Innervating cranial nerve (named) | Common lesion clue (movement deficit) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| "LR6" | Lateral rectus | Abducens nerve (CN VI) | Loss of abduction; eye drifts medially |
| "SO4" | Superior oblique | Trochlear nerve (CN IV) | Difficulty depressing eye in adduction; vertical diplopia |
| "All others are 3" | Superior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Weak elevation (esp. in abduction) |
| "All others are 3" | Inferior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Weak depression (esp. in abduction) |
| "All others are 3" | Medial rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Weak adduction |
| "All others are 3" | Inferior oblique | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Weak elevation in adduction |
| (Often included with CN III motor) | Levator palpebrae superioris | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Ptosis due to eyelid elevation weakness |
