# Yellow Fever Vaccine  Travel Recommendation Levels by Destination

| Destination | Recommendation level | Typical traveler scenarios |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Peru (Amazon basin) | Strongly recommended | Jungle excursions, multi-day treks |
| Argentina (Iguazú area) | Recommended | Visits to forested areas near borders |
| Tanzania (selected parks) | Consider based on itinerary | Safari routes near risk corridors |
| Senegal | Recommended | Urban + rural travel |
| Egypt | Not recommended | Most itineraries have no risk |
| Japan | Not recommended | No yellow fever risk |
