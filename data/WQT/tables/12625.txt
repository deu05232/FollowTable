# Windows 10 Support by Intel CPU Generations (Family-Level Summary)

| Intel generation / family | Codename | Example segment | Windows 10 support status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2nd Gen Intel Core | Sandy Bridge | Mobile/Desktop | Supported | General driver availability varies by OEM |
| 3rd Gen Intel Core | Ivy Bridge | Mobile/Desktop | Supported | Often paired with 7-series chipsets |
| 4th Gen Intel Core | Haswell | Mobile/Desktop | Supported | Common in business laptops |
| 5th Gen Intel Core | Broadwell | Mobile/Desktop | Supported | Limited desktop presence |
| 6th Gen Intel Core | Skylake | Mobile/Desktop | Supported | Widely deployed in enterprise |
| Intel Atom | Bay Trail | Tablet/Embedded | Mixed | Some devices had vendor driver constraints |
| Intel Atom / Celeron / Pentium | Braswell | Low-power desktop/mobile | Supported | Depends on specific platform integration |
| Intel Xeon | Nehalem/Westmere era | Workstation/Server | Supported | Requires chipset/board vendor drivers |
