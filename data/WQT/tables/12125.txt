# BMI categories with health risk notes for adults

| BMI range | Weight category | Typical health risk description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| < 18.5 | Underweight | Nutrient deficiency risk |
| 18.5–24.9 | Normal | Lower risk overall |
| 25.0–29.9 | Overweight | Elevated risk |
| ≥ 30.0 | Obese | Higher risk, consider clinical support |
