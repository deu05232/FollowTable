# Taxation in the United Kingdom
> Personal taxes

| Rate | Dividend income | Savings income | Other income (inc employment) | Tax Bracket (of income above tax-free allowance) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Basic rate | 10% | 20% | 20% | £0–£32,000 [21] |
| Higher rate | 32.5% | 40% | 40% | £32,001–£150,000 |
| Additional rate | 37.5% | 45% | 45% | Over £150,001 |
