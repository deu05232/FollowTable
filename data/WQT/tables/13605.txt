# Dietary fiber  definitions and classifications

| Term / Organization | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Dietary fiber | Nondigestible carbohydrates intrinsic in plants |
| Functional fiber | Isolated nondigestible carbohydrates with health benefits |
| Soluble fiber | Dissolves in water; forms a gel-like material |
| Insoluble fiber | Adds bulk to stool; helps food pass more quickly |
| Total fiber | Sum of dietary and functional fiber |
| Common sources | Fruits, vegetables, legumes, whole grains |
| Typical guidance | Increase intake gradually and drink water |
