# Pesticide Categories and Active Ingredients Used for Household & Garden Pest Control

| Category | Example Active Ingredient | Target Pests | Typical Form |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Insecticide (pyrethroid) | Permethrin | Mosquitoes, ants, roaches | Spray |
| Insecticide (neonicotinoid) | Imidacloprid | Aphids, whiteflies | Soil drench |
| Insecticide (spinosyn) | Spinosad | Thrips, caterpillars | Spray |
| Miticide | Abamectin | Spider mites | Spray |
| Molluscicide | Iron phosphate | Slugs, snails | Granules |
| Rodenticide | Bromadiolone | Rats, mice | Bait blocks |
| Fungicide | Copper hydroxide | Mildews, blights | Spray |
| Nematicide | Oxamyl | Plant-parasitic nematodes | Soil treatment |
