# KJV vs NIV vs ESV  Selected Verse Wording Differences (with Book Reference)

| Reference (Book-Chapter:Verse) | KJV | NIV | ESV | Difference Type |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Genesis 1:1 | In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth. | In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth. | In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth. | Singular/plural + punctuation |
| Psalm 23:1 | The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want. | The LORD is my shepherd, I lack nothing. | The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want. | Idiom update |
| Isaiah 7:14 | ...a virgin shall conceive... | ...the virgin will conceive... | ...the virgin shall conceive... | Modal/tense |
| Matthew 5:3 | ...poor in spirit... | ...poor in spirit... | ...poor in spirit... | Minimal |
| Luke 2:14 | ...good will toward men. | ...on whom his favor rests. | ...among those with whom he is pleased! | Translation choice |
| John 3:16 | ...whosoever believeth... | ...whoever believes... | ...whoever believes... | Modernization |
| Romans 8:1 | ...who walk not after the flesh... | ...who are in Christ Jesus. | ...who are in Christ Jesus. | Longer/shorter reading |
| Revelation 22:14 | Blessed are they that do his commandments... | Blessed are those who wash their robes... | Blessed are those who wash their robes... | Textual variant |
