# Raw Ingredient Storage Guide (Before Cooking)

| Raw Item | Refrigerator Time | Freezer Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Raw chicken (pieces) | 1-2 days | 9-12 months | Keep at 40°F/4°C or below |
| Raw ground beef | 1-2 days | 3-4 months | Store on bottom shelf |
| Raw pork chops | 3-5 days | 4-6 months | Wrap well to prevent freezer burn |
| Raw salmon | 1-2 days | 2-3 months | Use ice pack in fridge if possible |
| Raw shrimp | 1-2 days | 3-6 months | Freeze quickly |
| Raw eggs in shell | 3-5 weeks | Not recommended | Keep in carton |
| Fresh milk | 5-7 days | 1-3 months | Quality loss after freezing |
| Fresh spinach | 3-5 days | 10-12 months (blanched) | Best frozen after blanching |
