| Antibiotic | Route | Cmax (approx) | Tmax (h) | Half-life (h) | Protein binding | Primary elimination |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Ciprofloxacin | PO | 2–3 mg/L | 1–2 | 4 | 20–40% | Renal/hepatic |
| Levofloxacin | PO | 5–7 mg/L | 1–2 | 6–8 | 24–38% | Renal |
| TMP-SMX (TMP component) | PO | 1–2 mg/L | 1–4 | 8–12 | 40–45% | Renal |
| Doxycycline | PO | 2–3 mg/L | 2–4 | 18–22 | 80–90% | Biliary/fecal |
| Azithromycin | PO | 0.4 mg/L | 2–3 | 50–70 | 7–50% | Biliary |
| Fosfomycin | PO | 20–30 mg/L | 2–2.5 | 4–6 | <5% | Renal |
| Ceftriaxone | IV | 150–250 mg/L | end of infusion | 6–9 | 85–95% | Biliary/renal |
| Gentamicin | IV | 15–25 mg/L | end of infusion | 2–3 | <10% | Renal |
