# Urinalysis Reference  Urine Specific Gravity Ranges by Species and Condition

| Species | Condition | Collection Time | Urine Specific Gravity (range) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dog | Normal hydration | Morning sample | 1.015–1.045 | Typical adult range |
| Dog | Dehydration | Any | 1.045–1.070 | Concentrated urine |
| Cat | Normal hydration | Morning sample | 1.030–1.060 | Often higher than dogs |
| Horse | Normal hydration | Stall sample | 1.020–1.050 | Varies with diet |
| Cow | Normal hydration | Random | 1.025–1.045 | Ruminant baseline |
| Goat | Normal hydration | Random | 1.015–1.045 | Similar to sheep |
| Human | Normal hydration | First morning | 1.005–1.030 | Clinical reference |
| Human | Diabetes insipidus | Any | 1.001–1.005 | Very dilute urine |
