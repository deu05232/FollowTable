# State Income Tax Return Deadlines (Including Extension Notes)

| State | Regular Return Due Date | Extension Request Due Date | Extended Return Due Date | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| California | Apr 15 | Apr 15 | Oct 15 | Extension granted, but payment due Apr 15 |
| New York | Apr 15 | Apr 15 | Oct 15 | Must file extension form if you owe |
| Illinois | Apr 15 | Apr 15 | Oct 15 | Automatic extension for filing only |
| Virginia | May 1 | May 1 | Nov 1 | Extension requires payment estimate |
| Georgia | Apr 15 | Apr 15 | Oct 15 | Federal extension accepted if attached |
| Ohio | Apr 15 | Apr 15 | Oct 15 | IT 40P payment voucher recommended |
| New Jersey | Apr 15 | Apr 15 | Oct 15 | Extension denied if payment not included |
