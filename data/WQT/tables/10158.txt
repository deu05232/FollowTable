# Cooked Food Storage  Handling Methods and Safety Notes (No Time Ranges)

| Cooked Dish | Recommended Container | Cooling Method | Reheating Guidance | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cooked chicken curry | Shallow airtight container | Cool uncovered 20 min, then cover | Reheat until steaming hot | Avoid leaving on counter |
| Cooked spaghetti with sauce | Lidded glass container | Portion into small servings | Reheat in microwave with splash of water | Stir midway |
| Beef stew | Freezer-safe tub | Chill in ice bath before refrigerating | Simmer to a boil | Skim fat after chilling |
| Baked salmon | Airtight wrap + container | Cool quickly on rack | Reheat gently to avoid drying | Discard if off-odor |
| Vegetable stir-fry | Airtight container | Spread on tray to cool faster | Reheat in skillet | Add sauce after reheating |
| Cooked rice | Airtight container | Cool fast; do not keep warm | Reheat until piping hot | High-risk if mishandled |
| Chili con carne | Freezer-safe bag laid flat | Cool, then freeze flat | Boil/simmer to heat through | Label and date |
