# U.S. Passport Processing Add‑Ons (Expedited & Related Surcharges)

| Add-on (U.S. Passport) | When It Applies | Fee | Notes |
| --- | --- | ---:| --- |
| Expedited service surcharge | You request faster processing for an application filed in the U.S. | $60.00 | Added on top of the routine application fee(s) |
| 1–2 day delivery (passport book) | You want faster mailing of the completed passport book | $20.66 | Delivery add-on after issuance; not available for all cases |
| File search fee | You cannot present acceptable proof of U.S. citizenship and request a record search | $150.00 | Processing add-on that may be required during adjudication |
| Extra passport book pages | When requesting a larger book (where offered) | $0.00 | No extra charge; included for cost clarity |
| Service fees charged by acceptance facility | Applying in person at a local acceptance facility | Varies | Separate from State Department fees; not an expedite surcharge |
| Private courier / agency handling | Using a third-party courier/expediter | Varies | Not paid to the U.S. Department of State; optional add-on |
