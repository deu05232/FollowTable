# Uremia
> Signs and symptoms

| GFR (mL/min) | Effects |
| --- | --- |
| 100-120 | Normal GFR |
| <60 | Uremic symptoms may be present, Reduced well being |
| 30-60 | Cognitive impairment |
| 55 | Fatigue and reduced stamina |
| <50 | Insulin resistance |
| <30 | Possibly lead to coma |
| ≤15 | Kidney failure |
