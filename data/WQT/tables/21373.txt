# Alcohol Strength by Beverage Category (not individual beers)

| Category | Example | ABV (Typical) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Low-alcohol beer | Session beer | 0.5%–3.5% | Varies by country |
| Standard lager | Mainstream lager | 4.0%–5.2% | Most mass-market beers |
| IPA | India Pale Ale | 5.5%–7.5% | Includes substyles |
| Double IPA | Imperial IPA | 7.5%–10.5% | Often labeled "DIPA" |
| Stout/Porter | Dry stout | 4.0%–6.0% | Sweet stouts can be higher |
| Barleywine | English barleywine | 8.0%–12.0% | Vintage variation |
| Hard seltzer | Flavored seltzer | 4.0%–6.0% | Not beer, but comparable |
