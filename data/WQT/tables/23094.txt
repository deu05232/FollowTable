# Sports Personality of the Year  Award Rules and Eligibility Changes Over Time

| Year introduced/changed | Rule or eligibility clause | Applies to | Practical effect |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| 1960 | Non-UK athletes may be recognized | Overseas award | Adds separate overseas honor |
| 1967 | Team voting introduced | Team of the Year | Enables team-based recognition |
| 1996 | Career achievement category formalized | Lifetime Achievement | Adds non-annual-performance honor |
| 1999 | Youth category added | Young SPOTY | Recognizes under-18 athletes |
| 2004 | Grassroots/community recognition added | Unsung Hero | Shifts focus beyond elite sport |
| 2010 | Public vote weighting adjusted | Main award | Changes influence of audience voting |
| 2020 | Remote/virtual ceremony allowances | All categories | Ensures continuity during disruptions |
| 2023 | Expanded shortlist criteria published | Main award | Standardizes nomination process |
