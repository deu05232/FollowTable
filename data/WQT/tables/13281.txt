# Food Portions per 1,200–2,000 Calorie Diet Plans (Weight-Loss Menu Framework)

| Food Group | 1,200 kcal/day Plan | 1,500 kcal/day Plan | 1,800 kcal/day Plan | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vegetables | 3–4 cups | 4–5 cups | 5–6 cups | Non-starchy emphasized |
| Fruits | 1 cup | 1.5 cups | 2 cups | Whole fruit preferred |
| Grains | 3 oz-eq | 4 oz-eq | 5 oz-eq | Half whole grains |
| Protein Foods | 4 oz-eq | 5 oz-eq | 6 oz-eq | Lean meats/legumes |
| Dairy | 2 cups | 2–3 cups | 3 cups | Low-fat options |
| Oils | 4 tsp | 5 tsp | 6 tsp | Mostly unsaturated |
| Added Sugars | ≤ 80 kcal | ≤ 120 kcal | ≤ 160 kcal | Limit for deficit |
