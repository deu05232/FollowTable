# Venomous snakes in Kentucky (with in-state range notes)

| Snake (common name) | Scientific name | In-state distribution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Timber rattlesnake | Crotalus horridus | mostly eastern and south-central regions |
| Copperhead | Agkistrodon contortrix | widespread; more common in wooded hills |
| Cottonmouth | Agkistrodon piscivorus | western lowlands and wetlands |
| Eastern coral snake | Micrurus fulvius | far southern edge; rare sightings |
