# CIDR and subnet mask quick reference for common prefixes (used with private ranges)

| CIDR prefix | Subnet mask | Addresses per subnet | Typical subnetting note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| /8 | 255.0.0.0 | 16,777,216 | Often referenced when discussing large private blocks |
| /12 | 255.240.0.0 | 1,048,576 | Commonly seen in private addressing discussions |
| /16 | 255.255.0.0 | 65,536 | Frequently used for internal LAN addressing |
| /24 | 255.255.255.0 | 256 | Common small LAN subnet size |
