# Wallpaper Groups  Rotation Centers and Allowed Orders

| Wallpaper group | Rotation centers present | Highest rotation order | Typical tiling example |
|---|---|---|---|
| p1 | none | 1 | generic parallelogram lattice |
| p2 | 2-fold | 2 | brick-like translations |
| p3 | 3-fold | 3 | triangular lattice with 120° rotations |
| p4 | 4-fold, 2-fold | 4 | square grid tiling |
| p6 | 6-fold, 3-fold, 2-fold | 6 | hexagonal/triangular tiling |
| p4m | 4-fold + mirrors | 4 | checkerboard with reflections |
| p6m | 6-fold + mirrors | 6 | hexagonal tiling with mirror lines |
