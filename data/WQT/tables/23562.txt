# Canadian Atlantic shipwrecks (selected vessels)

| Ship | Date wrecked | Location (Canada) | Cause/Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| SS Atlantic | 1873-04-01 | Near Meagher's Island, Nova Scotia | Ran aground in heavy seas; significant loss of life |
| RMS Empress of Ireland | 1914-05-29 | St. Lawrence River, Quebec | Collision with SS Storstad; sank quickly |
| SS Caribou | 1942-10-14 | Cabot Strait (near Newfoundland) | Torpedoed during WWII; sank |
| SS Mont-Blanc | 1917-12-06 | Halifax Harbour, Nova Scotia | Collision led to explosion; vessel destroyed and sank |
| SS Edmund Fitzgerald | 1975-11-10 | Lake Superior (Ontario waters) | Sank in storm; wreck later located |
| SS Princess Sophia | 1918-10-25 | Lynn Canal (near Skagway; Canadian waters impacted via Yukon route) | Grounded on Vanderbilt Reef and sank after storm |
