# Leftover Cooked Foods  How long they last in the refrigerator or freezer

| Cooked food item | Refrigerator (how long it lasts) | Freezer (how long it lasts) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked pasta (plain) | 3–5 days | 2 months |
| Cooked chicken (pieces) | 3–4 days | 4 months |
| Cooked ground beef | 3–4 days | 3 months |
| Cooked soup (vegetable or chicken) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked fish (fillets) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
