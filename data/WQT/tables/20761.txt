# Autonomic ganglia  transmitter release sites (overview)

| Neurotransmitter | Released at preganglionic synapse? | Released at postganglionic synapse? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Acetylcholine | Yes | Yes (parasympathetic) |
| Norepinephrine | No | Yes (sympathetic) |
| Epinephrine | No | Not typically (mainly hormonal) |
