# Concerta (methylphenidate ER) — Age-Based Starting Dose and Dose Range (Once-Daily Morning)

| Age group | Typical starting dose (qAM) | Titration increment | Typical maintenance / dose range | Age-specific maximum | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Children (6–12 years) | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at weekly intervals | 18–54 mg/day | 54 mg/day | Once daily in the morning; dose adjusted based on response and tolerability |
| Adolescents (13–17 years) | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | 72 mg/day (not to exceed 2 mg/kg/day) | Consider weight-based cap in smaller adolescents |
| Adults (18–65 years) | 18 mg/day (some start at 36 mg/day) | +18 mg at weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | 72 mg/day | Morning dosing; do not exceed max daily dose |
| Adults (≥66 years) | Not established | — | Not established | Not established | Safety/efficacy not established in this age group |
| Children (<6 years) | Not recommended | — | Not recommended | Not recommended | Use in children under 6 typically not established |
| All age groups above | — | — | — | — | Doses are expressed as total daily dose of Concerta ER taken once daily |
