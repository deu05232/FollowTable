# Adult Vital Signs Reference Ranges (Not a Hypertension Classification Table)

| Measurement | Typical adult range | Units | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Temperature (oral) | 36.5–37.5 | °C | varies by method/time of day |
| Heart rate | 60–100 | bpm | athletes may be lower |
| Respiratory rate | 12–20 | breaths/min | at rest |
| Oxygen saturation | 95–100 | % | room air |
| Systolic blood pressure | 90–120 | mm Hg | general reference range |
| Diastolic blood pressure | 60–80 | mm Hg | general reference range |
| Mean arterial pressure (MAP) | 70–100 | mm Hg | depends on clinical context |
| Pulse pressure | 30–50 | mm Hg | SBP minus DBP |
| Capillary refill | < 2 | seconds | peripheral perfusion indicator |
