# Selected Egyptian gods and goddesses  domains and common symbols

| Deity | Domain / role | Common symbol / depiction |
|---|---|---|
| Ra | Sun, kingship | Sun disk, falcon-headed |
| Anubis | Embalming, funerary rites | Jackal-headed |
| Isis | Magic, motherhood | Throne glyph, cow horns |
| Horus | Kingship, sky | Falcon |
| Hathor | Love, music, joy | Cow horns and sun disk |
| Sekhmet | War, fire, vengeance | Lioness-headed |
