# Egyptian deities  domains and major festival associations

| Deity | Domain / role | Associated festival / rite |
|---|---|---|
| Osiris | Underworld, afterlife | Khoiak mysteries |
| Amun | Hidden god, state power | Opet Festival |
| Bastet | Protection, cats | Festival of Bastet (Bubastis) |
| Ptah | Creation, craftsmanship | Memphite rites |
| Sobek | Nile, crocodile power | Faiyum cult celebrations |
| Thoth | Writing, wisdom | Scribe and lunar rites |
