# Fresh herbs lifespan guide (not cooked leftovers)

| Herb | Storage method | Typical lifespan |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Basil | In a jar with water | 5–7 days |
| Cilantro | Wrapped in damp paper towel | 10–14 days |
| Dill | Plastic bag in crisper drawer | 7–10 days |
| Chives | Sealed container | 7–14 days |
