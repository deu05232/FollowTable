| Country/Territory | Region | Observes seasonal clock change | Clock change starts (typical rule/date) | Clock change ends (typical rule/date) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| United States | North America | Yes | Second Sunday in March | First Sunday in November |
| Mexico | North America | Yes (varies by area) | Early April (historical national rule) | Late October (historical national rule) |
| Japan | Asia | No | Not observed | Not observed |
| Jamaica | Caribbean | No | Not observed | Not observed |
| Egypt | Africa | Yes (reintroduced in some years) | Late April (policy-dependent) | Late October (policy-dependent) |
