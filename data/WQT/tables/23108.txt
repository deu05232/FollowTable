# World Wine Regions and Their Signature Grape Varieties (Regional Overview)

| Region | Country | Signature Grapes | Typical Aroma/Flavor Notes | Typical Body (Light–Full) | Climate Influence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Marlborough | New Zealand | Sauvignon Blanc | Passionfruit, gooseberry, grass | Light–Medium | Cool maritime |
| Napa Valley | USA | Cabernet Sauvignon | Cassis, oak spice, dark cherry | Full | Warm valley |
| Rioja | Spain | Tempranillo | Red cherry, leather, dill | Medium | Continental/Atlantic mix |
| Mosel | Germany | Riesling | Lime, slate, white flowers | Light | Cool river valleys |
| Barossa Valley | Australia | Shiraz | Blackberry, chocolate, pepper | Full | Warm continental |
| Burgundy (Côte d'Or) | France | Pinot Noir, Chardonnay | Cherry/earth; apple/butter | Light–Medium | Cool continental |
| Douro Valley | Portugal | Touriga Nacional (blend) | Dark fruit, floral, spice | Full | Hot, steep vineyards |
| Stellenbosch | South Africa | Chenin Blanc, Cabernet Sauvignon | Guava/honey; cassis/herbs | Medium–Full | Maritime influence |
