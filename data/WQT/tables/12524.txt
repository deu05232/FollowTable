# Pole Barn Price Drivers and Package Components (Example  40×60)

| Component / Choice | Typical Option | Cost Impact (USD) | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Base building shell | 40×60 post-frame kit | $22,000–$35,000 | Materials-only packages vary widely |
| Roof style | Gable vs. single-slope | +$1,500–$4,000 | Depends on spans and overhangs |
| Roof steel gauge | 29 ga vs. 26 ga | +$800–$2,500 | Thicker panels cost more |
| Insulation | null vs. spray foam | +$4,000–$12,000 | Climate and R-value drive range |
| Doors | (2) 12×12 overhead + entry door | +$2,500–$7,000 | Brand and opener options matter |
| Windows | (4) standard windows | +$800–$2,400 | Egress/tempered add cost |
| Concrete slab | 4" reinforced slab | +$8,000–$16,000 | Thickness, rebar, site prep vary |
| Electrical rough-in | Basic lighting/outlets | +$1,500–$5,000 | Local labor rates vary |
| Site prep | Minor grading | +$500–$4,000 | Access and soil conditions matter |
| Permits/engineering | Stamped plans | +$800–$3,000 | Jurisdiction-dependent |
