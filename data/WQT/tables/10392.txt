# Central Air Conditioner Repair Costs (Parts and Labor Tasks)

| Central AC Repair Task | Typical Cost | Common Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Capacitor replacement | $120–$350 | AC won’t start |
| Contactor replacement | $140–$380 | Intermittent cooling |
| Refrigerant leak repair | $250–$1,200 | Low cooling performance |
| Blower motor replacement | $450–$1,500 | Weak airflow |
| Circuit board replacement | $300–$1,100 | No response from system |
| Condenser fan motor | $400–$1,200 | Overheating/shutdown |
