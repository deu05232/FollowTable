# Deer behavior and habitat factors influencing garden browsing risk

| Factor category | Example factor | How it affects browsing | When it matters most |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Habitat | Nearby woodland edge | Increases visit frequency | Year-round |
| Water | Irrigation/pond | Attracts regular movement | Summer |
| Cover | Dense shrubs | Provides hiding spots | Fall/Winter |
| Human activity | Pets or fencing presence | Can reduce time in yard | Variable |
