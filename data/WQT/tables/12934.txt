# State-by-state statute of limitations for contract debt (oral vs written agreements)

| State | Oral contract / verbal debt | Written contract | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Alabama | 6 years | 6 years | General contract actions |
| Georgia | 4 years | 6 years | Different periods by contract form |
| Illinois | 5 years | 10 years | Written contracts longer |
| Michigan | 6 years | 6 years | Same period in many cases |
| North Carolina | 3 years | 3 years | Contract actions commonly 3 years |
| Texas | 4 years | 4 years | Contract limitations often aligned |
| Virginia | 3 years | 5 years | Oral shorter than written |
