# New York Statute of Limitations Cheat Sheet (Selected Claims)

| Claim type (New York) | Limitation period | Statute reference | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Written contract | 6 years | N.Y. CPLR 213(2) | Contract actions |
| Oral contract | 6 years | N.Y. CPLR 213(2) | Often treated the same |
| Fraud | 6 years (or 2 from discovery) | N.Y. CPLR 213(8) | Discovery rule applies |
| Medical malpractice | 2.5 years | N.Y. CPLR 214-a | Special accrual rules |
| Defamation (libel/slander) | 1 year | N.Y. CPLR 215(3) | Short period |
| Personal injury | 3 years | N.Y. CPLR 214(5) | Negligence-based |
| Property damage | 3 years | N.Y. CPLR 214(4) | Injury to property |
