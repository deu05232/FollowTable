# Cranial nerves involved in eye movement and what they generally control

| Cranial nerve | Primary role in eye movement | Example muscles mentioned |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Oculomotor (CN III) | Most extraocular movements | Recti and inferior oblique |
| Trochlear (CN IV) | Controls one extraocular muscle | Superior oblique |
| Abducens (CN VI) | Abduction of the eye | Lateral rectus |
| Optic (CN II) | Vision (sensory input) | Not a motor nerve |
