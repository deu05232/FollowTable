# Control Characters and ASCII Escape Sequences (C JSON)

| Name | ASCII DEC | ASCII HEX | Escape Sequence | Typical Use |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| NUL | 0 | 0x00 | \0 | string terminator (C) |
| TAB | 9 | 0x09 | \t | horizontal tab |
| LF | 10 | 0x0A | \n | line feed |
| CR | 13 | 0x0D | \r | carriage return |
| ESC | 27 | 0x1B | \x1B | escape/start control sequence |
| DEL | 127 | 0x7F | \x7F | delete |
| BEL | 7 | 0x07 | \a | bell/alert |
| BS | 8 | 0x08 | \b | backspace |
