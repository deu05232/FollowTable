|---|---:|---:|---|
| Tuition (per credit hour) | $165 | $320 | 12–18 credits typical |
| Mandatory student fee (per semester) | $210 | $210 | Applies to all students |
| Technology fee (per semester) | $95 | $95 | Applies to all students |
| Health services fee (per semester) | $60 | $60 | Waivable with proof of coverage |
| Online course fee (per online credit) | $25 | $25 | Only for fully online sections |
| Course lab fee (selected programs) | $0–$180 | $0–$180 | Varies by course |
| Parking permit (annual) | $75 | $75 | Optional |
| Late registration fee | $50 | $50 | If registering after deadline |
