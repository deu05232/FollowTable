# Texas Misdemeanor Punishment Ranges (Class A B C)

| Offense Level | Maximum Jail Term | Maximum Fine | Examples (Non-exhaustive) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Class A misdemeanor | 1 year | $4,000 | Assault (simple), DWI (first offense) |
| Class B misdemeanor | 180 days | $2,000 | Theft (low value), Criminal trespass |
| Class C misdemeanor | No jail | $500 | Disorderly conduct (some forms) |
| Fine-only misdemeanor (variant) | No jail | $2,000 | Certain regulatory offenses |
| Enhanced Class A (repeat) | 1 year | $4,000 | Repeat family violence contexts |
| Juvenile misdemeanor disposition | Varies | Varies | Community supervision-focused |
| Municipal ordinance violation | Up to 6 months | Varies | City code violations |
| State jail misdemeanor (local) | Up to 1 year | Varies | Local-only category references |
