# # Common kitchen herbs (growth and care overview)

| Herb | Growth habit | Sunlight needs | Typical uses |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| thyme | low-growing perennial | full sun | seasoning |
| mint | spreading perennial | part sun | tea and desserts |
| parsley | biennial | part sun | garnish |
| basil | tender annual | full sun | pesto and salads |
