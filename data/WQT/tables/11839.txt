# Antibiotic Regimens for Other Urinary Conditions (Not Prostatitis)

| Condition | Antibiotic option | Example adult dose | Duration | Comments |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Acute uncomplicated cystitis | Nitrofurantoin | 100 mg twice daily | 5 days | Not for tissue prostatitis penetration |
| Acute uncomplicated cystitis | Fosfomycin | 3 g single dose | 1 day | Convenient single dose |
| Acute uncomplicated cystitis | TMP-SMX | 160/800 mg twice daily | 3 days | Use if local resistance low |
| Pyelonephritis (outpatient) | Ciprofloxacin | 500 mg twice daily | 7 days | Add initial IV dose in some protocols |
| Pyelonephritis (outpatient) | Levofloxacin | 750 mg once daily | 5 days | Short-course option |
| Catheter-associated UTI | Ceftriaxone | 1 g IV once daily | 7-14 days | Adjust to cultures |
| Asymptomatic bacteriuria in pregnancy | Amoxicillin-clavulanate | 500/125 mg three times daily | 5-7 days | Pregnancy-specific |
| Urethritis (chlamydial) | Doxycycline | 100 mg twice daily | 7 days | STI-focused |
