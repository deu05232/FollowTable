# Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC) overview by filing situation and qualifying children

| Family situation | Income limit where the credit is no longer available | Maximum credit amount |
| --- | --- | --- |
| No qualifying children (single) | $17,640 | $600 |
| 1 qualifying child (single) | $46,560 | $3,900 |
| 2 qualifying children (single) | $52,900 | $6,400 |
| 3+ qualifying children (single) | $57,400 | $7,200 |
