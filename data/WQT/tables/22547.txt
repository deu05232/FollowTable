# Neurotransmitter Mapping at Autonomic Synapses (Ganglion vs Effector) Including Common Co-transmitters

| Synapse / site | Autonomic division & fiber type | Primary transmitter | Frequent co-transmitters / modulators | Target (examples) | Cholinergic or adrenergic? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Preganglionic → postganglionic neuron (paravertebral/prevertebral ganglia) | Sympathetic preganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Neuropeptides (e.g., substance P in some pathways) | Postganglionic neuron soma/dendrites | Cholinergic |
| Preganglionic → postganglionic neuron (terminal/intramural ganglia) | Parasympathetic preganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Neuropeptides (e.g., VIP in some pathways) | Postganglionic neuron near/within organ | Cholinergic |
| Postganglionic → effector (most organs) | Sympathetic postganglionic | Norepinephrine (NE) | ATP, neuropeptide Y (NPY) | Vascular smooth muscle, heart (many beds) | Adrenergic (often with co-transmission) |
| Postganglionic → effector (eccrine sweat glands) | Sympathetic postganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | VIP (reported in some sweat gland innervation) | Sweat glands | Cholinergic (sympathetic exception) |
| Postganglionic → effector (many parasympathetic targets) | Parasympathetic postganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nitric oxide (NO), VIP (varies by organ) | GI tract, bronchial smooth muscle, glands | Cholinergic (often modulators present) |
| Preganglionic → chromaffin cells | Sympathetic preganglionic to adrenal medulla | Acetylcholine (ACh) | — | Adrenal medulla (chromaffin cells) | Cholinergic (ganglion-like) |
| Chromaffin secretion → body tissues | Adrenal medulla endocrine output | Epinephrine (major) ± norepinephrine | — | Systemic targets via blood | Adrenergic (hormonal) |
| Enteric plexuses (autonomic-related) | ENS neurons (modulated by ANS) | Acetylcholine (ACh), serotonin (5-HT) | NO, VIP, substance P (common) | Intestinal smooth muscle & glands | Mixed (not purely adrenergic) |
