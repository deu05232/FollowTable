# # Autonomic neurotransmitters by receptor type (summary)

| Neurotransmitter | Main ANS division(s) | Typical fiber location | Common receptor type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | Sympathetic & Parasympathetic | Preganglionic (both divisions) | Nicotinic (ganglionic) |
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | Parasympathetic | Postganglionic | Muscarinic |
| Norepinephrine (NE) | Sympathetic | Postganglionic (most targets) | Adrenergic (α/β) |
| Epinephrine (Epi) | Sympathetic (via adrenal medulla) | Hormone released to blood | Adrenergic (α/β) |
