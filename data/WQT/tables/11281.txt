# GCSE Chemistry Revision  Common Acids and Their Uses

| Acid | Formula | Typical use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hydrochloric acid | HCl | Cleaning metals |
| Sulfuric acid | H2SO4 | Car batteries |
| Nitric acid | HNO3 | Fertilisers |
| Ethanoic acid | CH3COOH | Vinegar |
| Citric acid | C6H8O7 | Food preservative |
| Carbonic acid | H2CO3 | Fizzy drinks |
