# Typical Resistances of Everyday Objects and Components (Not Intrinsic Material Resistivity)

| Item | Geometry / condition | Typical resistance (Ω) | Measurement context |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 m copper hookup wire | ~24 AWG | 0.085 | Room temperature |
| 10 m speaker cable | 2×1.5 mm² | 0.23 | Round-trip |
| Carbon film resistor | 1/4 W | 1000 | Nominal component value |
| Nichrome toaster element | Operating hot | 10–30 | Depends on temperature |
| Incandescent bulb filament | Cold start | 5–15 | Inrush condition |
| Incandescent bulb filament | Lit (steady) | 150–300 | At operating temperature |
| Lead-acid car battery | Internal resistance | 0.003–0.02 | Depends on state of charge |
| Human body (hand-to-hand) | Dry skin | 10000–100000 | Highly variable |
| Human body (hand-to-hand) | Wet skin | 500–5000 | Highly variable |
