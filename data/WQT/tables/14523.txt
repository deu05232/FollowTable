# Plants That Attract Beneficial Insects (Pollinators and Predators)

| Plant | Beneficial Insects Attracted | Benefit Type | Bloom Period | Placement Tip |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dill | Hoverflies, parasitic wasps | Predator support | Summer | Let some plants flower |
| Fennel | Lacewings, lady beetles | Predator support | Summer–fall | Keep away from some crops |
| Yarrow | Lady beetles, predatory wasps | Predator support | Summer | Interplant in borders |
| Alyssum | Hoverflies, minute pirate bugs | Predator support | Spring–fall | Use as living mulch |
| Borage | Bees, syrphid flies | Pollination + predator support | Summer | Plant near fruiting crops |
| Calendula | Hoverflies, tachinid flies | Predator support | Spring–fall | Deadhead to extend bloom |
| Sunflower | Bees, predatory insects | Habitat support | Summer–fall | Use as windbreak |
| Buckwheat | Parasitoid wasps, bees | Rapid nectar source | Summer | Succession sow |
