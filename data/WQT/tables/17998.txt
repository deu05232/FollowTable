# Murder sentencing tiers in the United States (not broken down by state)

| Offense | Typical sentencing description | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| First-degree murder | Life without parole or death | Depends on jurisdiction |
| Second-degree murder | Long-term imprisonment | Varies widely |
| Manslaughter | Multi-year imprisonment | May be voluntary/involuntary |
| Capital murder | Death or life without parole | Often includes aggravating factors |
