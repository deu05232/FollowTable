# Seasonal mule deer diet composition and browsing pressure indicators

| Season | Primary forage focus | Typical browsing pressure | Notes on foraging |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Winter | Woody shrubs & trees | High | Limited green forage; more bark/twigs |
| Spring | New shoots and forbs | Medium | Moves to tender growth |
| Summer | Forbs and agricultural edges | Variable | Depends on moisture and cover |
| Fall | Mast and shrubs | Medium | Builds reserves before winter |
