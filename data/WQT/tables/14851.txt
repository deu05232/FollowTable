# Wine Varietal Body & Flavor Chart (Reds)

| Grape Variety | Typical Flavor Notes | Body (Descriptive) | Common Secondary Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pinot Noir | strawberry, cherry, cranberry | light–medium | earthy, mushroom, rose, subtle spice |
| Gamay (Beaujolais) | red cherry, raspberry, violet | light | bubblegum, banana (carbonic), pepper |
| Grenache (Garnacha) | ripe strawberry, raspberry, plum | medium | white pepper, dried herbs, warm spice |
| Sangiovese | sour cherry, red plum, tomato leaf | medium | leather, oregano, balsamic notes |
| Merlot | plum, blackberry, chocolate | medium–full | vanilla, mocha, soft tannins |
| Syrah (Shiraz) | blackberry, blueberry, black pepper | full | smoked meat, olive, cocoa |
| Cabernet Sauvignon | blackcurrant, cassis, blackberry | full | cedar, tobacco, graphite, oak spice |
| Malbec | black plum, blackberry, violet | medium–full | cocoa, sweet spice, smoky notes |
