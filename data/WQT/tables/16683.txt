# GCSE Subject Options Overview (by broad subject area)

| Subject area | Example GCSE subjects offered (choices) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| English | English Language; English Literature | Often both taken; some schools bundle as a pair |
| Mathematics | Mathematics; Further Mathematics | Further Maths typically for higher-attaining sets |
| Sciences | Biology; Chemistry; Physics; Combined Science (Trilogy); Computer Science | Students may choose Combined or separate sciences |
| Languages | French; German; Spanish; Mandarin Chinese; Latin | Availability varies by school staffing |
| Humanities | History; Geography; Religious Studies; Citizenship | RS sometimes compulsory or optional depending on policy |
| Arts & Performing | Art & Design; Music; Drama; Dance; Photography | Practical coursework-heavy options |
| Design & Technology | Product Design; Resistant Materials; Graphics; Textiles; Food Preparation & Nutrition | Often grouped under D&T with different specialisms |
| Business & Enterprise | Business Studies; Enterprise; Economics | Some schools offer only one of these at GCSE |
| Sport & Health | Physical Education (PE); Sport Studies; Health & Social Care | May be GCSE or vocational equivalent |
| Computing & ICT | ICT; Creative iMedia; Digital Media | ICT is sometimes replaced by more modern vocational specs |
| Vocational / Applied | BTEC Tech Awards (e.g., Business, Sport, Performing Arts) | Treated as “options” alongside GCSEs in many schools |
