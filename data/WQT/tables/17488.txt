# Starbucks caffeine overview by beverage family (not size-by-size)

| Starbucks beverage family | Common examples | Typical caffeine range (mg) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Brewed coffee | Pike Place Roast, Blonde Roast | 150-300 | Varies by roast and batch |
| Espresso-based | Latte, Cappuccino, Mocha | 75-225 | Depends on number of shots |
| Tea | Black tea, Green tea | 0-80 | Some are caffeine-free |
| Decaf options | Decaf brewed coffee, decaf espresso drinks | 0-25 | Not fully caffeine-free |
