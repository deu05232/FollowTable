# Maui Fever
> Cast

| Cast Member Information |
| --- |
| Chaunte LeBlanc (born c.1986 in the continental United States) moved to Maui at the age of four. [5] She graduated from King Kekaulike High School in Pukalani (on the island of Maui). [18] As of the time of filming Maui Fever , Chaunte worked as a restaurant hostess at Leilani's in Kaanapali. [19] According to MTV, she had a "rough childhood," which contributed to her fear of commitment and distaste for being "tied down." [20] Anthony was her on-again/off-again boyfriend throughout Maui Fever . [3] On the show Chaunte often told Anthony she wanted to be "just friends," but also found herself upset by the idea of him dating anyone else. [20] |
| Cheyne Magnusson (born c.1983 in Southern California ) moved to Hawaii with his family at the age of seven. [5] He graduated from Lahainaluna High School in Lahaina (on the island of Maui). [16] His father is professional skateboarder Tony Magnusson . [21] Cheyne is a professional surfer, with sponsorships from Body Glove , Osiris shoes, Dragon and Borst Surfboards. [22] Cheyne, also an excellent skateboarder, appeared in the film Lords of Dogtown and did his own skating. [23] On Maui Fever Cheyne was at the center of a love triangle between his girlfriend Rachael and his ex-girlfriend Anna. [3] He also was the show's narrator. [3] |
| Anna Stimson (born c.1986 in the United States) moved to Maui from Oregon in 2003. [5] On Maui Fever , she was Chaunte's roommate and a fellow restaurant hostess. [18] It was revealed on Maui Fever that Anna's father and Chaunte's father both died prior to the time of the show, which was another common bond between the girls. [24] Anna and Chaunte were "partners-in-crime," usually appearing together in scenes. [3] Throughout the series, Anna struggled with her lingering feelings for ex-boyfriend Cheyne. [3] Anna remained close to Cheyne even after he started dating Rachael, resulting in much drama. [25] Regarding the experience of filming Maui Fever , Anna has admitted that she often forgot the cameras were rolling. [26] According to Anna, some days after shooting she reviewed the events in her mind and thought to herself "What did I just do?" [26] |
| David Corbin (born c.1982 in California), goes by his last name on Maui Fever . [5] He has lived in Hawaii since the age of six and grew up on the Big Island . [5] [18] Corbin, a musician, expressed hope that Maui Fever would boost his rising career. [18] Corbin's main passion is to work with people in a professional capacity, such as in the field of real estate. [27] Corbin was referred to as the clown of the Maui Fever group. [18] He was often shown surfing the beaches of Hawaii. [3] |
| Anthony Vicar (born c.1987 on the Big Island , Hawaii) was raised on the island of Hawaiʻi . [18] He later moved to Maui and became good friends with Cheyne. [28] Like Cheyne, Anthony graduated from Lahainaluna High School on the island of Maui. [18] During the filming of Maui Fever Anthony lived in a loft space in Cheyne's apartment. [28] Anthony, an artist, [29] was portrayed as the somewhat reserved member of the cast. [3] He and Chaunte began dating before the start of filming. One of the featured storylines on Maui Fever followed Anthony's discomfort with Chaunte's party-girl behavior and demands for "space" from him. [28] With respect to his appearance on the show, Anthony has said "I'm comfortable with who I am. I don't care. They can make me out for who they want." [18] |
| Sean McClenahan (born c.1982 in the continental United States ) moved to Maui in 2000. [26] As of February, 2007, Sean was working at the Hula Grill Kaanapali restaurant. [5] When the cast of Maui Fever came under criticism for the carefree partying featured on the show, the restaurant's general manager defended Sean's character, saying that he was "a great server, . . . charming and very nice," even though the show did not depict him as a hard worker. [5] Most often, Sean was shown surfing and flirting with tourist chicks. [19] After one of Chaunte's breaks from Anthony during Maui Fever , Sean made a move on her with an invitation to go out for a good "steak and lob." [3] While filming Sean grew tired of the crowds that would gather to watch and said he could see how being famous "could get annoying." [26] |
| Jesse Beyer (born c.1985 in the midwest United States) moved to Maui from Chicago in 2004. [5] Prior to shooting Maui Fever , Jesse moved in with his good friend Corbin and become part of the Kaanapali crew. [30] Still, he often felt like the proverbial fish out of water . [31] He was often featured on the show picking up girls with Corbin, but eventually found himself breaking the Maui Fever crew's number one rule: don't fall for a tourist. [30] |
