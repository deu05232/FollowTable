# Infant Clothing Size Chart (Age-Based) with Height Weight Guidance

| Clothing size | Approx. age | Weight range (lb) | Length/height range (in) | Fit tips |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Preemie | 0–1 mo | 4–6 | 17–19 | Runs small; size up if between ranges |
| Newborn (NB) | 0–2 mo | 6–9 | 19–21 | Prioritize easy diaper access |
| 0–3M | 0–3 mo | 8–12 | 21–24 | Brands vary; check shrinkage |
| 3–6M | 3–6 mo | 12–16 | 24–26.5 | Consider stretchy fabrics |
| 6–9M | 6–9 mo | 16–20 | 26.5–28.5 | Roomy fit for diapers |
| 9–12M | 9–12 mo | 20–24 | 28.5–30.5 | Layering can affect fit |
| 12–18M | 12–18 mo | 24–27 | 30–32 | Many toddlers need larger waist |
| 18–24M | 18–24 mo | 27–30 | 32–34 | Often overlaps with 2T in some brands |
