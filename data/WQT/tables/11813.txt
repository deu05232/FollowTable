# U.S. Seat Belt Law Summary by Region (Not Individual States)

| U.S. Region | Typical Enforcement Pattern | Common Coverage Wording | Seating Positions Commonly Covered | Example States (Non-exhaustive) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Northeast | Mixed primary/secondary | Often includes rear seat requirements for minors | Front + rear for minors | NY, NJ, PA |
| Southeast | Often primary for front seats | Adults front-seat emphasized | Front (some all seats) | FL, GA, AL |
| Midwest | Mixed | Driver + front passenger common | Front (some all seats) | IL, OH, MI |
| Southwest | Often primary | Broad driver/passenger coverage | Front + all seats in some areas | AZ, NM, NV |
| Mountain West | More secondary | Adult front-seat focus | Front | ID, MT, WY |
| Pacific | Often primary | Broad occupant coverage | All seats | CA, OR, WA |
| U.S. Territories | Varies | Varies | Varies | PR, GU, VI |
