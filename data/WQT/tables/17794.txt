# Pantry Shelf Life of Unopened Packaged Foods (Best-By Focus)

| Food (Unopened) | Pantry / Room Temperature | Notes (Best-By / Quality) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Canned soup | 2–5 years | Quality best within 1–2 years |
| Dry pasta | 1–2 years | After best-by, texture may degrade |
| White rice | 4–5 years | Keep sealed and dry |
| Breakfast cereal | 6–12 months | Stales faster after opening |
| Peanut butter | 6–12 months | Oil separation is normal |
| Crackers | 6–9 months | Quality varies by fat content |
| Jarred salsa | 12–18 months | Unopened only |
| Shelf-stable UHT milk | 6–9 months | Until best-by date |
