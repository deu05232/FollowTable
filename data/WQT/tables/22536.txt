# Autonomic Neurotransmitters and Typical Receptor Targets (High-level)

| Neurotransmitter | Typical autonomic source | Primary synapse context | Typical receptor family |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | Preganglionic sympathetic neurons | Autonomic ganglia | Nicotinic (Nn) |
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | Preganglionic parasympathetic neurons | Autonomic ganglia | Nicotinic (Nn) |
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | Postganglionic parasympathetic neurons | Effector organs | Muscarinic (M) |
| Norepinephrine (NE) | Postganglionic sympathetic neurons | Effector organs | Adrenergic (α/β) |
| Epinephrine (Epi) | Adrenal medulla (sympathetic) | Circulation to effectors | Adrenergic (α/β) |
| Dopamine (DA) | Some sympathetic pathways (e.g., renal vasculature) | Effector organs | Dopaminergic (D) |
