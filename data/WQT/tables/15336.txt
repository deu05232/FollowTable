# Common Functional Groups (Oxygen-Containing and Halogenated Derivatives)

| Category | Functional group | General formula | Recognition cue | Example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Oxygen-containing | Ether | R–O–R' | Oxygen between two carbons | Diethyl ether |
| Oxygen-containing | Epoxide | cyclic R–O–R' | Three-membered ring with O | Oxirane |
| Oxygen-containing | Acyl halide | R–C(=O)X | Carbonyl attached to halogen | Acetyl chloride |
| Halogenated | Alkyl chloride | R–Cl | C–Cl single bond | Chloroethane |
| Halogenated | Alkyl bromide | R–Br | C–Br single bond | Bromoethane |
| Halogenated | Alkyl fluoride | R–F | C–F single bond | Fluoroethane |
| Oxygen-containing | Anhydride | (RCO)2O | Two acyl groups linked by O | Acetic anhydride |
