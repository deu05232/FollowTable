| Felony class | Example classification label | Maximum prison term | Maximum fine |
|---|---|---|---|
| Capital felony | Capital | Death or life imprisonment | Not specified |
| Life felony | Life | Life imprisonment | $15,000 |
| First-degree felony | F1 | Up to 30 years | $10,000 |
| Second-degree felony | F2 | Up to 15 years | $10,000 |
| Third-degree felony | F3 | Up to 5 years | $5,000 |
| Fourth-degree felony | (if applicable in specific statutes) | Up to 5 years (where defined) | (varies) |
