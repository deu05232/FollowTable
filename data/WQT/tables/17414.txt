# Career Paths for Economics Majors  Analyst, Policy, Research, and Market Roles (Example Job Titles)

| Role family | Specific job title | Typical employer types | What you do day-to-day (short) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Economic research | Research Assistant (Economics) | Universities, think tanks, central banks | Clean data, run regressions, draft memos |
| Economic research | Economic Research Analyst | Consulting firms, research institutes | Build forecasts, analyze indicators, present insights |
| Policy & public sector | Policy Analyst (Economic Policy) | Government agencies, NGOs | Evaluate policy options, cost-benefit analysis |
| Policy & public sector | Budget Analyst | City/state/federal agencies | Track spending, model scenarios, write budget reports |
| Data & analytics | Data Analyst (Economics) | Tech, finance, public sector | Query datasets, dashboard KPIs, interpret trends |
| Finance & markets | Equity Research Analyst | Investment banks, asset managers | Company/industry analysis, valuation models, reports |
| Finance & markets | Credit Risk Analyst | Banks, fintech lenders | Assess borrower risk, build scorecards, monitor portfolios |
| Business economics | Pricing Analyst | Retail, SaaS, airlines | Price tests, elasticity analysis, promo evaluation |
| Business economics | Market Research Analyst | Consumer goods, agencies | Survey analysis, segmentation, demand sizing |
| International & development | Development Economist (Junior/Associate) | NGOs, development banks | Program evaluation, impact measurement, field data |
| Regulation & competition | Antitrust Economist (Analyst) | Law firms, economic consulting | Market definition, damages models, expert support |
