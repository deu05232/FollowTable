# # Pantry Shelf Life of Common Foods (Opened vs Unopened)

| Food item | How long it lasts unopened at room temperature | How long it lasts after opening at room temperature | Notes about packaging |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jarred pasta sauce | 12–18 months | 5–7 days | Keep lid tightly closed |
| Peanut butter | 6–12 months | 2–3 months | Oil separation is normal |
| Maple syrup | 2–3 years | 6–12 months | Crystallization can occur |
| Boxed broth (shelf-stable) | 1 year | 2 days | Refrigerate after opening |
| Canned tuna | 3–5 years | 1 day | Transfer to a container after opening |
