# National Highways in Uttar Pradesh  key corridors and their in-state routes (sample extract)

| NH (current) | In-state route (Uttar Pradesh) | Major junctions/termini in UP | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| NH 19 | From Prayagraj through Mirzapur to Varanasi and onward toward Bihar border | Prayagraj – Mirzapur – Varanasi – Chandauli | Part of an east–west corridor |
| NH 27 | From Gorakhpur toward Ayodhya and Lucknow region | Gorakhpur – Basti – Ayodhya – Lucknow | Connects eastern UP to the capital region |
| NH 30 | From Lucknow region toward Prayagraj | Lucknow – Rae Bareli/Pratapgarh region – Prayagraj | Regional connector within UP |
| NH 34 | From Lucknow toward Sitapur/Shahjahanpur direction | Lucknow – Sitapur – Hardoi belt | North-central UP corridor |
| NH 31 | Along the Ganga belt in eastern UP | Ghazipur – Ballia belt (toward Bihar) | Focus on eastern riverine belt |
| NH 9 | Toward western UP from Ghaziabad area | Ghaziabad – Hapur – Moradabad | Western UP radial route |
