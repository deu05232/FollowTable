# Blood Pressure Categories Listed as Combined Readings (Single Value Format)

| Clinical label | Example combined reading (mm Hg) | Interpretation cue | Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Low (hypotension) | <90/60 | Both numbers low | Assess symptoms |
| Normal | 110/70 to 119/79 | Typical healthy range | Routine checks |
| Elevated | 120/80 to 129/80 | Slightly above ideal | Lifestyle changes |
| Stage 1 hypertension | 130/80 to 139/89 | Mildly high | Confirm with repeats |
| Stage 2 hypertension | 140/90 to 179/119 | Clearly high | Clinical evaluation |
| Hypertensive crisis | ≥180/120 | Danger zone | Urgent care |
