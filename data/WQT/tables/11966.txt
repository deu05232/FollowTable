# Cooked leftovers storage times (fridge vs freezer)

| Cooked food | Refrigerator | Freezer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked chicken (pieces) | 3–4 days | 4 months |
| Cooked ground beef | 3–4 days | 3 months |
| Cooked pasta (plain) | 3–5 days | 2 months |
| Cooked soup (vegetable) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
