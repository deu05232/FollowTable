# Shelf life of pantry staples (includes some fridge notes)

| Item | Pantry | Refrigerator | Freezer |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vegetable oil (opened) | 6–8 months | 9–12 months | Not recommended |
| Maple syrup (opened) | Not recommended | Indefinite | Not recommended |
| Fresh parsley | Not recommended | 3 weeks | Not recommended |
| Packaged bread (soft) | 5–7 days | Not recommended | 3 months |
| Corn syrup (unopened) | Indefinite | Not needed | Not needed |
| Walnut oil (unopened) | 1 year | 1 year | Not recommended |
