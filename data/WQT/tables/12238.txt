# Impedance Models of Basic Circuit Elements vs Frequency

| Circuit element | Symbol | Impedance Z(ω) | Magnitude behavior | Typical use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Resistor | R | R | Constant with frequency | Current limiting |
| Capacitor | C | 1/(jωC) | Decreases as ω increases | Filtering, coupling |
| Inductor | L | jωL | Increases as ω increases | Chokes, filters |
| Series RLC | R+L+C | R + j(ωL - 1/(ωC)) | Resonant peak | Tuned circuits |
| Parallel RC | R||C | 1/(1/R + jωC) | Frequency-dependent | Timing networks |
| Transmission line (lossless) | TL | jZ0 tan(βl) | Periodic vs frequency | RF matching |
| Warburg element | W | σ/√(jω) | Diffusion-like | Electrochemistry EIS |
