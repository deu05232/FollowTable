# # NPT Pipe Tapping Setup Guide (Reamer-focused)

| Pipe thread callout | Threads per inch | Reamer / taper-prep guidance | Drill size before reaming |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1/8 NPT | 27 | use taper pipe reamer to depth | 11/32 |
| 1/4 NPT | 18 | ream to fit gauge | 7/16 |
| 3/8 NPT | 18 | ream gradually, check frequently | 37/64 |
| 1/2 NPT | 14 | ream to proper taper | 23/32 |
