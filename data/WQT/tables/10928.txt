# # Minimum credit score requirements by peer-to-peer lending platform

| Platform | Minimum Credit Score | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Funding Circle (personal loans) | 660 | Minimum score for applicants; other underwriting applies |
| Peerform | 600 | Minimum score may vary by state |
| RateSetter (US, historical) | 650 | Requirement depended on product availability |
| StreetShares | 640 | May depend on business profile in addition to credit |
| Kiva (partner-facilitated) | No minimum listed | Uses character-based and partner screening rather than a published score cutoff |
