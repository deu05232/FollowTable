# State Seat Belt Laws (Selected States) — Coverage, Seating Position, and Enforcement Type

| State | Enforcement type | Who is covered | In what seat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Florida | Primary | Front-seat occupants; minors in rear seats | Front (all occupants); Rear (minors) |
| Texas | Primary | Drivers and passengers age 8+ | All |
| New Hampshire | Secondary / limited | Under 18 only | All |
| New York | Primary | Drivers and front-seat passengers; passengers under 16 | Front (all); Rear (under 16) |
