# Spring Material Selection Guide — Corrosion and Temperature Ratings (Without Mechanical Properties)

| Material Family | Example Grades | Corrosion Resistance | Max Service Temp (°C) | Electrical Conductivity | Magnetic | Typical Use Environment |
|---|---|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Carbon spring steel | A228, A229 | Low (needs coating) | 120 | Low | Ferromagnetic | Dry indoor mechanisms |
| Alloy spring steel | 5160, 9254 | Low–Medium | 200 | Low | Ferromagnetic | Automotive springs |
| Stainless steel | 302, 17-7PH | High | 300 | Low | Varies | Marine/light chemicals |
| Phosphor bronze | C51000 | High | 200 | Medium | Non-magnetic | Electrical contacts |
| Beryllium copper | C17200 | High | 250 | High | Non-magnetic | Precision connectors |
| Titanium | Grade 5 (Ti-6Al-4V) | Very high | 350 | Low | Non-magnetic | Aerospace/corrosive |
| Inconel | 718, X-750 | Very high | 650 | Low | Non-magnetic | High-temp turbines |
| Polymer (POM/PP) | Acetal, PP | Medium | 90 | Insulator | Non-magnetic | Low-load snap springs |
