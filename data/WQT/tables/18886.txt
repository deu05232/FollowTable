# Iran parental leave  eligibility rules and documentation requirements

| Policy element | Summary for Iran |
| --- | --- |
| Covered workers | Employees enrolled in social insurance |
| Minimum contribution period | 6 months of contributions in prior year |
| Employer size restrictions | null stated |
| Required documents | Birth certificate, employer confirmation, insurance record |
| Notice period | 30 days before expected due date (if possible) |
| Job protection | Position guaranteed during approved leave |
| Return-to-work conditions | Medical clearance and employer coordination |
