# Solubility Rules by Anion (Includes Ion List plus Rule Statements)

| Anion family | Example salts | Usually soluble? | Key exceptions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Nitrates | NaNO3, KNO3 | Yes | Rarely insoluble |
| Acetates | CH3COONa, Ca(CH3COO)2 | Yes | AgCH3COO low solubility |
| Chlorides | NaCl, CaCl2 | Yes | AgCl, PbCl2, Hg2Cl2 |
| Sulfates | Na2SO4, MgSO4 | Yes | BaSO4, PbSO4, CaSO4 (slightly) |
| Carbonates | Na2CO3, CaCO3 | No | Group 1 and NH4+ are soluble |
| Hydroxides | NaOH, Ca(OH)2 | No | Group 1 soluble; Ba(OH)2 somewhat |
| Sulfides | Na2S, FeS | No | Group 1 and Group 2 (some) |
