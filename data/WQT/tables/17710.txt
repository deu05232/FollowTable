# Extraocular Muscles  Orbital Passageways and Related Cranial Nerves

| Extraocular muscle | Main action (primary) | Orbital opening / route emphasized | Cranial nerve(s) mentioned (not assigned as the innervator) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Medial rectus | Adduction | Superior orbital fissure (tendinous ring region) | CN III listed among nerves traversing the fissure |
| Lateral rectus | Abduction | Superior orbital fissure (common tendinous ring) | CN VI appears in fissure contents lists |
| Superior rectus | Elevation | Superior orbital fissure (tendinous ring region) | CN III noted as present in the orbital apex |
| Inferior rectus | Depression | Superior orbital fissure (tendinous ring region) | CN III noted in the orbital apex |
| Superior oblique | Intorsion | Orbit → trochlea region; orbital roof landmarks | CN IV referenced in “structures near trochlea” notes |
| Inferior oblique | Extorsion | Orbital floor landmarks; infraorbital groove vicinity | CN III referenced as part of anterior orbit innervation |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Eyelid elevation | Superior orbital fissure | CN III mentioned in the fissure contents |
| Orbicularis oculi (comparison) | Eyelid closure | Stylomastoid foramen (outside orbit) | CN VII referenced for facial muscles |
| Superior tarsal (Müller) muscle (comparison) | Lid elevation (sympathetic) | Cervical sympathetic chain | Sympathetic fibers referenced (not a cranial nerve) |
