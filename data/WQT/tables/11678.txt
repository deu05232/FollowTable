# Common oxygen-containing functional groups and their naming (group-centric)

| Functional group | Typical structural notation | General formula | Common IUPAC prefix | Common IUPAC suffix | Example compound |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hydroxyl (alcohol) | R–OH | ROH | hydroxy- | -ol | ethanol |
| Ether | R–O–R' | ROR' | alkoxy- | (no suffix; use alkoxy naming) | diethyl ether |
| Aldehyde (formyl) | R–C(=O)H | RCHO | formyl- | -al | ethanal |
| Ketone (carbonyl) | R–C(=O)–R' | RCOR' | oxo- | -one | propanone |
| Carboxylic acid (carboxyl) | R–C(=O)OH | RCOOH | carboxy- | -oic acid | ethanoic acid |
| Ester | R–C(=O)–OR' | RCOOR' | alkoxycarbonyl- | -oate | ethyl ethanoate |
