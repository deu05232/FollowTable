# Meal Planning Guide  Adding Fiber Using Food Groups (Serving Counts Instead of Fiber Grams)

| Meal component | Food group | Practical examples | Suggested servings/day | Tracking tip |
|---|---|---|---:|---|
| Breakfast base | Whole grains | oatmeal, whole-wheat toast | 2 | choose whole grain |
| Breakfast add-on | Fruit | berries, pears | 1–2 | keep fruit visible |
| Lunch protein | Legumes | lentil soup, bean salad | 1 | batch cook |
| Lunch side | Vegetables | broccoli, carrots | 2 | frozen counts |
| Snack | Nuts/seeds | almonds, pumpkin seeds | 1 | portion into bags |
| Dinner base | Whole grains | brown rice, quinoa | 1 | swap refined grains |
| Dinner side | Vegetables | leafy greens, Brussels sprouts | 2 | aim for variety |
