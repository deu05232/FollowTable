# Cranial Nerve Nuclei and Functional Components Related to Eye Movements

| Nucleus / structure | Associated cranial nerve | Brain region | Functional role | Example clinical sign |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Oculomotor nucleus | CN III | Midbrain | Somatic motor to extraocular effectors | Ophthalmoplegia |
| Edinger–Westphal nucleus | CN III | Midbrain | Parasympathetic to pupil constriction | Dilated pupil, loss of light reflex |
| Trochlear nucleus | CN IV | Midbrain | Somatic motor for vertical/torsional control | Worse diplopia on stairs |
| Abducens nucleus | CN VI | Pons | Horizontal gaze generation | Failure of abduction |
| Medial longitudinal fasciculus (MLF) | III/IV/VI coordination | Brainstem tract | Internuclear coordination of gaze | Internuclear ophthalmoplegia |
| Superior colliculus | Eye movement pathways | Midbrain | Saccade initiation | Impaired saccades |
| Vestibular nuclei | Eye movement reflexes | Pons/medulla | Vestibulo-ocular reflex | Nystagmus |
