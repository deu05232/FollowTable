# Kitchen Storage Cheat Sheet  Leftovers, Herbs, Oils, and Condiments

| Section | Item | Refrigerator | Freezer | Extra Storage Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cooked leftovers | Cooked soup | 3–4 days | 2–3 months | Cool in shallow containers |
| Cooked leftovers | Cooked chicken | 3–4 days | 2–6 months | — |
| Cooked leftovers | Cooked pasta | 3–5 days | 1–2 months | Add sauce to prevent drying |
| Herbs | Fresh mint | 1–2 weeks | Not recommended | Keep stems in water |
| Herbs | Fresh parsley | 2–3 weeks | Not recommended | Bag loosely |
| Oils | Avocado oil (opened) | 9–12 months | Not recommended | Keep away from heat |
| Condiments | Pancake syrup (opened) | 6–12 months | Not recommended | Avoid contamination |
| Condiments | Soy sauce | 1–2 years | Not recommended | Quality varies |
