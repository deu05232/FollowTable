# State-by-state governor term-limit type (consecutive vs lifetime vs none)

| State | Limit type | What it means |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Florida | Consecutive | Limited to two terms in a row |
| Tennessee | Consecutive | Must sit out after serving the maximum consecutively |
| Missouri | Lifetime | No more than two terms total |
| Massachusetts | null | No restriction on number of terms |
| Washington | Consecutive | Two consecutive terms allowed |
| Vermont | null | No term limit for governor |
