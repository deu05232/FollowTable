# New York Drug Offense Sentencing (Selected Controlled Substance Felonies)

| Offense (NY Penal Law) | Felony Level | Maximum Prison Term | Maximum Fine | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Criminal possession of a controlled substance (7th) | Misdemeanor | 1 year | $1,000 | Often charged for small amounts |
| Criminal possession (5th) | Class D felony | 7 years | $5,000 | Quantity/prior record dependent |
| Criminal sale (5th) | Class D felony | 7 years | $5,000 | Sale element required |
| Criminal possession (3rd) | Class B felony | 25 years | $30,000 | Serious weight thresholds |
| Criminal sale (3rd) | Class B felony | 25 years | $30,000 | Higher-level trafficking |
| Criminal sale (1st) | Class A-I felony | Life | $100,000 | Highest-level drug sale |
| Conspiracy to sell (variant) | Class C felony | 15 years | $15,000 | Depends on charged conspiracy degree |
| Attempted sale (variant) | Reduced one class | Varies | Varies | Attempt reduces grade by one |
