# Exchange Market Microstructure  Tick Size and Trading Mechanism Overview

| Exchange | Market Model | Auction Phases | Lot Size | Tick Size Rule (Illustrative) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| NYSE | Hybrid (auction + continuous) | Open/Close auctions | 1 share | Price-tiered ticks |
| NASDAQ | Dealer/quote-driven + continuous | Open/Close crosses | 1 share | Sub-dollar increments vary |
| LSE | Order-driven | Opening/Closing auctions | 1 share | Tick table by price bands |
| TSE | Order-driven | Opening/Closing auctions | 100 shares (common) | Tick by price range |
| HKEX | Order-driven | Opening auction session | Board lot varies | Tick by price range |
| SSE | Order-driven | Call auctions at open/close | 100 shares | Tick by price range |
| TSX | Order-driven | Opening auction | 1 share | Tick by price bands |
| ASX | Order-driven | Opening/Closing auctions | 1 share | Tick by security/price |
| SGX | Order-driven | Open/Close auctions | 1 share | Tick by price bands |
| B3 | Order-driven | Opening auction | 1 share | Tick by price bands |
