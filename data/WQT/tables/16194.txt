# Solubility rules by anion (grouped, not one ion per row clearly)

| Anion group | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Halides (Cl-, Br-, I-) | Usually soluble except with Ag+, Pb2+, Hg2^2+ |
| Sulfates (SO4^2-) | Soluble except with Ba2+, Sr2+, Pb2+ |
| Carbonates (CO3^2-) | Usually insoluble except with Group 1 and NH4+ |
