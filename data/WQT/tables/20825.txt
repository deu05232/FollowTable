# Concerta Administration Tips, Timing, and Missed-Dose Instructions (No Dose Amounts)

| Scenario | Recommendation | Rationale | Applies to | Dose amounts included? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Morning dosing | Take once daily in the morning | May reduce insomnia risk | All patients | No |
| With food | May be taken with or without food | Minimal effect on absorption for many patients | All patients | No |
| Swallowing | Swallow whole; do not crush/chew/split | Protects extended-release mechanism | All patients | No |
| Missed dose (same morning) | Take when remembered if early | Maintains daytime coverage | All patients | No |
| Missed dose (afternoon/evening) | Skip and resume next morning | Avoids sleep disruption | All patients | No |
| Switching time zones | Keep morning schedule local time when possible | Maintains symptom control | Travelers | No |
| Storage | Store at room temperature; secure from diversion | Controlled substance precautions | Caregivers/adults | No |
