# Bottled Water pH Ranges by Category (No Brand Names)

| Bottled water category | Typical pH range |
|---|---|
| Purified water | 5.5–6.5 |
| Spring water | 6.0–7.5 |
| Artesian water | 6.5–8.0 |
| Mineral water | 5.0–7.0 |
| Alkaline-enhanced water | 8.5–10.0 |
| Sparkling water | 3.5–5.5 |
| Electrolyte water | 6.5–8.5 |
