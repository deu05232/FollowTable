# Seat Belt Laws by Country (Comparison Across Nations)

| Country | Enforcement Type | Who Must Wear a Belt | Seating Positions Covered | Typical Penalty |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| United States | Varies by state | Varies by state | Front and/or all seats | Varies |
| Canada | Primary in most provinces | Typically 16+ | All seats | CAD 100–300 |
| United Kingdom | Primary enforcement | Adults and children (with child restraint rules) | Front and rear | Up to GBP 500 |
| Australia | Primary enforcement | All occupants | All seats | Varies by state |
| Germany | Primary enforcement | All occupants | All seats | EUR 30–60 |
| Japan | Primary enforcement | Front always; rear often required | Front and rear | JPY 4,000–9,000 |
| Mexico | Varies by state | Varies | Front and/or all seats | Varies |
