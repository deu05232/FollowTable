# State Executive Term Limits (Governor, Lieutenant Governor, Attorney General)

| State | Governor term-limit rule | Lieutenant governor term limit | Attorney general term limit | Other statewide offices |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Florida | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms | CFO: 2 consecutive terms |
| Texas | No term limits | No term limits | No term limits | Land Commissioner: no limits |
| Colorado | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms | Treasurer: 2 consecutive terms |
| Delaware | 2 terms | 2 terms | 2 terms | Treasurer: 2 terms |
| Georgia | 2 consecutive terms* | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms | Insurance Commissioner: no limits |
| New Mexico | 2 consecutive terms | No term limits | No term limits | Treasurer: 2 consecutive terms |
| Alabama | 2 consecutive terms | No term limits | No term limits | Auditor: no limits |
| Idaho | No term limits | No term limits | No term limits | Controller: no limits |
