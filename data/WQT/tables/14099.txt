# Orbital routes  where eye-related cranial nerves pass

| Skull opening or canal | Nerves passing through | Orbital region reached |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Superior orbital fissure | Oculomotor (III), Trochlear (IV), Abducens (VI), V1 branches | Orbit |
| Optic canal | Optic (II) | Orbit |
| Inferior orbital fissure | Infraorbital nerve (V2 branch) | Orbital floor/face |
| Foramen rotundum | Maxillary nerve (V2) | Pterygopalatine fossa |
