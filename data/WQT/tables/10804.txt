# Oral antibiotic dosing options for chronic bacterial prostatitis (regimen-focused reference)

| Medication | Dose per administration | Dosing interval | Route | Comments |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ciprofloxacin | 500 mg | every 12 hours | PO | Common regimen in adults |
| Levofloxacin | 500–750 mg | every 24 hours | PO | Dose selection varies by clinical context |
| TMP-SMX (DS) | 160/800 mg | every 12 hours | PO | Use when susceptible isolate |
| Ofloxacin | 300 mg | every 12 hours | PO | Alternative fluoroquinolone |
| Doxycycline | 100 mg | every 12 hours | PO | Consider for atypical/STI coverage |
| Fosfomycin | 3 g | every 48–72 hours | PO | Sometimes used in resistant cases (specialist-directed) |
