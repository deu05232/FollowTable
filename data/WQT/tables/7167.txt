# Sweetness of wine
> Terms used to indicate sweetness of wine

| Rating | Sugar content (grams per litre) |
| --- | --- |
| Brut Nature (no added sugar) | 0–3 |
| Extra Brut | 0–6 |
| Brut | 0–12 |
| Extra Dry, Extra Sec, Extra seco | 12–17 |
| Dry, Sec, Seco | 17–32 |
| Demi-sec, Semi-seco | 32–50 |
| Doux, Sweet, Dulce | 50+ |
