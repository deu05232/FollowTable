# Cranial Nerve Nuclei and Target Muscles (Eye Movement Focus + Brainstem Levels)

| Nucleus | Brainstem level | Cranial nerve | Example target(s) | Clinical correlation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Oculomotor nucleus | Midbrain | CN III | Medial rectus; Inferior rectus | Ipsilateral ophthalmoplegia |
| Edinger–Westphal nucleus | Midbrain | CN III (parasympathetic) | Sphincter pupillae | Fixed dilated pupil |
| Trochlear nucleus | Midbrain (dorsal) | CN IV | Superior oblique | Vertical diplopia |
| Abducens nucleus | Pons | CN VI | Lateral rectus | Horizontal diplopia |
| Facial nucleus | Pons | CN VII | Orbicularis oculi | Can't close eye |
| Vestibular nuclei | Pons/Medulla | CN VIII | VOR pathways | Nystagmus |
| Param. pontine reticular formation (PPRF) | Pons | (Not a CN) | Horizontal gaze center | Gaze palsy |
