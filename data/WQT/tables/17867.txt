# Need for Speed Series Timeline by Era (Grouped Years)

| Era (Years) | Major Entries Included | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1994–1997 | The Need for Speed; Need for Speed II; Need for Speed III: Hot Pursuit | Early Road & supercar focus |
| 1998–2002 | High Stakes; Porsche Unleashed; Hot Pursuit 2 | Licensed brands & pursuit mechanics |
| 2003–2006 | Underground; Underground 2; Most Wanted; Carbon | Street racing & customization boom |
| 2007–2009 | ProStreet; Undercover; Shift; Nitro | Split between sim-leaning and arcade entries |
| 2010–2013 | World; Hot Pursuit (2010); The Run; Most Wanted (2012); Rivals | Online service title and reboots/remakes |
| 2015–2019 | Need for Speed (2015); Payback; Heat | Narrative-driven “Ghost era” |
| 2022–2023 | Unbound | Modern stylized reboot direction |
