# Diet Planning Tips Based on Glycemic Index (Meal-Level Guidance)

| Goal | Recommended Approach | Example Meal | Rationale | Practical Tip |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reduce post-meal spikes | Pair carbs with protein/fat | Chicken + quinoa + salad | Slower gastric emptying | Add olive oil dressing |
| Improve satiety | Choose high-fiber carbs | Lentil soup + whole-grain toast | Fiber slows absorption | Aim 8–12 g fiber/meal |
| Weight management | Control portions of starches | 1/2 plate veggies + 1/4 starch | Lower glycemic impact | Use a smaller plate |
| Pre-workout energy | Use moderate/fast carbs | Banana + yogurt | Quick fuel | Eat 30–60 min before |
| Diabetes-friendly meals | Prefer minimally processed foods | Steel-cut oats + berries | Less rapid digestion | Avoid sugary toppings |
| Better breakfast | Avoid refined cereals | Eggs + fruit + nuts | Fewer spikes than cereal | Prepare overnight options |
| Snack choices | Choose low-sugar snacks | Nuts + cheese | Lower carb load | Watch calorie density |
| Dessert swaps | Reduce added sugar | Greek yogurt + cinnamon | Lower sugar | Use berries for sweetness |
