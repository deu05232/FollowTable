# Super Smash Bros. Melee (GameCube) — Unlockable Fighters and How to Get Them

| Unlockable Fighter | Primary Unlock Requirement (Melee) | Alternate Requirement (if applicable) | Where the Challenger Appears |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jigglypuff | Clear **Classic Mode** once (any character, any difficulty) | — | After Classic clear (challenger battle) |
| Luigi | Play **800 VS. matches** | Clear **Adventure Mode** with the timer ending in **2** (e.g., x:x2) | Mushroom Kingdom II (challenger battle) |
| Dr. Mario | Play **100 VS. matches** | Clear **Classic Mode** with Mario | Mushroom Kingdom (challenger battle) |
| Falco | Play **300 VS. matches** | Clear **100-Man Melee** | Corneria (challenger battle) |
| Young Link | Clear **Adventure Mode** | Clear **Classic Mode** with Link | Great Bay (challenger battle) |
| Ganondorf | Play **600 VS. matches** | Clear **Event 29** (“Triforce Gathering”) | Brinstar Depths (challenger battle) |
| Mewtwo | Play **700 VS. matches** (often lowered by having more VS hours/players) | Clear **Classic Mode** with every character (including unlocks) | Final Destination (challenger battle) |
