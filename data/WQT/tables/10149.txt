# Orbit Neuroanatomy  Extraocular Muscles with Nerves, Vessels, and Fissure Passage

| Structure | Type | Passage/route | Associated cranial nerve(s) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Superior orbital fissure | Foramen | Between greater and lesser wing of sphenoid | CN III; CN IV; CN V1; CN VI | Main entry to orbit |
| Optic canal | Foramen | Sphenoid | CN II | Also transmits ophthalmic artery |
| Lateral rectus | Muscle | Orbit (lateral wall) | CN VI | Abducts eye |
| Superior rectus | Muscle | Orbit (roof) | CN III (superior division) | Elevates eye |
| Inferior oblique | Muscle | Anterior floor | CN III (inferior division) | Extorsion |
| Ophthalmic vein | Vessel | Superior orbital fissure | — | Drains to cavernous sinus |
| Ciliary ganglion | Ganglion | Posterior orbit | CN III (parasympathetic) | Pupil constriction pathway |
