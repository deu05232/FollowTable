# Köppen Climate Classification and Elevation Bands for Quito, Ecuador

| Zone Type | Köppen Code | Elevation Band | Typical Temperature Range | Typical Rain Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Urban basin | Cfb | 2,700–2,900 m | 50–73°F | Two wet seasons |
| Lower slopes | Cwb | 2,300–2,700 m | 55–78°F | Wettest Mar–May |
| Mid slopes | Cwb | 1,800–2,300 m | 60–82°F | Frequent afternoon showers |
| Cloud forest | Cwb/Cfb | 1,200–1,800 m | 62–86°F | High humidity |
| Highland páramo | ET | 3,200–4,200 m | 35–55°F | Cold, windy, mist |
| Glaciated peaks | EF | >4,800 m | <32°F | Permanent ice/snow |
