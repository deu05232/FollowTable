# Common Names of Some Chemical Compounds

| Common Name | Chemical Name | Formula |
| --- | --- | --- |
| baking soda | sodium hydrogen carbonate sodium bicarbonate | NaHCO3 |
| bleach (liquid) | sodium hypochlorite or hydrogen peroxide | NaClO H2O2 |
| bleach (solid) | sodium perborate | NaBO3 |
| Borax | sodium tetraborate decahydrate | Na2B4O7.10 H2O |
