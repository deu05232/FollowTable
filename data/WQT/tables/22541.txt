|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sympathetic preganglionic | B fibers | Myelinated | T1–L2 intermediolateral cell column | Sympathetic chain ganglia | Acetylcholine | Short preganglionic axons |
| Sympathetic postganglionic | C fibers | Unmyelinated | Cervical/Thoracic/Lumbar chain ganglia | Superior cervical ganglion | Norepinephrine | Long postganglionic axons |
| Parasympathetic preganglionic | B fibers | Myelinated | Brainstem nuclei, S2–S4 | Ciliary ganglion | Acetylcholine | Long preganglionic axons |
| Parasympathetic postganglionic | C fibers | Unmyelinated | Terminal ganglia | Intramural ganglia | Acetylcholine | Short postganglionic axons |
| Enteric interneurons | Mixed | Mixed | GI wall | Myenteric plexus | Serotonin | Coordinates peristalsis |
| Visceral afferents | Aδ/C fibers | Variable | DRG | Nodose ganglion | Glutamate | Sensory feedback to CNS |
