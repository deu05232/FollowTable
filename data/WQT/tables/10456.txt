# Comparative Book Lists by Bible Version (Old Testament + Deuterocanon overview)

| Bible translation / version | Canon tradition | Old Testament (sample set of book names) | Deuterocanonical / Apocryphal books included (and naming) | Notes on naming differences / inclusions |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Jewish Publication Society Tanakh (JPS, 1985) | Jewish Tanakh | Torah: Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, Deuteronomy; Nevi'im: Joshua, Judges, Samuel, Kings, Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, The Twelve; Ketuvim: Psalms, Proverbs, Job, Song of Songs, Ruth, Lamentations, Ecclesiastes, Esther, Daniel, Ezra–Nehemiah, Chronicles | null | Uses **Tanakh** divisions; counts **The Twelve** as one; often prints **Ezra–Nehemiah** as one book; no deuterocanon.
| Protestant English Bible (e.g., NIV/ESV pattern) | Protestant (66-book) | Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, Deuteronomy, Joshua, Judges, Ruth, 1 Samuel, 2 Samuel, 1 Kings, 2 Kings, 1 Chronicles, 2 Chronicles, Ezra, Nehemiah, Esther, Job, Psalms, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, Song of Songs, Isaiah, Jeremiah, Lamentations, Ezekiel, Daniel, Hosea…Malachi | Typically none (may publish separate “Apocrypha” edition) | Usually splits **1–2 Samuel/Kings/Chronicles**; calls “Song of Songs” (sometimes “Song of Solomon”).
| Anglican / Episcopal (e.g., NRSV with Apocrypha, lectionary use) | Protestant + Apocrypha (read in church) | Same core OT list as Protestant | Tobit, Judith, Additions to Esther, Wisdom of Solomon, Sirach (Ecclesiasticus), Baruch (incl. Letter of Jeremiah), Song of the Three, Susanna, Bel and the Dragon, 1 Maccabees, 2 Maccabees, Prayer of Manasseh, 1 Esdras, 2 Esdras (sometimes titled **4 Ezra**) | Often labels these as **Apocrypha**; “Ecclesiasticus” appears as alternate title for **Sirach**; extra Ezra books may be titled differently.
| Roman Catholic (e.g., NABRE / RSV-CE pattern) | Catholic (73-book) | Core OT plus books recognized as deuterocanonical | Tobit, Judith, Wisdom, Sirach, Baruch, 1 Maccabees, 2 Maccabees, **Additions to Daniel** (Song of the Three, Susanna, Bel and the Dragon), **Additions to Esther** | Usually calls them **Deuterocanonical** (not “Apocrypha”); Daniel and Esther are printed with additions integrated.
| Eastern Orthodox (e.g., Orthodox Study Bible pattern) | Eastern Orthodox | Similar to Catholic OT base | Tobit, Judith, Wisdom, Sirach, Baruch, 1–2 Maccabees, **3 Maccabees**, Prayer of Manasseh, Psalm 151, Additions to Daniel/Esther; often also **1 Esdras** | Uses “**Odes**” in some traditions; includes **Psalm 151**; presence of 3 Maccabees varies by jurisdiction.
| Ethiopian Orthodox (broad canon, overview) | Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo | Includes many OT books plus additional works used in Ethiopian tradition | Often includes **Jubilees**, **1 Enoch**, **1–3 Meqabyan** (not the same as Greek Maccabees), and other writings depending on edition | Book names differ significantly (e.g., **Meqabyan**); canon listings vary between “narrow” and “broader” enumerations.
