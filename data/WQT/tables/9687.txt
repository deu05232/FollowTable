# LINDOR Truffles  One-Color Wrapper Guide (Including Limited Seasonal Colors)

| Wrapper color (primary) | Flavour name | Notes for recognition |
|---|---|---|
| Light Blue | Stracciatella | Usually a pale/light blue wrapper; creamy white filling with cocoa pieces |
| Light Pink | Irish Cream | Pink wrapper; creamy liqueur-inspired truffle filling |
| Brown | Peanut Butter | Brown wrapper; peanut-butter style truffle center |
| Black with Green accent | Dark Peppermint | Black base with green accent; minty dark-chocolate profile |
| Yellow | Mango | Bright yellow wrapper; fruity mango-style truffle filling |
| Teal | Matcha | Green-blue/teal wrapper; mild green-tea matcha flavour |
| Burgundy | Raspberry | Deep red/burgundy wrapper; tart-sweet raspberry profile |
| Champagne (pale gold) | Champagne | Pale gold wrapper; sparkling-wine inspired creamy filling |
| Copper | Sea Salt | Copper-toned wrapper; chocolate with noticeable salt note |
| Lilac | Lavender | Light purple/lilac wrapper; floral lavender-cream profile |
