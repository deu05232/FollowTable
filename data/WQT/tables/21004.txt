# Unopened vs Opened Shelf Life for Prepared Meals (Packaging-Based)

| Item (ready-to-eat) | Condition | Pantry | Refrigerator | Freezer |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Deli potato salad | Unopened (sell-by based) | Not recommended | 3–5 days after sell-by date | 1–2 months (quality) |
| Deli potato salad | Opened | Not recommended | 3–4 days | 1–2 months (quality) |
| Store-bought hummus | Unopened (use-by based) | Not recommended | 7–10 days after use-by date | 4 months |
| Store-bought hummus | Opened | Not recommended | 5–7 days | 4 months |
| Packaged cooked chicken strips | Unopened (best-by based) | Not recommended | 3–5 days after best-by date | 2–3 months |
| Packaged cooked chicken strips | Opened | Not recommended | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Ready-to-eat soup (tub) | Unopened (use-by based) | Not recommended | 5–7 days after use-by date | 2–3 months |
