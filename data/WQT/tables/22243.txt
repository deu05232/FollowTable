# Cnidaria Classes Compared  Diversity, Examples, and Diagnostic Traits

| Attribute | Hydrozoa | Scyphozoa | Cubozoa | Anthozoa |
|---|---|---|---|
| Approx. described species | 3,700 | 230 | 50 | 6,200 |
| Typical life cycle | Polyp-dominant (often) | Medusa-dominant | Medusa-dominant | Polyp-only |
| Common examples | Hydra, siphonophores | true jellyfish | Box jellies | Corals, sea anemones |
| Mesoglea cellularity | Mostly acellular | Often cellular | Often cellular | Variable |
| Predominant habitat | Marine & freshwater | Marine | Marine (tropical) | Marine |
| Key stinging cell distribution | Tentacles and body | Tentacles and oral arms | Tentacles | Tentacles and mesenteries |
| Typical size range | mm to meters (colonies) | cm to >1 m | cm to ~30 cm | mm to meters (coral colonies) |
