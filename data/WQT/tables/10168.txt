# Shelf life of pantry staples after opening (not focused on fridge freezer leftovers)

| Food item | Pantry (after opening) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Peanut butter | 2–3 months | Keep lid tightly closed |
| Honey | Indefinite | May crystallize over time |
| Soy sauce | 1–2 years | Quality slowly declines |
| Hot sauce | 6–12 months | Refrigeration optional |
| Vinegar | Indefinite | Flavor may mellow |
