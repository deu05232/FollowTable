# Pacific War chronology leading up to the Hiroshima bombing (exact-date timeline)

| Date | Event |
| --- | --- |
| Dec 7, 1941 | Japan attacks Pearl Harbor, drawing the United States into the war. |
| Jun 4, 1942 | Battle of Midway shifts the balance in the Pacific. |
| Feb 19, 1945 | Battle of Iwo Jima begins. |
| Apr 1, 1945 | Battle of Okinawa begins. |
| Jul 16, 1945 | Trinity test successfully detonates the first atomic device. |
| Jul 26, 1945 | Potsdam Declaration calls for Japan's surrender. |
| Aug 6, 1945 | Atomic bomb is dropped on Hiroshima. |
