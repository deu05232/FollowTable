# Wars involving the Republic of India (selected modern and contemporary conflicts)

| War/Conflict | Years | India and allies (dedicated column) | Opponents / Other belligerents | Theater/Region | Outcome (brief) |
|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| Indo-Pakistani War | 1947–1948 | India; Jammu & Kashmir forces | Pakistan; Azad Kashmir forces | Kashmir | UN-mediated ceasefire; divided control |

| Bangladesh Liberation War (Indo-Pakistani War) | 1971 | India; Mukti Bahini | Pakistan | East Pakistan (Bangladesh) | Bangladesh independence; Pakistani surrender |

| Sino-Indian War | 1962 | India | China | Himalayan border | Chinese victory; ceasefire |
| Sino-Indian border clashes (Nathu La & Cho La) | 1967 | India | China | Sikkim sector | Indian tactical success (disputed) |
