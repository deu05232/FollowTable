# Manuscript Traditions and Their Associated Version Families (No Book-by-Book Listing)

| Tradition/Source | Typical Language | Key Witnesses | Commonly Associated Versions | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Masoretic Text | Hebrew | Leningrad Codex | KJV, ESV, NIV (OT base) | Primary Hebrew OT tradition |
| Septuagint (LXX) | Greek | Vaticanus, Alexandrinus | Orthodox OT editions, Brenton | Influences deuterocanon usage |
| Dead Sea Scrolls | Hebrew/Aramaic | Qumran manuscripts | Modern critical editions | Provides variant readings |
| Vulgate | Latin | Clementine Vulgate | Douay-Rheims | Historical Catholic text base |
| Peshitta | Syriac | Syriac manuscripts | Syriac Bible traditions | Some NT book coverage varies |
| Textus Receptus | Greek | Erasmus editions | KJV, some NKJV notes | Early printed NT base |
| NA/UBS Critical Text | Greek | Eclectic | NIV, NRSV, ESV (NT base) | Modern critical NT base |
