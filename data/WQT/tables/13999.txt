# Military Retirement Pay Plans (U.S.) — Computation Basis and Key Inputs

| Retirement plan name | Who it generally applies to (entry date) | Retired pay base used for calculation | How the multiplier is earned | Notes on calculation focus |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Final Pay ("Final Basic Pay") | Entered service before 8 Sep 1980 | Final monthly basic pay at retirement | 2.5% × years of creditable service | Uses last basic pay rate (not allowances) |
| High-36 ("High-3") | Entered service on/after 8 Sep 1980 (and not in REDUX/BRS) | Average of highest 36 months of basic pay | 2.5% × years of creditable service | Average is based on basic pay only; typically last 3 years |
| CBS/REDUX | Elected at 15 years for members under legacy system (historically) | Average of highest 36 months of basic pay | Reduced multiplier until age 62 (varies by service rules) | Includes a Career Status Bonus election; formula differs from standard High-36 |
| Blended Retirement System (BRS) | Entered service on/after 1 Jan 2018 (or opted in) | Average of highest 36 months of basic pay | 2.0% × years of creditable service | Retirement annuity is smaller vs High-36 but paired with TSP contributions |
| Reserve/Guard Non-Regular ("20-Year") Retirement | Reserve/Guard with qualifying years; pay starts at eligible age | Basic pay table for member's grade/years at pay start; uses High-36 style averaging for base | Multiplier based on retirement points (points ÷ 360) × 2.5% (legacy) or 2.0% (BRS) | Based on equivalent active-duty service computed from points; pay begins later |
| Temporary Early Retirement Authority (TERA) | Authorized early-retirement programs (varies by period) | Same retired pay base as member’s underlying plan (Final Pay or High-36) | Uses a reduced service credit/multiplier adjustment | Early retirement option; calculation depends on the underlying pay base type |
