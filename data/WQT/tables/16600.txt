# Health Effects of Fiber Intake (Summary of Reported Outcomes)

| Outcome area | Reported effect | Proposed mechanism | Strength of evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Satiety/weight | Increased fullness; may reduce energy intake | Viscosity, delayed gastric emptying | Moderate |
| Glycemic control | Reduced post-meal glucose spikes | Slower carbohydrate absorption | Moderate |
| Lipids | Lower LDL cholesterol | Bile acid binding; fermentation products | Moderate |
| Bowel function | Increased stool bulk; improved regularity | Water-holding capacity; fermentation | Strong |
| Gut microbiome | Increased short-chain fatty acid production | Fermentable substrates for microbes | Emerging |
| Cardiovascular risk | Lower risk in observational studies | Multi-factor (lipids, glucose, BP) | Moderate |
| Colon health | Possible reduced diverticular symptoms | Softer stools; lower pressure | Limited |
| Mineral absorption | May reduce absorption at very high intakes | Chelation/phytate interactions | Limited |
