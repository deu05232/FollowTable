# Party code legend used in Senate election and membership tables

| Code | Party label | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| D | Democratic | Standard abbreviation |
| R | Republican | Standard abbreviation |
| I | Independent | May caucus with either party |
| C | Conservative | Often caucused with Republicans in some periods |
| FL | Farmer–Labor | Historical party in Minnesota |
| P | Progressive | Used in several early-20th-century contexts |
| U | Unionist | Civil War era label |
| V | Vacant | Seat not filled |
