# Cardiovascular Autonomic Regulation Summary (includes transmitter names)

| Trigger | Autonomic branch | Mediator named | Immediate cardiac effect | Broader outcome |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Low blood pressure | Sympathetic up | Norepinephrine | HR increases | Cardiac output rises |
| Low blood oxygen | Sympathetic up | Epinephrine/NE | HR increases | Perfusion improves |
| High blood pressure | Parasympathetic up | Acetylcholine | HR decreases | Cardiac output falls |
| High blood oxygen | Parasympathetic up | Acetylcholine | HR decreases | Cardiac output falls |
| Stress response | Sympathetic up | Epinephrine | HR increases | Blood pressure rises |
| Rest state | Parasympathetic up | Acetylcholine | HR decreases | Energy conserved |
