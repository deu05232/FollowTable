# Derivative combination rules (single-variable) — quick reference

| Rule | Pattern | Derivative |
|---|---|---|
| Constant multiple | \(c\,f(x)\) | \(c\,f'(x)\) |
| Sum | \(f(x)+g(x)\) | \(f'(x)+g'(x)\) |
| Difference | \(f(x)-g(x)\) | \(f'(x)-g'(x)\) |
| Product | \(f(x)\,g(x)\) | \(f(x)g'(x)+f'(x)g(x)\) |
| Quotient | \(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\) | \(\frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{g(x)^2}\) |
| Reciprocal (special case) | \(\frac{1}{g(x)}\) | \(-\frac{g'(x)}{g(x)^2}\) |
