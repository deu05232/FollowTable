# Autonomic Neurotransmitters Mapped to Receptor Subtypes and Organ-Level Actions

| Neurotransmitter | Main receptor families | Example organ | Example action | Antagonist example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Acetylcholine | Nicotinic (Nn), Muscarinic (M2/M3) | Heart | Decreased SA node firing | Atropine |
| Acetylcholine | Muscarinic (M3) | Bronchi | Bronchoconstriction | Ipratropium |
| Norepinephrine | α1, α2, β1 (weak β2) | Arterioles | Vasoconstriction | Prazosin |
| Epinephrine | β1/β2, α1 (dose dependent) | Bronchi | Bronchodilation | Propranolol |
| Dopamine | D1, D2; β1 at higher dose | Kidney | Increased perfusion | Metoclopramide (D2) |
| Nitric oxide | sGC/cGMP pathway | Corpus cavernosum | Smooth muscle relaxation | PDE5 inhibitors potentiate |
| ATP | P2X/P2Y | Vas deferens | Cotransmission contraction | Suramin (experimental) |
| Neuropeptide Y | Y receptors | Vessels | Potentiates vasoconstriction | (varies) |
