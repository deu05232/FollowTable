# California Homicide Sentencing Overview (Selected Offenses)

| Offense | Felony/Misdemeanor | Maximum Prison Term | Maximum Fine | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| First-degree murder | Felony | 25 years to life / LWOP | $10,000 | Special circumstances can trigger LWOP |
| Second-degree murder | Felony | 15 years to life | $10,000 | Typically indeterminate term |
| Voluntary manslaughter | Felony | 11 years | $10,000 | Heat of passion/Imperfect self-defense context |
| Involuntary manslaughter | Felony | 4 years | $10,000 | Can be charged as misdemeanor in some cases |
| Vehicular manslaughter (gross negligence) | Felony | 6 years | $10,000 | DUI-related cases may be enhanced |
| Attempted murder | Felony | Life / 9 years | $10,000 | Varies by degree and enhancements |
| Felony murder (special circumstances) | Felony | Death / LWOP | $10,000 | Rare; depends on eligibility |
| Murder of a peace officer | Felony | Death / LWOP | $10,000 | Enhanced punishment category |
