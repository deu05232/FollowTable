# Handheld Console Hardware Specs (For Emulation Performance Reference)

| Device | CPU | RAM | Screen Resolution | Release Year | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Nintendo DS Lite | ARM9/ARM7 | 4 MB | 256×192 (dual) | 2006 | Popular for homebrew |
| PlayStation Portable (PSP-2000) | MIPS @ 333 MHz | 64 MB | 480×272 | 2007 | Common target for emulators |
| Nintendo 3DS | Dual-core ARM11 | 128 MB | 400×240 (top) | 2011 | More demanding to emulate |
| PlayStation Vita | Quad-core ARM Cortex-A9 | 512 MB | 960×544 | 2011 | Strong portable hardware |
| Game Boy Advance | ARM7TDMI | 256 KB WRAM | 240×160 | 2001 | Baseline for retro emulation |
| Sega Dreamcast | Hitachi SH-4 | 16 MB | 640×480 | 1998 | Harder to emulate accurately |
