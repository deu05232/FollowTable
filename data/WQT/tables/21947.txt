| Deity | Main cult center | Region | Notes about worship site |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ptah | Memphis | Lower Egypt | Major temple complex tied to craftsmen |
| Amun | Karnak (Thebes) | Upper Egypt | Large state cult center |
| Horus | Edfu | Upper Egypt | Temple famous for later-period inscriptions |
| Isis | Philae | Upper Egypt | Island sanctuary dedicated to her cult |
