# Mega Millions (US) — Play Types and Wagers

| Play option | How it works | Odds of winning something | Base cost | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Quick Pick | Terminal selects numbers for you | 1 in 24 | $2 | Convenience only, not a different prize tier |
| Single Play | You choose 5 + Mega Ball | 1 in 24 | $2 | Standard entry |
| Megaplier add-on | Multiplies non-jackpot prizes | No change to win odds | +$1 | Changes payouts but rows are not prize tiers |
| Subscription (weekly) | Auto-enter for multiple draws | 1 in 24 | Varies | Same underlying odds |
| Multiple draws | Same numbers across several draws | 1 in 24 (per draw) | Varies | Not a prize breakdown |
| Group play / pool | Share tickets with a group | Depends on tickets | Varies | Changes expected share, not prize-tier odds |
| Advance play (up to N draws) | Pre-buy entries | 1 in 24 (per draw) | Varies | No tier info |
| Retail vs online purchase | Different purchase channel | 1 in 24 | $2 | Channel doesn’t define prize tiers |
