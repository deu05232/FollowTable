# Planar Tilings and Lattices  Rotation Orders in Unit Cells

| Tiling / lattice type | Fundamental region | Rotation centers in the unit cell | Rotation orders present | Common notation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Square tiling | right isosceles triangle | corners, edge midpoints, cell center | 4, 2 | {4,4} |
| Triangular tiling | 30-60-90 triangle | vertices and face-centers | 6, 3, 2 | {3,6} |
| Hexagonal tiling | equilateral triangle | vertices and edge intersections | 3, 2 | {6,3} |
| Kagome tiling | triangle + hexagon decomposition | multiple sites per cell | 6, 3, 2 | (3,6,3,6) |
| Snub square tiling | irregular fundamental region | mixed centers | 4, 2 | (3,3,3,4,4) |
| Cairo pentagonal tiling | pentagonal fundamental region | sparse rotation centers | 2 | (5,4,5,4) |
| Rhombille tiling | 60° rhombus | vertices and rhombus centers | 3, 2 | dual of {3,6} |
| Semi-regular trihexagonal tiling | triangle/hexagon | vertices | 6, 3, 2 | (3,6,3,6) |
