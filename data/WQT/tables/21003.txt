# Raw Ingredient Cold Storage Times (Not Cooked)

| Ingredient (raw) | Refrigerator | Freezer | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Raw chicken breast | 1–2 days | 9 months | Keep below 40°F |
| Raw ground beef | 1–2 days | 3–4 months | Use quickly |
| Raw salmon fillet | 1–2 days | 2–3 months | Freeze ASAP for best quality |
| Fresh basil (whole) | 5–7 days | Not recommended | Keep stems in water |
| Fresh spinach | 5–7 days | 10–12 months (blanched) | Wash before use |
| Raw pork chops | 3–5 days | 4–6 months | Store on bottom shelf |
| Raw shrimp | 1–2 days | 3–6 months | Keep sealed to avoid odors |
