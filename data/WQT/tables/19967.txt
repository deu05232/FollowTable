| Intel chipset series | Example chipset models | Typical platforms | Windows 10 compatibility statement | Driver availability angle | Practical check items |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Intel 100 Series | H110, B150, H170, Z170 | 6th Gen Core desktops | Windows 10 supported | Intel chipset INF for Windows 10 | Confirm OEM BIOS updates and LAN/audio Win10 drivers |
| Intel 200 Series | B250, H270, Z270 | 7th Gen Core desktops | Windows 10 supported | Windows 10 driver packages widely available | Verify USB 3.x controller drivers via OEM if needed |
| Intel 8 Series (mobile) | HM87, QM87 | 4th Gen Core laptops/workstations | Windows 10 supported | Chipset device software for Windows 10 | Ensure graphics (HD 4xxx) Win10 driver availability from Intel/OEM |

| Intel 300 Series | H310, B360, Z370 | 8th/9th Gen Core desktops | Windows 10 supported | Windows 10 drivers standard | OEM USB and MEI components may require vendor package |
