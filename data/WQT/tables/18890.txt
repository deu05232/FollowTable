# Paid Time Off in Iran (Annual Leave, Public Holidays, Sick Leave)

| Leave category | Typical entitlement | Paid? | Key conditions | Remarks |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Annual vacation | 26 working days/year | Yes (100%) | Accrues with service | Carry-over limits may apply |
| Public holidays | ~25 days/year | Yes (100%) | National calendar | Varies by year |
| Sick leave | Up to 4 months (example) | Partially paid | Medical certificate required | Rate may vary |
| Compassionate/bereavement leave | 3–7 days | Yes | Relationship dependent | Employer documentation |
| Marriage leave | 3 days | Yes | Upon marriage registration | One-time benefit |
| Hajj pilgrimage leave | Up to 1 month | Typically unpaid | Eligibility rules apply | Not annual |
