# Postoperative CABG Care Pathway with Abbreviations Embedded in Orders

| Post-op Day | Typical orders (shorthand) | Expanded intent | Monitoring focus | Common complications |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| POD 0 | ICU, A-line, CVP, I&O q1h | ICU admit; arterial line; central venous pressure; strict intake/output hourly | Hemodynamics, bleeding | Tamponade, hypotension |
| POD 1 | OOB to chair, IS q1h, D/C foley | Mobilize; incentive spirometry hourly; discontinue urinary catheter | Pulmonary hygiene | Atelectasis |
| POD 2 | D/C CT if <100 mL/8h, PT/OT | Remove chest tubes if output low; therapy eval | Drain output | Pleural effusion |
| POD 3 | PO meds, D/C PCA, DC planning | Switch to oral meds; stop patient-controlled analgesia; discharge planning | Pain control | Delirium |
| POD 4 | FU CXR, ambulate TID | Follow-up chest X-ray; walk three times daily | Respiratory status | Pneumonia |
| POD 5 | D/C home if stable, FU clinic | Discharge if stable; outpatient follow-up | Wound care | SSI |
| POD 6+ | Cardiac rehab, INR checks if on warfarin | Rehabilitation; anticoagulation monitoring | Functional recovery | Arrhythmia |
