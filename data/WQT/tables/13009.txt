# # Egyptian gods and goddesses and their domains (selected list)

| Deity | Role / what they are associated with |
| --- | --- |
| Isis | Goddess of magic, healing, and motherhood |
| Horus | God of kingship and the sky |
| Anubis | God of embalming and protector of tombs |
| Ra | Sun god and a major creator deity |
| Hathor | Goddess of love, music, and joy |
| Thoth | God of writing, knowledge, and the moon |
