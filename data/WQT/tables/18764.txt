# Periodic Table Quick Guide  Typical Ion Charges by Group (Not Explicit Charged Formulas)

| Periodic group | Representative elements | Typical ionic charge | Rationale |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Group 1 (alkali metals) | Li, Na, K | +1 | Lose one electron |
| Group 2 (alkaline earths) | Mg, Ca, Sr | +2 | Lose two electrons |
| Group 13 | Al, Ga | +3 | Often form 3+ cations |
| Group 15 (pnictogens) | N, P, As | −3 | Gain three electrons in binary salts |
| Group 16 (chalcogens) | O, S, Se | −2 | Gain two electrons |
| Group 17 (halogens) | F, Cl, Br | −1 | Gain one electron |
| Transition metals | Fe, Cu, Zn | variable | Multiple oxidation states common |
