# Economics-Centric Career Paths for Economics Majors (Roles, Core Work, and Typical Employers)

| Track | Example Job Titles | How Economics Is Central to the Role | Common Employers/Settings |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Monetary & Financial Economics | Monetary Policy Analyst; Central Bank Economist; Financial Stability Analyst | Builds macro/financial models, interprets inflation/employment data, evaluates policy tools and bank risks | Central banks, bank regulators, international financial institutions |
| Labor & Education Economics | Labor Economist; Workforce Policy Analyst; Compensation & Benefits Analyst | Uses wage/employment microdata, estimates program impacts, studies labor markets and human capital outcomes | Ministries/departments of labor, think tanks, HR analytics teams |
| Industrial Organization & Competition | Antitrust Economist; Competition Analyst; Pricing Economist | Analyzes market structure, conducts merger analysis, estimates demand/price elasticities | Competition authorities, economic consulting firms, large firms with pricing teams |
| Public Finance & Tax | Public Finance Economist; Tax Policy Analyst; Revenue Forecast Analyst | Evaluates tax incidence, models budget impacts, forecasts revenues, designs fiscal policy | Treasuries, finance ministries, tax agencies, legislative budget offices |
| Development & Economic Growth | Development Economist; Economic Development Officer; Program Evaluation Analyst | Measures poverty/growth drivers, designs evaluations, conducts cost–benefit analysis | NGOs, development banks, city/regional development agencies |
| International & Trade | Trade Policy Analyst; International Economist; Customs/Trade Compliance Economist | Quantifies tariff/non-tariff impacts, analyzes trade flows, models exchange-rate and trade shocks | Trade ministries, international organizations, multinational firms |
| Health Economics | Health Economist; Outcomes Research Analyst; Cost-Effectiveness Analyst | Builds economic evaluations of interventions, models healthcare demand/supply and pricing | Public health agencies, insurers, pharma/biotech, hospitals |
| Environmental & Resource Economics | Environmental Economist; Climate Policy Analyst; Energy Market Analyst | Values externalities, designs market-based regulation, models energy/carbon markets | Environmental regulators, utilities, energy agencies, consultancies |
| Urban/Regional Economics | Urban Economist; Housing Market Analyst; Transit/Infrastructure Economist | Studies housing/land markets, assesses infrastructure returns, forecasts regional impacts | City planning offices, transport agencies, real estate analytics teams |
| Applied Econometrics & Data | Econometrician; Causal Inference Analyst; Economic Data Scientist | Designs identification strategies, estimates causal effects, builds forecasting/nowcasting models | Research units, analytics teams, consulting, government statistical agencies |
