# Chronic Bacterial Prostatitis  Common Antibiotic Regimens — Dose and Frequency (Adult)

| Antibiotic | Dose amount | Frequency | Typical duration |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Ciprofloxacin | 500 mg | every 12 hours | 4–6 weeks |
| Levofloxacin | 500 mg | once daily | 4–6 weeks |
| Trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole (DS) | 160 mg/800 mg | every 12 hours | 4–6 weeks |
| Doxycycline | 100 mg | every 12 hours | 4–6 weeks |
| Azithromycin | 500 mg | once daily | 7–14 days |
| Fosfomycin tromethamine | 3 g | every 48–72 hours | 6–12 weeks |
