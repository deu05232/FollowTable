# New York statute of limitations by case type (selected examples)

| Case type | Time limit | Statute citation |
|---|---|---|
| Personal injury | 3 years | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214(5) |
| Libel / slander | 1 year | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 215(3) |
| Medical malpractice | 2 years and 6 months | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214-a |
| Legal malpractice | 3 years | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214(6) |
