# GCSE Options  Subject Menu by Curriculum Area (Key Stage 4)

| Curriculum Area | GCSE Subjects Offered | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| English | English Language; English Literature | Core for most students |
| Mathematics | Mathematics; Further Mathematics | Further Maths typically by selection |
| Sciences | Biology; Chemistry; Physics; Combined Science (Trilogy) | Separate sciences or combined route |
| Humanities | History; Geography; Religious Studies | Students usually pick at least one |
| Languages | French; Spanish; German | Availability may depend on uptake |
| Arts | Art & Design (Fine Art); Photography; Music; Drama | Portfolio/performance assessment in many |
| Design & Technology | Product Design; Food Preparation & Nutrition; Textiles | Workshop/NEA components common |
| Computing & Business | Computer Science; Business Studies; Enterprise | Computing often requires strong maths |
| Sport & Health | Physical Education; Sports Studies | Some routes are vocational-equivalent |
| Vocational / Applied | BTEC Health & Social Care; BTEC Engineering; BTEC Creative Media | Equivalent qualifications may be offered alongside GCSEs |
