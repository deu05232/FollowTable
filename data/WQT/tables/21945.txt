# # Egyptian deities and their domains

| Deity | Main domain / what they are associated with | Common depiction or symbol |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Amun | Hiddenness, air, kingship | Man with tall plumes; ram |
| Anubis | Mummification, funerary rites, protection of the dead | Jackal-headed figure |
| Hathor | Love, music, motherhood | Cow or woman with cow horns |
| Isis | Magic, healing, motherhood | Woman with throne glyph |
| Osiris | Underworld, resurrection, afterlife | Mummified king with crook and flail |
| Ra | Sun, creation, order | Falcon-headed figure with sun disk |
