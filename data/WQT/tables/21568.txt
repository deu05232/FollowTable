# Typical Baby Growth Reference (Post-birth Age)  Length Height and Weight Ranges

| Age after birth | Length/Height (in) | Weight (lb) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Newborn (0–1 month) | 19.0–21.5 | 6.0–9.5 | Most full-term newborns fall in this range |
| 2 months | 21.5–24.0 | 9.0–13.0 | Rapid early growth |
| 3 months | 23.0–25.5 | 11.0–15.0 | Often steadier gain than first weeks |
| 6 months | 25.0–27.5 | 14.0–20.0 | Many babies roughly double birth weight by ~5–6 months |
| 9 months | 27.0–29.5 | 16.0–23.0 | Growth rate typically slows versus early infancy |
| 12 months | 28.5–31.0 | 18.0–26.0 | Commonly near triple birth weight by ~12 months |
| 18 months | 31.0–34.0 | 21.0–30.0 | Toddlers vary widely |
| 24 months (2 years) | 33.0–36.5 | 24.0–34.0 | Height becomes more predictive than weight |
