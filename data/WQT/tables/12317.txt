# Nazi Extermination Camps — Locations and Operational Notes (Death-Camp-Only List)

| Extermination camp | Nearest major town/region | Present-day country | Main victim groups targeted (summary) | Distinguishing operational note | Status after 1945 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Auschwitz-Birkenau (Auschwitz II) | Oświęcim region (Upper Silesia) | Poland | Jews; also Roma, Soviet POWs, others | Largest killing center; combined camp complex with industrialized gassing | Preserved as memorial/museum |
| Treblinka II | Near Treblinka (Masovia) | Poland | Primarily Jews; also Roma | Operation Reinhard killing center; rapid-processing extermination site | Site memorialized; camp dismantled by perpetrators |
| Bełżec | Near Bełżec (Lublin region) | Poland | Primarily Jews | Operation Reinhard; camp demolished and area disguised | Memorial and museum site |
| Sobibór | Near Sobibór (Lublin region) | Poland | Primarily Jews | Operation Reinhard; prisoner uprising in 1943 | Memorial/museum site |
| Chełmno (Kulmhof) | Chełmno on the Ner (Łódź region) | Poland | Jews; Roma; others | Early killing center using gas vans | Memorial site |
| Majdanek (KL Lublin) | Lublin | Poland | Jews; Polish prisoners; Soviet POWs; others | Included mass shootings and gassing; large-scale atrocities | Preserved largely intact as museum |
