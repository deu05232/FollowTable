# Disorder-to-Gene and Locus Mapping for Selected Microdeletions and Imprinting Syndromes

| Condition | Key gene(s) / critical region | Chromosomal locus / cytoband | Notes on mechanism |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Prader–Willi syndrome | 15q11-q13 (paternal region; e.g., SNORD116 cluster) | 15q11.2-q13.1 | Paternal deletion or maternal UPD/imprinting |
| Angelman syndrome | UBE3A (maternal expression) | 15q11.2-q13.1 | Maternal deletion/UBE3A pathogenic variant/imprinting |
| DiGeorge syndrome (22q11.2 deletion) | TBX1 (critical gene in region) | 22q11.2 | Recurrent microdeletion |
| Williams–Beuren syndrome | ELN (critical gene in region) | 7q11.23 | Recurrent microdeletion |
| Smith–Magenis syndrome | RAI1 | 17p11.2 | Typically deletion; sometimes RAI1 variants |
| Potocki–Lupski syndrome | RAI1 (duplication region) | 17p11.2 | Reciprocal microduplication |
| Wolf–Hirschhorn syndrome | 4p16.3 critical region (e.g., WHSC1/NSD2) | 4p16.3 | Terminal 4p deletion |
| Miller–Dieker syndrome | PAFAH1B1 (LIS1) | 17p13.3 | Contiguous gene deletion causing lissencephaly |
| 1p36 deletion syndrome | 1p36 critical region (multiple genes) | 1p36 | Terminal/interstitial deletion |
| 16p11.2 deletion syndrome | 16p11.2 recurrent CNV region (multiple genes) | 16p11.2 | Recurrent microdeletion associated with neurodevelopmental traits |
