# Types of Springs and Typical Applications (No Material Properties)

| Spring Type | Common Geometry | Typical Applications | Key Design Notes | Common Failure Mode |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Compression spring | Helical, closed ends | Pens, valves, suspensions | Buckling check, solid height | Fatigue cracking |
| Extension spring | Helical with hooks | Trampolines, door hardware | Hook stress concentration | Hook break |
| Torsion spring | Helical with legs | Clothespins, hinges | Leg angle, wind-up direction | Set/relaxation |
| Leaf spring | Stacked plates | Trucks, railcars | Interleaf friction | Corrosion + fatigue |
| Belleville washer spring | Conical disc | Bolted joints | Stack in series/parallel | Permanent set |
| Constant force spring | Pre-stressed strip coil | Retractors, cable management | Spool diameter constraints | Edge wear |
| Wave spring | Wavy ring | Bearings, seals | Axial space saving | Relaxation |
| Gas spring | Piston + pressurized gas | Liftgates, chairs | Seal life, temperature effects | Seal leakage |
