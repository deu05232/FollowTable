# Dog Food Recommendation Matrix by Specific Breed (Diet Style + Key Nutritional Target)

| Breed | Breed Size/Class | Recommended Diet Style (Examples) | Key Nutritional Target | Common Reason for This Fit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Siberian Husky | Medium (Northern) | Fish-based / limited-ingredient kibble | Omega-3 emphasis; moderate-to-high protein | Coat/skin support; some are sensitive to certain ingredients |
| Pit Bull–type (Adult) | Medium | High-protein, muscle-support formulas | Higher protein with quality animal sources | Lean muscle maintenance; active temperament |
| Pit Bull–type (Puppy) | Medium | Puppy formula (not adult high-protein only) | DHA for brain/vision; balanced Ca:P | Growth support without mineral excess |
| Great Dane | Giant | Giant/large-breed formula | Controlled calcium; joint support | Reduce risk from overly rapid growth; support joints |
| German Shepherd | Large | Sensitive digestion / large-breed recipes | Prebiotics; moderate fat; joint support | Breed often needs digestive support and hip/joint care |
| Labrador Retriever | Large | Weight-control or portion-friendly adult formula | Controlled calories; higher fiber | High tendency toward weight gain |
| Chihuahua | Toy | Small-breed formula | Energy-dense; tiny kibble; omega-3/6 | Small stomach; higher metabolic needs |
| French Bulldog | Small (Brachycephalic) | Brachy-friendly kibble shape; sensitive skin formulas | Easily chewable kibble; omega fatty acids | Flat-faced chewing needs; common skin sensitivity |
| Border Collie | Medium (Working) | Performance/active formulas | Higher fat + protein; antioxidant support | High energy output; endurance needs |
| Shih Tzu | Toy | Small-breed / skin & coat formulas | Omega-3/6; moderate protein | Long coat grooming/skin support; small kibble preference |
