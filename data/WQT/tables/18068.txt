# Insurance prior-authorization checklist for MRI of the spine

| Spine MRI requested | CPT code | Documentation/coverage requirement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lumbar spine MRI without contrast | 72148 | Prior authorization required; provide 6 weeks conservative therapy notes |
| Thoracic spine MRI without contrast | 72146 | Coverage only if neurologic deficit documented |
| Cervical spine MRI with and without contrast | 72156 | Must include prior imaging report and specialist referral |
