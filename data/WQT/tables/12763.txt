# Nazi Camp Network by Region (Rows Are Regions, Not Camps)

| Region | Example Camps Included | Dominant Camp Functions | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| General Government (occupied Poland) | Auschwitz, Bełżec, Sobibór | Extermination, forced labor | Includes Operation Reinhard sites |
| Wartheland (annexed Poland) | Chełmno, Łódź Ghetto | Extermination (mobile gassing), ghetto | Mixed site types |
| Protectorate of Bohemia and Moravia | Theresienstadt, Litoměřice | Transit, forced labor | Often labeled as “ghetto/camp” |
| Germany (Reich) | Dachau, Buchenwald, Sachsenhausen | Concentration, forced labor | Central camp administrations |
| Austria (annexed) | Mauthausen, Gusen | Forced labor, exterminatory labor | Quarry and armaments labor |
| Netherlands (occupied) | Westerbork, Vught | Transit, concentration | Deportation-focused |
| Baltic States (occupied) | Kaiserwald, Salaspils | Forced labor, transit | Many smaller sites not listed |
