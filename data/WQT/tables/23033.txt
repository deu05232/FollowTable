# Essential Oils and Botanical Extracts Used as Natural Pest Repellents (Non-Planting Options)

| Substance | Source (Plant) | Reported Repelled Pests | Common Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Citronella oil | Cymbopogon nardus | Mosquitoes | Candles, topical formulations |
| Neem oil (azadirachtin-containing extract) | Azadirachta indica | Aphids, whiteflies, mites | Foliar spray |
| Peppermint oil | Mentha × piperita | Ants, spiders, mice | Spray, diffuser |
| Eucalyptus oil | Eucalyptus globulus | Mosquitoes, flies | Spray, rub-on |
| Clove oil | Syzygium aromaticum | Mosquitoes, ants | Spray |
| Tea tree oil | Melaleuca alternifolia | Fleas, lice (reported) | Shampoo, topical |
| Cedarwood oil | Juniperus virginiana (cedarwood) | Moths, ticks | Sachets, sprays |
| Garlic extract | Allium sativum | Beetles, aphids (reported) | Spray concentrate |
