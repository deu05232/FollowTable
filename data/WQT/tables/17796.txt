# Refrigerator Storage Guide for Fresh Produce and Herbs (Mostly Uncooked)

| Produce/Herb | Typical State | Refrigerator Life | Freezer Life | Storage Tips |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Lettuce | Fresh (raw) | 5–7 days | Not recommended | Keep dry in crisper |
| Spinach | Fresh (raw) | 3–5 days | 10–12 months (blanched) | Wash only before use |
| Cilantro | Fresh herb (raw) | 7–14 days | 2–3 months | Store stems in water |
| Parsley | Fresh herb (raw) | 2–3 weeks | 2–3 months | Cover loosely |
| Basil | Fresh herb (raw) | 3–5 days | 2–3 months | Prefer cool room temp |
| Broccoli | Fresh (raw) | 3–5 days | 10–12 months (blanched) | Ventilated bag |
| Carrots | Fresh (raw) | 3–4 weeks | 10–12 months (blanched) | Remove greens |
| Strawberries | Fresh (raw) | 3–7 days | 8–12 months | Don’t wash until needed |
