# Extraocular muscle actions and their cranial nerve innervation (direct motor supply)

| Extraocular muscle | Primary action (brief) | Cranial nerve innervation |
|---|---|---|
| Superior rectus | Elevation | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Inferior rectus | Depression | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Medial rectus | Adduction | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Lateral rectus | Abduction | Abducens nerve (CN VI) |
| Superior oblique | Intorsion/depression | Trochlear nerve (CN IV) |
| Inferior oblique | Extorsion/elevation | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
