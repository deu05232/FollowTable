# Public Holidays in Australia — Long Weekend Rules (Not Date-Specific)

| Holiday | Observed When | States/Territories | Rule Detail |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Australia Day | Nearest Monday to 26 January | Nationwide | If 26 Jan falls Tue–Fri, some regions create long weekend |
| Good Friday | Friday before Easter | Nationwide | Based on Easter calculation |
| Easter Monday | Monday after Easter Sunday | Nationwide | Movable |
| ANZAC Day | April 25 (or Mondayized in some states) | Varies | Observed on Monday if it falls on weekend in some jurisdictions |
| Queen's/King's Birthday | Second Monday in June (most) | Most states | WA observes in late September/early October |
| Labour Day | First Monday in Oct/Mar/May (varies) | Varies | Different months by state |
| Christmas Day | 25 December (and weekday substitution) | Nationwide | Substitution day if weekend |
| Boxing Day | 26 December (and weekday substitution) | Nationwide/varies | Substitution day rules differ |
| Local Show Day | Varies by locality | Local | Date set by local council/region |
