# Freezer Life of Cooked Leftovers (Freezer Storage Only)

| Cooked item | Packaging recommendation | Freezer (0°F / -18°C) | Quality note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cooked chicken (pieces) | Double-wrap + freezer bag | 2–6 months | Drier after 4 months |
| Cooked turkey (sliced) | Airtight container | 2–3 months | Best for sandwiches |
| Cooked beef stew | Rigid container, headspace | 2–3 months | Thaw overnight |
| Cooked meatballs | Tray-freeze then bag | 2–3 months | Prevent sticking |
| Cooked rice | Portion + freezer bag | 1 month | Texture softens |
| Cooked pasta with sauce | Airtight container | 2–3 months | Cream sauces may separate |
| Cooked chili | Airtight container | 4–6 months | Flavor holds well |
| Cooked fish (baked) | Wrap tightly | 2–3 months | Strong odors possible |
| Cooked vegetables | Vacuum-seal if possible | 8–12 months | Mushier after thaw |
| Cooked pancakes/waffles | Stack with parchment | 1–2 months | Reheat from frozen |
