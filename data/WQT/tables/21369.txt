# Royal Navy Rank Pay Bands (Annual Salary Ranges)

| Rank | Typical Role | Annual Salary Range (GBP) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Able Seaman | Junior sailor | £18,000–£23,000 | Varies by branch and time served |
| Leading Hand | Team lead | £23,500–£28,000 | Often includes trade pay |
| Petty Officer | Section supervisor | £28,500–£34,500 | Includes responsibility increments |
| Chief Petty Officer | Senior supervisor | £35,000–£42,000 | Higher bands for technical trades |
| Warrant Officer 2 | Senior leader | £41,000–£48,000 | Appointment dependent |
| Warrant Officer 1 | Top enlisted | £46,000–£55,000 | Limited positions |
| Sub-Lieutenant | Junior officer | £27,000–£33,000 | Officer pay spine |
| Lieutenant | Officer | £35,000–£49,000 | Experience-dependent |
