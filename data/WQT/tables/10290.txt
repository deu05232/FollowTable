# Newborn to toddler clothing size guide (by label)  typical height and weight ranges

| Clothing label | Approx. age | Height range (in) | Weight range (lb) | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Preemie | 0–1 mo | 16–19 | 4–6 | Special care sizes |
| Newborn (NB) | 0–1 mo | 19–21 | 6–9 | Brand varies |
| 0–3M | 0–3 mo | 21–24 | 9–12.5 | Often overlaps NB |
| 3–6M | 3–6 mo | 24–26.5 | 12.5–16.5 | Fit depends on build |
| 6–9M | 6–9 mo | 26.5–28.5 | 16.5–20.5 | Consider diaper bulk |
| 9–12M | 9–12 mo | 28.5–30.5 | 20.5–24.5 | Size up for length |
| 12–18M | 12–18 mo | 30.5–32.5 | 24.5–27.5 | Not a growth percentile |
| 18–24M | 18–24 mo | 32.5–34.5 | 27.5–30.5 | Different by brand |
