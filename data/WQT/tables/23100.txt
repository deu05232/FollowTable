# Fortified Wine Styles – ABV and Sugar

| Fortified wine style | Typical ABV (%) | Sugar level | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fino Sherry | 15–17 | Very dry | Yeasty, saline |
| Ruby Port | 19–20 | Sweet | Dark fruit |
| Madeira (Bual) | 18–20 | Medium-sweet | Caramel, nuts |
| Marsala | 17–20 | Varies | Oxidative character |
