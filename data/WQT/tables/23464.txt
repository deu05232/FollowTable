# Typical hourly limousine rental rates by vehicle style (weekday vs weekend)

| Vehicle type | Typical hourly rate (weekday) | Typical hourly rate (weekend) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sedan (luxury car service) | $65–$95/hr | $80–$120/hr | Often 2–3 hr minimum |
| Standard stretch limo | $95–$150/hr | $120–$200/hr | Gratuity may be extra |
| Stretch SUV | $125–$200/hr | $150–$250/hr | Popular for groups |
| Stretch Hummer | $175–$300/hr | $200–$350/hr | Higher fuel surcharge common |
| Sprinter-style limo van | $120–$190/hr | $150–$240/hr | Good for airport groups |
| Party bus | $160–$275/hr | $200–$325/hr | Prices vary by size |
