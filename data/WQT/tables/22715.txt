| Rank | Abbreviation | Typical command level |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Second Lieutenant | 2d Lt | Flight/element leader |
| First Lieutenant | 1st Lt | Flight commander |
| Captain | Capt | Squadron staff/flight commander |
| Major | Maj | Squadron executive officer |
| Lieutenant Colonel | Lt Col | Squadron commander |
| Colonel | Col | Group commander |
| Brigadier General | Brig Gen | Wing vice commander |
| Major General | Maj Gen | Numbered Air Force commander |
| Lieutenant General | Lt Gen | Major command deputy |
| General | Gen | Major command commander/CSA-level |
