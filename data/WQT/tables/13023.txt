# Inhaler and Device Abbreviations Used in Medication Packaging

| Abbreviation | Expansion (Device/Packaging Term) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| MDI | Metered-dose inhaler | Pressurized canister device |
| DPI | Dry powder inhaler | Breath-actuated device |
| SMI | Soft mist inhaler | Mechanical mist device |
| neb | Nebulizer / nebulized | Often appears on product directions |
| HFA | Hydrofluoroalkane propellant | Propellant type on inhaler labels |
| CFC | Chlorofluorocarbon propellant | Older propellant labeling |
| ACT | Actuator | Device component in packaging inserts |
| VHC | Valved holding chamber | Accessory device term |
| spacer | Spacer device | Accessory packaging term |
| can | Canister | Component referenced in instructions |
| priming | Priming sprays | Device setup wording |
| dose ctr | Dose counter | Label/instruction feature |
