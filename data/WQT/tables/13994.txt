# # Military Retirement Pay Plans — What Each Plan Is Based On

| Retirement Plan | Basis | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Final Pay | Final basic pay | Typically applies to members with a Date of Initial Entry before Sep 8, 1980 |
| High-36 | Average of highest 36 months of basic pay | Uses the highest-paid 36 months (not necessarily last 36 months) |
| CBS/REDUX | Average of highest 36 months of basic pay | Usually paired with a career status bonus decision |
| BRS (Blended Retirement System) | High-36 style retired pay base | Combines a pension with government TSP contributions |
| Temporary Early Retirement Authority (TERA) | Final Pay or High-36 retired pay base (as applicable) | Early retirement program; still based on the underlying retired pay base |
| Disability Retirement | Final Pay or High-36 (as applicable) | Based on the member’s retired pay base, with disability rules applied |
