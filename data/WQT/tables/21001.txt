# Leftover Safety Guide  Cooked Foods vs. Room Temperature Holding

| Food (cooked/ready-to-eat) | Counter (max time) | Refrigerator (notes) | Freezer (notes) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked chicken pieces | 2 hours | “Refrigerate promptly” (no duration) | “Freezes well” (no duration) |
| Cooked ground beef | 2 hours | “Use soon” (no duration) | “Can be frozen” (no duration) |
| Cooked rice | 1 hour | “Keep cold” (no duration) | “Okay to freeze” (no duration) |
| Cooked pasta | 2 hours | “Store covered” (no duration) | “Quality changes” (no duration) |
| Cooked fish | 1 hour | “Smells spoil first” (no duration) | “Freeze if not eating” (no duration) |
| Soup (cooked) | 2 hours | “Chill fast in shallow containers” (no duration) | “Freeze in portions” (no duration) |
| Pizza slices | 2 hours | “Reheat until hot” (no duration) | “Wrap tightly” (no duration) |
