# Adult Blood Pressure Categories (mm Hg) — Classification Cutoffs by SBP DBP

| Category | Systolic (SBP, mm Hg) | Diastolic (DBP, mm Hg) |
|---|---:|---:|
| Optimal | <120 | and <80 |
| Normal | 120–129 | and/or 80–84 |
| High Normal | 130–139 | and/or 85–89 |
| Hypertension — Grade 1 | 140–159 | and/or 90–99 |
| Hypertension — Grade 2 | 160–179 | and/or 100–109 |
| Hypertension — Grade 3 | ≥180 | and/or ≥110 |
| Isolated Systolic Hypertension | ≥140 | and <90 |
