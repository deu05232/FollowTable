# U.S. Vehicle Safety Equipment Standards (Federal Compliance Snapshot)

| Regulation/Standard | Governing Authority | Applies To | Requirement Summary | Non-Compliance Consequence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| FMVSS 208 | NHTSA (Federal) | Passenger vehicles | Occupant crash protection incl. seat belt systems | Civil penalties/recalls |
| FMVSS 209 | NHTSA (Federal) | Seat belt assemblies | Performance requirements for belts | Civil penalties/recalls |
| FMVSS 210 | NHTSA (Federal) | Seat belt anchorages | Anchorage strength/location requirements | Civil penalties/recalls |
| FMVSS 213 | NHTSA (Federal) | Child restraint systems | Child seat performance requirements | Civil penalties/recalls |
| FMVSS 214 | NHTSA (Federal) | Side impact protection | Structural/airbag performance | Civil penalties/recalls |
| FMVSS 302 | NHTSA (Federal) | Interior materials | Flammability resistance | Civil penalties/recalls |
| DOT Compliance Labeling | USDOT | Manufacturers | Certification/labeling for compliance | Stop-sale/penalties |
| NHTSA Recall Process | NHTSA | Manufacturers | Remedy safety-related defects | Mandatory recall actions |
