# Verse wording differences between two Bible versions (mentions many books but not a book list)

| Reference | KJV wording (excerpt) | NIV wording (excerpt) | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Genesis 1:1 | In the beginning God created... | In the beginning God created... | Similar phrasing |
| Psalm 23:1 | The LORD is my shepherd... | The LORD is my shepherd... | Minor punctuation |
| Isaiah 7:14 | ...a virgin shall conceive... | ...the virgin will conceive... | Tense difference |
| Matthew 5:3 | Blessed are the poor in spirit... | Blessed are the poor in spirit... | Similar |
| Luke 2:14 | ...good will toward men. | ...on whom his favor rests. | Translation choice |
| Romans 8:1 | ...who walk not after the flesh... | ...who are in Christ Jesus. | Clause differs |
| Revelation 22:14 | Blessed are they that do his commandments... | Blessed are those who wash their robes... | Variant reading |
