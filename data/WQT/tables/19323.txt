# Inorganic Ligand Field Indicators and Related Properties (Mixed Reference Table)

| Ligand | Formula | Charge | Field strength (qualitative) | Typical complex color note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| iodide | I− | −1 | weak | often yellow/brown with many metals |
| bromide | Br− | −1 | weak | variable; many complexes green/brown |
| chloride | Cl− | −1 | weak–medium | many Cu(II) chloro species appear green |
| fluoride | F− | −1 | medium | Co(III) fluoro complexes often pale |
| cyanide | CN− | −1 | strong | Fe(II/III) cyanides often deep colored |
| carbon monoxide | CO | 0 | very strong | metal carbonyls frequently colorless |
| nitrosyl | NO+ | +1 | strong | nitrosyl complexes can be intensely colored |
| oxalate | C2O4^2− | −2 | medium | many oxalato complexes appear green |
