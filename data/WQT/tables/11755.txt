# Egyptian Gods and Goddesses  Domains with Iconography Notes

| Name | God/Goddess of | Typical depiction | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Anubis | Mummification, funerary rites | Jackal-headed man | Associated with embalming |
| Ma'at | Truth, cosmic order | Woman with feather | Feather of Ma'at used in judgment |
| Set | Chaos, storms | Set-animal head | Rival of Osiris |
| Nephthys | Mourning, protection | Woman with hieroglyphs | Sister of Isis |
| Thoth | Writing, knowledge | Ibis or baboon | Patron of scribes |
| Nut | Sky | Woman arching over earth | Mother of Osiris |
| Geb | Earth | Man reclining | Father of snakes |
| Sekhmet | War, plague, healing | Lioness-headed woman | “Powerful One” |
| Aten | Sun disk | Radiating disk | Amarna period cult |
| Bes | Household protection | Dwarf figure | Protector of childbirth |
