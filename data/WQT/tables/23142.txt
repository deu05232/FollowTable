# Smoke Point and High-Heat Performance of Cooking Oils (No Fat Profile)

| Oil | Smoke point (°C) | Practical use | Notes on heating behavior |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Avocado oil (refined) | 270 | High-heat searing | Stable for short high-temp cooking |
| Canola oil (refined) | 205 | Medium-high heat | Neutral flavor, common for frying |
| Olive oil (extra virgin) | 190 | Medium heat | Flavorful; may smoke sooner if impurities present |
| Sunflower oil (refined) | 225 | High-heat frying | Often used for deep frying |
| Peanut oil (refined) | 230 | High-heat frying | Popular for wok cooking |
| Sesame oil (unrefined) | 175 | Low-medium heat | Strong flavor; not ideal for long high heat |
| Coconut oil (virgin) | 175 | Medium heat | Solid at room temperature |
| Butter | 150 | Low heat | Milk solids brown quickly |
