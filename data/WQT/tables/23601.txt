# Operating Income vs. EBIT Definitions and Common Adjustments (Reference Table)

| Topic | Operating income (EBIT from operations) | EBIT (reported) |
|---|---|---|
| Core definition | Profit from primary operations | Earnings before interest and taxes |
| Includes non-operating income? | Typically no | May include certain non-operating items |
| Includes restructuring charges | Often yes (if operating) | Yes (depends on classification) |
| Includes impairment | Often yes | Yes |
| Includes interest income/expense | No | No |
| Includes FX gains/losses | Usually operating only if operational | Sometimes included as other income/expense |
| Includes equity-method earnings | Usually excluded | Sometimes included in EBIT by some issuers |
| Typical bridge items | Other income, non-operating gains/losses | N/A |
| Common pitfalls | Company-specific classification | Lack of standardization |
| Where found | Income statement operating section | Income statement or notes |
| Best practice | Provide explicit reconciliation | Provide explicit reconciliation |
