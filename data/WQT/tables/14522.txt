# Pest Symptoms and Recommended Control Actions (Not Plant-Based)

| Pest | Common Signs on Plants | Seasonality | Recommended Action | Prevention Tip |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Aphids | Sticky honeydew, curled leaves | Spring–summer | Spray water jet; insecticidal soap | Avoid excess nitrogen |
| Slugs | Irregular holes, slime trails | Wet periods | Hand-pick; iron phosphate bait | Mulch management |
| Spider mites | Fine webbing, stippling | Hot/dry | Increase humidity; miticide | Reduce drought stress |
| Whiteflies | Flying cloud when disturbed | Warm seasons | Yellow sticky traps; soap spray | Remove weeds |
| Cabbage worms | Ragged holes, frass | Spring–fall | Bt (Bacillus thuringiensis) | Use row covers |
| Fungus gnats | Larvae in soil, weak seedlings | Indoors/greenhouse | Reduce watering; sticky cards | Sterile potting mix |
| Carrot fly | Rusty tunnels in roots | Spring–fall | Row cover; timing plantings | Thin seedlings quickly |
