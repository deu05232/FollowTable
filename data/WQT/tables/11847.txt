# Prostatitis antibiotics  oral vs IV options with dosing frequency

| Medication | Route/regimen description | Practical comment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ceftriaxone | 1 g IV once daily | Sometimes used initially in severe cases before stepping down |
| Ciprofloxacin | 500 mg by mouth twice daily | Common outpatient option |
| Levofloxacin | 750 mg by mouth once daily | Once-daily alternative |
| TMP-SMX | 160/800 mg by mouth twice daily | Option when culture suggests susceptibility |
