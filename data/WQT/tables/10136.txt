# Coma and Pupillary Patterns with Cranial Nerve Correlates (Including CN III)

| Pupils (size/reactivity) | Eye position | Likely neuroanatomic issue | Cranial nerve mentioned | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Equal, reactive | Normal | Not coma / sedative effect | — | Physiologic |
| Pinpoint, reactive | Variable | Pontine lesion or opioid intoxication | — | Consider naloxone |
| Unilateral dilated, nonreactive | "Down and out" possible | CN III compression | CN III | Consider aneurysm |
| Bilateral dilated, nonreactive | Fixed | Severe hypoxia or anticholinergic toxicity | CN III (indirect) | Poor prognosis |
| Small, irregular | Variable | Possible midbrain involvement | CN III | Evaluate imaging |
| Light-near dissociation | Normal motility | Dorsal midbrain syndrome | CN III pathway | Neuro-ophthalmology consult |
