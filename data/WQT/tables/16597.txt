# Dietary Fiber  Definitions by Regulatory and Scientific Organizations

| Organization | Term used | Formal definition excerpt | Year |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: |
| Codex Alimentarius Commission | Dietary fiber | Carbohydrate polymers not hydrolyzed in the small intestine; typically ≥10 monomeric units | 2009 |
| Institute of Medicine | Dietary fiber / Functional fiber | Nondigestible carbohydrates intrinsic to plants; isolated fibers with physiologic benefit | 2005 |
| EFSA | Dietary fiber | Non-digestible carbohydrates plus lignin; includes resistant starch and non-digestible oligosaccharides | 2010 |
| FDA | Dietary fiber | Non-digestible soluble/insoluble carbohydrates with beneficial physiological effects | 2016 |
| AACCI | Dietary fiber | Edible plant parts resistant to digestion with complete/partial fermentation in the large intestine | 2001 |
| Health Canada | Dietary fiber | Carbohydrate polymers and lignin not digested in the small intestine | 2012 |
| WHO/FAO | Dietary fiber | Non-starch polysaccharides and resistant starch components associated with plant foods | 2003 |
