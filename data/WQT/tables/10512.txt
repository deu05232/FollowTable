# Daylight saving time overview by country (selected examples)

| Country/Territory | Continent | DST observed | DST start (clocks forward) | DST end (clocks backward) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| United States | North America | Yes | Second Sunday in March | First Sunday in November |
| Mexico | North America | Yes (varies by region) | First Sunday in April (typical) | Last Sunday in October (typical) |
| Japan | Asia | No | - | - |
| Nicaragua | Central America | No | - | - |
| Egypt | Africa | Yes (reintroduced in 2016) | Late April (varies) | Late October (varies) |
