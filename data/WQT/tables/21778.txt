# Concerta dosing guide by age group (starting dose and typical range)

| Age group | Starting dose (once daily, morning) | Typical dose range |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Children (6–12 years) | 18 mg/day | 18–54 mg/day |
| Adolescents (13–17 years) | 18 mg/day | 18–72 mg/day |
| Adults (18–65 years) | 18 or 36 mg/day | 18–72 mg/day |
