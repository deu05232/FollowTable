# Freezer storage times for raw meats and seafood (shopping guide)

| Item (raw) | Freezer time | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Chicken breasts (raw) | 9 months | Keep in original packaging |
| Ground beef (raw) | 3–4 months | Freeze as soon as purchased |
| Pork chops (raw) | 4–6 months | Wrap tightly |
| Salmon fillets (raw) | 2–3 months | Best quality if vacuum sealed |
| Shrimp (raw) | 3–6 months | Remove air from bag |
| Turkey (whole, raw) | 1 year | Thaw in refrigerator |
