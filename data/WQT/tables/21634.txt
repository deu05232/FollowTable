# Bottled Water Acidity Rating Guide (No Measured Brand pH Values)

| Category | Example Products/Brands | Typical pH Range (Generalized) | Simplified Rating | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Strongly acidic beverages | Sports drinks, flavored waters | 2.5–4.0 | Bad | may contribute to enamel erosion |
| Mildly acidic bottled water | Some sparkling waters | 4.0–6.0 | Caution | carbonation can lower pH |
| Near-neutral water | Many purified waters | 6.5–7.5 | OK | considered neutral zone |
| Mildly alkaline water | Some spring waters | 7.5–8.5 | Good | marketed as alkaline |
| High-alkaline water | Alkaline enhanced waters | 8.5–10.0 | Best | often promoted for wellness |
| "pH balanced" products | Purified + minerals | 7.0–8.0 | OK | broad, non-specific guidance |
| Mineral-rich waters | Mineral water | 6.0–8.5 | Varies | mineral profile influences acidity |
| Carbonated mineral waters | Sparkling mineral | 4.0–6.5 | Caution | dissolved CO2 increases acidity |
