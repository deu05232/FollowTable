# # Glycemic Load Guide for Common Foods (focus on GL rather than GI)

| Food item | Typical portion | Available carbohydrate description | Glycemic load category |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Watermelon | 1 cup diced | moderate carbs per portion | Medium |
| Lentils, cooked | 1 cup | steady carbs per portion | Low |
| White rice, cooked | 1 cup | high carbs per portion | High |
| Apple | 1 medium | moderate carbs per portion | Low |
