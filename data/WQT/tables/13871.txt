# Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC) income limits by filing status (Single HOH) and number of qualifying children

| Qualifying children | Filing status | Phaseout begins (AGI) | Credit ends (AGI) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | Single or Head of Household | $8,000 | $15,000 |
| 1 | Single or Head of Household | $18,000 | $40,000 |
| 2 | Single or Head of Household | $18,000 | $45,000 |
| 3+ | Single or Head of Household | $18,000 | $49,000 |
| Any | Married Filing Separately | Not eligible | Not eligible |
