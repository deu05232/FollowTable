# BMI categories table used for weight status screening

| BMI Range | Category | What it means |
| --- | --- | --- |
| < 18.5 | Underweight | Below recommended range |
| 18.5–24.9 | Normal | Recommended range |
| 25.0–29.9 | Overweight | Above recommended range |
| ≥ 30.0 | Obese | Significantly above recommended range |
