# Blood Pressure Category Buckets with Side-by-Side Systolic Diastolic Cutoffs (Adults)

| Category label | Systolic cutoff (mm Hg) | Diastolic cutoff (mm Hg) | Typical interpretation rule |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Optimal | < 120 | < 80 | Both numbers below these cutoffs |
| Normal | 120–129 | 80–84 | Category met when either SBP or DBP lands in this band while the other is not higher |
| High-normal | 130–139 | 85–89 | If SBP 130–139 and/or DBP 85–89 |
| Hypertension (Grade 1 / Mild) | 140–159 | 90–99 | If SBP 140–159 or DBP 90–99 |
| Hypertension (Grade 2 / Moderate) | 160–179 | 100–109 | If SBP 160–179 or DBP 100–109 |
| Hypertension (Grade 3 / Severe) | ≥ 180 | ≥ 110 | If SBP ≥180 or DBP ≥110 |
| Isolated systolic hypertension | ≥ 140 | < 90 | SBP in hypertensive range but DBP <90 |
| Isolated diastolic hypertension | < 140 | ≥ 90 | DBP in hypertensive range but SBP <140 |
