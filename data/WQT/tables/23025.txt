# Common Garden Pests and Typical Host Crops (Damage-Focused Overview)

| Pest | Common Host Plants | Typical Damage | Peak Season |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Aphids | Roses, beans, kale | Leaf curl, sticky honeydew | Spring–summer |
| Cabbage white caterpillars | Cabbage, broccoli, kale | Holes in leaves, frass | Spring–fall |
| Carrot fly | Carrot, parsnip, celery | Rusty tunnels in roots | Spring–summer |
| Japanese beetle | Roses, grapes, raspberries | Skeletonized leaves | Summer |
| Slugs & snails | Lettuce, hosta, seedlings | Ragged holes, slime trails | Spring–fall |
| Spider mites | Tomatoes, cucumbers, houseplants | Stippling, webbing, leaf drop | Summer |
| Whiteflies | Tomatoes, peppers, ornamentals | Yellowing, sooty mold | Summer–greenhouse |
| Root-knot nematodes | Tomatoes, okra, cucurbits | Root galls, stunting | Warm season |
