# Garden Pest Control Cheat Sheet  Plants, Pests, and Non-Plant Repellents

| Repellent | Category | Targets (Pests) | Application |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Citronella grass | Plant | Mosquitoes | Plant near seating areas |
| Marigold | Plant | Aphids, whiteflies | Border planting |
| Neem oil | Botanical pesticide | Aphids, mites, whiteflies | Spray on foliage |
| Diatomaceous earth | Mineral | Slugs, ants, beetles | Dust around plants |
| Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt) | Microbial | Caterpillars | Foliar spray |
| Garlic spray | Homemade extract | Spider mites, aphids | Spray weekly |
| Row covers | Physical barrier | Cabbage moth, flea beetles | Cover seedlings |
| Peppermint oil | Essential oil | Ants, spiders | Wipe entry points |
| Beer trap | Attractant trap | Slugs | Set cups in soil |
