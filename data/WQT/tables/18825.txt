# Route Status Legend for Airline Destination Lists

| Symbol/Label | Meaning | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Future | Not yet launched | Announced but not operating |
| Seasonal | Operates part of the year | Often summer/winter only |
| Terminated | No longer served | Historical route |
| Charter | Non-scheduled service | Usually tour operator driven |
| Codeshare | Sold by partner airline | Operated by another carrier |
| Hub | Main connecting airport | High frequency operations |
| Focus city | Secondary base | Limited connections |
| Suspended | Temporarily stopped | May return later |
