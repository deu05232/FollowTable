| Bible Version/Tradition | Old Testament | New Testament | Deuterocanon/Apocrypha Section | Number of Books (approx.) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Protestant (typical) | Included | Included | Excluded or separate | 66 | Apocrypha often omitted |
| Roman Catholic (typical) | Included | Included | Included (Deuterocanon) | 73 | Tobit, Judith, etc. |
| Eastern Orthodox (typical) | Included | Included | Included (broader) | 76–81 | Varies by jurisdiction |
| Ethiopian Orthodox (broad canon) | Included | Included | Included (very broad) | 80+ | Includes additional writings |
| Syriac Peshitta (traditional) | Included | Included | Limited/varies | ~66–73 | Some books differ historically |
| Latin Vulgate (historical) | Included | Included | Included | ~73 | Traditional Western canon |
| Septuagint (LXX, as collection) | Included (Greek OT) | N/A | Includes extra Greek books | N/A | Not a full Bible by itself |
| Jewish Tanakh | Hebrew Bible | Excluded | Excluded | 24 | Different ordering/combining |
