# Common oxygen-containing functional groups (condensed structures only)

| Functional group | Condensed structural example | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Alcohol | CH3CH2OH | hydroxyl present |
| Aldehyde | CH3CHO | terminal carbonyl |
| Ketone | CH3COCH3 | internal carbonyl |
| Carboxylic acid | CH3CO2H | acidic |
| Ester | CH3CO2CH2CH3 | fruity odors |
| Ether | CH3CH2OCH2CH3 | relatively inert |
