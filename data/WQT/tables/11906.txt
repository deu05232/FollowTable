# Pacific War endgame timeline (spring–summer 1945) before Hiroshima

| Date | Event | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Apr 1, 1945 | Okinawa campaign begins | Signals fierce resistance close to Japan |
| May 8, 1945 | Germany surrenders in Europe (VE Day) | War focus shifts fully to the Pacific |
| Jul 16, 1945 | Trinity test conducted | Demonstrates atomic weapon capability |
| Jul 26, 1945 | Potsdam Declaration | Calls for Japan’s surrender |
| Aug 6, 1945 | Hiroshima bombed | First wartime atomic bombing |
| Aug 9, 1945 | Nagasaki bombed | Second atomic bombing |
| Aug 15, 1945 | Japan announces surrender | Pacific war ends |
