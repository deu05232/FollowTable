# Music-wire spring steel (ASTM A228) — typical mechanical properties

| Property | Typical value | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tensile strength | 2300–2600 MPa | Drawn wire, depends on diameter |
| Yield strength (0.2% offset) | 1900–2200 MPa | Approximate range |
| Modulus of elasticity (E) | ~207 GPa | At room temperature |
| Shear modulus (G) | ~79 GPa | Used for torsion springs |
| Hardness | 48–55 HRC | Typical after drawing |
| Elongation at break | 2–5% | Low ductility typical of high-strength wire |
