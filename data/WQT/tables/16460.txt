# Public holidays in Botswana (with variable dates and background notes)

| Month/Window | Holiday | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| January | New Year’s Day | First day of the year |
| March/April (varies) | Good Friday | Date depends on Easter |
| April/May (varies) | Easter Monday | Moves each year |
| May | Labour Day | Workers’ holiday |
| July (first Monday) | Presidents’ Day | Observed on a weekday |
| December (late month) | Christmas period | Year-end celebrations |
