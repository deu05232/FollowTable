# Limousine Hourly Pricing (Varies by City and Season)

| Limo type | Hourly price | Why it changes | Common constraints |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Stretch limousine | Varies | Weekends vs weekdays, demand spikes | 2–4 hour minimum |
| SUV limousine | Varies | Vehicle age, operator insurance | May require deposit |
| Hummer limousine | Varies | Fuel prices, special-event surcharges | Limited pickup zones |
| Luxury sedan (executive) | Varies | Airport vs point-to-point | Gratuity sometimes excluded |
| Sprinter limo van | Varies | Event season, driver availability | Overtime billed in 30-min blocks |
| Small party bus | Varies | A/V package, bar setup | Cleaning fee possible |
| Coach/large party bus | Varies | Route length, parking permits | Driver hours-of-service limits |
