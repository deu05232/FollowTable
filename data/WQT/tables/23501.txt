# DLNA common image formats and basic characteristics

| Format | Typical file extension(s) | Media type | Compression | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| JPEG | .jpg, .jpeg | Image | Lossy | Commonly used for photos on DLNA servers |
| PNG | .png | Image | Lossless | Often supported for images; transparency may vary |
| GIF | .gif | Image | Lossless (palette) | Usually limited to simple graphics/animation handling |
| BMP | .bmp | Image | Uncompressed | Large files; support may be optional |
| TIFF | .tif, .tiff | Image | Lossless/varies | Not always supported on TVs/receivers |
