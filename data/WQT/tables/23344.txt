# WWII Pacific War Timeline and Aftermath (1941–1947)

| Date | Event (Short) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dec 7, 1941 | Attack on Pearl Harbor | US enters WWII (Pacific focus) |
| Jun 4–7, 1942 | Battle of Midway | Major US naval victory |
| Oct 20, 1944 | Battle of Leyte Gulf | Largest naval battle of the war |
| Jul 16, 1945 | Trinity nuclear test | First detonation of an atomic device |
| Aug 6, 1945 | Atomic bombing of Hiroshima | “Little Boy” dropped |
| Aug 9, 1945 | Atomic bombing of Nagasaki | Second atomic bombing |
| Sep 2, 1945 | Japan signs surrender | Formal end of WWII |
| 1946–1947 | Tokyo War Crimes Tribunal begins | Postwar legal proceedings |
| 1947 | Constitution of Japan promulgated | Postwar political reforms |
