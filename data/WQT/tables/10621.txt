# Civilian-to-Military Rank Equivalency (GS SES vs Military Pay Grades)

| Category | Civil Service Level | Typical Civilian Role | Military Equivalent | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Senior Executive | SES | Agency executive | O-7 to O-10 | Broad equivalency only |
| Field Grade Officer | GS-15 | Division director | O-6 | Not a pay table |
| Field Grade Officer | GS-14 | Branch chief | O-5 | Not a pay table |
| Company Grade Officer | GS-12 | Program manager | O-3 | Not a pay table |
| Warrant/Junior Officer | GS-11 | Technical lead | WO-2 / O-2 | Not a pay table |
| Senior NCO | GS-09 | Senior analyst | E-7 | Not a pay table |
| NCO | GS-07 | Supervisor | E-5/E-6 | Not a pay table |
| Junior Enlisted | GS-05 | Technician | E-3/E-4 | Not a pay table |
