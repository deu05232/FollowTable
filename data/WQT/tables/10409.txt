# Seasonal Deer Diet Breakdown by Forage Class (Applied to Landscaping Decisions)

| Season | Woody Browse (Shrubs/Trees) | Forbs (Wildflowers/Weeds) | Grasses & Sedges | Mast (Acorns/Nuts) | Notes for Home Landscapes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Winter | High | Low | Medium | Low | Deer rely on twigs and evergreen foliage |
| Early Spring | Medium | Medium | Medium | Low | Tender new growth is attractive |
| Late Spring | Medium | High | Low | Low | Forbs increase as they emerge |
| Summer | Low | High | Low | Medium | Broadleaf plants dominate diet |
| Fall | High | Medium | Low | High | Acorns and woody browse increase |
| Drought Years | Very High | Low | Medium | Low | Browse pressure rises when herbaceous plants fail |
| Heavy Snow | Very High | Very Low | Low | Very Low | Deer concentrate on accessible shrubs and trees |
