# Stainless Steel Grades (304, 310, 316) - Corrosion Resistance and Application Guidance

| Grade | General Corrosion Resistance | Pitting Resistance | Typical Environments | Common Uses | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 304 | Good | Moderate | Indoor, mild outdoor | Tanks, food equipment | Economical general-purpose |
| 304L | Good | Moderate | Welded structures | Piping, vessels | Lower sensitization risk |
| 310 | Fair–Good | Moderate | High-temperature oxidizing | Furnace parts | Best for heat resistance |
| 310S | Fair–Good | Moderate | High-temp, welded | Heat exchangers | Lower C improves weldability |
| 316 | Very good | Good | Marine, chloride exposure | Marine fittings | Mo improves pitting resistance |
| 316L | Very good | Good | Welded marine service | Chemical processing | Low C for welding |
| 321 | Good | Moderate | Elevated temp | Exhaust, heat equipment | Ti stabilized |
| 347 | Good | Moderate | Elevated temp | Refining equipment | Nb stabilized |
