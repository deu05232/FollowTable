# Extraocular Muscles and CN Innervation with Orbital Passageways

| Extraocular muscle | Primary cranial nerve | Main action | Nerve skull opening / route (focus) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Medial rectus | Oculomotor (CN III) | Adduction | Superior orbital fissure → within common tendinous ring |
| Lateral rectus | Abducens (CN VI) | Abduction | Superior orbital fissure → through common tendinous ring |
| Superior rectus | Oculomotor (CN III) | Elevation | Superior orbital fissure → superior division pathway |
| Inferior rectus | Oculomotor (CN III) | Depression | Superior orbital fissure → inferior division pathway |
| Inferior oblique | Oculomotor (CN III) | Extorsion/elevation | Inferior division → courses along orbital floor |
| Superior oblique | Trochlear (CN IV) | Intorsion/depression | Superior orbital fissure → along orbital roof to trochlea |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Oculomotor (CN III) | Eyelid elevation | Superior division → enters orbit via superior orbital fissure |
| Orbicularis oculi (eyelid closure) | Facial (CN VII) | Eye closure | Facial canal → exits stylomastoid foramen |
| Corneal sensation (not a muscle) | Trigeminal V1 | Afferent limb blink | Superior orbital fissure → nasociliary branch |
