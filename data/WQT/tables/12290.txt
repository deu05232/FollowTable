# Foraging preference of deer for garden plant types across seasons

| Season | Most targeted plant types | Less targeted plant types | Typical feeding pattern |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Spring | Tender perennials, buds | Aromatic herbs | Browsing new growth |
| Summer | Annual flowers, vegetables | Tough-leaved shrubs | Night feeding near cover |
| Fall | Shrub tips, fallen fruit | Many bulbs | Opportunistic feeding |
| Winter | Evergreen shrubs, twigs | Dormant perennials | Concentrated browsing |
