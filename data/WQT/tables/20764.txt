# Neurotransmitter Map of Autonomic Pathways  Release Site and Fiber Category

| Neurotransmitter | Where it is released in the autonomic pathway | Fiber / cell that releases it | Typical pathway label | Immediate postsynaptic target | Notes (about location, not organ effect) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | At autonomic ganglia (both divisions) | All preganglionic autonomic neurons | Sympathetic preganglionic; Parasympathetic preganglionic | Ganglionic neuron (nicotinic Nn) | Defines the common preganglionic → ganglion handoff for ANS |
| Norepinephrine (NE) | At neuroeffector junctions | Most postganglionic sympathetic neurons | Sympathetic postganglionic (adrenergic) | Effector cell (adrenergic α/β) | Canonical sympathetic postganglionic transmitter |
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | At neuroeffector junctions | Postganglionic sympathetic neurons to sweat glands | Sympathetic postganglionic (cholinergic) | Effector cell (muscarinic) | Key exception: sympathetic but cholinergic after the ganglion |
| Dopamine (DA) | At neuroeffector junctions | Some postganglionic sympathetic neurons (classically renal vascular) | Sympathetic postganglionic (dopaminergic) | Effector cell (D1 receptors) | Included as a postganglionic sympathetic variant transmitter |
| Epinephrine (Epi) | Into bloodstream (endocrine release) | Adrenal medulla chromaffin cells | Modified sympathetic postganglionic output | Distant effector organs | Triggered by preganglionic ACh at the adrenal medulla |
| Norepinephrine (NE) | Into bloodstream (endocrine release) | Adrenal medulla chromaffin cells | Modified sympathetic postganglionic output | Distant effector organs | Often co-released with epinephrine; still a “postganglionic-like” output |
| Acetylcholine (ACh) | At neuroeffector junctions | Postganglionic parasympathetic neurons | Parasympathetic postganglionic (cholinergic) | Effector cell (muscarinic M receptors) | Canonical parasympathetic postganglionic transmitter |
| Nitric oxide (NO) | At neuroeffector junctions (co-transmission) | Some parasympathetic postganglionic neurons (NANC) | Parasympathetic postganglionic (NANC co-transmitter) | Effector cell (NO signaling pathways) | Not the primary ganglionic transmitter; placed here specifically as a postganglionic co-transmitter |
