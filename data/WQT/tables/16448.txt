# Public Holidays Overview in Botswana (Typical Months, No Fixed Dates)

| Holiday | Typical timing | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| New Year's Day | Early January | Start of the year observance |
| Good Friday | March or April | Depends on Christian calendar |
| Easter Monday | March or April | Follows Easter Sunday |
| Labour Day | Around May | Worker celebrations |
| Sir Seretse Khama Day | Early July | National commemoration |
| Christmas Day | Late December | Christian holiday |
| Boxing Day | Late December | Day after Christmas |
