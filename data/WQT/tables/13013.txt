# Ancient Egyptian Iconography  Deities and Common Depictions

| Deity | Typical depiction | Animal / symbol | Common artifact context |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Anubis | Jackal-headed man | Jackal | Mummification equipment; tomb paintings |
| Horus | Falcon-headed man | Falcon | Royal statuary; temple reliefs |
| Thoth | Ibis-headed man | Ibis / baboon | Scribal palettes; judgment scenes |
| Sekhmet | Lioness-headed woman | Lioness | Healing stelae; temple statues |
| Hathor | Woman with cow horns and sun disk | Cow | Sistrum; festival scenes |
| Sobek | Crocodile-headed man | Crocodile | Fayum temple reliefs |
| Khnum | Ram-headed man | Ram | Elephantine temple scenes |
| Bastet | Cat-headed woman | Cat | Amulets; household cult objects |
