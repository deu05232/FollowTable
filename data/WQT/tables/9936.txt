# # Egyptian deities and their domains (selected list)

| Deity | Type | Domain / Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Isis | Goddess | Magic, motherhood, healing |
| Horus | God | Kingship, sky, protection |
| Hathor | Goddess | Love, music, joy |
| Thoth | God | Writing, wisdom, knowledge |
| Set | God | Chaos, storms, deserts |
| Nephthys | Goddess | Protection of the dead, mourning |
