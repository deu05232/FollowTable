|---|---:|---|---|---|

| Carbon | 4 | CH4 | tetrahedral | valency as bond count |
| Nitrogen | 3 | NH3 | trigonal pyramidal | lone pair present |
| Oxygen | 2 | H2O | bent | - |
| Fluorine | 1 | HF | single bond | - |
| Phosphorus | 3 (or 5) | PH3 / PCl5 | expanded octet case | bonding-focused |
| Sulfur | 2 (or 6) | H2S / SF6 | expanded octet case | bonding-focused |
| Chlorine | 1 (or 7) | HCl / Cl2O7 | hypervalent molecules | - |
| Silicon | 4 | SiH4 | tetrahedral | - |
| Boron | 3 | BH3 | electron-deficient | - |
