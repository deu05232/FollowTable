# Selected Verse Variant Readings Across Editions (KJV vs Vulgate vs NIV)

| Reference | KJV wording | Vulgate wording (Latin) | NIV wording | Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Genesis 1:2 | "And the Spirit of God moved..." | "spiritus Dei ferebatur..." | "...the Spirit of God was hovering..." | Translation of motion verb |
| Psalm 23:1 | "The LORD is my shepherd" | "Dominus regit me" | "The LORD is my shepherd" | Different verb choice in Latin |
| Isaiah 7:14 | "a virgin shall conceive" | "virgo concipiet" | "the virgin will conceive" | Famous prophecy rendering |
| Matthew 5:3 | "poor in spirit" | "pauperes spiritu" | "poor in spirit" | Close agreement |
| Romans 5:1 | "we have peace" | "pacem habeamus" | "we have peace" | Indicative vs hortatory debate |
| 1 John 5:7 | Includes Comma Johanneum | Absent in most manuscripts; varies | Omitted | Text-critical issue |
| Mark 16:9–20 | Included | Included with notes in editions | Marked as later addition | Long ending discussion |
