# Child Passenger Safety  Recommended Restraint Use by Age (General Guidance)

| Child Age | Recommended Restraint | Typical Seating Position Guidance | Notes | Source Type |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0–12 months | Rear-facing infant seat | Rear seat preferred | Keep rear-facing as long as allowed by seat | Guidance |
| 1–3 years | Rear-facing convertible seat | Rear seat preferred | Transition only when max height/weight reached | Guidance |
| 4–7 years | Forward-facing seat with harness | Rear seat preferred | Use harness until outgrown | Guidance |
| 8–12 years | Belt-positioning booster | Rear seat preferred | Lap belt low on hips; shoulder belt across chest | Guidance |
| 13–15 years | Adult seat belt | Rear seat preferred | Proper belt fit check | Guidance |
| 16–17 years | Adult seat belt | Any seat with belt | Encourage consistent use | Guidance |
| 18+ years | Adult seat belt | Any seat with belt | Universal best practice | Guidance |
