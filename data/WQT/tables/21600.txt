# Bottled Water Brand Families and Typical pH Range (Grouped by Type)

| Water Type | Brand Family / Examples | Typical pH Range | Comments |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Purified (RO) | Aquafina, Dasani | 5.0–7.0 | Can vary with minerals added |
| Spring | Poland Spring, Deer Park | 6.0–8.0 | Depends on source geology |
| Artesian | Fiji, Waiakea | 7.0–8.5 | Often marketed as alkaline |
| Mineral | Perrier, S.Pellegrino | 4.5–7.5 | Carbonation affects acidity |
| Alkaline enhanced | Essentia, Smartwater Alkaline | 8.0–10.0 | Typically ionized or mineralized |
| Flavored water | Propel, Vitaminwater | 2.5–4.5 | Acids for flavor/preservation |
| Carbonated plain | Topo Chico, LaCroix | 3.0–4.5 | CO₂ lowers pH |
