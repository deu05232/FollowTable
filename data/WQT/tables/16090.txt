# Rotational Symmetry Summary for Polygons (Using Fraction of a Turn)

| Shape | Repeat rotation (as a fraction of a full turn) | Equivalent rotation | Comment |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Equilateral triangle | 1/3 turn | 120° | three repeats per full turn |
| Square | 1/4 turn | 90° | four repeats per full turn |
| Regular pentagon | 1/5 turn | 72° | five repeats per full turn |
| Regular hexagon | 1/6 turn | 60° | six repeats per full turn |
| Regular heptagon | 1/7 turn | ~51.43° | seven repeats per full turn |
| Regular octagon | 1/8 turn | 45° | eight repeats per full turn |
| Rectangle | 1/2 turn | 180° | two repeats per full turn |
| Parallelogram | 1/2 turn | 180° | two repeats per full turn |
| Scalene triangle | 1 turn | 360° | only once per full turn |
| Circle | any fraction | any angle | continuous symmetry |
