| West Bengal | Bengali | Bengali | Kolkata | Also used: Nepali (in hills) |
| Tamil Nadu | Tamil | Tamil | Chennai | English used in administration |

| Kerala | Malayalam | Malayalam | Thiruvananthapuram | English in education |
