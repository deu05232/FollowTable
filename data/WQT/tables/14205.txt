# Extra Fees You Might Pay on Top of Routine Passport Service (Speed-Up & Processing Fees)

| Extra fee category | When it applies | Amount |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Expedited service surcharge | You request faster processing | $60 |
| Passport book overnight delivery | You want faster return shipping | $20.66 |
| File search fee | You can’t provide citizenship evidence and need a records search | $150 |
| Special issuance fee | Replacement needed under special circumstances | $0 |
| Appointment handling fee (third-party) | Optional paid appointment booking services | Varies |
