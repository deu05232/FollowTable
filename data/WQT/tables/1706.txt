# Statutory holidays in Canada both national and provincial .

| Holiday | Day Observed | Observance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Thanksgiving | Second Monday in October | Nationwide except NB, NS, PE, NL |
| Remembrance Day | 11-Nov | Nationwide except ON, QC, NS, NL |
| Christmas Day | 25-Dec | Nationwide |
| Boxing Day | 26-Dec | ON |
