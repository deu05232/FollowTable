# Felony sentencing tiers (general guideline table, no state information)

| Felony level | Description | Maximum prison term |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Level 1 | Most serious felonies | Life |
| Level 2 | Serious violent felonies | Up to 25 years |
| Level 3 | Mid-level felonies | Up to 10 years |
| Level 4 | Lower-level felonies | Up to 5 years |
| Level 5 | Least serious felonies | Up to 2 years |
