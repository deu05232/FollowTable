# Orbital Pathways  Extraocular Muscles, Cranial Nerves, and Foramina Canals

| Extraocular muscle | Motor nerve | Main orbital entry (foramen/canal) | Typical route | Clinical note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Superior rectus | CN III (superior division) | Superior orbital fissure | Through annulus of Zinn | Ptosis may accompany CN III lesions |
| Inferior rectus | CN III (inferior division) | Superior orbital fissure | Through annulus of Zinn | Diplopia on downgaze |
| Medial rectus | CN III (inferior division) | Superior orbital fissure | Through annulus of Zinn | Impaired adduction |
| Inferior oblique | CN III (inferior division) | Superior orbital fissure | Enters orbit then to anteromedial floor | Often confused with superior oblique |
| Superior oblique | CN IV | Superior orbital fissure | Outside annulus, then to trochlea | Vertical diplopia |
| Lateral rectus | CN VI | Superior orbital fissure | Through annulus of Zinn | Abduction deficit |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | CN III (superior division) | Superior orbital fissure | Through annulus of Zinn | Ptosis |
