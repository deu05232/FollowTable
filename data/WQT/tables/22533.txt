# Autonomic Fibers  Anatomy and Conduction Properties

| Fiber type | Typical origin | Myelination | Diameter (µm) | Conduction velocity (m/s) | Common description |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Preganglionic sympathetic | Thoracolumbar (T1–L2) | Lightly myelinated | 1–5 | 3–15 | White rami communicantes |
| Postganglionic sympathetic | Sympathetic chain/prevertebral ganglia | Unmyelinated | 0.2–1.5 | 0.5–2 | Gray rami communicantes |
| Preganglionic parasympathetic | Brainstem & sacral (S2–S4) | Lightly myelinated | 1–5 | 3–15 | Long fibers to terminal ganglia |
| Postganglionic parasympathetic | Terminal/intramural ganglia | Unmyelinated | 0.2–1.5 | 0.5–2 | Short fibers to effectors |
| Visceral afferent (pain) | DRG → CNS | Variable | 0.5–2 | 1–30 | Referred pain pathways |
| Enteric interneurons | Myenteric/submucosal plexus | Mostly unmyelinated | ~0.2–1 | Slow | Local reflex circuits |
