# Extraocular Muscle Innervation (Cranial Nerve Mapping for Eye Movements)

| Muscle group | Specific extraocular muscle | Primary action (for recall) | Innervating cranial nerve (named) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Rectus muscles | Superior rectus | Elevation (especially in abduction) | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Rectus muscles | Inferior rectus | Depression (especially in abduction) | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Rectus muscles | Medial rectus | Adduction | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Rectus muscles | Lateral rectus | Abduction | Abducens nerve (CN VI) |
| Oblique muscles | Superior oblique | Intorsion; depression in adduction | Trochlear nerve (CN IV) |
| Oblique muscles | Inferior oblique | Extorsion; elevation in adduction | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Eyelid (often tested with EOMs) | Levator palpebrae superioris | Elevates upper eyelid | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
