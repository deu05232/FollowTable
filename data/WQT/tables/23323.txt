# Autonomic Neurotransmitters by Target Organ (Common Effects)

| Organ/System | Parasympathetic messenger (typical) | Sympathetic messenger (typical) | Primary response described |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Eye (iris) | Acetylcholine | Norepinephrine | Pupil constriction vs dilation |
| Heart | Acetylcholine | Norepinephrine | Heart rate decreases vs increases |
| Bronchi | Acetylcholine | Epinephrine (circulating) | Bronchoconstriction vs bronchodilation |
| GI tract motility | Acetylcholine | Norepinephrine | Motility increases vs decreases |
| Salivary glands | Acetylcholine | Norepinephrine | Watery vs viscous secretion |
| Sweat glands | — | Acetylcholine | Increased sweating |
| Blood vessels (skeletal muscle) | — | Epinephrine | Vasodilation |
| Bladder detrusor | Acetylcholine | Norepinephrine | Contraction vs relaxation |
