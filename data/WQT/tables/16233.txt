# EITC Maximum Credit by Number of Qualifying Children (Missing Income Limits)

| Qualifying children | Maximum credit | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | $506 | Smaller credit for workers without children |
| 1 | $3,359 | Credit rises substantially with one child |
| 2 | $5,548 | Higher maximum with two children |
| 3 or more | $6,242 | Largest maximum for three or more children |
| 4 or more | $6,242 | Same as 3+ (cap) |
| Any | Subject to rules | Must meet residency/relationship tests |
