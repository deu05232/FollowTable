# Autonomic Nervous System  Target-Organ Responses (Selected Examples)

| Organ/Function | Parasympathetic effect | Sympathetic effect | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Eye (pupil) | Miosis (constricts) | Mydriasis (dilates) | Light reflex vs arousal |
| Heart | Decreases heart rate | Increases heart rate & contractility | Also changes AV node conduction |
| Bronchi | Bronchoconstriction | Bronchodilation | Influences airway resistance |
| GI motility | Increases peristalsis | Decreases motility | Alters sphincter tone |
| Salivary glands | Watery secretion | Viscous secretion | Stress vs rest states |
| Bladder | Detrusor contraction | Detrusor relaxation | Coordinated with sphincters |
| Skin | Minimal | Sweating, piloerection | Thermoregulation |
| Blood vessels | Little direct effect | Vasoconstriction/vasodilation patterns | Depends on vascular bed |
