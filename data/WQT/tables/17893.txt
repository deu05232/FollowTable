# Leaf spring types and layouts (no material property data)

| Leaf spring layout | Common name | Typical vehicle use | Key feature |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Full elliptic | Elliptic | Early wagons | Two semi-elliptics back-to-back |
| Single leaf | Mono-leaf | Light trucks | Reduced weight |
| Multi-leaf | Semi-elliptic | Trucks/SUV | Higher load capacity |
| Quarter elliptic | Quarter-elliptic | Custom builds | Space-saving mount |
| Three-quarter elliptic | 3/4 elliptic | Classic designs | Combined mounting styles |
| Transverse | Transverse leaf | Sports cars | Spring runs across chassis |
