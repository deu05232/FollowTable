# Common clinical note abbreviations and their full meanings (general medicine)

| Abbreviation | Full meaning (written out) | Common context in notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| SOB | shortness of breath | Symptom description (e.g., “SOB on exertion”) |
| CP | chest pain | Triage / cardiology complaints |
| N/V | nausea and vomiting | GI symptoms and side-effect lists |
| DOE | shortness of breath with exertion | Cardio-pulmonary review of systems |
| HTN | high blood pressure (hypertension) | Problem list and past medical history |
| DM | diabetes mellitus | Problem list and chronic disease management |
| CHF | congestive heart failure | Cardiology history and assessment |
| CKD | chronic kidney disease | Nephrology history and lab interpretation |
| DVT | deep vein thrombosis | Vascular history and imaging impressions |
| PE | pulmonary embolism | Emergency notes, CT angiography results |
| UTI | urinary tract infection | Urinalysis interpretation and antibiotic plans |
| COPD | chronic obstructive pulmonary disease | Pulmonary history, smoking-related illness |
| CVA | stroke (cerebrovascular accident) | Neurology history and imaging summaries |
| MI | heart attack (myocardial infarction) | Cardiology assessments and discharge summaries |
