# State Return Due Dates After Federal Tax Day (General Deadline Matrix)

| Category | States | Regular Due Date | Extension Reference |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Same as federal (Apr 15) | NY, IL, GA | Apr 15 | Extensions generally run to Oct 15 |
| Later than federal | VA, IA | May 1 / Apr 30 | Some accept federal extension |
| Weekend/holiday shift | NJ, PA | Next business day | Extension request due same day |
| Special disaster relief | CA (selected counties) | Oct 16 | Separate relief extension rules |
| Fiscal-year filers | Multi-state | 15th day of 4th month | Extension periods vary |
| Military overseas | Multi-state | +60 days | Extension may be automatic |
| Other exceptions | Multi-state | Varies | See state guidance |
