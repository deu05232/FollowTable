# # Climate Zones of the Rockies  Average Annual Snowfall

| Climate zone | Typical locations | Average annual snowfall (inches) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| High alpine | Above treeline areas | 220–350 |
| Montane | Mid-elevation forests | 120–200 |
| Foothills | Lower mountain slopes | 40–90 |
| Plains | Adjacent lowlands | 10–30 |
