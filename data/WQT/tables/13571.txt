# U.S. Navy Enlisted Pay Grades and Ratings (E-1 to E-9) with Common Rate Abbreviations

| Pay Grade | Navy Rate (Enlisted) | Common Abbreviation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| E-1 | Seaman Recruit | SR | Also used for general entry-level pay grade |
| E-2 | Seaman Apprentice | SA | Junior enlisted |
| E-3 | Seaman | SN | Sometimes shown as “Seaman” for E-3 |
| E-4 | Petty Officer Third Class | PO3 | Petty officer begins at E-4 |
| E-5 | Petty Officer Second Class | PO2 | Mid-level petty officer |
| E-6 | Petty Officer First Class | PO1 | Senior petty officer |
| E-7 | Chief Petty Officer | CPO | Chief’s Mess begins at E-7 |
| E-8 | Senior Chief Petty Officer | SCPO | Senior chief |
| E-9 | Master Chief Petty Officer | MCPO | Master chief |
| E-9 | Master Chief Petty Officer of the Navy | MCPON | Senior enlisted advisor to CNO |
