# BMI Categories for Adults — Quick Lookup of BMI Cutoffs and Weight-Status Labels (Detailed)

| BMI (kg/m²) | Weight-status label | Risk note (brief) |
|---|---|---|
| < 18.5 | Underweight | Possible nutritional deficiency |
| 18.5–22.9 | Normal weight (lower range) | Generally lower metabolic risk |
| 23.0–24.9 | Normal weight (upper range) | Monitor trends if gaining |
| 25.0–27.4 | Overweight (lower range) | Elevated risk begins |
| 27.5–29.9 | Overweight (upper range) | Higher risk than lower range |
| 30.0–32.4 | Obesity (Class I, lower) | Increased cardiometabolic risk |
| 32.5–34.9 | Obesity (Class I, upper) | Risk increases with BMI |
| 35.0–37.4 | Obesity (Class II, lower) | High risk |
| 37.5–39.9 | Obesity (Class II, upper) | Very high risk |
| ≥ 40.0 | Obesity (Class III) | Extremely high risk |
