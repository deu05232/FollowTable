# Megapixels and Common Aspect Ratios Used by Digital Cameras

| Megapixels Class | Typical Aspect Ratios | Example Use Cases | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 MP | 4:3 | Compact cameras | Older point-and-shoot models |
| 8 MP | 4:3, 3:2 | Compact / early mirrorless | Mixed sensor formats |
| 12 MP | 3:2, 4:3 | Entry DSLR, Micro Four Thirds | Often offers multiple crops |
| 16 MP | 3:2, 16:9 | Mirrorless/DSLR | 16:9 usually a crop |
| 20 MP | 3:2, 4:3, 1:1 | Enthusiast cameras | Square mode is cropped |
| 24 MP | 3:2, 16:9 | APS-C/Full-frame | Very common stills format |
| 33 MP | 3:2 | High-res full-frame | Detail-oriented shooting |
| 45 MP | 3:2, 4:5 | Pro full-frame | 4:5 often for portrait prints |
