# Iran parental leave benefits by leave type (duration and wage replacement)

| Leave type | Eligible parent | Duration | Pay / wage replacement | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Maternity leave | Mother | 26 weeks | 100% of salary | Standard paid maternity leave entitlement |
| Paternity leave | Father | 2 weeks | 100% of salary | Taken around childbirth |
| Parental leave (shared) | Mother/Father | Not specified in law | Not specified | No separate shared-parental scheme listed |
| Adoption leave | Adoptive parent(s) | 14 weeks | Paid (rate varies by employer) | Applies to formal adoption |
| Pregnancy medical leave | Mother | Up to 2 weeks | Paid sick leave | For pregnancy-related complications |
| Nursing breaks | Mother | Until child is 24 months | Paid break time | Daily paid breastfeeding breaks |
