# # Freezer-Only Storage Times for Foods

| Food (frozen) | Typical time it keeps good quality in the freezer | Packaging tips |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Whole chicken | 12 months | Wrap airtight |
| Chicken pieces | 9 months | Remove excess air |
| Ground turkey | 3–4 months | Use freezer bags |
| Bread dough | 2–3 months | Double wrap |
| Cooked casserole | 2–3 months | Freeze in portions |
