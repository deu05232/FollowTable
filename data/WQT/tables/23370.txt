| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| <5th | Normal | <90th percentile | <90th percentile | 3–12 years |
| <5th | Elevated | 90th–<95th percentile | 90th–<95th percentile | 3–12 years |
| 5th–95th | Normal | <90th percentile | <90th percentile | 3–12 years |
| 5th–95th | Elevated | 90th–<95th percentile | 90th–<95th percentile | 3–12 years |
| >95th | Stage 1 hypertension | ≥95th–<95th+12 mm Hg | ≥95th–<95th+12 mm Hg | 3–12 years |
| >95th | Stage 2 hypertension | ≥95th+12 mm Hg | ≥95th+12 mm Hg | 3–12 years |
