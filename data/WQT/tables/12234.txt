# Electrical Resistivity of Selected Insulators and Semiconductors (Approx. 20–25°C)

| Class | Material | Condition | Electrical resistivity (Ω·m) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: | --- |
| Insulator | Glass (soda-lime) | ~20°C, dry | 1e10–1e14 | Wide range by composition/humidity |
| Insulator | Rubber (natural) | ~20°C | 1e13–1e16 | Strongly formulation-dependent |
| Insulator | PTFE (Teflon) | ~23°C | 1e16–1e18 | Very high resistivity |
| Insulator | Quartz (fused silica) | ~20°C | 1e16–1e18 | Extremely insulating when dry |
| Semiconductor | Silicon (intrinsic) | ~25°C | ~2.3e3 | Intrinsic (undoped) |
| Semiconductor | Germanium (intrinsic) | ~25°C | ~0.47 | Intrinsic (undoped) |
| Semiconductor | Graphite | ~20°C, along basal plane | ~3e-5 | Highly anisotropic (direction matters) |
