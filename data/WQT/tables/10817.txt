# Extraocular muscles and their cranial nerve innervation (including branch when relevant)

| Extraocular muscle | Cranial nerve innervation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Superior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III, superior division) | Elevates the eye |
| Inferior oblique | Oculomotor nerve (CN III, inferior division) | Elevates in adduction |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Oculomotor nerve (CN III, superior division) | Raises upper eyelid |
| Lateral rectus | Abducens nerve (CN VI) | Abducts the eye |
| Superior oblique | Trochlear nerve (CN IV) | Depresses in adduction |
