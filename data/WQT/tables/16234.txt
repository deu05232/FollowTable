# EITC Phase-In and Phase-Out Stages (Illustrative Parameters)

| Stage | Description | Key income point (Single/HOH) | Key income point (MFJ) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Phase-in | Credit increases with earned income | up to ~$7,000 | up to ~$7,000 | Rate depends on child count |
| Plateau | Credit reaches maximum | ~$7,000–$18,000 | ~$7,000–$23,000 | Maximum differs by child count |
| Phase-out threshold | Credit begins to decrease | ~$8,000 / ~$18,000 | ~$13,000 / ~$23,000 | Threshold varies by children |
| Phase-out | Credit decreases as income rises | varies | varies | Different slopes per child count |
| Credit ends | No credit available | up to ~$48,000 | up to ~$54,000 | End point differs by children |
| Investment income test | Must be below limit | <= ~$3,650 | <= ~$3,650 | Separate eligibility rule |
