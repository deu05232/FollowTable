# Fuels and blends  boiling range rather than single-compound boiling point

| Product | Main components (example) | Boiling range (°C) |
|---|---|---|
| Gasoline | C4–C12 hydrocarbons (mixture) | 30–210 |
| Kerosene | C9–C16 hydrocarbons (mixture) | 150–300 |
| Diesel fuel | C10–C22 hydrocarbons (mixture) | 180–360 |
| LPG | Propane/Butane blend | -42 to -1 |
| Mineral spirits | mixed aliphatic hydrocarbons | 150–200 |
| Naphtha | mixed hydrocarbons | 60–200 |
