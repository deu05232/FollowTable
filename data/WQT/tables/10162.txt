# Leftover Storage by Broad Category (Not Specific Cooked Items)

| Category | Examples (Non-exhaustive) | Refrigerator Time | Freezer Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cooked poultry | chicken, turkey, duck | 3-4 days | 2-6 months |
| Cooked red meat | beef, lamb, pork | 3-5 days | 2-8 months |
| Cooked seafood | fish, shrimp, shellfish | 1-2 days | 2-3 months |
| Cooked grains | rice, quinoa, pasta | 3-5 days | 1-2 months |
| Cooked vegetables | roasted, steamed, sautéed | 3-5 days | 8-12 months |
| Mixed casseroles | baked dishes with multiple ingredients | 3-4 days | 2-3 months |
| Soups & sauces | broth-based or creamy | 3-4 days | 2-3 months |
| Dairy-based dishes | cream sauces, custards | 2-3 days | 1-2 months |
