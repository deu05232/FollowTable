# Child safety seat
> Law

| Age group of children | First Preference (recommended by law) | Second Preference (To be followed if first preference cannot be sufficed) | Third Preference Preference (minimal mandatory requirement by law if first/second preference cannot be sufficed) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Age 0–6 | Use an approved child restraint | – | – |
| Seven years | Use a child restraint | Use a seat belt | Travel seated at the back of the vehicle (if possible) |
| Eight to fourteen years | Use a seat belt | Travel seated at the back of the vehicle (if possible) | – |
| Fourteen years and above | Use a seat belt (where fitted) | – | – |
