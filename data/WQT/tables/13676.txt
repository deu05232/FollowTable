# German two-way prepositions with their case depending on meaning

| Preposition | Accusative use (direction) | Dative use (location) |
|---|---|---|
| in | in + acc (into) | in + dat (in/inside) |
| an | an + acc (onto/to) | an + dat (at/on) |
| auf | auf + acc (onto) | auf + dat (on/top of) |
| über | über + acc (across/over to) | über + dat (above/over) |
