| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2nd gen | 3.5-inch | 30-pin | 8GB, 16GB, 32GB | Curved metal back |
| 3rd gen | 3.5-inch | 30-pin | 32GB, 64GB | Similar to 2nd gen exterior |
| 4th gen | 3.5-inch | 30-pin | 8GB, 16GB, 32GB, 64GB | Front camera era |
| 5th gen | 4-inch | Lightning | 16GB, 32GB, 64GB | Taller display, more colors |
