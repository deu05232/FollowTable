# Pain & Inflammation Drugs and Common Drug Interactions (Includes Anticoagulants and Antibiotics)

| Drug (generic) | Type | Selected brands | Typical use | Notable interaction example | Interaction category |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ibuprofen | NSAID | Advil, Motrin | Pain, inflammation | warfarin (↑ bleeding risk) | Cardiovascular/anticoagulant interaction |
| naproxen | NSAID | Aleve, Naprosyn | Pain, inflammation | aspirin (additive GI bleeding) | Antiplatelet/NSAID interaction |
| diclofenac | NSAID | Voltaren, Cataflam | Pain, inflammation | losartan (↓ antihypertensive effect) | Cardiovascular interaction |
| ketorolac | NSAID | Toradol | Short-term severe pain | clopidogrel (↑ bleeding risk) | Antiplatelet interaction |
| celecoxib | COX-2 NSAID | Celebrex | Arthritis pain | fluconazole (↑ celecoxib levels) | Antibiotic/antifungal interaction |
| acetaminophen | Non-NSAID analgesic | Tylenol | Pain/fever | alcohol (↑ liver toxicity risk) | Safety/toxicity note |
| amoxicillin | Antibiotic | Amoxil | Infection | warfarin (↑ INR) | Antibiotic interaction |
| atorvastatin | Statin | Lipitor | Hyperlipidemia | clarithromycin (↑ statin toxicity) | Cardiovascular/safety |
