# Selected Scientific Discoveries Across Eras (time shown as  years ago  or broad era)

| Approx. time (broad) | Discovery / discovered item | Field | What was discovered (item-by-item) | Key context / why it mattered |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~4.6 billion years ago (early Solar System) | Formation age of Earth inferred from radiometric dating | Geochemistry / Planetary science | The approximate age of Earth derived from isotopic ratios in ancient minerals/meteorites | Put Earth’s history on a quantitative timescale and enabled comparison of planetary formation models |
| ~3.5+ billion years ago (early Earth) | Earliest evidence for microbial life | Geobiology | Microfossils/stromatolite-like structures indicating early life | Anchored the minimum age for life and guided searches for biosignatures on other planets |
| ~300–200 thousand years ago | Anatomically modern Homo sapiens fossils | Paleoanthropology | Fossil evidence for early Homo sapiens morphology | Clarified the timeline of human origins and the spread of modern traits |
| ~12–10 thousand years ago (Neolithic) | Domestication of wheat/barley in the Fertile Crescent | Archaeobotany | Transition from wild grasses to domesticated cereals | Helped explain the rise of agriculture, permanent settlements, and population growth |
| ~2,400 years ago (Classical antiquity) | Earth is spherical (observational/geometric arguments) | Astronomy / Geography | A reasoned model of Earth’s shape using shadows and geometry | Enabled improved navigation and laid groundwork for later quantitative Earth measurement |
| ~400 years ago (Scientific Revolution) | Telescopic observations of Jupiter’s moons | Astronomy | Moons orbiting Jupiter rather than Earth | Supported heliocentric thinking by showing not everything orbits Earth |
| ~200 years ago (Industrial-era science) | Electromagnetic induction | Physics | Changing magnetic fields generate electric currents | Became foundational for generators, transformers, and modern electrical infrastructure |
| ~70 years ago (mid-20th century) | DNA double-helix structure | Molecular biology | A physical model of DNA explaining base pairing and replication | Provided the mechanism for heredity and launched modern genetics and biotechnology |
| ~60 years ago | Cosmic microwave background (CMB) detected | Cosmology | Relic radiation from the early universe | Strong evidence for the Big Bang model and constraints on cosmological parameters |
| ~10 years ago (2010s) | Gravitational waves directly detected | Astrophysics | Ripples in spacetime from merging compact objects | Opened gravitational-wave astronomy and new tests of general relativity |
| ~5 years ago (2020s) | First widely deployed mRNA vaccines | Biomedical science | A vaccine platform using mRNA to induce immune responses | Demonstrated fast, adaptable vaccine development and expanded therapeutic possibilities |
