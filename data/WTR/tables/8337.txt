# Common Over-the-Counter (OTC) Pain Relievers  Generic-to-Brand Name Cross-Reference

| Pain medicine (generic) | Common brand name(s) at the pharmacy | Drug type | Typical uses (pain/fever) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Acetaminophen (Paracetamol) | Tylenol, Panadol | Analgesic/antipyretic | Mild–moderate pain, fever |
| Ibuprofen | Advil, Motrin | NSAID | Pain, inflammation, fever |
| Naproxen sodium | Aleve | NSAID | Longer-lasting pain relief, inflammation |
| Aspirin (Acetylsalicylic acid) | Bayer Aspirin, Ecotrin | NSAID/salicylate | Pain, fever (also used for heart protection at low dose) |
| Diclofenac topical | Voltaren Arthritis Pain Gel | NSAID (topical) | Local joint/muscle pain |
| Ketoprofen | Orudis | NSAID | Pain, inflammation |
| Magnesium salicylate | Doan’s Pills | Salicylate | Backache, muscle pain |
| Methyl salicylate + Menthol (topical) | Bengay, Icy Hot | Topical analgesic (counterirritant) | Muscle aches, minor sprains |
| Lidocaine (topical) | Aspercreme Lidocaine, Lidoderm | Local anesthetic | Local nerve/muscle pain, patches/creams |
| Capsaicin (topical) | Zostrix, Capzasin-HP | Topical analgesic | Nerve pain, arthritis pain |
| Bismuth subgallate + topical anesthetic (rectal) | Preparation H Rapid Relief (varies by region) | Topical pain relief | Localized discomfort (formulations vary) |
