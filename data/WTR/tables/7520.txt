# Steroid hormone biosynthesis enzymes and activities

| Enzyme | Gene | Activity |
|---|---|---|
| Aromatase | CYP19A1 | Converts androgens to estrogens |
| 5α-reductase | SRD5A2 | Converts testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT) |
| 21-hydroxylase | CYP21A2 | Required for cortisol and aldosterone synthesis |
| 11β-hydroxylase | CYP11B1 | Key step in cortisol synthesis |
| Aldosterone synthase | CYP11B2 | Final steps in aldosterone synthesis |
