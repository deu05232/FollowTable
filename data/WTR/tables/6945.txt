# Medications Commonly Used in Arthritis Care (Pain vs Disease Control)

| Medication (main entry) | Category | Primary Purpose in Arthritis | Brand/Other Names | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Methotrexate | DMARD | Disease control (not primarily analgesic) | Rheumatrex | Requires monitoring |
| Adalimumab | Biologic DMARD | Disease control | Humira | Infection risk |
| Etanercept | Biologic DMARD | Disease control | Enbrel | Injection site reactions |
| Hydroxychloroquine | DMARD | Disease control | Plaquenil | Eye exams recommended |
| Sulfasalazine | DMARD | Disease control | Azulfidine | GI upset possible |
| Leflunomide | DMARD | Disease control | Arava | Liver toxicity risk |
| Prednisone | Corticosteroid | Inflammation control (bridge therapy) | — | Not a classic painkiller; systemic effects |
