# Hormones and their effects (physiological functions) — quick reference

| Hormone | Primary effect (what it does) |
|---|---|
| Insulin | Lowers blood glucose by promoting glucose uptake and storage |
| Glucagon | Raises blood glucose by stimulating glycogen breakdown and glucose release |
| Cortisol | Helps the body respond to stress; affects metabolism and inflammation |
| Aldosterone | Increases sodium and water retention, helping regulate blood pressure |
| Thyroid hormones (T3/T4) | Increase metabolic rate and support growth and development |
| Oxytocin | Supports uterine contractions and milk let-down |
| Antidiuretic hormone (ADH/vasopressin) | Promotes water reabsorption in the kidneys |
| Parathyroid hormone (PTH) | Raises blood calcium levels |
