# Waterborne disease risk by season (single setting over time)

| Season | Main disease concern | Typical trigger | Common prevention |
|---|---|---|---|
| Spring | Norovirus outbreaks | Crowded indoor settings | Hand hygiene, surface disinfection |
| Summer | Vibrio infections | Warm coastal water exposure | Avoid raw shellfish, protect wounds |
| Rainy season | Cholera spikes | Flooding and sewage contamination | Safe water, sanitation measures |
| Winter | Rotavirus (children) | Household transmission | Vaccination, hygiene |
