# Arthropod-borne disease burden table emphasizing cases and deaths

| Disease | Vector involved | Estimated cases per year | Estimated deaths per year | Endemic regions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Malaria | Anopheles mosquitoes | ~250 million | ~600,000 | Sub-Saharan Africa, parts of Asia |
| Dengue | Aedes mosquitoes | ~100 million | ~20,000 | Tropics and subtropics |
| Yellow fever | Aedes mosquitoes | ~200,000 | ~30,000 | Africa, South America |
| Filariasis | Mosquitoes (various genera) | ~120 million | low/rare | Tropical regions |
