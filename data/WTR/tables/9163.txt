# Infectious Encephalitis and Meningitis — Pathogens, Exposure Routes, and Prevention

| Condition | Typical pathogen | Exposure route | Seasonality/geography | Prevention |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Japanese encephalitis | Flavivirus | Mosquito-borne | Asia-Pacific | Vaccination, repellents |
| West Nile neuroinvasive disease | Flavivirus | Mosquito-borne | Americas/Europe | Mosquito control |
| St. Louis encephalitis | Flavivirus | Mosquito-borne | Americas | Repellents |
| Eastern equine encephalitis | Alphavirus | Mosquito-borne | Eastern US | Avoid dusk exposure |
| Tick-borne encephalitis | Flavivirus | Tick bites | Central/Eastern Europe | Vaccination |
| Meningococcal meningitis | Neisseria meningitidis | Droplets/close contact | Worldwide | Vaccination, prophylaxis |
| Pneumococcal meningitis | Streptococcus pneumoniae | Respiratory spread | Worldwide | Pneumococcal vaccine |
| Cryptococcal meningitis | Cryptococcus spp. | Environmental inhalation | Immunocompromised | HIV control, prophylaxis in select cases |
