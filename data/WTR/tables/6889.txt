# # Additives and preservatives found in foods often labeled as healthier

| Additive type | Example compound | Commonly found in | Why it’s used | Typical consumer concern |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Preservative | potassium sorbate | packaged “healthy” snacks | shelf life | “processed” perception |
| Sweetener | erythritol | protein bars | sweetness | gut tolerance |
| Stabilizer | xanthan gum | low-fat dressings | texture | sensitivity |
| Colorant | caramel color | flavored drinks | color | “artificial” label |
