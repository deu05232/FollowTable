# Country-Level Infectious Disease Risk Profile (Includes Food Waterborne and Person-to-Person Spread)

| Country | High-Risk Diseases (summary) | Primary Transmission Mentioned | Notable Animal Exposure Notes | Typical Prevention Advice |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| India | Cholera, Typhoid, Dengue, Rabies | Waterborne, vectorborne, animal bites | Stray dogs (rabies) | Avoid unsafe water; avoid animal bites |
| Brazil | Dengue, Yellow fever, Leptospirosis | Vectorborne, water contact | Rodents (leptospirosis) | Mosquito control; avoid floodwater |
| Nigeria | Malaria, Cholera, Lassa fever | Vectorborne, waterborne, person-to-person | Rodents (Lassa) | Bed nets; safe water |
| Thailand | Dengue, Hepatitis A, Rabies | Vectorborne, food/waterborne, animal bites | Dogs (rabies) | Vaccination; food hygiene |
| Kenya | Malaria, Schistosomiasis, Rabies | Vectorborne, water contact | Dogs/wildlife (rabies) | Avoid freshwater exposure |
| Egypt | Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Avian influenza | Food/waterborne, close contact | Poultry (avian influenza) | Food hygiene; avoid sick birds |
| Haiti | Cholera, Hepatitis A | Waterborne | Limited | Water sanitation |
| Russia | Tick-borne encephalitis, Influenza | Vectorborne, person-to-person | Ticks; wildlife reservoirs | Vaccination; avoid ticks |
