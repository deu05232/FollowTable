# Landmark Discoveries in Physics & Chemistry with Approximate Time Framing

| Approx. time (broad) | Discovery / discovered item | Discipline | Core finding (one item per row) | Typical evidence / method | Impact / context |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~2,500 years ago (Ancient Greece) | Atomism (matter made of tiny units) | Natural philosophy / Proto-chemistry | Proposal that matter is composed of indivisible particles | Philosophical reasoning about division of matter | Influenced later atomic theory even though not experimentally grounded at the time |
| ~1,000 years ago (medieval era) | Distillation refined for laboratory use | Chemistry (technique) | Reliable separation/purification using distillation apparatus | Repeated heating/condensation; apparatus design | Enabled systematic preparation of substances and reproducible experiments |
| ~350 years ago (17th century) | Gas pressure–volume relationship (Boyle’s law) | Physics / Chemistry | Inverse relationship between pressure and volume for gases (under conditions) | Controlled measurements with air pumps | Helped formalize gas behavior and quantitative experimental science |
| ~230 years ago (late 18th century) | Oxygen’s role in combustion (end of phlogiston theory) | Chemistry | Combustion and oxidation explained via oxygen | Careful mass balance experiments | Shifted chemistry to modern conservation-based explanations |
| ~160 years ago (mid-19th century) | Periodic pattern of elements | Chemistry | Elements show repeating properties when ordered by atomic characteristics | Comparative classification of known elements | Predicted missing elements and organized chemical knowledge |
| ~130 years ago (late 19th century) | Electron discovered | Physics | Existence of a negatively charged subatomic particle | Cathode-ray experiments | Launched modern atomic physics and electronics |
| ~110 years ago (early 20th century) | Atomic nucleus identified | Physics | Atom has a dense central nucleus | Scattering experiments | Enabled nuclear physics and new models of atomic structure |
| ~100 years ago | Quantum description of electrons in atoms | Physics / Chemistry | Quantized energy levels and wave-like behavior explain spectra | Spectroscopy + theoretical modeling | Explained periodicity and chemical bonding foundations |
| ~80 years ago (mid-20th century) | Controlled nuclear fission chain reaction | Nuclear physics | Self-sustaining fission reaction achieved | Reactor design; neutron moderation/control | Led to nuclear power and nuclear weapons; major societal implications |
| ~40 years ago (late 20th century) | High-temperature superconductivity | Condensed matter physics | Superconductivity at temperatures far above earlier materials | Materials synthesis + resistivity measurements | Revitalized superconductivity research and potential applications |
| ~20 years ago (early 21st century) | Graphene isolated as a stable 2D material | Materials science | Single-atom-thick carbon sheet with exceptional properties | Mechanical exfoliation + characterization | Opened 2D materials research and novel electronic/thermal applications |
