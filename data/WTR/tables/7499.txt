# Tourist Attractions in Ottawa (Capital of Canada) — Places to Visit

| Attraction | Type | Notes for Visitors |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Parliament Hill | Landmark / historic site | Iconic government precinct; tours and seasonal ceremonies |
| Rideau Canal | UNESCO / waterfront | Scenic promenades; boating in summer and skating route in winter |
| National Gallery of Canada | Art museum | Canadian and international art; notable architecture and exhibits |
| Canadian Museum of History (Gatineau) | Museum | Major national museum across the river with large galleries |
| ByWard Market | Market / neighborhood | Food stalls, shops, and lively streets near downtown |
| Canadian War Museum | Museum | Military history exhibits and memorial-focused galleries |
| Major’s Hill Park | Park / viewpoint | Green space with views toward Parliament and the river |
| Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica (Ottawa) | Church / architecture | Historic basilica known for ornate interior and stained glass |
| Dows Lake (part of the Rideau Canal system) | Waterfront / park | Paths and paddle rentals; popular for relaxed lakeside walks |
