# Dog boot size guide (includes only broad breed groups, not specific breeds)

| Size | Paw width range | Suggested dog type |
|---|---|---|
| XS | up to 1.6 in | Toy breeds |
| S | 1.6–2.1 in | Small terriers |
| M | 2.1–2.6 in | Medium mixed breeds |
| L | 2.6–3.1 in | Large working dogs |
| XL | 3.1–3.9 in | Giant breeds |
