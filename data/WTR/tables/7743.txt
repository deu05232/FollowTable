# Pregnancy safety categories for infection-related drugs (antifungals mixed with antibiotics and antivirals)

| Drug | Drug type | Common use | Pregnancy note (high-level) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fluconazole | Antifungal | Candidiasis | Use depends on dose/trimester and guidance |
| Amphotericin B | Antifungal | Severe systemic mycoses | Often considered when therapy is needed |
| Acyclovir | Antiviral | HSV infection | Used when indicated |
| Oseltamivir | Antiviral | Influenza | Used for treatment/prophylaxis when indicated |
| Amoxicillin | Antibiotic | Bacterial respiratory/skin infections | Commonly used when susceptible |
| Azithromycin | Antibiotic | Bacterial respiratory/STI infections | Option in selected cases |
| Metronidazole | Antibiotic/antiprotozoal | BV, trichomoniasis | Used when indicated |
| TMP-SMX | Antibiotic | UTI, PCP prophylaxis | Use depends on timing and indication |
