# # iPod models overview with capacities and form-factor notes

| Model line | Common capacity tiers | Physical form factor | Primary navigation method | Typical use case |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| iPod touch | 16GB / 32GB / 64GB | Slate, full front screen | Touch interface | Apps, games, streaming media |
| iPod nano | 8GB / 16GB | Small pocket device | Buttons / click wheel depending on era | Lightweight music and workouts |
| iPod classic | 80GB / 120GB / 160GB | Larger hard-drive style | Click wheel | Large offline music collection |
| iPod shuffle | 1GB / 2GB | Clip-on, screenless | Physical buttons | Minimalist playback |
