| Discovery | Exact Year | Field | Key Evidence/Method | Primary Location/Facility | Why It Mattered |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| First synthetic bacterial genome cell | 2010 | Synthetic biology | Genome synthesis + transplantation | J. Craig Venter Institute | Demonstrated programmable life systems |
