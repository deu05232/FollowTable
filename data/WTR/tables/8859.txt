# Skin infections overview (organized by infection, not by drug)

| Infection | Common cause | First-line treatment | Alternatives | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cellulitis | Streptococci, MSSA | Cephalexin | Clindamycin | Assess for abscess |
| Impetigo | Staph/Strep | Mupirocin topical | Cephalexin | Highly contagious |
| Folliculitis | Staphylococcus aureus | Topical antiseptics | Oral antibiotics if severe | Often self-limited |
| Paronychia | Mixed bacteria/fungi | Warm soaks + drainage | Antibiotics if needed | Consider Candida in chronic cases |
