# Meal-prep cost overview for healthier weekday lunches

| Homemade lunch (health-focused) | Key ingredients | Approx cost per serving | What drives the cost | Typical cheaper lunch alternative |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Brown rice bowl with vegetables | brown rice, frozen veg, soy sauce | $2–$4 | adding protein increases price | instant noodles |
| Turkey chili | ground turkey, beans, tomatoes | $3–$5 | lean meat cost | canned soup |
| Lentil salad | lentils, cucumbers, olive oil | $2–$3 | olive oil and fresh veg | deli sandwich |
| Greek yogurt + fruit | yogurt, banana/berries | $2–$4 | fruit choice changes cost | flavored pudding |
| Egg veggie scramble | eggs, spinach, onions | $2–$3 | fresh greens | breakfast pastry |
| Chickpea wrap | tortillas, chickpeas, hummus | $2–$4 | hummus/toppings | fast-food value wrap |
