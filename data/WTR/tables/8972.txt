# Ototoxicity and Adverse-Event Grading for Selected Drugs

| Drug | Drug Class | Ototoxicity (Hearing) | Vestibular Toxicity | CTCAE Grade Focus | Key Non-Pain Clinical Context |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cisplatin | Platinum chemotherapy | High (dose-related) | Possible | Grade 1–4 hearing loss | Solid tumors |
| Carboplatin | Platinum chemotherapy | Moderate | Rare | Grade 1–3 | Solid tumors |
| Gentamicin | Aminoglycoside antibiotic | Moderate–High | High | Grade 1–4 | Severe Gram-negative infections |
| Amikacin | Aminoglycoside antibiotic | High | Moderate | Grade 1–4 | Resistant infections |
| Vancomycin | Glycopeptide antibiotic | Low–Moderate | Low | Grade 1–3 | MRSA infections |
| Furosemide | Loop diuretic | Moderate (IV/high dose) | Low | Grade 1–3 | Heart failure/edema |
| Aspirin (high dose) | Salicylate | Reversible tinnitus | Low | Grade 1–2 | Anti-inflammatory/antiplatelet |
| Quinine | Antimalarial | Tinnitus common | Possible | Grade 1–3 | Malaria/leg cramps (off-label) |
