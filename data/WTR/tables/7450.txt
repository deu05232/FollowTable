# Household chemicals — storage and compatibility notes (no strength column)

| Substance commonly found at home | Main household use | Incompatible with | Storage guidance | Common hazards |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Bleach-based cleaners | Disinfection/whitening | Acids, ammonia products | Keep sealed, away from heat/light | Irritant, releases chlorine gas if mixed |
| Ammonia-based cleaners | Degreasing | Bleach products | Ventilated area, tightly closed | Respiratory irritant |
| Vinegar-based cleaners | Descaling | Bleach products | Normal cool storage | Mild irritant |
| Hydrogen peroxide solutions | Stain removal | Strong reducers, metals | Opaque container, cool place | Oxidizer |
| Drain openers (caustic) | Clog removal | Acids | Keep dry, avoid moisture | Corrosive burns |
