# Comparative Burden of Selected Infectious Diseases (Cases and Fatalities)

| Disease | Typical transmission | Estimated annual cases (global) | Estimated annual deaths (global) | Case fatality rate (approx.) | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Malaria | Mosquito-borne | 249,000,000 | 608,000 | 0.2% | Higher mortality in children under 5 |
| Tuberculosis | Airborne | 10,600,000 | 1,300,000 | 12% | Drug-resistant TB increases mortality |
| Seasonal influenza | Respiratory droplets | 1,000,000,000 | 290,000–650,000 | 0.03–0.07% | Burden varies by season and strain |
| Cholera | Water/foodborne | 1,300,000–4,000,000 | 21,000–143,000 | 1–3% (treated) | CFR can rise without rehydration |
| Measles | Airborne | 9,000,000 | 128,000 | 1–2% | Vaccine-preventable |
| Dengue | Mosquito-borne | 100,000,000–400,000,000 | 20,000–40,000 | <0.1% | Severe dengue is uncommon but dangerous |
| Ebola virus disease | Body fluids | 1,000–5,000 (outbreak years) | 500–3,000 | 25–70% | Highly variable by outbreak and care access |
| Rabies | Animal bites | 59,000 | 59,000 | ~100% | Nearly always fatal after symptom onset |
