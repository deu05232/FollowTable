# Breath biomarkers and associated conditions (includes infections but not exposure routes)

| Biomarker | Example associated conditions | Typical use context |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Carbon monoxide (CO) | Hemolysis, asthma exacerbation | Clinical monitoring |
| Hydrogen peroxide (H2O2) | Airway inflammation, asthma | Research marker |
| Acetone | Diabetes, fasting states | Metabolic assessment |
| Ammonia | Renal impairment | Diagnostic support |
| Nitrogen monoxide (NO) | Asthma, bronchiectasis | Inflammation tracking |
| Hydrogen cyanide (HCN) | Pseudomonas infection in cystic fibrosis | Infection indicator (not transmission) |
