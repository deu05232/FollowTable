# ERP systems pricing overview (subscription vs perpetual)

| ERP system | Pricing model | Typical starting price | Per-user price | Deployment | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| SAP S/4HANA Cloud | Subscription | Quote-based | Quote-based | Cloud | Pricing usually depends on edition and scope |
| Oracle NetSuite | Subscription | Quote-based | Quote-based | Cloud | Often priced by modules and users |
| Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance | Subscription | $180/user/month | $180/user/month | Cloud | Finance app subscription list price |
| Odoo Enterprise | Subscription | $31.10/user/month (Standard) | $31.10/user/month | Cloud/On-prem | Pricing varies by plan and billing term |
| Acumatica | Subscription | Quote-based | Not per-user | Cloud | Often sold by resource-based tiers |
| Epicor Kinetic | Subscription | Quote-based | Quote-based | Cloud/On-prem | Sold via partners; varies by edition |
| Sage X3 | Subscription | Quote-based | Quote-based | Cloud/On-prem | Typically based on users and modules |
