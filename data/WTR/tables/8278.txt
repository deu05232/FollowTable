| Condition | Incidence estimate |
|---|---|
| Urinary tract infection (adult) | approx 1 in 25 or 4.0% |
| Pyelonephritis | approx 1 in 1,000 or 0.10% |
| Kidney stones (symptomatic) | approx 1 in 200 or 0.50% |
| Interstitial cystitis | approx 1 in 1,250 or 0.08% |
