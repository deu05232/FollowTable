# Skin and soft tissue infections  treatments overview

| Infection | Treatment |
| --- | --- |
| Cellulitis | Antibiotics; elevation and pain control |
| Abscess | Incision and drainage; antibiotics in selected cases |
| Tinea corporis (ringworm) | Topical antifungal; oral antifungal for extensive disease |
| Necrotizing fasciitis | Urgent surgical debridement plus broad-spectrum antibiotics |
