# Fungal Infections

| Disease | Sporotrichosis | Chromoblastomycosis | Mycetoma |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Etiological agent | Sporothrix schenckii yeast mold | Fonsecaea pedrosoi or compacta, Wangiella dermatitidis | Pseudallescheria boydii, Madurella grisea or mycetomatis |
| Symptoms | Nodules and ulcers along lymphatics and at site of inoculation | warty nodules that progress to "cauliflower-like" appearance a inoculation site. | draining sinus tracts at site of inoculation |
| ID of organism | Yeast in tissue; mold at rm temp with "rosette pattern" | copper-colored spherical yeasts called "Medlar bodies" in tissue | white, brown, yellow or black granules in exudate that are fungal colonies |
