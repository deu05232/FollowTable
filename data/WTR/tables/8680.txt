# Common respiratory infections and typical treatments

| Infection | Typical treatment |
| --- | --- |
| Acute bacterial sinusitis | Antibiotics when bacterial features are present; otherwise symptomatic care |
| Community-acquired pneumonia | Empiric antibiotics (choice depends on severity and risk factors) |
| Influenza | Antiviral therapy when indicated; supportive care |
| Streptococcal pharyngitis | Antibiotics after confirmation; analgesics for symptoms |
