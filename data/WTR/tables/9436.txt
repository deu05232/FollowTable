# Lunar month checkpoints (phase progression with timing cues)

| Checkpoint in the lunar month | Typical phase name | How it relates to the repeating pattern |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Day 0 | New Moon | Cycle begins; Moon and Sun appear aligned |
| Days 1–6 | Waxing Crescent | Illumination increases night by night |
| Around Day 7 | First Quarter | Roughly one quarter of the month completed |
| Days 8–13 | Waxing Gibbous | Approaching the brightest night |
| Around Day 14–15 | Full Moon | Midpoint of the cycle |
| Days 16–21 | Waning Gibbous | Illumination decreases night by night |
| Around Day 22 | Third Quarter | Roughly three quarters through |
| Days 23–29 | Waning Crescent | Approaching the next restart |
