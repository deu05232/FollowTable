# Incidence estimates for selected respiratory infectious diseases (US)

| Disease | Incidence estimate | US people estimate | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Influenza (seasonal) | approx 1 in 9 or 11% per year | ~36,000,000 | Typical seasonal estimate |
| RSV infection (symptomatic) | approx 1 in 20 or 5% per year | ~16,500,000 | Population-wide symptomatic estimate |
| Pertussis (whooping cough) | approx 1 in 7,500 or 0.013% per year | ~44,000 | Reported cases based estimate |
| Legionnaires' disease | approx 1 in 25,000 or 0.004% per year | ~13,000 | Surveillance-based estimate |
| Measles | approx 1 in 3,000,000 or <0.0001% per year | ~110 | Outbreak-dependent estimate |
