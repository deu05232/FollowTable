# Broker Research Action Guide — Rating Meanings and Target-Price Framework

| Rating Label | Implied View (12M) | Typical Target Upside | Risk Note | Action Guidance | Review Frequency |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Strong Buy | Highest conviction | 20–40% | Higher volatility possible | Accumulate aggressively | Monthly |
| Buy | Positive | 10–25% | Normal | Add on weakness | Quarterly |
| Overweight | Above benchmark | 5–15% | Benchmark-relative | Hold/add vs sector | Quarterly |
| Hold | Neutral | -5% to +5% | Limited edge | Maintain position | Quarterly |
| Underweight | Below benchmark | -5% to -15% | Drawdown risk | Reduce exposure | Monthly |
| Sell | Negative | -15% to -30% | Elevated downside | Exit positions | Monthly |
| Not Rated | No coverage | N/A | Info gaps | Use other sources | Ad hoc |
