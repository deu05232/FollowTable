# Selected Space Science Discoveries and What They Revealed

| Discovery | Year reported | Observed by / source | What it changed our understanding of | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| First image of a black hole (M87*) | 2019 | Event Horizon Telescope | Confirmed that a black hole shadow can be imaged and matches predictions of strong gravity | Based on a global radio telescope array |
| Gravitational waves from a binary neutron-star merger | 2017 | LIGO/Virgo + electromagnetic follow-up | Linked neutron-star mergers to heavy-element production and multi-messenger astronomy | Enabled comparisons across signals |
| Evidence of water vapor in an exoplanet atmosphere (K2-18b) | 2019 | Space telescope observations | Showed that small planets can have detectable atmospheric signatures | Interpreted from spectral features |
