# Common hormone therapy drugs and what they do (medication actions, not natural hormone effects)

| Drug (generic) | Class | What it does | Typical use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tamoxifen | SERM | Blocks estrogen receptors in breast tissue | ER+ breast cancer |
| Anastrozole | Aromatase inhibitor | Reduces estrogen production | Postmenopausal ER+ breast cancer |
| Leuprolide | GnRH agonist | Suppresses LH/FSH after initial surge | Prostate cancer, endometriosis |
| Medroxyprogesterone | Progestin | Progestin effect; alters endometrium | Contraception, abnormal bleeding |
| Levothyroxine | Thyroid hormone replacement | Replaces deficient thyroid hormone | Hypothyroidism |
| Hydrocortisone | Glucocorticoid | Anti-inflammatory immunosuppression | Adrenal insufficiency, inflammation |
| Spironolactone | Aldosterone antagonist | Blocks mineralocorticoid receptor | Heart failure, hyperaldosteronism |
