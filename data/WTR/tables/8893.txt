# Endocrine axis signaling overview (releasing hormones and stimulating hormones)

| Target gland/hormone | Hypothalamic releasing hormone | Pituitary stimulating hormone | Downstream gland response |
|---|---|---|---|
| Thyroid (T3/T4) | TRH | TSH | Increases thyroid hormone release |
| Adrenal cortex (cortisol) | CRH | ACTH | Increases cortisol synthesis |
| Gonads (estrogen/testosterone) | GnRH | LH/FSH | Stimulates sex steroid production |
| Growth (IGF-1) | GHRH | GH | Promotes IGF-1 production |
| Lactation (prolactin) | Dopamine (inhibitory) | Prolactin | Supports milk production |
| Water balance (ADH) | Osmoreceptor input | (Posterior pituitary release) | Conserves water |
| Melatonin rhythm | SCN signals | (Pituitary-independent) | Regulates sleep timing |
