# Used Smartphone Buyer Checklist  Typical Wear, Repairs, and Risks by Component

| Component | What to Inspect on a Used Phone | Common Failure Symptoms | Quick Test | Typical Repair Cost Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Battery | Health %, swelling, charge cycles | Fast drain, sudden shutdown | Play video 10 min; check % drop | Medium | Battery health may be hidden on some Android skins |
| Display | Dead pixels, burn-in, touch response | Ghost touches, discoloration | Dialer test codes / touch grid apps | High | OLED burn-in is common on older flagships |
| Cameras | Focus, stabilization, dust | Blurry photos, shaking | Record 10s video + tap to focus | Medium-High | OIS can rattle after drops |
| Buttons | Power/volume feel | Double-clicks, stuck keys | Long-press, repeated presses | Low-Medium | Water damage often affects side keys |
| Charging Port | Fit, wobble, charging speed | Intermittent charge | Try known-good cable + wiggle | Low-Medium | Lint buildup can mimic port failure |
| Speakers/Mics | Distortion, low volume | Crackling, muffled calls | Voice memo + speakerphone test | Low | Earpiece mesh clog is common |
| Connectivity | Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, cellular | Drops, no SIM detection | Pair headset; run GPS lock | Medium | IMEI/SIM-lock issues are deal-breakers |
| Frame/Back | Bends, cracks, gaps | Creaks, loose back | Press edges; check seam uniformity | Low-High | Prior repair can leave gaps |
| Water Indicators | LCI color, corrosion | Random reboots | Inspect SIM tray LCI | — | Water indicators can be removed/hidden |
| Software Lock | Activation lock, FRP | Can’t sign in/reset | Factory reset + setup | — | Avoid if seller won’t unlock fully |
