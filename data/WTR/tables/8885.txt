# Student dining survey  price perceptions of healthier cafeteria options

| Campus dining item | Considered a healthier choice? | Typical price range | Student price perception | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Salad bar (per bowl) | Yes | $7–$10 | Expensive | Often costs more than hot entrées |
| Grilled chicken + vegetables plate | Yes | $9–$12 | Expensive | Priced above pasta specials |
| Veggie wrap | Yes | $8–$10 | Somewhat expensive | Add-ons raise the final price |
| Fresh fruit cup | Yes | $3–$5 | Expensive | Small portion for the price |
| Water or unsweetened tea | Yes | $1–$3 | Affordable | Cheaper than specialty drinks |
| Daily pizza slice | No | $3–$4 | Affordable | Common low-cost fallback |
| Fries (side) | No | $2–$4 | Affordable | Often bundled in meal deals |
