# Antibiotics for common infections (drug-focused quick reference)

| Drug | Drug class | Typical infections treated | Route | Key cautions |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Amoxicillin | Penicillin antibiotic | Otitis media, sinusitis, strep pharyngitis | Oral | Adjust dose in renal impairment; allergy risk |
| Doxycycline | Tetracycline antibiotic | Acne, chlamydia, tick-borne infections | Oral | Avoid in pregnancy/young children; photosensitivity |
| Nitrofurantoin | Urinary antiseptic | Uncomplicated UTI | Oral | Avoid at term pregnancy and in severe renal impairment |
| Fluconazole | Azole antifungal | Vulvovaginal candidiasis, candidemia | Oral/IV | Drug interactions; dose adjust in renal impairment |
| Acyclovir | Antiviral | HSV infections, VZV infections | Oral/IV | Dose adjust in renal impairment; ensure hydration |
