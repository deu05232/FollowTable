# Hormone-related drugs and their actions

| Drug (generic) | Target pathway | Action description |
|---|---|---|
| Tamoxifen | Estrogen receptor | Selective estrogen receptor modulator (anti-estrogen in breast tissue) |
| Fulvestrant | Estrogen receptor | Estrogen receptor antagonist/downregulator |
| Levothyroxine | Thyroid hormone receptor | Hormone replacement to increase thyroid hormone activity |
| Spironolactone | Mineralocorticoid receptor | Aldosterone antagonist (blocks receptor signaling) |
| Desmopressin | Vasopressin receptor | ADH analog that increases water reabsorption |
