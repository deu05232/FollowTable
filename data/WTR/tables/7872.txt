# Common OTC Non-Opioid Analgesics (NSAIDs) and Brand Names

| Generic name | Common brand names | Typical use | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ibuprofen | Advil, Motrin | Mild to moderate pain, fever | NSAID; take with food if GI upset |
| Naproxen | Aleve, Naprosyn | Musculoskeletal pain, cramps | Longer-acting NSAID |
| Diclofenac | Voltaren (gel/tablet) | Joint and soft-tissue pain | Often used topically for localized pain |
| Ketorolac | Toradol | Short-term moderate to severe pain | NSAID; usually short duration |
| Meloxicam | Mobic | Arthritis-related pain | Prescription NSAID |
| Celecoxib | Celebrex | Pain/inflammation with lower GI risk for some | COX-2 selective NSAID |
