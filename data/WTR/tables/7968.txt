# Ibanez guitars — fretboard radius reference across common lines

| Ibanez line (category) | Typical fingerboard radius | Notes about setup |
| --- | --- | --- |
| RG-style | 16 in | Often paired with low action setups |
| S-style | 16 in | Similar feel to RG in many cases |
| AZ-style | 12 in | More rounded feel compared to 16 in |
| Artcore-style | 12 in | Common on many hollow/semi-hollow electrics |
