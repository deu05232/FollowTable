# Topical antifungal options and the fungal conditions they target

| Active ingredient | Common product form | Fungal condition | Typical site | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Clotrimazole | Cream | Tinea corporis (ringworm) | Trunk/limbs | OTC in many regions |
| Miconazole | Cream/spray | Tinea pedis (athlete’s foot) | Feet | Often used for interdigital disease |
| Terbinafine | Cream/gel | Tinea cruris (jock itch) | Groin | Short-course regimens exist |
| Ketoconazole | Shampoo | Seborrheic dermatitis (Malassezia-related) | Scalp | Also used for tinea versicolor |
| Nystatin | Cream/ointment | Cutaneous candidiasis | Skin folds | Targets Candida rather than dermatophytes |
| Ciclopirox | Nail lacquer | Mild onychomycosis | Nails | Long treatment duration |
| Econazole | Cream | Tinea versicolor | Trunk | Useful for superficial yeast infections |
