# Top Tourist Attractions in Canberra (Australia's Capital City)

| Attraction | Type | Why Visit (One-line) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Australian War Memorial | Museum / Memorial | National military history museum and memorial with exhibitions and ceremonies |
| Parliament House | Government landmark | Guided tours, architecture, and views over the Parliamentary Triangle |
| National Gallery of Australia | Art museum | Major collection of Australian, Indigenous, and international art |
| National Museum of Australia | History museum | Exhibits on Australia’s people, places, and stories on Lake Burley Griffin |
| Lake Burley Griffin | Waterfront / parkland | Walks, cycling loops, kayaking, and skyline viewpoints |
| Questacon – National Science and Technology Centre | Science museum | Hands-on interactive science exhibits popular for families |
| Mount Ainslie Lookout | Viewpoint | Panoramic views of Canberra’s planned layout and key monuments |
| Australian National Botanic Gardens | Gardens | Native flora, rainforest gully, and scenic trails near Black Mountain |
| National Library of Australia | Cultural site | Exhibitions and iconic reading rooms; often hosts rotating displays |
