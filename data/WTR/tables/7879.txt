# Non-Opioid Pain Reliever Classes With Examples

| Analgesic class | What it targets | Example generics | Example brands |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nonselective NSAIDs | Pain + inflammation | ibuprofen, naproxen | Advil, Aleve |
| COX-2 selective NSAIDs | Pain + inflammation | celecoxib | Celebrex |
| Topical NSAIDs | Localized inflammatory pain | diclofenac (topical) | Voltaren Gel |
| Salicylates (analgesic doses) | Pain + fever | aspirin | Bayer Aspirin |
| Acetic acid NSAIDs | Pain + inflammation | indomethacin, ketorolac | Indocin, Toradol |
| Oxicam NSAIDs | Chronic inflammatory pain | meloxicam, piroxicam | Mobic, Feldene |
