# Stroke Prevention and Cardiovascular Medicines (Often Confused with Pain Relievers)

| Medication (main entry) | Drug Type | Main Use | Brand/Other Names | Why It Is Not a Typical Painkiller |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Clopidogrel | Antiplatelet | Stroke/MI prevention | Plavix | Not used for analgesia |
| Warfarin | Anticoagulant | Prevent blood clots | Coumadin | Bleeding risk; not analgesic |
| Dabigatran | Anticoagulant | Atrial fibrillation clot prevention | Pradaxa | Not pain relief |
| Atorvastatin | Statin | Cholesterol lowering | Lipitor | No direct pain relief role |
| Losartan | ARB | Hypertension | Cozaar | Blood pressure control only |
| Alteplase | Thrombolytic | Acute clot dissolution | Activase (tPA) | Emergency clot-buster, not pain relief |
| Dipyridamole | Antiplatelet | Stroke prevention adjunct | Persantine | Not an analgesic |
