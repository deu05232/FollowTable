# Rabies risk level by country (travel health summary)

| Country | Rabies risk level | Common exposure setting | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| India | High | Stray dog bites | Pre-exposure vaccine often recommended for long stays |
| Thailand | Moderate | Dog/monkey bites | Risk higher in rural areas |
| Mexico | Low to moderate | Dog bites | Risk varies by region |
| United States | Low | Bat exposure | Human cases are rare |
| Tanzania | High | Dog bites | Limited access to post-exposure prophylaxis in some areas |
