# ERP Total Cost Breakdown by Deployment Model (Subscription, Implementation, and Ongoing Support)

| Cost Component | Cloud ERP (SMB, 25–100 users) | Cloud ERP (Mid-market, 100–500 users) | On-Prem ERP (Mid-market, 100–500 users) | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Software subscription / license | $75–$180 per user/month | $90–$220 per user/month | $1,200–$3,500 per named user (one-time) | Subscription is recurring; on‑prem is upfront licensing.
| Minimum platform fee | $1,000–$4,000 per month | $3,000–$12,000 per month | $0 | Some vendors enforce minimum monthly spend.
| Implementation services (one-time) | $30,000–$150,000 | $150,000–$900,000 | $250,000–$1,500,000 | Depends on modules, integrations, data migration.
| Data migration (one-time) | $5,000–$40,000 | $25,000–$150,000 | $25,000–$200,000 | Often priced by complexity and number of legacy systems.
| Integrations (one-time) | $10,000–$60,000 | $50,000–$350,000 | $60,000–$500,000 | API/EDI, WMS, CRM, payroll, etc.
| Training & change management (one-time) | $5,000–$30,000 | $20,000–$150,000 | $20,000–$200,000 | Includes role-based training and adoption support.
| Infrastructure / hosting | Included | Included | $20,000–$120,000 per year | On‑prem: servers, DB, backups, DR, monitoring.
| Annual maintenance & support | Included or $0–$15,000 per year add-on | Included or $0–$60,000 per year add-on | 18%–25% of license value per year | On‑prem maintenance often mandatory.
| Typical Year-1 total (illustrative) | $60k–$280k | $250k–$1.4M | $450k–$2.3M | Sum of software + services; ranges overlap by scope.
| Typical Year-2+ recurring | $25k–$220k per year | $120k–$1.2M per year | $120k–$700k per year | Subscription or maintenance + infra + support.
