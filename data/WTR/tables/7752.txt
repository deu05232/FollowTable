# # Hormone therapy drugs and their actions

| Drug (used in hormone therapy) | Target pathway | Action/Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Tamoxifen | Estrogen receptor | Anti-estrogen (selective estrogen receptor modulator) |
| Toremifene | Estrogen receptor | Anti-estrogen |
| Fulvestrant | Estrogen receptor | Estrogen receptor antagonist/degrader |
| Anastrozole | Aromatase | Lowers estrogen production by inhibiting aromatase |
