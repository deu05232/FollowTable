# Feeding Value of Cereal Grains for Livestock (as-fed basis)

| Cereal Grain | Typical Use | Metabolizable Energy (MJ/kg) | Crude Protein (%) | Crude Fiber (%) | Palatability | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|---|---|
| Maize (corn) | Poultry, swine | 13.5 | 8.5 | 2.2 | High | Energy-dense feed |
| Barley | Cattle, sheep | 12.0 | 11.0 | 5.0 | Medium | Risk of acidosis if overfed |
| Oats | Horses | 11.5 | 12.0 | 10.0 | High | Higher fiber, lower energy |
| Wheat | Swine | 13.0 | 12.5 | 2.5 | Medium | Fine grinding recommended |
| Sorghum | Cattle | 12.5 | 10.0 | 2.8 | Medium | Tannins may reduce intake |
| Rye | Cattle | 12.0 | 10.5 | 3.0 | Low–Medium | Anti-nutritional factors |
| Rice bran | Cattle | 11.8 | 13.0 | 7.0 | Medium | Higher fat, can go rancid |
