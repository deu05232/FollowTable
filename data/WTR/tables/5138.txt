# Birth Control Pills - Birth Control Pill - The Pill

| What Happened | I took 1 hormone pill late. (Less than 1 day since I should have taken my pill.) | I missed 1 hormone pill. (One to less than 2 days since I should have taken my pill.) | I missed 2 or more hormone pills in a row. (2 or more days since I should have taken a pill) | I missed 2 or more hormone pills in a row. (2 or more days since I should have taken a pill) | I missed reminder pills. (It doesn't matter how many.) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| When in the pill pack? | Anytime. | Anytime. | Week 1 or 2 | Week 3 | Week 4 |
| What should I do? | Take the late pill as soon as possible. Take the rest of your pills at the usual time. This means you may take 2 pills in 1 day. | Take the missed pill as soon as possible. Take the rest of your pills at the usual time. This means you may take 2 pills in 1 day. | Take the most recent missed pill as soon as possible. Throw away any other missed pills. Keep taking the remaining pills in your pack, even if you take 2 pills in 1 day. | Take the most recent missed pill as soon as possible. Throw away any other missed pills. Finish the rest of the hormone pills in your pack, then start a new pack the next day. This means you will skip the reminder pills. | Throw away. Take the next reminder pill at your usual time. |
| Do I need to use back-up birth control or not have sex? | No. | No. | Yes, for 7 days. | Yes, for 7 days. | No. |
