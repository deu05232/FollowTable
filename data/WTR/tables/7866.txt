# Analgesics and Safety Monitoring Checklist (NSAIDs + Opioids + Toxicity Focus)

| Medication | Category | Brand example | Key risk to monitor | Example monitoring item |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| naproxen | NSAID | Aleve | GI bleeding/ulcer | Ask about melena; consider PPI |
| ketorolac | NSAID | Toradol | Renal injury | Serum creatinine; limit duration |
| diclofenac | NSAID | Voltaren | Hepatotoxicity | ALT/AST if prolonged use |
| aspirin | NSAID/antiplatelet | Ecotrin | Bleeding | Bruising, hemoglobin trends |
| acetaminophen | Non-NSAID analgesic | Tylenol | Liver toxicity | Total daily dose; alcohol use |
| tramadol | Opioid-like analgesic | Ultram | Dependence/serotonin syndrome | Review SSRI/SNRI co-use |
| oxycodone | Opioid | OxyContin | Respiratory depression | Sedation scale; naloxone access |
| prednisone | Corticosteroid | Deltasone | Hyperglycemia, infection | Glucose checks; infection screening |
