# Generic Apparel Size Labels (No Measurement Chart)

| Size Label | Fit Description | Typical Shopper Guess | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| XXS | Very tight | "Petite" | Brand-dependent |
| XS | Tight | "Small adult" | May run short |
| S | Regular | "Average" | Often overlaps with XS/M |
| M | Regular | "Medium" | Most common default |
| L | Relaxed | "Large" | Can vary widely |
| XL | Very relaxed | "Oversized" | Sometimes equals 1X |
| XXL | Extra oversized | "Big" | Not standardized |
