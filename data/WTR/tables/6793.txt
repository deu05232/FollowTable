# Electric guitar bridge types and what they do (examples)

| Bridge type | Commonly seen on | Why players choose it |
|---|---|---|
| Floyd Rose style | Ibanez, Jackson, ESP | Stable tuning for heavy tremolo use |
| Hardtail fixed | Ibanez, Fender, PRS | Simpler setup and strong sustain |
| Vintage tremolo | Fender-style guitars | Classic feel and tone |
