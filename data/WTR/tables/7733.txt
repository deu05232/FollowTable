# Antifungal therapy mixed with antibacterial therapy for skin infections (combined reference)

| Condition | Likely cause | First-line medication | Alternative medication | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tinea pedis | Fungus (dermatophytes) | Terbinafine cream | Clotrimazole cream | Keep feet dry |
| Intertrigo with Candida | Yeast (Candida) | Nystatin cream | Clotrimazole cream | Address moisture/friction |
| Impetigo | Bacteria (Staph/Strep) | Mupirocin ointment | Cephalexin (oral) | Contagious in children |
| Cellulitis | Bacteria (Strep/Staph) | Cephalexin (oral) | Clindamycin (oral) | Seek care if systemic symptoms |
| Paronychia | Bacteria or yeast | Warm soaks + topical antibiotic | Topical azole if yeast suspected | Drain abscess if needed |
| Folliculitis | Bacteria | Benzoyl peroxide wash | Doxycycline (oral) | Consider MRSA risk |
