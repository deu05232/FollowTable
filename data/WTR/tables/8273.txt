# Common Conditions Compared by Typical Treatments (with Occasional Epidemiology Notes)

| Condition | First-line treatment | Second-line treatment | Typical course | Incidence/Prevalence note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hypertension | Lifestyle changes; thiazide diuretics | ACE inhibitor/ARB | Chronic, often asymptomatic | Common (rate not specified) |
| Type 2 diabetes mellitus | Metformin; diet/weight loss | GLP-1 RA/SGLT2 inhibitor | Chronic; progressive without control | Increasing worldwide (no numeric rate) |
| Asthma | Inhaled corticosteroid | LABA add-on | Variable; episodic exacerbations | Frequent in children (no % given) |
| Migraine | NSAIDs/triptans | CGRP antagonist | Recurrent attacks | Very common (no numeric) |
| Major depressive disorder | SSRIs; psychotherapy | SNRIs/augmentation | Episodic; recurrent | High lifetime prevalence (not quantified) |
| Osteoarthritis | Exercise/weight loss; NSAIDs | Intra-articular steroids | Slowly progressive | Common in older adults (no rate) |
| GERD | PPIs | H2 blockers | Chronic/relapsing | Seen often in clinics (no numeric) |
| Seasonal allergic rhinitis | Antihistamines | Intranasal steroids | Seasonal | Common condition (no % provided) |
