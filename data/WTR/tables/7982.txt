# Clean-Eating Grocery List  Ingredients, Additives to Avoid, and Health Claims (No Pricing)

| Item Category | Example Product | Common Additives Mentioned | Label/Claim Often Seen | Why Considered 'Healthy' | Where Typically Found |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Yogurt | Flavored low-fat yogurt | Artificial flavors, carrageenan | "Low fat" | Lower fat content | Supermarkets |
| Bread | Multigrain sandwich bread | Calcium propionate, DATEM | "Whole grain" | Added grains | Grocery stores |
| Deli meat | Turkey slices | Sodium nitrite/nitrate | "Lean" | Lower calories per serving | Deli counters |
| Snack bars | Protein bar | Sugar alcohols, soy lecithin | "High protein" | Higher protein | Convenience stores |
| Salad dressing | Light ranch | Xanthan gum, preservatives | "Light" | Reduced calories | Supermarkets |
| Juice | Shelf-stable orange juice | Ascorbic acid | "Vitamin C" | Added vitamin claim | Grocery aisles |
| Plant milk | Almond beverage | Emulsifiers, stabilizers | "Dairy-free" | Lactose-free | Supermarkets |
| Cereal | Bran flakes | BHT/BHA (in packaging), added color | "Fiber" | Higher fiber | Grocery stores |
