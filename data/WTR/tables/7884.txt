# Secondary Stroke Prevention Medications (Antiplatelets, Anticoagulants, Statins, and Antihypertensives)

| Category | Generic Name | Brand Name(s) | Primary Indication | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Antiplatelet | Clopidogrel | Plavix | Prevent recurrent stroke/MI | Bleeding risk; not an analgesic |
| Antiplatelet | Aspirin (low-dose) | Bayer Low Dose, Ecotrin | Stroke prevention | Sometimes used for pain at higher doses |
| Antiplatelet | Dipyridamole + aspirin (ER) | Aggrenox | Stroke prevention | Headache common |
| Anticoagulant | Apixaban | Eliquis | Atrial fibrillation stroke prevention | Not for pain |
| Anticoagulant | Warfarin | Coumadin, Jantoven | Thromboembolism prevention | INR monitoring |
| Statin | Atorvastatin | Lipitor | LDL reduction after stroke/TIA | Muscle aches possible |
| Antihypertensive | Losartan | Cozaar | Blood pressure control | Kidney function monitoring |
| Thrombolytic (acute) | Alteplase | Activase (tPA) | Acute ischemic stroke | Time-critical therapy |
