# Cat nutrition guide by life stage (focuses on feeding details, not lifespan outcomes)

| Life stage | Typical daily feeding approach | Protein/fat emphasis | Common advice focus |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kitten | Multiple small meals | Higher energy | Growth support |
| Adult | Scheduled meals | Balanced | Weight maintenance |
| Senior | Smaller, digestible meals | Moderate | Kidney-friendly options |
