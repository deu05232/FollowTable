# Major world religions (overview by religion)

| Religion | Origin region | Approx. followers | Core idea (very short) | Major branches | Common symbols/notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Christianity | Levant / Roman Empire | Global | Jesus as Messiah; salvation | Catholic, Protestant, Orthodox | Cross |
| Islam | Arabian Peninsula | Global | Submission to God; Qur'an | Sunni, Shia | Crescent/star (often), mosque |
| Hinduism | Indian subcontinent | Global | Dharma, karma, moksha | Vaishnavism, Shaivism, Shaktism | Om, many deities |
| Buddhism | South Asia | Global | End suffering via enlightenment | Theravada, Mahayana, Vajrayana | Dharma wheel |
| Judaism | Levant | Global | Covenant with God; Torah | Orthodox, Conservative, Reform | Star of David |
| Sikhism | Punjab (South Asia) | Global | One God; teachings of Gurus | Khalsa tradition (within Sikh community) | Khanda |
