| Food products that contain the additive | Sodium nitrate is used in corned beef, packaged pork products, luncheon meats, smoked fish and other processed meats. | Potassium Bromate is used in bread products to increase their volume. | butylated hydroxytoluene (BHT) and Butylated hydroxyanisole (BHA) are used to preserve oils and prevent them from becoming rancid. They are generally used in vegetable oils as well as cereals and potato chips. | Propyl Gallate is also used to prevent oils from going rancid. It can also be found in meat products, potato chips, and chicken soup powder. | Acesulfame-K is an artificial sweetener and is used in soft drinks, chewing gum, and baked food products. | Aspartame is an artificial sweetener that is often used in diet sodas and other diet foods. | Olestra is a synthetic fat that is used in potato chips and crackers. | Hydrogenated vegetable oil can be found in cookies, bread, and salad dressings. | Food colorings- Yellow 6, Red 3, Green 3, Blue 1 and 2 can be found in candy, beverages, and gelatin products. |
| Risks of consuming the additive | Sodium Nitrate has been linked to cancer in humans. | Potassium Bromate has been identified as a carcinogen in animals. In fact, it is banned in almost every country, except for the US and Japan. | BHA and BHT research has shown that they cause cancer in rats. | Propyl Gallate is suspected of causing cancer. | Acesulfame-K research shows that there is a probability that it is a cancer causing agent. When it is broken down, it forms acetoacetamide which affects thyroid functioning in rats and rabbits. | Aspartame has been found to cause brain tumors in rats and even a small dose increased the incidence of leukemia in rats. | Olestra can cause relatively minor problems like diarrhea and abdominal cramps but it also reduces the body's ability to absorb essential fat-soluble nutrients. | Hydrogenated vegetable oil contains trans fats which promote diabetes and heart disease. | These coloring agents have been linked to health problems such as brain tumors, kidney tumors, and cancer |
