# Artificial Sweeteners  Reported Risks, Complaints, and Regulatory Notes (Minimal Nutrition)

| Sweetener | Calories (per packet) | Relative Sweetness (vs sugar) | Reported Symptoms | Controversial Claims | Regulatory/Warning Notes | Typical User Complaints |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|---|---|
| Aspartame | 0–4 | ~200x | Headache, dizziness | “Neurotoxic”, “linked to seizures” | Avoid in PKU | “Aftertaste”, “migraine trigger” |
| Sucralose | 0 | ~600x | Bloating, GI discomfort | “Harms gut bacteria” | Heat stability debated | “Chemical taste” |
| Saccharin | 0 | ~300x | Nausea | “Cancer risk in rats” | Pregnancy caution (some sources) | “Metallic taste” |
| Acesulfame-K | 0 | ~200x | Nausea | “May affect insulin response” | Often blended with others | “Too sweet” |
| Stevia extract | 0 | ~200–300x | Nausea (rare) | “Hormone effects” (disputed) | Quality varies by brand | “Licorice note” |
| Monk fruit | 0 | ~150–200x | null common | “Candida-safe” (marketing) | Often mixed with erythritol | “Cooling sensation” |
| Erythritol | 0 | ~70x | Gas, diarrhea at high dose | “Blood clot risk” (debated) | Laxation threshold varies | “Stomach rumbling” |
