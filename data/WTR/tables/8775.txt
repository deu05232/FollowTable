# Veterinary vaccination schedule for cats (age-based care plan, not lifespan)

| Life stage | Core vaccines commonly recommended | Typical timing pattern | Follow-up focus |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kitten series | FVRCP | Every 3–4 weeks | Build immunity |
| First-year booster | Rabies, FVRCP | Around 1 year | Confirm protection |
| Adult | Rabies, FVRCP | Every 1–3 years | Maintain protection |
| Senior | Risk-based boosters | Varies by health and exposure | Monitor chronic issues |
