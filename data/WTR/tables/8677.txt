# Antibiotic options used in infections (drug-focused list)

| Drug | Common use in infections |
| --- | --- |
| Amoxicillin-clavulanate | Used for a variety of bacterial infections |
| Azithromycin | Used for certain respiratory and atypical infections |
| Levofloxacin | Used for susceptible bacterial infections |
| Clindamycin | Used for some skin/soft tissue infections |
