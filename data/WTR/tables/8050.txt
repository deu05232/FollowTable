# ICD-10 Cause-of-Death Glossary (Selected Human Conditions)

| Term | Abbreviation | ICD-10 Code Range | Plain-language definition | Inclusion notes | Exclusion notes | Example wording on death certificate |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Ischemic heart disease | IHD | I20–I25 | Reduced blood flow to the heart muscle | Includes myocardial infarction | Excludes congenital heart disease | "Acute MI" |
| Cerebrovascular disease | CVD | I60–I69 | Disorders of blood vessels in the brain (e.g., stroke) | Includes ischemic & hemorrhagic stroke | Excludes traumatic brain injury | "CVA" |
| Chronic lower respiratory disease | CLRD | J40–J47 | Chronic lung/airway disorders | Includes COPD and chronic bronchitis | Excludes acute infections | "COPD" |
| Malignant neoplasm | CA | C00–C97 | Cancerous tumor growth | Includes all primary malignancies | Excludes benign neoplasms | "Metastatic cancer" |
| Diabetes mellitus | DM | E10–E14 | Disorders of glucose regulation | Includes type 1 and type 2 | Excludes gestational diabetes | "Type 2 DM" |
| Influenza and pneumonia | Flu/PNA | J09–J18 | Viral influenza and lung infection | Includes bacterial pneumonia | Excludes aspiration pneumonitis | "Pneumonia" |
| Septicemia | Sepsis | A40–A41 | Bloodstream infection with systemic response | Includes streptococcal sepsis | Excludes localized infections only | "Septic shock" |
| Nephritis/nephrosis | Renal dz | N00–N07, N17–N19 | Kidney inflammation or failure syndromes | Includes chronic kidney failure | Excludes kidney stones | "ESRD" |
| Alzheimer's disease | AD | G30 | Neurodegenerative dementia | Includes early/late onset | Excludes vascular dementia | "Alzheimer's" |
