# Risk Factors and Causes Associated With Selected Conditions (No Incidence Rates)

| Condition | Major risk factors | Suspected causes/mechanisms | Modifiable? | Strength of association |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Coronary artery disease | Smoking, hypertension, dyslipidemia | Atherosclerosis | Yes | Strong |
| Lung cancer | Smoking, radon exposure | DNA damage, oncogenic mutations | Partly | Strong |
| Type 2 diabetes mellitus | Obesity, inactivity, family history | Insulin resistance, beta-cell dysfunction | Yes | Strong |
| Osteoporosis | Age, low BMI, glucocorticoids | Bone resorption > formation | Partly | Moderate |
| COPD | Smoking, occupational dust | Chronic airway inflammation | Yes | Strong |
| Stroke (ischemic) | Atrial fibrillation, hypertension | Thromboembolism | Yes | Strong |
| Peptic ulcer disease | NSAIDs, H. pylori | Mucosal injury, acid exposure | Yes | Strong |
| Cervical cancer | High-risk HPV | Viral oncogenesis | Yes (via vaccination/screening) | Strong |
