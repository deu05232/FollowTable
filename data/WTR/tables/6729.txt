# Religions compared by key characteristics (entity-per-row)

| Religion | Founded (approx.) | Primary texts | Worship place | View of afterlife (very brief) | Geographic concentration |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Christianity | 1st century CE | Bible | Church | Heaven/hell (varies) | Americas, Europe, Sub-Saharan Africa |
| Islam | 7th century CE | Qur'an, Hadith | Mosque | Paradise/hell | Middle East, North Africa, South & Southeast Asia |
| Buddhism | 5th century BCE | Tripitaka and later sutras | Temple/monastery | Rebirth; nirvana | East & Southeast Asia |
| Hinduism | Ancient | Vedas, Upanishads, epics | Temple/shrine | Rebirth; moksha | India, Nepal |
| Judaism | Ancient | Tanakh, Talmud | Synagogue | Varies by tradition | Israel, United States |
| Shinto | Ancient | Kojiki/Nihon Shoki (texts), rituals | Shrine | Focus on this world; ancestor/spirits | Japan |
