# Hormone synthesis enzymes and genes involved in steroidogenesis (with brief notes)

| Enzyme/Gene | Common name | Role in synthesis | Typical tissue |
|---|---|---|---|
| CYP11A1 | P450scc (desmolase) | Converts cholesterol to pregnenolone | Adrenal, gonads |
| CYP17A1 | 17α-hydroxylase/17,20-lyase | Produces androgen precursors | Adrenal, gonads |
| HSD3B2 | 3β-HSD | Converts pregnenolone to progesterone | Adrenal, gonads |
| CYP21A2 | 21-hydroxylase | Produces cortisol/aldosterone intermediates | Adrenal |
| CYP11B1 | 11β-hydroxylase | Produces cortisol | Adrenal |
| CYP19A1 | Aromatase | Converts androgens to estrogens | Gonads, fat |
| SRD5A2 | 5α-reductase | Converts testosterone to DHT | Prostate, skin |
