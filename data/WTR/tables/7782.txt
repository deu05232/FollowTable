# Religious Teachings Compared Across World Religions (Doctrinal, not composition)

| Religion | Core scripture(s) | View of afterlife (summary) | Dietary rules (summary) | Clergy structure | Major holidays |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Christianity | Bible | Heaven/hell; salvation emphasized | Varies by denomination | Priests/pastors (varies) | Easter, Christmas |
| Islam | Qur'an, Hadith | Paradise/hell; judgment day | Halal; fasting in Ramadan | Imams/scholars (varies) | Ramadan, Eid al-Fitr |
| Hinduism | Vedas, Upanishads, epics | Reincarnation; moksha | Often vegetarian norms | Diverse gurus/priests | Diwali, Holi |
| Buddhism | Tripitaka (varies) | Rebirth; nirvana | Often vegetarian tendencies | Monastic sangha | Vesak |
| Judaism | Tanakh, Talmud | Varied; world-to-come | Kosher | Rabbis | Passover, Yom Kippur |
| Sikhism | Guru Granth Sahib | Reincarnation; liberation | No halal/kosher; some restrictions | Granthi | Vaisakhi |
| Shinto | Kojiki/Nihon Shoki (traditions) | Ancestor veneration; varied | Few formal rules | Shrine priests | New Year, matsuri |
