# Migraine ER Medications (Mixed  Non-Opioid Analgesics + Antiemetics + Opioids)

| Medication group | Example drugs | Main role in migraine care |
|---|---|---|
| NSAIDs (non-opioid analgesics) | ketorolac, diclofenac | Pain relief |
| Triptans | sumatriptan | Abort migraine attack |
| Antiemetics | metoclopramide, prochlorperazine | Nausea control / adjunct |
| Opioids | tramadol, meperidine | Rescue analgesia (not preferred) |
| Ergots | dihydroergotamine | Abort migraine attack |
| Adjuncts | diphenhydramine | Side-effect management |
