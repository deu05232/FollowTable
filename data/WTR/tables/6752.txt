# Nutrition Concepts Cheat Sheet  Macronutrients and Their Roles

| Concept | Definition | Why it matters | Typical sources | Quick tip |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Calories | Unit of energy | Weight maintenance/gain/loss | All foods | Track weekly average |
| Protein | Amino-acid building blocks | Muscle repair, satiety | Meat, dairy, legumes | Aim per meal |
| Carbohydrates | Sugars + starch + fiber | Training fuel, gut health | Grains, fruit, beans | Prefer higher fiber |
| Dietary Fiber | Non-digestible carbs | Gut health, cholesterol | Whole grains, veg | Increase gradually |
| Total Fat | Lipids in food | Hormones, absorption | Oils, nuts, fish | Balance types |
| Saturated Fat | Fat type | Heart health marker | Butter, fatty meats | Limit frequency |
| Sodium | Mineral electrolyte | Blood pressure | Processed foods | Compare per serving |
| Micronutrients | Vitamins/minerals | Metabolism, immunity | Varied diet | Rotate food variety |
