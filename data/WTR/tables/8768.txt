# Cat lifespan ranges across common breeds (typical pet conditions)

| Breed group | Typical lifespan (years) | Size/Build tendency | Common context |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Siamese | 12–16 | Medium, lean | Often indoor |
| Maine Coon | 10–13 | Large | Needs heart screening |
| Persian | 12–17 | Medium | Indoor, grooming-heavy |
| Ragdoll | 12–15 | Large | Indoor companion |
| Sphynx | 12–14 | Medium | Indoor, skin care |
