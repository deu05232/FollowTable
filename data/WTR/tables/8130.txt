# Exposure-Driven Infectious Disease Risks by Transmission Route (Examples and Typical Settings)

| Transmission route | Disease (example) | Causative agent | Primary exposure pathway | Common exposure settings | Typical health risk / severity | Key prevention focus |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Waterborne (fecal–oral) | Cholera | *Vibrio cholerae* | Drinking contaminated water | Poor sanitation, disasters, refugee camps | Severe dehydration; can be fatal without rehydration | Safe water, sanitation, oral rehydration, vaccination in outbreaks |
| Waterborne (recreational water) | Cryptosporidiosis | *Cryptosporidium* spp. | Swallowing contaminated pool/lake water | Pools, water parks, childcare outbreaks | Prolonged diarrhea; severe in immunocompromised | Exclude ill swimmers, filtration, hygiene |
| Foodborne (undercooked foods) | Campylobacteriosis | *Campylobacter jejuni* | Eating undercooked poultry / cross-contamination | Home kitchens, restaurants, poultry handling | Diarrhea; possible Guillain–Barré syndrome | Cook poultry, prevent cross-contamination |
| Vector-borne (mosquito) | Malaria | *Plasmodium* spp. | Bite of infected *Anopheles* mosquito | Rural/forest travel, night exposure | Severe anemia, cerebral malaria, death risk | Bed nets, repellents, chemoprophylaxis |
| Vector-borne (mosquito) | Dengue | Dengue virus | Bite of infected *Aedes* mosquito | Urban/peri-urban daytime exposure | Hemorrhagic complications in some cases | Source reduction, repellents, community control |
| Airborne / droplet | Measles | Measles virus | Inhalation of airborne particles | Crowded indoor settings, low vaccination areas | High fever, pneumonia/encephalitis risk | Vaccination, isolation, ventilation |
| Animal bite / saliva | Rabies | Rabies virus | Bite/scratch from infected mammal | Dog bites, bat exposure | Nearly universally fatal once symptomatic | Post-exposure prophylaxis, animal vaccination |
| Animal contact (urine-contaminated water/soil) | Leptospirosis | *Leptospira* spp. | Skin/mucosa contact with contaminated water | Floodwater cleanup, farming, sewage work | Kidney/liver failure risk in severe disease | Protective clothing, rodent control |
| Soil-borne (wounds) | Tetanus | *Clostridium tetani* | Contamination of wound with spores | Puncture wounds, unsterile procedures | Life-threatening muscle spasms | Vaccination, wound care |
| Healthcare-associated (blood) | Hepatitis B | Hepatitis B virus | Needlestick, blood/body fluid exposure | Healthcare, unsterile injections | Chronic infection, cirrhosis/cancer risk | Vaccination, sharps safety |
| Sexual / bloodborne | HIV infection | HIV | Sexual contact or blood exposure | Unprotected sex, needle sharing | Progressive immunodeficiency without treatment | Condoms, PrEP, harm reduction |
| Aerosolized animal products | Anthrax (inhalational) | *Bacillus anthracis* | Inhalation of spores from animal hides/wool | Tannery work, handling animal products | High fatality without rapid treatment | PPE, vaccination in high-risk jobs |
