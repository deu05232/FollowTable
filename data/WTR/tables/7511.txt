# Endocrine control relationships (releasing and stimulating hormones)

| Target hormone | Pituitary stimulating hormone | Hypothalamic releasing hormone |
|---|---|---|
| Thyroid hormones (T3/T4) | Thyroid-stimulating hormone (TSH) | Thyrotropin-releasing hormone (TRH) |
| Cortisol | Adrenocorticotropic hormone (ACTH) | Corticotropin-releasing hormone (CRH) |
| Estrogen/Testosterone | Follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH), Luteinizing hormone (LH) | Gonadotropin-releasing hormone (GnRH) |
| IGF-1 | Growth hormone (GH) | Growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) |
