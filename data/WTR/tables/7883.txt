# Post-Stroke Medication Overview (Includes Antiplatelets, Anticoagulants, Statins, and an NSAID)

| Therapeutic area | Generic name | Brand example | Drug class | Why it is used |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Secondary prevention | clopidogrel | Plavix | Antiplatelet | Prevent recurrent stroke |
| Secondary prevention | aspirin | Bayer Aspirin | Antiplatelet/NSAID | Prevent clotting (low-dose) |
| Secondary prevention | warfarin | Coumadin | Anticoagulant | Afib-related stroke prevention |
| Blood pressure control | losartan | Cozaar | ARB | Reduce stroke risk via BP control |
| Lipid management | atorvastatin | Lipitor | Statin | Lower LDL cholesterol |
| Spasticity | baclofen | Lioresal | Muscle relaxant | Reduce spasticity |
| Pain (adjunct) | ibuprofen | Advil | NSAID | Treat pain/inflammation |
| Thrombolysis (acute) | alteplase | Activase | Thrombolytic | Break up acute clot |
