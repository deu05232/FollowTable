# Helsinki (Finland) sightseeing list  major attractions

| Attraction | Category | District | Why people go |
|---|---|---|---|
| Suomenlinna Sea Fortress | Historic site | Suomenlinna | UNESCO-listed island fortress |
| Helsinki Cathedral | Landmark | Kruununhaka | Iconic neoclassical cathedral |
| Market Square (Kauppatori) | Market | Eteläsatama | Food stalls and harbor views |
| Temppeliaukio Church | Church | Töölö | Rock-hewn interior |
| Ateneum Art Museum | Museum | Kluuvi | Finnish art collections |
| Sibelius Monument | Monument | Töölö | Tribute to composer Sibelius |
| Oodi Central Library | Architecture | Töölönlahti | Modern public library and design |
