# Laboratory Findings Commonly Seen in Selected Diseases (Qualitative Frequency)

| Disease/condition | Key lab finding | Typical direction | Frequency descriptor | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Iron deficiency anemia | Ferritin | Low | Often | May be low-normal with inflammation |
| Hypothyroidism | TSH | High | Common | Free T4 low in overt disease |
| Viral hepatitis | ALT/AST | High | Frequently | Pattern depends on virus and phase |
| Rheumatoid arthritis | RF/anti-CCP | Positive | Sometimes | Seronegative RA exists |
| Systemic lupus erythematosus | ANA | Positive | Usually | Not specific without clinical context |
| Acute pancreatitis | Lipase | High | Typically | Degree not proportional to severity |
| Chronic kidney disease | Creatinine | High | Often | eGFR provides staging |
| Celiac disease | tTG-IgA | Positive | Common | Total IgA deficiency can confound |
