# Solar composition by mass  hydrogen, helium, and metals

| Component | Mass fraction (approx.) | What it represents |
|---|---:|---|
| Hydrogen | 0.73 | Most of the Sun’s mass |
| Helium | 0.25 | Second most abundant |
| Metals (all elements heavier than He) | 0.02 | Combined trace elements |
| Oxygen (subset of metals) | ~0.009 | Major contributor among metals |
| Carbon (subset of metals) | ~0.003 | Major contributor among metals |
| Iron (subset of metals) | ~0.001 | Important metal by mass |
