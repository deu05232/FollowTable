# Household drain opener comparison (strength plus safety and use notes)

| Product type | Primary active chemical | Typical concentration (%) | Key hazard note | Typical use |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Lye-based drain opener | Sodium hydroxide | 30-50 | Strongly corrosive | Hair/grease clogs |
| Acid-based drain opener | Sulfuric acid | 80-93 | Severe burns, reacts violently | Paper/organic clogs |
| Bleach-based opener | Sodium hypochlorite | 5-10 | Do not mix with acids/ammonia | Light clogs |
| Enzymatic opener | Enzyme blend | <1 | Low hazard | Maintenance |
| Foaming opener | Sodium hydroxide + aluminum | 10-20 | Pressure/heat generation | Standing-water clogs |
| Gel opener | Sodium hydroxide | 15-25 | Corrosive | Slow drains |
