# Kidney Disorders and Suspected Exposures (Occupational & Drug-Related) — Summary Matrix

| Condition | Common occupational exposures | Medications implicated | Diagnostic clues | Prevention/management focus |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Acute tubular necrosis | Solvents, heavy metals | Aminoglycosides, amphotericin B | Rising creatinine, muddy casts | Avoid nephrotoxins, IV fluids |
| Interstitial nephritis | Industrial chemicals | NSAIDs, PPIs, beta-lactams | Eosinophiluria, rash | Stop drug, steroids if severe |
| Nephrolithiasis | Dehydration, heat work | Topiramate, loop diuretics | Flank pain, hematuria | Hydration, diet modification |
| CKD (diabetic) | Shift work, sedentary | — | Albuminuria, long DM | Glycemic/BP control |
| Bladder cancer | Aromatic amines | Cyclophosphamide | Painless hematuria | Smoking cessation, surveillance |
| Urinary tract infection | Catheter use | SGLT2 inhibitors (risk) | Dysuria, bacteriuria | Hygiene, catheter protocols |
| Polycystic kidney disease | — | — | Enlarged kidneys, cysts | BP control, tolvaptan |
| Rhabdomyolysis-associated AKI | Heat stress | Statins (rare) | High CK, dark urine | Stop trigger, aggressive fluids |
