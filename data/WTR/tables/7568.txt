# Prime Ministers of England (names with short notes, no dates)

| Prime Minister | Notable note |
|---|---|
| Sir Robert Walpole | Often regarded as the first de facto prime minister |
| Earl of Chatham | Also known as William Pitt the Elder |
| Neville Chamberlain | Associated with the policy of appeasement |
| Winston Churchill | Led Britain during WWII |
| Margaret Thatcher | First female prime minister |
| Tony Blair | Associated with New Labour |
| Gordon Brown | Former Chancellor before becoming PM |
| David Cameron | Led the Conservative Party in the 2010s |
