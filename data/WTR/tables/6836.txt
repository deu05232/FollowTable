| Discovery / Found item | Approx. time (years ago) | Where found | What it suggested |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Denisovan finger bone | ~50,000 | Denisova Cave, Siberia | Evidence of a distinct hominin group identified via DNA |

| Kennewick Man remains | ~8,500 | Washington State, USA | Sparked debate on early population history in North America |
