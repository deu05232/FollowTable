# Food Additives in  Health  Products (Preservatives & Claims)

| Product Type | Common Additives | Purpose of Additives | Where Often Found | Labeling/Health Claim |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Protein bar | Sugar alcohols, soy lecithin | Texture, shelf stability | Convenience stores | "High protein" |
| Low-calorie soda | Aspartame, acesulfame K | Sweetness without sugar | Supermarkets | "Zero sugar" |
| Packaged salad dressing | Xanthan gum, potassium sorbate | Thickening, preservation | Refrigerated aisles | "Made with olive oil" |
| Plant-based meat | Methylcellulose, natural flavors | Binding, flavor | Frozen section | "Plant-powered" |
| Flavored yogurt | Pectin, carrageenan | Thickening | Dairy case | "Probiotic" |
| Bottled green juice | Ascorbic acid | Prevent oxidation | Beverage aisle | "Cold-pressed" |
| Gluten-free bread | Calcium propionate | Mold prevention | Bakery aisle | "Gluten-free" |
