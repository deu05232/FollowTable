# Measurement Units Commonly Used in Paleoanthropology Field Reports

| Measurement Category | Unit | Symbol | Used For | Example Field Note | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Length | meter | m | Site grid spacing | "Trench length: 3 m" | SI base |
| Mass | kilogram | kg | Sample mass | "Sediment: 2.4 kg" | SI base |
| Time | year | yr | Dating notation | "~160 kyr" | Not SI base |
| Area | square meter | m² | Excavation area | "Unit: 1 m²" | Derived |
| Volume | liter | L | Water/solutions | "Consolidant: 0.5 L" | Accepted |
| Energy | joule | J | Lab measurements | "Impact: 12 J" | Derived |
| Radiation dose | gray | Gy | Imaging safety | "CT dose: 0.02 Gy" | Derived |
| Temperature | kelvin | K | Lab conditions | "Stored at 293 K" | SI base |
