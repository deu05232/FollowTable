| Description | Google Account: Select your account to use with Google Play Music. Equalizer: Fine tune your audio settings. | Cache during playback: Temporarily store music while streaming. Automatically cache: Automatically cache music while charging your device and connected to Wi-Fi. Clear cache: Clear stored music that has been cached to your device. This doesn't affect the availability of music you've made available offline or music that has been temporarily cached. Mobile networks stream quality: Select low, normal, or high quality streaming.
