# Hormone Effects by Target Organ (Hormone Names Spread Across Columns)

| Target organ/system | Insulin | Glucagon | Cortisol | Thyroid hormones (T3/T4) | ADH (vasopressin) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Liver | ↑ glycogen synthesis, ↓ gluconeogenesis | ↑ glycogenolysis, ↑ gluconeogenesis | ↑ gluconeogenesis | ↑ oxygen consumption | minimal direct effect |
| Skeletal muscle | ↑ glucose uptake (GLUT4), ↑ protein synthesis | minimal | protein catabolism (chronic) | ↑ metabolic rate | minimal |
| Adipose tissue | ↑ lipogenesis, ↓ lipolysis | ↑ lipolysis | ↑ lipolysis (permissive) | ↑ lipolysis | minimal |
| Cardiovascular | indirect vasodilation via glucose control | indirect | maintains vascular tone | ↑ heart rate/contractility | minimal |
| Kidney | mild Na+ retention | minimal | ↑ GFR (context-dependent) | ↑ renal blood flow | ↑ water reabsorption (V2) |
| Brain/appetite | satiety signaling | hunger signaling | stress response | alertness/thermogenesis | thirst modulation |
| Reproductive | permissive metabolic support | permissive metabolic support | suppresses GnRH if high | required for fertility | minimal |
