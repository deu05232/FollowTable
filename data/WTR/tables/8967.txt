# Cardiovascular Prevention Medications and Primary Non-Pain Indications

| Drug Category | Example Medicines (Generic / Brand) | Primary Intended Use (Non-pain) | Typical Patient Goal | Monitoring / Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Statins | Atorvastatin (Lipitor), Rosuvastatin (Crestor) | Lower LDL cholesterol | Reduce ASCVD risk | Lipid panel; liver enzymes if indicated |
| Antiplatelets | Clopidogrel (Plavix), Aspirin (low-dose) | Prevent platelet aggregation | Prevent heart attack / stroke | Bleeding risk, drug interactions |
| Anticoagulants | Apixaban (Eliquis), Warfarin (Coumadin) | Prevent/treat thrombosis | Prevent stroke in AF; treat DVT/PE | INR for warfarin; renal dosing for DOACs |
| Antihypertensives (ACEi/ARB) | Lisinopril (Prinivil), Losartan (Cozaar) | Lower blood pressure | Reduce stroke/MI risk | Potassium/creatinine monitoring |
| Beta blockers | Metoprolol (Toprol XL), Carvedilol (Coreg) | Control heart rate/BP | Reduce angina, post-MI benefit | HR/BP monitoring |
| Antianginals | Nitroglycerin (Nitrostat), Isosorbide mononitrate (Imdur) | Relieve angina via vasodilation | Prevent/stop chest pain from ischemia | Avoid PDE5 inhibitors; headaches common |
| Lipid absorption inhibitors | Ezetimibe (Zetia) | Lower cholesterol | Additional LDL reduction | Often combined with statins |
