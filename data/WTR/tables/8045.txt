# # Cause-of-death terminology mapping (short vs full descriptions)

| Common label | Formal cause description | Included conditions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Heart disease | Diseases of the heart | Multiple cardiac conditions |
| Cancer | Malignant neoplasms | Solid tumors and hematologic malignancies |
| Stroke | Cerebrovascular diseases | Ischemic and hemorrhagic events |
