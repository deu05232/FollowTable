# Closest-constellation relationships for selected zodiac constellations

| Constellation | Closest constellation | Where the boundary is closest |
|---|---|---|
| Aries | Taurus | Along the Aries–Taurus border |
| Taurus | Gemini | Near the Taurus–Gemini boundary |
| Gemini | Cancer | Adjacent on the ecliptic strip |
| Leo | Virgo | Shares border near the ecliptic |
| Libra | Scorpius | Tight border region in the south |
| Sagittarius | Capricornus | Neighboring along ecliptic band |
