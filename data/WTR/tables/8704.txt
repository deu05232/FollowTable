# Science Discovery Resources and Feeds (Mixed Topics)

| Source | Main focus | Example content | Region | Access type | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| University press office feed | Research news | Weekly lab announcements | Global | Free | Often summarizes papers without full data |
| Futurity-style aggregator | New studies | Short explainers of published work | US/UK/CA/AU | Free | Good for headlines, not discovery timelines |
| Popular science magazine | General science | Feature stories and interviews | Global | Subscription | Mixes tech, health, and environment |
| Museum exhibition page | Public science | Exhibit highlights and events | Local | Free | Focus is outreach rather than discovery details |
| Education testing report | Science literacy | Country score tables | Global | Free | About education outcomes, not discoveries |
| Research society portal | Discipline updates | Conference programs | Global | Membership | Emphasizes events and networking |
