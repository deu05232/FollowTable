# Household Chemical Ingredients — Safety & First Aid Narrative Table

| Ingredient (Common Name) | Typical Product | Primary Hazard | Symptoms | First Aid | PPE Recommendation | Disposal Guidance |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sodium hypochlorite | Bleach | Corrosive oxidizer | Eye/skin burns | Rinse with water 15 min | Gloves, goggles | Do not mix; follow local rules |
| Sodium hydroxide | Drain cleaner | Strong base | Severe burns | Flush; seek medical care | Face shield, gloves | Neutralize only if trained |
| Hydrochloric acid | Toilet bowl cleaner | Strong acid | Respiratory irritation | Fresh air; rinse skin | Gloves, ventilation | Keep in original container |
| Ammonium hydroxide | Glass cleaner | Irritant | Coughing, tearing | Fresh air; rinse eyes | Gloves; avoid mixing | Do not pour large amounts |
| Isopropyl alcohol | Rubbing alcohol | Flammable | Dizziness, irritation | Move to fresh air | Avoid ignition sources | Small amounts evaporate safely |
| Ethylene glycol | Some cleaners/antifreeze | Toxic if ingested | Nausea, kidney damage | Call poison control | Avoid skin contact | Hazardous waste |
| Hydrogen peroxide | Antiseptic/cleaner | Oxidizer | Whitening/burning | Rinse; avoid ingestion | Gloves | Store away from light |
| Quaternary ammonium compounds | Disinfectant sprays | Irritant | Skin/eye irritation | Rinse; wash skin | Gloves | Avoid aquatic release |
