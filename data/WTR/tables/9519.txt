# Vector-Borne Disease Burden by Insect Species (Mosquito Tick Focus)

| Insect Vector | Diseases Commonly Transmitted | Typical Setting | Main Route to Humans | Annual Global Cases (approx.) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Anopheles mosquito | Malaria | Tropical/subtropical | Bite (human blood meal) | 200M+ |
| Aedes aegypti | Dengue, Zika, Yellow fever | Urban/peri-urban | Bite | 100M+ (dengue) |
| Aedes albopictus | Dengue, Chikungunya | Expanding temperate range | Bite | Regional outbreaks |
| Culex mosquito | West Nile fever, Japanese encephalitis | Wetlands/irrigation | Bite | Variable |
| Ixodes tick | Lyme disease, Anaplasmosis | Woodland/grassland | Tick bite | Millions (Lyme, endemic regions) |
| Glossina (tsetse fly) | African trypanosomiasis | Riverine/savannah | Bite | Thousands |
| Phlebotomine sandfly | Leishmaniasis | Arid/forest margins | Bite | 1M+ |
| Triatomine bug | Chagas disease | Rural housing | Bite/contamination | 6-7M infected (prevalence) |
