# Constellation visibility by month (single constellation across time)

| Month | Orion visibility (Northern mid-latitudes) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| November | Rises late evening | Appears in the east |
| December | High at midnight | Prominent winter constellation |
| January | High most of the night | Best viewing season |
| February | Sets after midnight | Still prominent |
| March | Visible early evening | Moves toward western sky |
