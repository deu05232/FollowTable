# A-level Biology/Transport/mammalian transport - Wikibooks, open books for an open world

| Type of organism | Size Range | Example | Activity Level | Transport system |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Single-celled | Microscopic | E.Coli | Move using flagellum in search of food | No specialised transport system |
| Jellyfish and sea anemones | Up to 60cm | Sea anemone | Anemones are sedantry, jellyfish swim slowly | No specialised transport system |
| Insects | 1mm to 13cm | Locust | Active movement, many fly | Blood system (has pumps) |
| Green plants | 1mm to 150m | Oak tree | No movement | Xylem/phloem (no pump) |
| Fish | 12mm to 10m | Minnow | Active movement | Blood system (has pump) |
| Mammals | 35mm to 34m | Human | Most active movement | Blood system (has pump) |
