# Nutrient Category List for Label Reading (No Product Linkage)

| Label category | Example nutrients included | Common unit | Where it appears on labels | Why people track it |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Energy | Calories | kcal | Top of panel | Weight goals |
| Macronutrients | Protein, total carbs, total fat | g | Middle | Fitness/satiety |
| Sugars | Total sugars, added sugars | g | Under carbs | Glycemic control |
| Fiber | Dietary fiber | g | Under carbs | Gut/heart health |
| Fats (detail) | Saturated, trans, poly/mono | g | Under total fat | Lipids quality |
| Minerals | Sodium, potassium, calcium, iron | mg | Lower | Blood pressure/bone |
| Vitamins | A, C, D, B-complex | µg/mg | Lower | Deficiency prevention |
| %DV | Percent Daily Value | % | Right side | Quick comparison |
| Ingredients | Sweeteners, oils, additives | — | Below panel | Allergies/preferences |
