# Clinical Biomarkers and Associated Diseases (No Animal Exposure Information)

| Biomarker | Sample Type | Elevated In | Possible Interpretation | Typical Follow-up |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| C-reactive protein (CRP) | Blood | Pneumonia, autoimmune disease | Inflammation | Repeat CRP; clinical exam |
| D-dimer | Blood | Pulmonary embolism, severe infection | Coagulation activation | Imaging; anticoagulation eval |
| ALT (alanine aminotransferase) | Blood | Hepatitis, fatty liver disease | Liver injury | Hepatitis panel; ultrasound |
| Procalcitonin | Blood | Bacterial sepsis | Bacterial infection likelihood | Cultures; antibiotics decision |
| Troponin | Blood | Myocardial infarction | Cardiac injury | ECG; cardiology consult |
| HbA1c | Blood | Diabetes | Long-term glucose control | Lifestyle/medication adjustment |
| Ferritin | Blood | Inflammation, hemochromatosis | Iron storage/acute phase | Iron studies; genetics as needed |
| BNP/NT-proBNP | Blood | Heart failure | Ventricular strain | Echocardiography |
