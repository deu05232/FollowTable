# Common Pain-Relief Medications (Generic to Brand)

| Generic name | Common brand names | Drug type | Typical use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Acetaminophen | Tylenol | Analgesic/antipyretic | Mild to moderate pain, fever |
| Aspirin | Bayer, Ecotrin | NSAID (salicylate) | Pain, inflammation, fever |
| Celecoxib | Celebrex | NSAID (COX-2 selective) | Arthritis pain and inflammation |
| Diclofenac | Voltaren, Cataflam | NSAID | Musculoskeletal pain, inflammation |
| Ibuprofen | Advil, Motrin | NSAID | General pain, inflammation |
| Ketorolac | Toradol | NSAID | Short-term moderate to severe pain |
| Naproxen | Aleve, Naprosyn | NSAID | Pain and inflammation |
| Tramadol | Ultram | Opioid-like analgesic | Moderate pain (prescription) |
