# Home water treatment disinfectants — adoption and cost overview (no concentration focus)

| Disinfectant option | Household adoption pattern | Typical user concerns | Training/equipment burden | Relative cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free chlorine products | Common in many regions | Taste/odor complaints | Low | Low |
| Chloramines | Less common at home | Hard to dose correctly | Medium | Moderate |
| Ozone devices | Rare at home | No residual disinfectant | High | High |
| Chlorine dioxide | Rare at home | Handling precautions | High | High |
| Acid-based methods | Occasional | Material compatibility | Medium | Low–Moderate |
