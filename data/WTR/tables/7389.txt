# Corrosive classes for household-relevant acids and bases (category-focused, not ingredient strength)

| Substance | Hazard category | Condition text |
|---|---|---|
| Acetic acid solution | Corrosive (acidic) | More than 10% and less than 50% acid by mass |
| Ammonia solution | Corrosive (basic) | More than 10% but not more than 35% ammonia |
| Sodium hydroxide solution | Corrosive (basic) | Concentration-dependent classification |
| Hydrochloric acid | Corrosive (acidic) | Concentration-dependent classification |
