# Religion Comparison Matrix (Sacred texts, practices, worship spaces, leadership, holidays)

| Religion | Sacred text(s) | Key practices / pillars | Typical worship space | Clergy / leadership (typical) | Major festivals / holy days |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Christianity | Bible (Old & New Testaments) | Prayer; sacraments/ordinances; worship services; charity | Church / cathedral | Priests/pastors; bishops (varies by tradition) | Easter; Christmas; Pentecost |
| Islam | Qur'an; Hadith | Five Pillars (shahada, salat, zakat, sawm, hajj) | Mosque | Imams; scholars (ulama) | Ramadan; Eid al-Fitr; Eid al-Adha |
| Hinduism | Vedas; Upanishads; Bhagavad Gita; epics | Puja; festivals; yoga/meditation; pilgrimage; dharma duties | Temple; home shrine | Priests (purohit); gurus (varies) | Diwali; Holi; Navaratri |
| Buddhism | Pali Canon (Tipitaka); Mahayana sutras | Meditation; ethical precepts; chanting; monastic discipline | Temple / monastery | Monks/nuns; teachers/lamas (by tradition) | Vesak; Obon (Japan); Losar (Tibetan) |
| Judaism | Tanakh; Talmud | Sabbath observance; kosher laws; prayer; lifecycle rites | Synagogue | Rabbis; cantors | Passover; Rosh Hashanah; Yom Kippur |
| Sikhism | Guru Granth Sahib | Naam simran; kirtan; langar; Five Ks (Khalsa) | Gurdwara | Granthi (reader); committee leadership | Vaisakhi; Gurpurabs |
| Shinto | Kojiki; Nihon Shoki (texts of tradition) | Shrine rites; offerings; purification (misogi) | Shinto shrine | Kannushi (priests); miko attendants | New Year (Shogatsu); Matsuri festivals |
| Taoism (Daoism) | Dao De Jing; Zhuangzi; ritual texts | Meditation; ritual offerings; feng shui; internal alchemy | Taoist temple | Taoist priests; masters by lineage | Lunar New Year rites; Lantern Festival (often shared) |
| Jainism | Agamas; later commentaries | Ahimsa (non-violence); ascetic vows; meditation; fasting | Derasar (temple) | Monks/nuns; lay teachers | Paryushana; Mahavir Jayanti |
| Zoroastrianism | Avesta | Fire temple worship; purity practices; ethical triad (good thoughts/words/deeds) | Fire temple | Mobeds (priests) | Nowruz; Gahambar festivals |
