# # Enzymes involved in steroid hormone synthesis and their roles

| Enzyme | Gene | Role in pathway |
|---|---|---|
| Aromatase | CYP19A1 | Converts androgens to estrogens |
| 21-hydroxylase | CYP21A2 | Produces precursors for cortisol and aldosterone |
| 11β-hydroxylase | CYP11B1 | Final steps in cortisol synthesis |
| 5α-reductase type 2 | SRD5A2 | Converts testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT) |
