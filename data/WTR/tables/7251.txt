# Number fraction of major particles in the Sun (approximate)

| Species | Approx. fraction by number | Description |
|---|---:|---|
| Hydrogen nuclei (protons) | 0.92 | Dominant nuclei |
| Helium nuclei (alpha particles) | 0.078 | Next most common |
| Heavier nuclei (C, N, O, …) | 0.002 | All metals combined |
| Electrons | ~1.00 | Balances positive charge |
| Photons (in the interior) | varies | Not a chemical constituent |
