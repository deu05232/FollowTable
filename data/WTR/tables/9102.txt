# Named Moon Phases Grouped by Waxing (Bright Half) vs Waning (Dark Half)

| Half of cycle | Sequence order | Phase name | Common visual cue (Northern Hemisphere) | What typically comes next |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| Waxing (bright half) | 1 | New Moon | Moon not visibly illuminated | Waxing Crescent |
| Waxing (bright half) | 2 | Waxing Crescent | Thin crescent; right side lit | First Quarter |
| Waxing (bright half) | 3 | First Quarter | Half-lit; right half bright | Waxing Gibbous |
| Waxing (bright half) | 4 | Waxing Gibbous | More than half lit; right side dominant | Full Moon |
| Waning (dark half) | 5 | Full Moon | Fully illuminated disk | Waning Gibbous |
| Waning (dark half) | 6 | Waning Gibbous | More than half lit; left side dominant | Last (Third) Quarter |
| Waning (dark half) | 7 | Last (Third) Quarter | Half-lit; left half bright | Waning Crescent |
| Waning (dark half) | 8 | Waning Crescent | Thin crescent; left side lit | New Moon |
