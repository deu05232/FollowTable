# Google product review acquisition workflow (not a rating table)

| Stage | What happens | Data collected | Example fields |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Eligibility check | Detect items with few/no reviews | Inventory signals | SKU, category, availability |
| Reviewer selection | Identify likely contributors | User activity | past purchases, clicks |
| Prompting | Ask users to write a review | Prompt metrics | open rate, response rate |
| Moderation | Filter spam/toxicity | Content signals | language, duplicates |
| Publication | Display review on product page | Display metadata | timestamp, locale |
| Feedback loop | Improve future prompts | Performance logs | conversion lift, dwell time |
