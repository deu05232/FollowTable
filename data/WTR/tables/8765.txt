# Average lifespan of pet cats by lifestyle and sex

| Cat group | Typical lifespan (years) | Common living situation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Indoor male (neutered) | 13–17 | Strictly indoor | Lower trauma risk |
| Indoor female (spayed) | 14–18 | Strictly indoor | Often slightly longer |
| Indoor/outdoor mixed | 10–14 | Goes outside daily | More injury and disease exposure |
| Outdoor community cat | 3–7 | Mostly outdoors | Higher hazards and limited care |
| Feral cat | 2–5 | Fully outdoors | Little to no veterinary care |
