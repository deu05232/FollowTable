# Notable Physics Discoveries (approximate era)

| Discovery | When (approx.) | Key scientists / team | Short description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cosmic microwave background (CMB) detected | mid-1960s | Penzias & Wilson | First strong observational support for the Big Bang model |
| Higgs boson observation | early 2010s | ATLAS & CMS (CERN) | Particle consistent with the Higgs field mechanism |
| Gravitational waves detected (GW150914) | mid-2010s | LIGO Scientific Collaboration | First direct detection of spacetime ripples from black-hole merger |
| First pulsar discovered | late 1960s | Jocelyn Bell Burnell & team | Discovery of rapidly pulsing radio sources (neutron stars) |
