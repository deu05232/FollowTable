# Major lunar phases and where they fall in the cycle

| Phase point | What you see from Earth | Approx. position in the cycle |
| --- | --- | --- |
| New Moon | Moon is not visibly lit | Start of the cycle |
| Waxing Crescent | Thin crescent grows each night | Early cycle |
| First Quarter | Half-lit, growing | About one week in |
| Waxing Gibbous | More than half lit, still growing | Mid-cycle |
| Full Moon | Fully lit face | Middle of the cycle |
| Waning Gibbous | More than half lit, shrinking | After full |
| Third Quarter | Half-lit, shrinking | About three weeks in |
| Waning Crescent | Thin crescent fades | End of the cycle |
