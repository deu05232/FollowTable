# Adverse Events by Antifungal Agent — Common Reactions and Monitoring Parameters

| Drug | Common adverse events | Major drug interactions | Monitoring tests | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Itraconazole | Rash, GI upset, edema | CYP3A4 inhibitors/substrates | LFTs | Avoid in heart failure |
| Fluconazole | Nausea, QT prolongation | Warfarin, phenytoin | LFTs, ECG if risk | Renal dose adjust |
| Voriconazole | Visual changes, photosensitivity | Many via CYP2C19/3A4 | LFTs, trough | Hallucinations possible |
| Posaconazole | Diarrhea, hepatic enzyme rise | CYP3A4 interactions | LFTs, trough | Absorption with food |
| Amphotericin B | Nephrotoxicity, hypokalemia | — | Creatinine, K/Mg | Infusion reactions |
| Terbinafine | Taste disturbance, rash | CYP2D6 | LFTs | Onychomycosis use |
| Caspofungin | Fever, phlebitis | Cyclosporine | LFTs | Echinocandin class |
| Nystatin | Local irritation | — | null usually | Topical/oral suspension |
