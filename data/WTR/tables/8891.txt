# Digestive and appetite-related hormones and their main effects

| Hormone | Main effect on digestion/appetite | Typical source |
|---|---|---|
| Ghrelin | Increases hunger and food intake signals | Stomach |
| Leptin | Promotes satiety; reduces appetite | Adipose tissue |
| Cholecystokinin (CCK) | Promotes satiety; stimulates pancreatic enzyme secretion and gallbladder contraction | Small intestine |
| Secretin | Stimulates bicarbonate secretion to neutralize acid | Small intestine |
| Gastrin | Stimulates gastric acid secretion | Stomach |
| GLP-1 | Enhances insulin secretion after meals; increases satiety | Intestine |
| Somatostatin | Inhibits several digestive hormone secretions | Pancreas, GI tract |
