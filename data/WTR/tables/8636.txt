# Used phone grading standards (not organized by phone model)

| Grade | Screen condition | Body condition | Battery health | Included accessories |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | like new | like new | 90–100% | full |
| B | light marks | light wear | 80–89% | partial |
| C | visible scratches | noticeable wear | 70–79% | minimal |
| D | cracks/defects | heavy wear | <70% | none |
