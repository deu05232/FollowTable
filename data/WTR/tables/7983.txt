# Common food preservatives and typical uses (no pricing)

| Preservative | Common purpose | Typical foods it appears in | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sodium benzoate | Prevents mold/yeast | Soft drinks, sauces | Works best in acidic foods |
| Calcium propionate | Prevents mold | Bread, baked goods | Common in commercial baking |
| BHT | Prevents oxidation | Snack foods, cereals | Antioxidant preservative |
| Ascorbic acid | Prevents oxidation | Packaged foods, beverages | Also known as vitamin C |
