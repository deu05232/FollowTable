# Household disinfectants comparison (advantages and cost only, no concentration)

| Disinfectant | Typical household use | Advantages | Disadvantages | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free chlorine | Yes | Strong against many pathogens | Taste/odor concerns | Low |
| Chloramines | Rare | Longer-lasting residual | Less effective than free chlorine | Moderate |
| Ozone | Rare | Very fast action | No residual, needs equipment | High |
| Chlorine dioxide | Rare | Effective | On-site generation needed | High |
