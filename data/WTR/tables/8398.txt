# Second-hand phone condition grading guide by component (no price or seller channel columns)

| Phone | Screen condition notes | Battery health indication | Frame/back cosmetic condition | Camera and speakers check | Buttons/ports check | Typical issues to inspect |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
