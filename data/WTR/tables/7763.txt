# Board Game Player Count Guide (Minimum and Maximum Players)

| Game | Min Players | Max Players | Best With | Notes on Participation |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Patchwork | 2 | 2 | 2 | Strictly two-player only |
| Jaipur | 2 | 2 | 2 | Two-player card game |
| Carcassonne | 2 | 5 | 3–4 | Supports larger group with shared tile drafting |
| 7 Wonders | 2 | 7 | 4–6 | Plays simultaneously; scales well to bigger groups |
| Codenames | 4 | 8 | 6–8 | Team-based; can add more by allowing extra guessers |
| Decrypto | 4 | 8 | 6–8 | Team-based clue/guess game |
| The Resistance: Avalon | 5 | 10 | 7–9 | Social deduction; needs a larger group |
| Dixit | 3 | 8 | 5–7 | Party-style; works best with mid-sized groups |
