# Sugar Substitutes Safety & Side-Effect Summary (No Nutrient Values Provided)

| Sweetener | Common Use | Main Health Claims | Reported Side Effects | Warnings / Contraindications | Regulatory Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Aspartame | Diet soda, tabletop packets | "Zero calories" | Headache, nausea (reported) | Not for people with PKU | Approved with ADI limits |
| Sucralose | Baked goods, drinks | "Heat-stable" | Bloating, GI discomfort (reported) | May alter gut microbiota (animal studies) | Approved in many countries |
| Saccharin | Beverages, packets | "Very sweet" | Bitter aftertaste | Avoid if sulfonamide sensitivity (precaution) | Warning label removed in US |
| Stevia extract | Drinks, yogurt | "Natural" | Aftertaste, nausea (some) | Possible BP lowering interactions | Varies by extract purity |
| Erythritol | Keto products | "Low net carbs" | Gas, diarrhea at high doses | Use caution with IBS | Generally recognized as safe |
| Xylitol | Gum, candies | "Tooth-friendly" | Diarrhea at high intake | Extremely toxic to dogs | Allowed with labeling |
| Cyclamate | Soft drinks (some regions) | "Stable sweetener" | Limited human data | Restricted/banned in some countries | Regional approvals differ |
