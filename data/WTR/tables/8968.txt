# Chronic Disease Control Medicines and Their Primary Targets (Non-Analgesic)

| Condition Focus | Medication (Generic) | Brand Example | Primary Mechanism/Target | Main Treatment Goal | Typical Non-Pain Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Type 2 diabetes | Metformin | Glucophage | ↓ hepatic gluconeogenesis | Lower A1C | Better glycemic control |
| Type 2 diabetes | Semaglutide | Ozempic | GLP-1 receptor agonist | Lower A1C/weight | Weight loss, CV benefit |
| Hypertension | Amlodipine | Norvasc | Calcium channel blockade | Lower BP | Stroke/MI risk reduction |
| Hypertension | Hydrochlorothiazide | Microzide | Thiazide diuresis | Lower BP | Less edema/BP |
| Hyperlipidemia | Atorvastatin | Lipitor | HMG-CoA reductase inhibition | Lower LDL | ASCVD prevention |
| Hypothyroidism | Levothyroxine | Synthroid | Thyroid hormone replacement | Normalize TSH | Improved metabolism |
| GERD | Pantoprazole | Protonix | Proton pump inhibition | Reduce acid | Less reflux injury |
| Asthma/COPD | Budesonide-formoterol | Symbicort | ICS + LABA | Control airway inflammation | Fewer exacerbations |
