# Cardiovascular Medications Often Confused With Pain Drugs (Not Analgesics)

| Drug | Drug class | Primary indication |
|---|---|---|
| atorvastatin | statin | high cholesterol |
| clopidogrel | antiplatelet | prevent blood clots |
| warfarin | anticoagulant | prevent/treat thrombosis |
| losartan | ARB | high blood pressure |
| dabigatran | anticoagulant | atrial fibrillation stroke prevention |
| alteplase | thrombolytic | dissolve acute clots |
