# # Plant hormones and their key effects

| Hormone | Main effect in plants | Typical example outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Auxins | Control tropisms and apical dominance | Shoots bend toward light; side buds suppressed |
| Cytokinins | Promote cell division and delay aging | Leaves stay green longer |
| Gibberellins | Promote stem elongation and break dormancy | Seeds germinate after dormancy |
| Abscisic acid (ABA) | Promotes dormancy and closes stomata | Stomata close during drought |
| Ethylene | Drives ripening and abscission | Fruit ripens; leaves drop |
