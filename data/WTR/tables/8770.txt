# Common cat diseases and typical signs (no lifespan comparison)

| Condition | Main body systems affected | Typical signs owners notice | Usual diagnostic approach |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chronic kidney disease | Urinary | Increased thirst, weight loss | Bloodwork, urinalysis |
| Diabetes mellitus | Endocrine | Increased appetite, weight loss | Blood glucose testing |
| Hyperthyroidism | Endocrine | Weight loss, hyperactivity | T4 blood test |
| Feline leukemia virus infection | Immune/Blood | Poor coat, recurrent illness | Antigen test |
| Feline infectious peritonitis | Immune | Fever, fluid in abdomen | Imaging, lab work |
