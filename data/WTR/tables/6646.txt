# Religions and core beliefs (high-level comparison)

| Religion | View of afterlife | Concept of deity | Key scripture(s) | Typical worship setting |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Christianity | Heaven/Hell | Monotheism (Trinity) | Bible | Church |
| Islam | Paradise/Hell | Monotheism | Qur'an | Mosque |
| Hinduism | Reincarnation/liberation | Diverse | Vedas, Upanishads | Temple/Home |
| Buddhism | Rebirth/nirvana | Non-theistic (varies) | Tripitaka | Temple/Monastery |
| Sikhism | Reincarnation/liberation | Monotheism | Guru Granth Sahib | Gurdwara |
