Likewise, the conventional cosmology includes the microwave background and the neutrino sector. The number and energy density of photons is fixed by the observed black body temperature of the microwave background. The parameter h describes the expansion rate of the universe today, H 0 = 100 ⁢ h km s - 1 Mpc - 1 . “Spectrum” refers to the primordial scalar or density perturbations, parameterized by A s ⁢ ( k / k ⋆ ) n s - 1 , where k ⋆ = 0.002 ⁢ Mpc - 1 is a specified but otherwise irrelevant pivot scale.</caption>
