Be able to consider new opinions without bias, listen to all viewpoints, and synthesize perspectives from all parties.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>A4: Due Diligence</td><td>Be diligent and responsible; complete tasks within the agreed time and be accountable for the evaluation report. Based on user needs, explain evaluation results to help them correctly understand and appropriately use the results.   <tr><td>A5: Integrity</td><td>Be honest and trustworthy; do not falsify information in the evaluation process or results; do not issue evaluation reports that are false or have重大遗漏. Maintain professional prudence in accepting information; verify and validate information. For matters that cannot be verified or validated, appropriately disclose them in the report according to the degree of impact on the evaluation conclusion; if they have a significant impact on the evaluation conclusion, refuse to issue an evaluation report.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>A6: Rigor</td><td>Carry out evaluation activities according to evaluation requirements; work rigorously and meticulously; use precise wording.</td></tr>

  <tr><td>A10: Team Awareness</td><td>Have a big-picture mindset, a spirit of cooperation, and a spirit of service.</td></tr>
