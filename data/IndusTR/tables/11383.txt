<table>

  <tr><th>Work Output</th><th>Typical Content</th></tr>

  <tr><td>Indicator System</td><td>Lists indicators, definitions, data sources, and scoring rules used in the evaluation.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Evidence Package</td><td>Includes collected documents, interview notes, site-visit records, and data extracts.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Findings Summary</td><td>Organizes key findings, supporting evidence, and limitations.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Recommendation List</td><td>Provides actionable suggestions for improvement and follow-up actions.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Disclosure/Confidentiality Note</td><td>Specifies what can be published and what must remain internal.</td></tr>
