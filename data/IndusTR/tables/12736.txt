<table>
  <caption>Volleyball skill pass criteria (written descriptions) for student assessment</caption>
  <tr><th>Skill assessed</th><th>What the instructor observes</th><th>Pass requirement (written standard)</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Overhand serve</td><td>Contact point, body alignment, ball trajectory, serve consistency</td><td>Serves from behind the end line with correct overhand form; ball clears the net with a controlled trajectory and can be placed into the court intentionally.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Forearm pass (serve receive)</td><td>Platform angle, footwork to the ball, direction control</td><td>Moves to get behind the ball and produces a stable platform; pass is directed to the setter area and is playable for a set without double contact.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Setting</td><td>Hand shape, release timing, ball spin, location to hitter</td><td>Sets with clean contact (no carry) and delivers a hittable ball to a target attacker, allowing an organized attack to be run.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Spike/attack hit</td><td>Approach steps, jump timing, arm swing, contact in front</td><td>Uses a proper approach and arm swing to contact the ball at an appropriate height; attack is directed into the opponent court with control rather than a free ball.</td></tr>
</table>
