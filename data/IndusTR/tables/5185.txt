  <caption>Role Definition of Parties Involved in Animal Traceability Business</caption>
  <tr><td>Role</td><td>Role (English)</td><td>Description</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Holder</td><td>Holder</td><td>The party responsible for raising and caring for animals at a certain location</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Transporter</td><td>Transporter</td><td>The party responsible for transporting animals from one place to another and providing the relevant required means of transport</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Trader</td><td>Trader</td><td>The party involved in animal trading</td></tr>
  <tr><td>T&T Repository</td><td>Tracking andtracing repository</td><td>A database used by business partners to declare all TT events. Each party can search and retrieve TT information in the database</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Authority</td><td>Authority</td><td>The relevant agency responsible for legal administration of animals, husbandry, transport, or fishing, or responsible for public health, food and feed safety, animal health, or environmental issues</td></tr>
  <tr><td>T&T Requesting Party</td><td>Tracking and tracingrequesting party</td><td>The party that searches for, requests, and receives TT information about animals, herds, or animal products</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Repository Party</td><td>Repository party</td><td>The party that provides and operates the TT repository and all related services</td></tr>
  <tr><td>TT Party</td><td>TT party</td><td>The party responsible for declaring animal TT events to the TT repository. Usually the party responsible for tracking animals, referencing locations, means of transport, identifying cargo, or animal transport</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Animal Registration Office</td><td>Animal registration office</td><td>The party that provides (legal) animal registration and related services</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Track and Trace Event</td><td>Track and trace event</td><td>An event related to animals, (herds of) animals, or animal products, related to the event's "what", "where", "when" "who" and "why"</td></tr>
