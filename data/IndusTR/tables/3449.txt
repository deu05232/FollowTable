During walking turns, step forward with the left foot, the right foot lifts in place then returns; on landing, externally rotate the right foot while turning clockwise 180°; then step forward again with the left foot, the right foot lifts in place then returns; on landing, externally rotate the right foot while turning clockwise 180°. Maintain elasticity throughout; arm swings are powerful with range; upper body keeps abdomen engaged and torso lifted</td></tr>

During step with back leg bend, fold the thigh and shank backward and upward with pointed toes; the knee joint is vertical to the ground; steps are elastic and landings are cushioned. Arm positions are clear and well-defined; coordination between arms and legs</td></tr>

During pendulum jumps, keep the body upright; one leg swings straight to the side while the other supports; alternate left and right swings; ensure one movement per beat. Keep the center of gravity over the supporting leg; jumps are light and elastic with buoyancy. Arm pathways are clear, accurate, powerful, and with range</td></tr>
