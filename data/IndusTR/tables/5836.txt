    <tr><td rowspan="3">Curves & Path Control</td><td>Forward Outside Edge Circle</td><td>Outside edge maintained; consistent lean; circle size stable; head looks along path</td><td>Complete 1 full circle (8 m diameter) without stepping; time ≤ 14 s</td><td>Deduct if circle expands/contracts noticeably; fail if stepping 2+ times</td></tr>
    <tr><td>S-Curve One-Foot Glide</td><td>Clear S-shaped track; free leg aligned; no shoulder over-rotation; smooth weight shifts</td><td>Complete 12 m course in ≤ 9.0 s (men) / ≤ 10.0 s (women); stay within 1 m corridor</td><td>Fail if leaving corridor or touching down with free foot</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Slalom with Cross-Step</td><td>Cross-step timing; feet do not overlap dangerously; cones passed cleanly; speed controlled</td><td>12 cones, 1.2 m spacing: finish ≤ 12.5 s; cone hits ≤ 1</td><td>Fail if 2+ cone hits or any missed cone</td></tr>
    <tr><td rowspan="3">Speed & Braking Control</td><td>30 m Sprint Glide</td><td>Efficient push; full extension; stable recovery; minimal upper-body swing</td><td>30 m time ≤ 8.5 s (men) / ≤ 9.2 s (women)</td><td>Fail if fall or if course boundary crossed</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Plow Stop Accuracy</td><td>Heels apart/toes in; knees bent; even pressure on both skates; controlled deceleration</td><td>From 15 m approach: stop within 1.5 m stop box; total time ≤ 5.5 s</td><td>Fail if stop occurs outside box or if skates cross</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Backward Glide Stability</td><td>Hips and shoulders square; eyes scanning; no excessive toe/heel rocking</td><td>Glide backward ≥ 12 m without stepping; remain within 0.8 m lane</td><td>Fail if stepping occurs more than once or lane exit happens</td></tr>
