  <tr><td>Intro</td><td>2-count prep bounce, then feet together ready position</td><td>Arms at sides, then lift to a relaxed bent guard</td><td>Set the posture before leaving the floor; keep the core lightly engaged so landings stay quiet and controlled.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>1×8</td><td>Small feet-together hops (8 hops)</td><td>Small forearm pulses close to the torso</td><td>Use elastic ankles and a soft knee bend; land under the hips to reduce impact; keep the rebound consistent rather than high.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>2×8</td><td>Hop left-right-left-right with minimal travel</td><td>Hands stay in front of the chest to stabilize</td><td>Each landing is toe-to-midfoot first with immediate cushioning; avoid knee collapse inward and keep weight centered.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>3×8</td><td>Two hops open stance, two hops feet together, repeat</td><td>Arms open/close matching the feet</td><td>Absorb impact evenly on both legs; keep the pelvis level; let the heels settle softly after the initial absorption.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>4×8</td><td>Run-jump switches in place (4 switches), then hold</td><td>Natural arm swing kept compact</td><td>Land softly on every switch with the same cushioning pattern; stay springy without stomping; finish by stabilizing the landing before the hold.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>5×8</td><td>Controlled deceleration: 4 hops decreasing height, then stand</td><td>Arms lower gradually to the sides</td><td>Show control by reducing bounce while keeping landings quiet; maintain knee bend and alignment until fully still.</td></tr>
