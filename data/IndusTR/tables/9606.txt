  <thead>
    <tr><th>Competency Area</th><th>Competency Item</th><th>Plain-Language Description (What it looks like on the job)</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Ethics & Professional Conduct</td><td>Integrity and Confidentiality</td><td>Handles sensitive project, funding, and organizational information responsibly; does not disclose data, interviews, or draft conclusions; documents decisions honestly and avoids altering findings to satisfy stakeholders.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Ethics & Professional Conduct</td><td>Conflict-of-Interest Management</td><td>Actively identifies personal, financial, or institutional interests that could bias an evaluation; declares them early; follows recusal and mitigation procedures; keeps a written record of disclosures and actions taken.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Methodology & Evidence</td><td>Evaluation Design</td><td>Translates evaluation questions into a practical plan (scope, indicators, methods, timeline, and responsibilities); ensures the design matches the purpose (ex-ante, mid-term, ex-post) and the decision needs of the client.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Methodology & Evidence</td><td>Data Collection and Verification</td><td>Uses multiple sources (documents, interviews, surveys, site visits, databases) and cross-checks them; detects inconsistencies; keeps traceable notes and evidence so another reviewer can follow how conclusions were formed.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Methodology & Evidence</td><td>Analytical Reasoning</td><td>Connects evidence to findings logically; distinguishes correlation from causation when possible; states assumptions and limitations; avoids over-claiming and explains uncertainty in understandable terms.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Domain & Policy Understanding</td><td>S&T and Innovation Policy Literacy</td><td>Understands common science and technology programs (R&D funding, labs, innovation platforms) and the policy goals behind them; can interpret how program rules and incentives affect behavior and outcomes.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Communication & Stakeholder Work</td><td>Stakeholder Interviewing</td><td>Plans and conducts interviews neutrally; asks clear questions, probes for evidence, and manages dominant voices; builds trust while maintaining professional distance; summarizes responses accurately for analysis.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Communication & Stakeholder Work</td><td>Report Writing for Decision-Makers</td><td>Produces concise reports with clear findings, supporting evidence, and actionable recommendations; separates facts from judgments; uses tables/figures to make results easy to scan for leaders and non-technical readers.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Project Delivery</td><td>Time and Quality Management</td><td>Manages schedules, version control, peer review, and sign-off; ensures consistency between data, analysis, and conclusions; delivers outputs on time while meeting agreed quality standards.</td></tr>
  </tbody>
