They are knowledge- and technology-intensive, consume fewer material resources, have strong growth potential, and deliver good comprehensive benefits. They represent the direction of a new round of scientific and technological revolution and industrial transformation, and are key fields for cultivating and developing new growth drivers and gaining new competitive advantages in the future. As China enters the decisive stage of building a moderately prosperous society in all respects, the connotation of industrial strategic emerging industries has also been continuously expanding. The national 14th Five-Year Plan proposes accelerating the growth of strategic emerging industries such as next-generation information technology, biotechnology, new energy, new materials, high-end equipment, new energy vehicles, green environmental protection, as well as aerospace and marine equipment. The National Bureau of Statistics has carried out statistical surveys and monitoring of strategic emerging industries, in which industrial strategic emerging industry statistical monitoring mainly adopts direct survey methods and indirect estimation methods. Increasing the share of total output value of industrial strategic emerging industries in total industrial output value is one of the core goals for promoting green growth and is of great significance for the evaluation of Beautiful China</td></tr>
