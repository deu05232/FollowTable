    <tr><td>Phrase 4 (49–64)</td><td>Feet-together jumps in place, then side-close jumps to change facing; finish with an open stance pulse.</td><td>Elastic bends/extends above the shoulder, then a clap-like swing action; return to strong set positions.</td><td>Maintain a steady pulse; arms sharp; do not let shoulders rise.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Phrase 5 (65–80)</td><td>March and cross toe-tap, side-close, then open stance and pulse twice before transition steps.</td><td>Cross fists at chest then diagonal raise; return to sides; repeat the pulse-arm pattern.</td><td>Toe-tap line extended; pulses controlled; keep breath consistent.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Phrase 6 (81–96)</td><td>Diagonal forward steps with hip drives, chest pulses, then half-squat body wave and jump to feet together.</td><td>Alternating hand crosses near forehead; push out from chest; hands to knees during body wave.</td><td>Wave is continuous; chest proud; jump ends clean with feet together.</td></tr>
