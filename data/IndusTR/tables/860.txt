  <tr><td>2×8</td><td>1–2 right and left feet alternately step to the diagonal front while driving right and left hips; 3–4 maintain an open stance, chest pulse twice; 5–6 bend both knees into a half squat, body waves once from right through front to the left side; 7–8 both legs jump up together to feet together, body upright</td><td>1–2 left and right hands alternately swing up and cross in front of the forehead, palms facing inward, flower-hand; 3–4 both hands at chest level push outward twice, palms facing outward; 5–6 hands support the knees, elbows out, moving with the body wave; 7–8 arms at the sides</td><td>Step the left foot to the left-diagonal front; on landing, drive the hip up-left and promptly transfer weight onto the left leg; the right leg slightly bends and lifts upward; hip drive is large but controlled; during the forward body circle, bend knees into a half squat; keep a proud chest and lifted head, twist to face point 2, use the left shoulder to drive the right shoulder to brace, creating opposing left-right forces; circle forward and to the right a half turn then return upright; throughout, maintain lifted head/chest and an engaged abdomen with a lifted waist; movements flow smoothly and continuously</td></tr>
  <tr><td>3×8</td><td>1–2 face point 3, step with the left foot then curl the right leg back; 3–4 face point 5, step with the right foot then curl the left leg back; 5–8 alternate left-right run-jump steps 4 times, face point 5</td><td>1–2 both arms swing from low in front to the left: left arm side horizontal raise, right arm horizontal bend in front of the chest, flower-hand, palm facing up; 3–4 arms swing via the sides to a diagonal side-up raise, flower-hand, palms facing inward; 5–8 arms straight, shake wrists from top to bottom 4 times, flower-hand</td><td rowspan="2">When doing step-then-hamstring-curl, fold the lower leg backward and upward with pointed toes, knee joint vertical to the ground; steps are springy with cushioned landings; turning direction accurate; arms swing powerfully in front, positions clear and well placed; coordinate hands and feet; in alternating run-jump steps, lift one leg with pointed toes while the other leg performs a small in-place hop; on landing, transition cushioning from toes to heels and switch legs; jumps should be relaxed and springy; throughout, keep the abdomen engaged and waist lifted, and face accurately</td></tr>
