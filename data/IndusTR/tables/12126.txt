  <caption>Pre-Climb Lock-Status Checklist for Ladder Use (Verification Steps and Pass/Fail Criteria)</caption>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Serial No.</th><th>Verification item</th><th>How to verify the lock is engaged</th><th>Pass criteria (Locked)</th><th>Fail criteria (Unlocked / not secured)</th><th>Action if fail</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>1</td><td>All locking mechanisms present</td><td>Inspect that required locking devices (pins, latches, hinge locks) are installed and not missing</td><td>All required locks present and undamaged</td><td>Missing/loose lock, cracked housing, bent pin</td><td>Remove from service; repair/replace before use</td></tr>
    <tr><td>2</td><td>Indicator shows LOCKED</td><td>Check any indicator window/marking shows the locked position after setup/height adjustment</td><td>Indicator reads “LOCK/LOCKED” or alignment mark matches locked position</td><td>Indicator reads “UNLOCKED” or mark misaligned/unclear</td><td>Re-engage lock; do not climb until confirmed</td></tr>
    <tr><td>3</td><td>Left/right symmetry (telescopic/section locks)</td><td>Verify locks on both sides of each rung/section are engaged (not just one side)</td><td>Both sides engaged on every extended rung/section</td><td>Any rung/section locked on one side only</td><td>Lower and re-extend; re-check both sides</td></tr>
    <tr><td>4</td><td>Physical engagement test</td><td>Apply a light reverse force (pull down/press fold direction) without climbing to confirm no movement occurs</td><td>No slip, no fold, no disengagement under light test force</td><td>Any slip, fold, or partial disengagement</td><td>Reset and retest; if repeated, tag out</td></tr>
    <tr><td>5</td><td>Lock release requires deliberate action</td><td>Attempt to move the mechanism without pressing the release button/lever (do not force)</td><td>Cannot be released without using the release control</td><td>Releases/moves freely without using release control</td><td>Do not use; service required</td></tr>
    <tr><td>6</td><td>Post-move recheck</td><td>After repositioning/carrying the ladder, recheck all locks (vibration can unset locks)</td><td>Locks still indicate locked; engagement test still passes</td><td>Any lock shifted to unlocked or feels loose</td><td>Re-engage and repeat checklist before climbing</td></tr>
    <tr><td>7</td><td>Contamination/obstruction at lock interface</td><td>Check for debris/ice/paint that prevents full seating of the lock</td><td>Contact surfaces clean; lock seats fully</td><td>Debris prevents full seating or causes partial lock</td><td>Clean/dry; re-lock and verify again</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
