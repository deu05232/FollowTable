<table>
  <caption>Specific Requirements for Instrument Fault Diagnostics Features</caption>
  <tr><td colspan="2">Instrument Category</td><td>Feature Requirements</td></tr>
  <tr><td colspan="2">Measuring Instruments</td><td>Provide a diagnostic information table with no fewer than 20 entries; in addition to the self-diagnosis requirements specified in 5.3.8, it shall at least cover power supply diagnosis, output feedback or loop current diagnosis, and communication status diagnosis</td></tr>
  <tr><td colspan="2">Analytical Instruments</td><td>Provide a diagnostic information table with no fewer than 20 entries; in addition to the self-diagnosis requirements specified in 5.3.8, it shall at least cover power supply diagnosis, output feedback or loop current diagnosis (exceptions for those without output functions), and communication status diagnosis</td></tr>
  <tr><td rowspan="2">Actuators</td><td>Electric/Electro-hydraulic Actuators</td><td>Provide a diagnostic information table with no fewer than 20 entries; in addition to the self-diagnosis requirements specified in 5.3.8, it shall at least cover excessive torque during opening/closing, valve stuck during opening/closing, mid-position alarm during opening/closing, communication status diagnosis, etc.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Pneumatic/Pneumatic-hydraulic Actuators and Their Matched Valve Positioners</td><td>Provide a diagnostic information table with no fewer than 20 entries; in addition to the self-diagnosis requirements specified in 5.3.8, by analyzing valve performance parameters (e.g., valve friction, seat shutoff force, valve linearity, hysteresis, spring elastic coefficient, etc.) and the condition of key components (e.g., packing, valve seat, stem, plug, spring, etc.), realize diagnostics for valve sticking, internal leakage, external leakage, actuator health status, etc.; some functions can be realized in conjunction with software systems</td></tr>
