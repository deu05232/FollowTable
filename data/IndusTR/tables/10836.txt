    <tr><td>Mixed plastic fraction (post-shredder, mostly polyolefins)</td><td>Screening → density separation (sink-float) → hot washing → drying → melt filtration → extrusion compounding → pelletizing</td><td>Compounded recycled polymer pellets</td><td>Injection-molded non-safety parts (underbody shields, wheel-arch liners), pallets/crates</td><td>Odor control and melt filtration are key for stable molding performance</td></tr>
    <tr><td>ASR light fluff (textiles/foams/plastics blend)</td><td>Drying → size reduction → air classification → removal of metals/minerals → densification (pellet/RDF) → controlled combustion in cement kiln</td><td>Refuse-derived fuel (RDF) pellets</td><td>Thermal substitution fuel for cement production (energy recovery with mineral incorporation)</td><td>Requires chlorine monitoring to protect kiln and emissions limits</td></tr>
    <tr><td>ASR mineral-rich fraction (glass, sand, fillers)</td><td>Screening → magnetic/eddy current removal → washing → stabilization/solidification → grading</td><td>Stabilized aggregate-like material</td><td>Road subbase / embankment fill (where regulations allow)</td><td>Leaching tests determine acceptable use; not suitable where high groundwater sensitivity</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Polyurethane seat foam (dismantled)</td><td>Sorting → shredding → rebonding with binder → pressing/curing → cutting</td><td>Rebond foam blocks/sheets</td><td>Carpet underlay, acoustic panels, packaging cushions</td><td>Clean dismantling avoids dirt/oil that weakens rebond products</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Mixed plastics with higher contamination</td><td>Pre-sorting → shredding → solvent-based dissolution/precipitation (selective) → solvent recovery → extrusion → pelletizing</td><td>Higher-purity polymer fraction</td><td>Higher-value molded goods (electrical housings, consumer durable parts) depending on polymer recovered</td><td>Capital intensive; solvent recovery efficiency drives economics</td></tr>
    <tr><td>ASR fraction containing brominated flame retardants (from legacy interiors)</td><td>Targeted separation (sensor sorting) → thermal treatment (high-temp incineration) → flue-gas cleaning → inert ash handling</td><td>Energy + treated residues</td><td>Energy recovery with compliant hazardous constituent destruction; residues to controlled landfill</td><td>Route chosen for regulatory compliance when material recycling is not feasible</td></tr>
