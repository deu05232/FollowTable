  <caption>Casting Structural Design Checklist (Recommendations Only)</caption>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Design Topic</th><th>Recommended Practice</th><th>Rationale / Benefit</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Wall thickness</td><td>Keep wall thickness as uniform as possible across the part</td><td>Promotes directional solidification and reduces shrinkage risk</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Thickness transitions</td><td>Use gradual tapers and generous fillets at section changes</td><td>Avoids hot spots and thermal gradients</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Ribs</td><td>Use multiple thin ribs rather than one thick rib</td><td>Maintains stiffness while limiting local mass</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Boss design</td><td>Core out bosses and connect to walls with fillets</td><td>Reduces isolated heavy sections and porosity</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Sharp corners</td><td>Round internal corners with adequate radii</td><td>Minimizes stress concentration and crack initiation</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Machining allowance</td><td>Specify machining allowances on functional surfaces</td><td>Ensures final dimensions and surface finish are achievable</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Draft angle</td><td>Provide sufficient draft on vertical surfaces</td><td>Improves mold release and reduces sand damage</td></tr>
