<table>
  <caption>Experience Requirements for Science & Technology Evaluation Personnel (industry practice variants)</caption>
  <tr><th>Experience Item</th><th>Description</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Field Investigation Participation</td><td>Has taken part in at least one on-site investigation or verification activity within an evaluation project, including evidence collection and interview support.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Survey / Questionnaire Work</td><td>Has experience designing or administering questionnaires for evaluation purposes and summarizing the responses into an evaluation memo.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Data & Indicator Work</td><td>Has supported building evaluation indicators, preparing data dictionaries, or cleaning and validating datasets used in evaluation.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Evaluation Reporting</td><td>Has drafted sections of an evaluation report (methods, findings, limitations) and participated in internal review cycles.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Stakeholder Communication</td><td>Has coordinated with evaluated units and third parties to obtain materials, schedule meetings, and document follow-ups.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Ethics/Compliance Training Attendance</td><td>Has completed structured training relevant to evaluation practice (e.g., conflict-of-interest handling, confidentiality, and compliance).</td></tr>
</table>
