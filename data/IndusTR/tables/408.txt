<table>
  <caption>Cumulative Increase Rate of the Share of Non-Fossil Energy in Total Energy Consumption Indicator</caption>
  <tr><td>Indicator Name (Unit)</td><td>Cumulative increase rate of the share of non-fossil energy in total energy consumption (%)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Indicator Code</td><td>C4</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Basic Definition</td><td>Compared with the base year, the cumulative increase in the share of non-fossil energy in total energy consumption</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Significance for Evaluating the Progress of Beautiful China Construction</td><td>Increasing the share of non-fossil energy use is a key measure for achieving the “dual carbon” goals. The State Council issued the “Action Plan for Carbon Dioxide Peaking Before 2030”, which states that by 2025, the share of non-fossil energy consumption will reach about 20%, and by 2030, it will reach about 25%. Increasing the share of non-fossil energy use is a key focus of energy structure transformation and is of great significance for the evaluation of Beautiful China construction</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Formula</td><td>C4 = C31 - C30 where C4 is the cumulative increase rate of the share of non-fossil energy in total energy consumption within the administrative region; C3 is the share of non-fossil energy consumption in the current year; C3 is the share of non-fossil energy consumption in the base year</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Data Source</td><td>Energy authorities</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Data Period</td><td>Current-status data are the 10 years before the evaluation year; target data are the 10 years after the evaluation year; annual</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Spatial Scale</td><td>Nationwide; provinces (autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the central government); prefecture-level administrative units</td></tr>
