  <tr><td>Indicator Name (Unit)</td><td>Ratio of Days with Good Air Quality in Prefecture-Level and Above Cities (%)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Indicator Code</td><td>C13</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Basic Connotation</td><td>According to HJ633—2012, the proportion of the sum of days with AQI reaching 0–50 (Level I, Excellent) and 51–100 (Level II, Good) to the number of days with valid monitoring</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Importance for Evaluating the Progress of Beautiful China Construction</td><td>Clean air is a basic requirement for Beautiful China construction. The proportion of days with good air quality is a core indicator for testing the effectiveness of air pollution prevention and control, and is also a core monitoring indicator of the Air Pollution Prevention and Control Action Plan (referred to as the “Air Ten Measures”), which is of great significance for the evaluation of Beautiful China construction</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Indicator Calculation Formula</td><td>C13 =BK/CK×100% where C13 is the proportion of days with good air quality within the administrative region; BK is the sum of the number of days in a calendar year with AQI reaching 0–50 (Level I, Excellent) and 51–100 (Level II, Good); CK is the number of days in a calendar year with valid air quality monitoring</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Data Source</td><td>Ecological and environmental departments (field monitoring data)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Data Time</td><td>Status data are for the 10 years prior to the evaluation year, and target data are for the 10 years after the evaluation year, on an annual basis</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Spatial Scale</td><td>National, provincial (autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government), prefecture-level administrative units</td></tr>
