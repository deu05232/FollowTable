  <tr><td>Stacking test</td><td>The specimen shall continuously withstand, for 24 h, the greater of the following compressive loads, unless the shape of the specimen can effectively prevent stacking: -Equivalent to 5 times the maximum mass of the package; Equivalent to the product of 13 kPa and the vertical projected area of the package</td></tr>
