  <tr><td>2×8</td><td>1—4 Left-foot cross step to the left; 5—8 Start with the right foot and complete one clockwise “three-step turn” to finish feet together</td><td>1—4 Arms lift upward in front of the body to left arm side lateral raise, open palm facing forward; right hand bent in front of chest, open palm facing back; 5—7 Right hand upright palm side lateral raise, left hand on hip; 8—Arms at sides</td><td>For the cross step, on the first step the toes point in the traveling direction and the heel lands first; the body’s center of gravity moves quickly. On the second step, the other foot crosses behind; then the right foot steps again to the same side, and the right foot closes with a knee-bent touch, maintaining the bounce of the knee and ankle joints. During turning, the toes alternately open outward and turn inward along the turning direction; step size should not be too large or too small. Arm movements are strong with large range; upper body keeps abdomen engaged and torso lifted</td></tr>
  <tr><td>3×8</td><td>1—4 "V" step (start with left foot); 5—6 Left side step-together; 7—8 Left hip shift hop</td><td>1—Left arm raises diagonally upward to the side, right arm bent above shoulder, open palm facing forward; 2—Same as 1, opposite direction; 3—Left arm side lateral raise, right arm bent horizontally in front of chest, palms together with palms facing down; 4—Same as 3, opposite direction; 5—Right hand extends forward through bent arm to front raise, flower hand palm facing up, left hand at side; 6—Left hand extends forward with bent arm crossing above the right hand, flower hand; 7—Arms bent above shoulders, open palms facing forward; 8—Return to sides</td><td>During the “V" step, both feet step in turn to diagonal forward directions, then both knees bend into a half squat; the center of gravity shifts slightly back; movement is elastic with cushioned landings. During hip shift hops, feet together pass through a half squat and push off the ground to jump; on landing, form a right-leg straight leg with the right heel on the ground, left knee bent while the hip joint forcefully thrusts to the left; center of gravity slightly shifts to the left; keep the left knee joint above the left foot. Keep the body upright throughout; steps are elastic; arm pathways and positions are clear; movements are powerful with large range, coordinated and extended</td></tr>
  <tr><td>4×8</td><td>1—4 Alternating left and right backward step-together jumps 1 time each, facing point 7 and point 2 respectively; 5-6 Start with left foot and do marching turn 270°; 7—8 Jump to a straddle stance</td><td>1—2 Right arm bent in front of chest, left arm lowered diagonally to the side, fists with palms facing out; 3—4 Same as 1—2, opposite direction; 5—6 Arms lower diagonally to the sides; 7—8 Right arm moves from low through front to overhead, one finger, left hand on hip</td><td>Step-together jump: powerful takeoff; in the air, actively squeeze the inner thighs to bring legs together; land with knees bent for cushioning; body is controlled. During turning, the crossing range of the two feet is large; control the body and keep the center of gravity stable; the finishing posture is elegant</td></tr>
