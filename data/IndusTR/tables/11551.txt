    <tr><td>Cumulative reduction rate of carbon intensity relative to a base year (%)</td><td>C1-E</td><td>(Carbon intensity in base year − carbon intensity in current year) / carbon intensity in base year × 100%.</td><td>Links directly to policy commitments expressed as reductions versus a benchmark year; clarifies long-term trend.</td></tr>

    <tr><td>GDP deflator/constant price requirement (qualifier)</td><td>NOTE-4</td><td>Use constant-price GDP (or explicitly stated deflator method) when computing intensity.</td><td>Avoids artificial improvements due to inflation, making carbon-intensity change interpretable as real efficiency/structure improvement.</td></tr>
