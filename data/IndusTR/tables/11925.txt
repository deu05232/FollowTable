    <tr><td>Odor (after heating)</td><td>No burnt, chemical, or spoiled odor</td><td>Heat to serving temperature, cover for 1 min, then smell upon uncovering.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Surface condition</td><td>No slime, no mold spots, no sticky film</td><td>Touch with gloved finger; check for tackiness; visually confirm under light.</td></tr>
