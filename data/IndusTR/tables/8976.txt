    <tr><th>Feature Topic</th><th>Unfavorable (Bad)</th><th>Preferred (Good)</th><th>Why It Matters</th></tr>

    <tr><td>Section uniformity</td><td>Large abrupt section changes</td><td>Gradual transitions</td><td>Improves metal flow and reduces laps</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Ribs and bosses</td><td>Very tall slender ribs</td><td>Moderate ribs with generous blends</td><td>Reduces underfill risk</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Pockets/cavities</td><td>Deep narrow pockets</td><td>Shallow wide pockets</td><td>Lowers risk of sticking and die wear</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Inside corners</td><td>Sharp re-entrant corners</td><td>Rounded corners</td><td>Avoids stress concentration and improves fill</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Parting line choice</td><td>Parting line crossing small local features</td><td>Parting line near maximum outline</td><td>Helps demolding and reduces mismatch sensitivity</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Symmetry</td><td>Highly asymmetric outlines</td><td>Symmetric or near-symmetric shapes</td><td>Improves material distribution</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Machining allowance</td><td>Nonuniform machining stock</td><td>Uniform stock where possible</td><td>Stabilizes finishing operations</td></tr>
