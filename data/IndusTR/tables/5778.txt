    <tr><td>Hydrology</td><td>Lake Inflow Variability Index</td><td>Describes inter-annual fluctuation of lake inflow relative to a baseline period; reflects regulation and climate variability.</td><td>Hydrometric stations; modelled inflow series</td><td>Higher values often imply stronger alteration; local context (dams, diversions) can dominate signal.</td><td>Adjust reservoir operation rules; reduce diversion peaks; implement environmental flow releases.</td></tr>
