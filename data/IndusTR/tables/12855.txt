<table>
  <caption>Cybersecurity Incident Reporting Elements (Narrative Descriptions)</caption>
  <tr><th>Information Item</th><th>Description</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Incident Name</td><td>A short title used to reference the incident consistently across reports and tickets.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Affected Asset</td><td>The system, service, or application impacted (e.g., hostname, business system name), written in a way stakeholders can recognize.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Impact Description</td><td>Plain-language explanation of what was affected (availability, integrity, confidentiality) and what users experienced.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Detection Method</td><td>How the issue was identified (alert rule, user report, log review, third-party notification) and any relevant context.</td></tr>
</table>
