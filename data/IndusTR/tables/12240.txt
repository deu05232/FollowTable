  <tr><td>2×8</td><td>Diagonal steps with controlled hip accents (right-left-right-left)</td><td>Hands trace an arc in front of the face, then push outward at chest height</td><td>Hip accents should be large but controlled; the chest stays proud and lifted, and the rhythm is clear without collapsing through the ribs.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>3×8</td><td>Open stance, half-squat, body wave from right through front to left; recover to standing</td><td>Hands support the knees during the wave, then release to the sides</td><td>Make the body wave continuous and smooth; keep the head lifted to maintain style; coordinate torso timing with the music rather than focusing on power.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>4×8</td><td>March forward 4 counts, march back 4 counts, keeping the same facing point</td><td>Alternating overhead reach with a soft wrist finish</td><td>Steps stay light and even; arms should finish in clear shapes with consistent height and clean pathways.</td></tr>
