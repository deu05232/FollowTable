On right hip thrust, both legs bend with right knee forward and left knee to the side; the hip joint presses down-right then sits back.   <tr><td>2×8</td><td>1—left foot back step small hop; da—right foot small hop in place; 2—left foot steps forward to close with the right foot into feet together; 3—left knee-lift jump; 4—left foot lands, jump to feet together; 5–7 left foot side step, hips trace a “8” shape; 8—right foot steps forward while turning left 90 into straddle half squat, face point 7</td><td>1–2 arms cross in front and circle from down to up to bent behind the head, open palms supporting the head; 3—arms extend to the sides and push outward to side raises, vertical palms outward; 4—arms up and clap; 5–7 hands clasp in front of the body and trace an “8"” from upper right–upper left–lower left–upper right; 8—arms chest-level front bend pull to the sides</td><td>In the knee-lift jump, lift the bent knee up parallel to the floor with pointed toes; the other foot small hops in place; cushion on landing. Keep the torso engaged with core tight and waist lifted; jumps should be relaxed and springy. The hip “8"” action should be smooth with large range; keep both legs bent. Lead with the left hip to circle left-front and left-back, then lead with the right hip to circle right-front and right-back, forming an “8"”. Keep the body upright and follow the hips to circle; movement is smooth and coordinated</td></tr>
  <tr><td>3×8</td><td>1—left foot steps forward, face point 7; 2—right foot small front kick, body turns to point 1; 3–4 after the right foot lands, left foot steps forward and begins one exchange-leg jump upward (left takeoff, left landing) once; 5—right foot steps forward; 6—left front kick jump while turning 270° counterclockwise; 7—jump and land into half squat, face point 1; 8—jump to feet together</td><td>1—left arm diagonal side down, right arm front-diagonal-down, fists knuckles down; 2—right arm circles back to shoulder-side bend, elbow level with the tip, flower-hand palm back; left arm holds the right waist; 3–4 arms circle from the right via down to the left, ending with left arm bent in front of the body; right arm bent slides past the face; 5—arms at the sides; 6—hands clasp in front of the abdomen; 7—hands support the knees with the web between thumb and index finger inward; 8—arms at the sides</td><td>In the front kick, straighten with pointed toes; kick height does not exceed 30°. In the airborne small jump, single takeoff and single landing; knees bend and lift high; steps are light; land on the balls of the feet with cushioning</td></tr>
  <tr><td>4×8</td><td>1—right foot steps forward, head down; 2—left foot steps forward; 3—left foot small kick in front of the right foot; 4—jump to open stance half squat; 5–6 open stance half squat; 7—left side lunge jump; 8—jump to feet together</td><td>1—left arm chest-level front bend, fist knuckles down, right arm at the side; 2—left arm at the side, right arm moves from back via front up to overhead, one finger; 3—right arm chest-level front bend, fist knuckles down, head tilts to the left; 4—right arm opens to side raise, fist knuckles down, left arm at the side; 5–6 right arm side raise, chest pop 2 times, fist knuckles down; 7–8 right arm circles in front of the body from right via up to left, to the side</td><td>In the small kick, the free leg is straight with pointed toes, quickly kicking to the left-front; steps are clear and firm.
