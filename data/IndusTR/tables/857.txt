  <tr><td>4×8</td><td>1–4 step with the right foot and lift the knee once, face point 2; 5–6 face point 3, step right to the side, left foot side toe-tap, shift weight to the right foot; 7–8 left foot back closes with a jump to stand upright, face point 1</td><td>1–4 fists, alternate arm swings 3 times then drop to the sides; 5–6 right arm straight overhead, left arm straight to a side horizontal raise, palms together; 7–8 arms drop to the sides</td><td>Step-and-knee-lift: step with one foot, lift the other knee up with the thigh parallel to the ground; the support leg stays straight with the knee extended, land with cushioning; for side toe-taps, keep the torso braced with abdomen engaged and waist lifted, turn the body to point 3, step the right foot back, keep the left leg straight and land on the inner side of the big toe, keep the instep in a straight extension line with the lower leg and thigh; arm positions are clear and strong, upper and lower limbs coordinated. Keep the torso braced and center of gravity lifted</td></tr>
