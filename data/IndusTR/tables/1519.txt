<table>
  <caption>Sensory requirements for Chaozhou-style mooncakes</caption>
  <tr><td>Item</td><td>Requirements</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Appearance</td><td>Round and regular shape, flat top and bottom, surface slightly flattened and domed; bottom seam centered with no leakage; no exposed flaky pastry, collapse/tilting, sugar leakage, or exposed filling</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Color</td><td>With the characteristic color of the variety, not stained with miscellaneous colors, no contamination</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Texture</td><td>Distinct flaky layers; even crust-to-filling thickness; filling not underbaked and without large voids; for paste/moist-sand mooncakes and fruit-and-vegetable mooncakes, the filling is soft, oily and moist; for nut mooncakes, the filling is loose and not tough, with nuts evenly distributed</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Flavor and mouthfeel</td><td>With the characteristic flavor of the variety, no off-odor</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Foreign matter</td><td>No visible foreign matter under normal vision</td></tr>
</table>
