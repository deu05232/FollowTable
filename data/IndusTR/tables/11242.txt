    <tr><td>9. Melt Filtration & Extrusion</td><td>Homogenized flakes (single polymer stream)</td><td>Extrusion with screen pack / melt filter</td><td>Filtered polymer melt</td><td>Filtration removes fine solids/paint chips remaining after washing</td></tr>
