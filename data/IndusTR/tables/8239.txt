  <tr><td>Frozen (≤ -18°C)</td><td>Frozen ground meat, frozen patties</td><td>Frosting level, freezer burn spots</td><td>Dull odor if oxidized; drier texture after cooking</td><td>Inspect ice crystals, note discoloration, cook from thawed state, compare juiciness vs control</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Vacuum-packed</td><td>Vacuum steaks, vacuum ham</td><td>Darkened bloom before airing, uniform slice</td><td>Slight package odor that dissipates; elastic texture</td><td>Air 10 min for bloom, sniff before/after airing, press for springiness</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Modified Atmosphere (MAP)</td><td>MAP minced meat, MAP sausages</td><td>Stable red/pink color, low drip</td><td>Clean aroma; no sour notes; cohesive bite</td><td>Check headspace integrity, observe color stability, sniff at opening, chew and rate cohesiveness</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Fermented (Low pH)</td><td>Fermented salami, fermented sausage</td><td>Deep red interior, white surface mold acceptable</td><td>Tangy aroma; acidic taste; chewy-dry</td><td>Inspect surface mold type, smell after slicing, taste small piece and record sourness</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Dehydrated/Dried</td><td>Jerky, dried beef strips</td><td>Dark brown color, uniform drying</td><td>Roasted notes; very tough chew; salty finish</td><td>Bend/tear test, sniff after tearing, chew 10 times and rate toughness</td></tr>
