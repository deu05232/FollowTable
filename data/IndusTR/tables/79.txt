<table>
  <caption>Appearance Quality Requirements for Coatings Films on Suspended Ceiling Panels</caption>
  <tr><td>Category</td><td>Appearance quality requirements</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Roll coating</td><td>There shall be no missed coating, ripples, or blistering.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Liquid spraying</td><td>The coating shall be free of runs, cracks, bubbles, inclusions, or other surface defects.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Powder spraying</td><td>The coating shall be smooth and uniform; wrinkles, runs, blistering, cracking, and tackiness are not permitted.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Laminating</td><td>No pinholes, fisheyes, blistering, creases, impurity marks, bubbles, burrs, water marks, delamination, peeling, film wrinkling, or film scratches, etc.; the pattern shall have no differences.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Anodizing</td><td>Defects such as pits, scratches, electrical burn marks, and anodic film peeling or cracking are not permitted.</td></tr>
</table>
