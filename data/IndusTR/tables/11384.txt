<table>
  <caption>Experience-Based Requirements by Role Level for Science & Technology Evaluation Personnel</caption>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Role level</th><th>Required training / certification experience</th><th>Evaluation task participation (examples of deliverables)</th><th>Research / method work experience</th><th>Governance & documentation experience</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Entry participant</td><td>Completed at least 1 introductory course on S&T evaluation standards, methods, and procedures (e.g., indicator design, survey basics, confidentiality briefings)</td><td>Participated in at least 1 sub-task such as desk review, data cleaning, or assisting an on-site visit; produced a simple deliverable (minutes, evidence checklist, basic data table)</td><td>Assisted in literature review or benchmarking for an evaluation study; contributed notes or reference lists</td><td>Read and followed an evaluation handbook or SOP; no drafting responsibility required</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Independent evaluator</td><td>Completed systematic training covering at least: evaluation design, sampling/survey, on-site verification, and reporting; passed an internal assessment or workshop exam</td><td>Deep participation across multiple phases (e.g., planning → data collection → analysis → reporting) with responsibility for at least 1 work package such as survey implementation or interview plan; authored a section of the final report</td><td>Participated in at least 1 evaluation-related research project (methods, tool development, or impact analysis) with documented contribution</td><td>Contributed to drafting or updating working documents (templates, scoring rubrics, evidence rules) used in projects</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Project workstream lead</td><td>Completed advanced training (e.g., causal inference / bibliometrics / multi-criteria decision methods) or equivalent professional development hours</td><td>Led a complete module (e.g., indicator system design, on-site investigation team, expert panel process); coordinated team outputs and quality checks; signed off module deliverables</td><td>Co-led a research task (method validation, pilot testing, toolchain development) and presented findings internally or at a workshop</td><td>Drafted normative project documents (evaluation plan, review protocol, conflict-of-interest checklist) for adoption in at least 1 project</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Project leader</td><td>Maintained continuing education; facilitated training as an instructor or mentor for junior evaluators</td><td>Led end-to-end evaluation (scope, method selection, stakeholder engagement, reporting); managed timeline/budget; handled disputes and change requests; delivered final report to commissioning body</td><td>Led an evaluation research project (grant/contract) related to methods or governance; produced a publicly available report, working paper, or internal standard</td><td>Led drafting of guidance documents or standards (e.g., evaluation guidelines, standard indicator library) used across multiple projects</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Senior / major project director</td><td>Recognized as trainer, reviewer, or auditor of evaluation practice; completed specialized compliance training (procurement, audit, data protection)</td><td>Directed major or high-stakes evaluations; chaired steering committees; approved methodological choices and final conclusions; oversaw external peer review</td><td>Led major research programs on S&T evaluation (multi-year / multi-partner) and established reusable methods or platforms</td><td>Led drafting of laws/regulations/standards or sector-wide normative documents; coordinated multi-stakeholder consultation and revisions</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Methodology & quality assurance specialist</td><td>Completed specialized QA training (audit techniques, reproducibility, traceability) and tool training (statistical software / bibliometric tools)</td><td>Reviewed project designs and reports for methodological rigor; conducted reproducibility checks; issued QA findings and corrective actions</td><td>Developed or validated evaluation instruments (rubrics, survey instruments, coding schemes) and documented reliability/validity testing</td><td>Authored QA procedures, review checklists, and version-controlled templates adopted by the organization</td></tr>
  </tbody>
