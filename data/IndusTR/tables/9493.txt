  <thead>
    <tr><th>Competency Area</th><th>Behavioral/Ethical Expectation (Observable Indicators)</th><th>Common Risks to Avoid</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Impartiality</td><td>Uses consistent criteria across evaluation objects; avoids favoritism; documents rationale for ratings and recommendations.</td><td>Preferential treatment; selectively citing evidence.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Independence</td><td>Declares conflicts of interest; refuses undue influence; maintains professional distance from evaluated parties throughout the process.</td><td>Undisclosed relationships; accepting gifts or hospitality that may bias judgment.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Fairness and Equal Treatment</td><td>Ensures comparable information is collected for all parties; applies procedures uniformly; allows reasonable opportunities for response.</td><td>Unequal evidence thresholds; procedural shortcuts for certain subjects.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Evidence-Based Judgment</td><td>Verifies sources; distinguishes facts from opinions; triangulates data from multiple channels before concluding.</td><td>Overreliance on single-source claims; confirmation bias.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Confidentiality and Data Protection</td><td>Limits access to sensitive information; anonymizes when required; follows secure storage and transmission practices.</td><td>Informal sharing; using unapproved personal devices or channels.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Integrity and Anti-Corruption</td><td>Reports improper requests; follows anti-bribery rules; avoids quid-pro-quo interactions and hidden benefits.</td><td>Kickbacks; “revolving door” arrangements; undisclosed sponsorships.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Accountability and Traceability</td><td>Keeps complete records of methods, evidence, and decisions; can explain judgment steps to oversight bodies.</td><td>Missing documentation; unverifiable scoring logic.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Professional Respect and Non-Discrimination</td><td>Engages stakeholders respectfully; avoids discriminatory language; considers diverse viewpoints without prejudice.</td><td>Bias against certain institutions, regions, disciplines, or teams.</td></tr>
