    <tr><td>Upper-body control</td><td>Arm/torso connection; chest posture</td><td>Arms keep opponent tight to trunk; chest stays proud (no forward fold) while loading</td><td>Arms extend away; bends at waist causing loss of load and counter risk</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Force direction</td><td>Lift and rotation vector</td><td>Force is upward through legs then rotating over the hip; no pure forward bend pull</td><td>Pulls straight down/forward; rotation occurs without lift, leading to stumble</td></tr>

    <tr><td>Repetition / consistency</td><td>Quality across attempts</td><td>Meets pass thresholds for ≥4 of 5 attempts within the evaluation window</td><td>Large variability; meets criteria in ≤3 of 5 attempts</td></tr>
