<table>
  <caption>Elements for the Level and Classification of Vulnerability Information</caption>
  <tr><td>Information Item</td><td>Description</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Discovery Time</td><td>In seconds</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Vulnerability Type</td><td>Information on the classification attribution of the vulnerability; for vulnerability types and classification methods, see GB/T30279</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Vulnerability Severity Level</td><td>Information on the severity grading attribution of the vulnerability; vulnerability severity levels can be divided into four levels: critical, high, medium, and low; for grading methods, see GB/T 30279</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Vulnerability Description</td><td>A detailed description of the vulnerability, which may include the triggering conditions of the vulnerability, the generation mechanism or proof of existence, methods for vulnerability scanning or testing, and other relevant information that needs to be explained</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Potentially Affected Objects</td><td>The organization to which the product or service with the vulnerability belongs</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Supporting Materials</td><td>Including analysis reports, verification tools, etc. related to this vulnerability</td></tr>
