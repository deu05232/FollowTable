  <tr><td>SSR provider ID</td><td>Used to identify the SSR service and shall be unique globally: where: 0～255: reserved; 256～65535: assignable provider ID number</td></tr>
  <tr><td>SSR solution ID</td><td>Used to identify different SSR solutions of a given provider</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Number of satellites</td><td>Represents the number of satellites</td></tr>
  <tr><td rowspan="2">Data field</td><td>Satellite number</td><td>Represents BDS, GPS, GALILEO, GLONASS satellite ID</td></tr>
  <tr><td>SSR URA</td><td>URA accuracy (one-sigma) of the SSR range correction value; this value is calculated from the complete SSR data in the RTCMSSR message; the meaning of this field and the calculation formula shall be implemented in accordance with the provisions of A.1 in Annex A of GB/T 44175.2—2024</td></tr>
