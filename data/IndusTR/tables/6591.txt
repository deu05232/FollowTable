<table>
  <caption>Time Elements to Capture When Reporting a Cybersecurity Incident</caption>
  <tr><th>Information Item</th><th>Description</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Occurrence Time</td><td>When the incident actually happened (as precisely as known)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Discovery Time</td><td>When the incident was first detected by monitoring or personnel</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Reporting Time</td><td>When the incident was first reported internally to the security team</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Escalation Time</td><td>When the incident was escalated to management or an external authority</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Containment Time</td><td>When containment actions first took effect</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Resolution Time</td><td>When the incident was considered resolved/closed</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Time Zone / Time Format</td><td>Time zone used and format (e.g., ISO 8601) to avoid ambiguity</td></tr>
</table>
