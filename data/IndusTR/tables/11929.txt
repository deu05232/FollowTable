<table>
  <caption>Sensory evaluation items and methods for cooked meat products (including taste)</caption>
  <tr><th>Item</th><th>Content</th><th>Evaluation Method</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Color</td><td>Muscle color and fat color of the sample surface and cut surface</td><td>Observe with normal vision under natural light or equivalent lighting</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Odor</td><td>Characteristic odor and any off-odor before and after cooking</td><td>Hold the sample below the nostrils and inhale gently 2–3 times</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Taste</td><td>Characteristic taste after heating; no bitter, sour, or abnormal taste</td><td>Place a small bite in the mouth, chew for 3–5 s, evaluate flavor with tongue and teeth, then spit out and rinse the mouth</td></tr>
</table>
