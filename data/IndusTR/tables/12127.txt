  <tbody>
    <tr><td>LC-01</td><td>Hinge lock (folding joint)</td><td>Lock indicator window shows “LOCK” or green mark; locking pin fully seated through the hinge hole</td><td>No visible gap; pin cannot be pulled out by hand</td><td>Keep hands clear of hinge pinch points while engaging the lock</td></tr>
    <tr><td>LC-02</td><td>Side rail extension lock (spring latch)</td><td>Audible “click”; latch tooth fully engaged in the nearest slot; latch returns by spring force</td><td>Latch tooth is fully inside the slot; no partial engagement</td><td>Do not release the section until the click and visual engagement are confirmed</td></tr>
    <tr><td>LC-03</td><td>Telescopic rung section lock (dual buttons)</td><td>Both left and right buttons pop out flush; colored ring disappears (if present)</td><td>Both sides indicate locked simultaneously</td><td>Never lock only one side—uneven locking can cause sudden collapse</td></tr>
    <tr><td>LC-04</td><td>Automatic locking device (self-lock)</td><td>Device returns to lock position without manual holding; indicator shows locked state</td><td>Lock engages automatically after adjustment</td><td>Still perform a visual check—automatic does not mean verified</td></tr>
    <tr><td>LC-05</td><td>Manual locking device (lever/cam)</td><td>Lever fully rotated to the “LOCK” stop; cam sits tight against the rail</td><td>Lever cannot be moved back without deliberate force</td><td>Do not use tools to force the lever; inspect for misalignment instead</td></tr>
    <tr><td>LC-06</td><td>Sliding section retention lock</td><td>Pull test: apply a short downward pull on the moving section; it must not slip</td><td>No movement under a firm hand pull</td><td>Perform the pull test while maintaining stable footing and clear hands</td></tr>
    <tr><td>LC-07</td><td>Lock engagement symmetry (paired locks)</td><td>Left/right locks show the same position and indicator color</td><td>Both sides match “LOCK”</td><td>Do not climb if indicators disagree—re-adjust and re-lock</td></tr>
