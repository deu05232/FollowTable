<table>
  <caption>Sensory requirements for Cantonese-style mooncakes (appearance, color, texture)</caption>
  <tr><th>Item</th><th>Requirements</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Appearance</td><td>Neatly shaped; clear stamp/pattern; no cracking, deformation, collapse, or filling leakage; no scorched spots or blistering</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Color</td><td>Characteristic golden-brown surface; even coloring; no greenish tint; free from foreign discoloration and contamination</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Texture (crust)</td><td>Crust is fine and even; no raw flour taste; not overly hard; no separation between crust and filling</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Texture (filling)</td><td>Filling is smooth and delicate; not underbaked; no gritty particles; for egg-yolk types, yolk is centered and fully wrapped</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Odor and taste</td><td>Normal aroma of the variety; no rancid, sour, or other off-odors; taste is coordinated without bitterness</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Defects not allowed</td><td>Mold, foreign matter, obvious sandiness, excessive oil seepage, and any signs of spoilage</td></tr>
</table>
