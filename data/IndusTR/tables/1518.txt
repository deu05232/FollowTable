<table>
  <caption>Sensory requirements for Cantonese-style mooncakes</caption>
  <tr><td>SAC Item</td><td>Requirements</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Appearance</td><td>Plump appearance, distinct contours, clear patterns, no spreading/collapsing, no sugar leakage or filling leakage</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Color</td><td>With the characteristic color of the variety</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Texture</td><td>Even crust thickness; no separation between crust and filling; filling not underbaked; for nut mooncakes, nut particle size is appropriate; for egg-yolk mooncakes, the filling should completely wrap the egg yolk; for molten-core mooncakes, the filling has certain flowability</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Flavor and mouthfeel</td><td>Soft and tender crust, with the characteristic flavor of the variety, no off-odor</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Foreign matter</td><td>No visible foreign matter under normal vision</td></tr>
</table>
