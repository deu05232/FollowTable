  <tr><td>Local thick pad</td><td>Replace pad with ribs or hollow out the pad</td><td>Solid pad thicker than surrounding wall</td><td>Center of pad (internal void)</td></tr>

  <tr><td>Closed ring / thick loop</td><td>Keep section uniform; introduce relief to avoid thick loops</td><td>Thick closed loop that cools last</td><td>Inside the loop (hot spot)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Boss-to-wall connection</td><td>Use cored boss and thin web connection</td><td>Solid boss with heavy root fillet radius creating mass</td><td>Boss root / underside</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Thick-to-thin transition</td><td>Use long taper and avoid abrupt shoulders</td><td>Shoulder step and sharp section jump</td><td>Thick side near the shoulder</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Corner massing</td><td>Relieve the corner and avoid stacking thickness</td><td>Stacked thickness at corners</td><td>Corner interior (shrinkage porosity)</td></tr>
