    <tr><td>Coordination / Breathing</td><td>Block completes before punch launches: clear separation of phases; exhale/kime (focus) on the final moment of the punch without stiffening early.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Targeting / Line</td><td>Block covers an attack line: forearm ends at correct height (e.g., temple/cheek level); punch aims to a defined target (midline chest/solar plexus) with correct height.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Power Transmission</td><td>Ground-to-fist linkage: push from the rear foot, hips connect to shoulder and fist; impact is stable without losing stance integrity.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Finish / Control</td><td>Ends in a stable stance with guard/chamber correct: no extra steps; hands stop cleanly with no overextension; balance maintained for at least one second.</td></tr>
