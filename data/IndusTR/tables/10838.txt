  <caption>Reuse Routes for Metals Recovered from End-of-Life Road Vehicles (ELVs)</caption>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Recovered Stream</th><th>Pre-treatment & Conditioning</th><th>Transformation / Treatment Route (Stepwise)</th><th>Main Product / End Use</th><th>Key Notes (Quality & Constraints)</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Ferrous scrap (body-in-white, frames)</td><td>Depollution → dismantling of bulky parts → shredder feed preparation</td><td>Shredding → magnetic separation → scrap sizing → EAF/BOF melting → refining/degassing → continuous casting → hot rolling</td><td>Steel coil/plate for construction sections, rebar, or new automotive-grade sheet (after refining)</td><td>Cu contamination from wiring can limit high-grade sheet; needs good dismantling and sorting</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Cast aluminum (engine blocks, housings)</td><td>De-oiling/degreasing → paint removal (thermal or chemical) → size reduction</td><td>Sorting by alloy family → melting in reverberatory furnace → fluxing/degassing → filtration → alloy adjustment → casting (ingots or foundry feed)</td><td>Secondary Al ingots for die-cast parts (gearbox housings, brackets) or general foundry products</td><td>Mixed alloys raise Fe/Si; filtration and alloying needed to meet spec</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Wrought aluminum (doors, hoods)</td><td>Dismantling (panel separation) → decoating</td><td>Closed-loop segregation → melting → refining → rolling slab casting → hot/cold rolling → heat treatment</td><td>Rolled sheet for new panels, appliance sheet, or packaging stock</td><td>Requires tight segregation for true closed-loop recycling</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Copper from wiring harness</td><td>Harness removal → insulation separation preparation</td><td>Granulation → air separation (plastics) → electrostatic separation → smelting/refining (if needed)</td><td>Copper granules/cathode for electrical conductors, new wiring, motors</td><td>Residual plastics/oils affect smelting; dry and clean feed improves yield</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Zinc-coated steel fines (galvanized scrap)</td><td>Dust control → fraction sizing</td><td>Melting in EAF → zinc volatilization → off-gas capture → dust collection → Waelz/other treatment of dust → zinc oxide recovery</td><td>Steel product plus ZnO for zinc smelter feed or rubber/ceramics additive</td><td>EAF dust is hazardous; requires controlled capture and downstream processing</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Lead from starter batteries (vehicle depollution stream)</td><td>Battery removal → draining/neutralization of electrolyte</td><td>Battery breaking → separation (lead grids/paste/plastics) → desulfurization of paste → smelting → refining → alloying</td><td>Refined lead alloys for new starter batteries or radiation shielding products</td><td>Strict environmental controls; electrolyte handling is critical</td></tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
