  <caption>Specific Requirements for Instrument Self-Learning Features</caption>
  <tr><td>Instrument Category</td><td>Feature Requirements</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Measuring Instruments</td><td>Meets the following requirements: a) supports model libraries for multiple scenarios; measures standard quantities under the corresponding scenarios and can output accurately (meeting the instrument’s nominal requirements); b) under alternating conditions at the upper and lower ambient temperature limits, the output meets the instrument’s nominal requirements; c) when the scenario changes dynamically, the dynamic changes of instrument parameters can be viewed via specific tools or software</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Analytical Instruments</td><td>Meets the following requirements: a) can automatically adjust its measurement algorithm based on historical data to improve accuracy; b) automatically optimizes instrument parameters to adapt to different measurement conditions and environmental changes; c) identifies and learns typical patterns and trends during online analysis processes; d) predicts the time for equipment maintenance and component replacement;</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Actuators</td><td>e) supports model libraries for multiple scenarios or media and can measure accurately in various different scenarios or media; can automatically recognize different operating-condition application scenarios, proactively select the corresponding output model and algorithm, and dynamically adjust tuning parameters to achieve valve optimization and loop optimization, etc.</td></tr>
