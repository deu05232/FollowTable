<table>
  <caption>Share of Green and High-Quality Agricultural Product Production Area in Edible Agricultural Products Indicator</caption>
  <tr><td>Indicator Name (Unit)</td><td>Share of green and high-quality agricultural product production area in edible agricultural products (%)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Indicator Code</td><td>C55</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Basic Definition</td><td>Refers to the proportion of the production area of green and high-quality agricultural products in crop farming to the arable land area for edible agricultural products. Green and high-quality agricultural products include green food, organic agricultural products, geographical indication agricultural product production bases, and the production area of high-quality agricultural products at the ministry/provincial level</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Significance for Evaluating the Progress of Beautiful China Construction</td><td>The share of green and high-quality agricultural product production area in edible agricultural products reflects the development level of green and high-quality agricultural products in a country or region. Increasing this share is one of the core goals for promoting green agricultural development and supporting rural revitalization and Beautiful China construction, and it is of great significance for the evaluation of Beautiful China</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Formula</td><td>C55 = LS/BG X100% where C55 is the share of green and high-quality agricultural product production area in edible agricultural products; LS is the production area of green and high-quality agricultural products in crop farming; BG is the arable land area for edible agricultural products</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Data Source</td><td>Agriculture and rural affairs authorities</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Data Period</td><td>Current-status data are the 10 years before the evaluation year; target data are the 10 years after the evaluation year; annual</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Spatial Scale</td><td>Nationwide; provinces (autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the central government); prefecture-level administrative units</td></tr>
</table>
