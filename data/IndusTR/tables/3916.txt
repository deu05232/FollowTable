<table>
  <caption>Criteria for Determining Temperature Sensitivity of Foam Concentrates</caption>
  <tr><td>Item</td><td>Determination criteria</td></tr>
  <tr><td>pH</td><td>pH deviation of the foam concentrate before and after temperature treatment is greater than 1.0</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Surface tension (film-forming type)</td><td>After temperature treatment, the surface tension of the foam solution is lower than 0.95 times that before temperature treatment or higher than 1.05 times that before temperature treatment</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Interfacial tension (film-forming type)</td><td>Deviation of interfacial tension before and after temperature treatment is greater than 0.5mN/m</td></tr>
  <tr><td colspan="2">“Deviation” refers to the absolute value of the difference between the two.</td></tr>
</table>
