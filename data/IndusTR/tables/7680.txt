    <tr><td>8</td><td>ShapingPressureActual</td><td>Measured shaping pressure during forming; used to confirm process stability.</td><td>Numeric; interpret as stable when within ±X% of setpoint for Y seconds</td><td>Stability rule: if not stable, mark cycle as ReworkCandidate.</td><td>Depends on ShapingPressureSetpoint; must be stable before TBM_CycleEnd is accepted.</td></tr>
