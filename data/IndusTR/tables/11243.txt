<table>
  <caption>Mechanical recycling route for ELV PP/ABS bumper and trim plastics into regranulate</caption>
  <tr><th>Item</th><th>Content</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Input</td><td>End-of-life vehicle bumper skins and exterior trim (mainly PP/PP-EPDM and ABS), painted and with minor fastener residues</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Pre-treatment</td><td>Dismantling → removal of metal clips/fasteners → coarse size reduction</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Sorting & Separation</td><td>Density separation (float-sink) → air classification for light contaminants → optical/NIR check for polymer family</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Cleaning</td><td>Friction washing → hot washing (detergent) → rinsing</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Reprocessing</td><td>Drying → melt filtration → twin-screw extrusion with stabilizer masterbatch → strand cooling → pelletizing</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Output</td><td>Recycled PP/ABS regranulate pellets suitable for injection molding of non-structural automotive parts and general-purpose plastic products</td></tr>
</table>
