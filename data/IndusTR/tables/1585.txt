<table>
  <caption>Specific Requirements for Instrument Process Diagnostics Features</caption>
  <tr><td colspan="2">Instrument Category</td><td>Feature Requirements</td></tr>
  <tr><td rowspan="2">Measuring Instruments</td><td>Pressure, Level, and Temperature Measuring Instruments</td><td>Pressure, level, and temperature measuring instruments can perform abnormality diagnosis and recording for process variables, with a diagnosis accuracy for a single abnormality not less than 95%; pressure measuring instruments shall have pressure impulse line blockage diagnosis and wetted-temperature estimation functions</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Flow Measuring Instruments</td><td>Flow measuring instruments can have different process abnormality diagnostic functions depending on the principle. Vortex flow measuring instruments can, by measuring the amplitude distribution of the measurement signal, comprehensively determine process abnormalities such as steam condensation or the resulting risk of water hammer; flow measuring instruments with viscosity measurement functions can, by measuring viscosity parameters, determine the stability of the process state and whether process abnormalities exist; electromagnetic flow measuring instruments can, by measuring adhesion parameters, indicate process abnormalities such as adhesion in the process piping around the sensor; thermal gas mass flow measuring instruments can diagnose condensation and adhesion abnormalities; ultrasonic flow measuring instruments can, by measuring sound-speed signal strength, quality, signal-to-noise ratio, etc., comprehensively determine the purity of the measured fluid and indicate process abnormalities such as impurities in the fluid. For each of the above single abnormalities, the diagnosis accuracy shall not be less than 95%</td></tr>
  <tr><td colspan="2">Analytical Instruments</td><td>Implements one of the following functions: a) diagnoses abnormal behaviors or deviations in industrial processes; b) uses historical data for trend analysis to diagnose potential process problems</td></tr>
