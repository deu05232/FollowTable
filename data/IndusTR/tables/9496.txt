  <caption>Professional Judgment Traits for Science and Technology Evaluators (Decision Quality Under Uncertainty)</caption>
  <thead>
    <tr><th>Judgment Trait</th><th>What It Looks Like in Evaluation Work</th><th>How It Is Demonstrated (Behavioral Examples)</th></tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr><td>Analytical Rigor</td><td>Builds coherent logic from indicators, methods, and evidence; checks assumptions and limitations.</td><td>States evaluation assumptions explicitly; conducts sensitivity checks when indicators change.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Insight and Situational Awareness</td><td>Detects key signals affecting conclusions; notices anomalies and emerging risks.</td><td>Flags inconsistent data early; identifies missing stakeholder perspectives and requests supplements.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Adaptability</td><td>Adjusts data collection or method selection when constraints or new information arise without sacrificing fairness.</td><td>Revises sampling plan transparently; explains why changes do not bias outcomes.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Sound Decision-Making Under Pressure</td><td>Makes timely and rational judgments in disputes or time constraints.</td><td>Uses pre-defined decision rules; records dissenting opinions and the final rationale.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Balanced Skepticism</td><td>Questions claims appropriately while remaining open to valid evidence.</td><td>Requests corroboration; avoids dismissing minority viewpoints without examination.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Consistency and Standard Adherence</td><td>Applies standards uniformly across cases and time; avoids shifting criteria mid-process.</td><td>Uses standardized scoring rubrics; calibrates with peers to reduce variability.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Communication for Fair Understanding</td><td>Explains conclusions clearly and neutrally; separates findings from recommendations.</td><td>Writes plain-language summaries; labels uncertainty levels and evidence strength.</td></tr>
    <tr><td>Reflective Learning</td><td>Reviews outcomes and feedback to improve future evaluations while maintaining independence.</td><td>Conducts post-evaluation self-audits; updates checklists to prevent repeated errors.</td></tr>
