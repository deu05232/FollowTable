<table>
  <caption>Competency Traits for Science & Technology Evaluators (principles and soft skills)</caption>
  <tr><th>Competency</th><th>Description</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Integrity</td><td>Acts honestly, avoids manipulation of evidence, and maintains confidentiality of sensitive evaluation information.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Neutrality</td><td>Maintains a neutral stance when faced with competing stakeholder interests and avoids advocacy.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Analytical Thinking</td><td>Breaks complex evaluation questions into testable components and reasons from evidence to conclusions.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Communication</td><td>Explains evaluation methods and findings clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Stress Tolerance</td><td>Remains calm and consistent when timelines are tight or when there is external pressure.</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Continuous Learning</td><td>Keeps up with new evaluation methods and emerging science & technology trends.</td></tr>
</table>
