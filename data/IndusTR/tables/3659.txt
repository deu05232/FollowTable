  <tr><td>2×8</td><td>1—4 start with the left foot, V-step; 5—8 start with the left foot, A-step</td><td>1— arms side raise to horizontal, palms together with palms facing down; 2— arms overhead cross, palms together with palms facing forward; 3— arms side raise to horizontal, palms together with palms facing down; 4— arms at the sides of the body; 5—8 same as 1—4</td><td>V-step: after the feet step alternately to the diagonal front, keep the feet on the same straight line and bend the knees, then return to the original position in sequence; steps are springy with cushioned landings; movements should be elastic. A-step is the opposite of the V-step. Arm movements are clear and smooth, with large range and strong force</td></tr>
  <tr><td>3×8</td><td>1—8 alternate knee-lift jumps 4 times</td><td>1—2 arms bend in front of the body, then lower back and down to the sides; 3—4 arms swing outward to shoulder level with elbows bent, elbows level with shoulders; 5—8 same as 1—4</td><td>During the knee-lift jump, lift the bent knee upward parallel to the floor with pointed toes; the other leg performs small jumps in place; land with cushioning. Jumps should be relaxed and springy; coordinate breathing. Arm movements are clear and smooth, with large range and strong force; engage the core and keep the torso tall</td></tr>
  <tr><td>4×8</td><td>1—2 step with the left foot and perform a hamstring curl; 3—4 step with the right foot and perform a hamstring curl; 5—8 step with the left foot and perform continuous 2 hamstring curls</td><td>1— arms forward horizontal raise, fists clenched, knuckles facing down; 2— arms bent horizontally in front of the chest, fists clenched, knuckles facing down; 3—8 same as 1—2</td><td>Step with hamstring curl: after passing through a half-squat, shift the center of gravity to the left; the right leg folds at the knee and curls backward, heel pointing to the buttocks, knee vertical to the floor; the left leg slightly bends and remains stable for support. Arm movements are clear and smooth, with large range and strong force</td></tr>
