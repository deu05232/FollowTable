  <caption>Qualitative do/don’t list for forging-friendly shapes (no angle values)</caption>
  <tr><th>Unfavorable feature</th><th>Preferred alternative</th><th>Explanation</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Wedge-like outer profile</td><td>Parallel planes or cylindrical walls</td><td>Reduces local die wear and improves flow</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Sharp re-entrant corner</td><td>Rounded transition with fillet</td><td>Helps metal fill and avoids laps</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Highly irregular perimeter</td><td>Uniform cross-section where possible</td><td>Improves symmetry of deformation</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Long slender rib</td><td>Shorter rib with increased thickness</td><td>Improves fill reliability</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Sudden section change</td><td>Gradual section change</td><td>Reduces turbulence and fold risk</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Complex compound curves</td><td>Simplified straight/arc geometry</td><td>Easier die manufacturing and forming</td></tr>
