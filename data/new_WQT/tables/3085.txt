# How Long Does Oatmeal Last ? Shelf Life , Storage , Expiration

| Unopened or Opened (and re-sealed) | Pantry | Freezer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 minute Oatmeal lasts for | 2-3 Years | -- |
| Steel Cut Oatmeal (Irish Oatmeal) lasts for | 1-2 Years | -- |
| Oatmeal Cookies last for | 2-3 Days | 4-5 Months |
| Prepared | Refrigerator | Freezer |
