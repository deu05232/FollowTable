# White Wine Varietals by Body Level with Typical Flavor Notes

| Varietal | Typical Aromas & Flavors | Typical Body |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sauvignon Blanc | Citrus, gooseberry, grass, green pepper | Light |
| Pinot Grigio | Lemon, pear, white flowers, minerality | Light |
| Riesling (dry) | Lime, green apple, slate, jasmine | Light to medium |
| Chenin Blanc | Quince, apple, honeycomb, chamomile | Medium |
| Chardonnay (oaked) | Apple, vanilla, butter, toast | Medium to full |
| Viognier | Apricot, peach, floral, spice | Medium to full |
| Gewürztraminer | Lychee, rose, ginger, spice | Medium |
