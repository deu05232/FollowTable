# Extraocular Muscles  Actions and Clinical Tests (Innervation Only Implicit)

| Extraocular muscle | Primary action | Secondary action | Common bedside test | Typical deficit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Superior rectus | Elevation | Intorsion, adduction | Ask patient to look up and out | Poor upgaze in abduction |
| Inferior rectus | Depression | Extorsion, adduction | Ask patient to look down and out | Poor downgaze in abduction |
| Medial rectus | Adduction | — | Follow finger medially | Inability to adduct |
| Lateral rectus | Abduction | — | Follow finger laterally | Inability to abduct |
| Superior oblique | Intorsion | Depression, abduction | Look down and in | Vertical diplopia, head tilt |
| Inferior oblique | Extorsion | Elevation, abduction | Look up and in | Difficulty elevating in adduction |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Eyelid elevation | — | Sustain upgaze | Ptosis |
