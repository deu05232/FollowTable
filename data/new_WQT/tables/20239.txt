# Chromosomal Microdeletion Duplication Syndromes and Their Key Loci (Representative Genes When Known)

| Genetic disorder (syndrome) | Chromosomal region (locus) | Representative gene(s) in region | CNV type | Core clinical hallmark |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 22q11.2 deletion syndrome (DiGeorge/VCFS) | 22q11.2 | TBX1 | Deletion | Conotruncal heart defects, immune issues |
| Williams–Beuren syndrome | 7q11.23 | ELN | Deletion | Supravalvular aortic stenosis, characteristic facies |
| Prader–Willi syndrome (typical) | 15q11–q13 (paternal) | SNORD116 region | Deletion / imprinting | Hyperphagia, hypotonia |
| Angelman syndrome (typical) | 15q11–q13 (maternal) | UBE3A | Deletion / imprinting | Severe developmental delay, ataxia |
| Wolf–Hirschhorn syndrome | 4p16.3 | WHSC1 (NSD2) | Deletion | “Greek warrior helmet” facies, seizures |
| 1p36 deletion syndrome | 1p36 | SKI (region-associated) | Deletion | Cardiomyopathy, developmental delay |
| Smith–Magenis syndrome | 17p11.2 | RAI1 | Deletion | Sleep disturbance, self-injury |
| Potocki–Lupski syndrome | 17p11.2 | RAI1 (dosage) | Duplication | Hypotonia, autism spectrum features |
