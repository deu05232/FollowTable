| Region Type | Peak Browse Season | Dominant Natural Foods | When Ornamental Plants Are Targeted | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Suburban woodland interface | Winter | Twigs, buds | Winter–early spring | Deer shift to yards when understory is depleted |
| Agricultural fringe | Late summer–fall | Crops, mast | Post-harvest | After harvest, deer move into neighborhoods |
| Mountain foothills | Winter | Browse, conifers | Deep-snow periods | Snowpack pushes deer downslope into residential zones |
| Coastal mild-winter areas | Spring | Forbs | Spring flush | New growth in gardens competes with native forage |
| Drought-prone inland | Summer | Sparse grasses | Mid-summer drought | Irrigated ornamentals become key water/food source |
| Urban parks network | Fall | Mast, ornamental berries | Fall (mast years) | Movement along green corridors increases yard visits |
| Northern cold climates | Late winter | Woody browse | Late winter | Nutritional stress increases browsing intensity |
