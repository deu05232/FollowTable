# Breed Health Issues and General Diet Tips (No Specific 'Best Food' Picks)

| Breed (or Group) | Common Concern | General Nutrition Tip | Avoid/Limit | Example Ingredient Focus |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Labrador Retriever | Weight gain | Measure portions | Excess treats | Higher fiber |
| German Shepherd | Sensitive digestion | Choose easily digestible recipes | Sudden diet switches | Prebiotics |
| Bulldog (English/French) | Skin folds/allergies | Consider limited-ingredient diets | Common allergens | Novel protein |
| Dachshund | Back health | Maintain lean body condition | Overfeeding | Controlled calories |
| Poodle (Toy/Mini) | Dental plaque | Consider smaller kibble size | Sticky soft treats | Crunchy kibble |
| Boxer | Heart health | Monitor taurine sources | Low-quality fillers | Animal protein |
| Siberian Husky | Energy needs | Ensure adequate protein | Very high carbs | Moderate fat |
