# Recommended Daily Intake Levels for Selected Minerals (Children and Teens)

| Mineral | 1–3 years | 4–8 years | 9–13 years | 14–18 years |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Calcium (mg/day) | 700 | 1,000 | 1,300 | 1,300 |
| Iron (mg/day) | 7 | 10 | 8 | 11 (boys) / 15 (girls) |
| Zinc (mg/day) | 3 | 5 | 8 | 11 (boys) / 9 (girls) |
| Magnesium (mg/day) | 80 | 130 | 240 | 410 (boys) / 360 (girls) |
| Iodine (µg/day) | 90 | 90 | 120 | 150 |
| Potassium (mg/day) | 2,000 | 2,300 | 2,500 (boys) / 2,300 (girls) | 3,000 (boys) / 2,300 (girls) |
| Selenium (µg/day) | 20 | 30 | 40 | 55 |
