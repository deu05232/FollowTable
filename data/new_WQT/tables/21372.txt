# VA Disability Compensation vs. Military Retirement Pay Interactions (Comparison)

| Compensation Category | Eligibility Trigger | How the Amount Is Determined | Payment Timing / Form | Typical Interaction With Other Pay |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| VA Disability Compensation | Service-connected disability with assigned rating | Monthly compensation set by disability rating; may increase with eligible dependents and special factors | Monthly payment | Can affect/offset certain forms of military retired pay unless concurrent receipt rules apply |
| Military Longevity Retired Pay | Completion of required years of service (active or reserve rules) | Pay base × years-of-service multiplier (retirement system dependent) | Monthly payment | May be reduced by VA waiver in some cases; some retirees qualify for concurrent receipt |
| Disability Retired Pay (DoD) | Found unfit for duty; meets criteria for disability retirement | Greater of disability-based computation or longevity-based computation (subject to applicable limits) | Monthly payment | Often coordinated with VA compensation; may involve offsets or special programs |
| Concurrent Receipt Program (Retired Pay + VA Pay) | Retiree meets program thresholds (e.g., sufficient service/qualifying rating) | Not a new “rate table”; restores ability to receive both streams under defined rules | Monthly (via retirement and VA systems) | Reduces/eliminates the need to waive retired pay to receive VA compensation for eligible retirees |
| Combat-Related Special Compensation (CRSC) | Retiree has disabilities deemed combat-related by service | Special compensation based on approved combat-related disabilities and restored retired-pay amount | Monthly (separate special compensation) | Intended to compensate for retired pay waived due to VA compensation when disability is combat-related |
| Disability Separation Severance Pay | Disability separation without retirement | Lump-sum severance computed from years of service and basic pay using statutory factor | One-time lump sum | May be subject to recoupment/offset against later VA disability compensation in certain situations |
| Special Monthly Compensation (SMC) | Very severe disabilities or specific loss/impairment conditions | Additional amount layered on top of base VA disability compensation | Monthly payment | Stacks with VA compensation; does not depend on military rank tables but on medical/severity criteria |
| Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC) | Surviving spouse/eligible dependent of service member who died in service or from service-connected causes | Flat statutory rate with possible add-ons (e.g., dependent children) | Monthly payment | Survivor-focused; separate from SBP though coordination rules may exist |
