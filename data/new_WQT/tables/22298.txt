# Sympathetic Outflow by Spinal Level and Major Splanchnic Nerves (Transmitters Secondary)

| Target region | Major sympathetic nerves | Spinal cord origin (typical) | Ganglion/plexus | Predominant effector transmitter |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Head/neck | Superior cervical ganglion pathways | T1–T3 | Cervical sympathetic chain | Norepinephrine |
| Heart | Cardiopulmonary splanchnic nerves | T1–T5 | Cervical/upper thoracic ganglia | Norepinephrine |
| Lungs | Pulmonary plexus fibers | T2–T6 | Thoracic ganglia | Norepinephrine |
| Stomach | Greater splanchnic nerve | T5–T9 | Celiac ganglion | Norepinephrine |
| Small intestine | Greater/lesser splanchnic nerves | T8–T12 | Superior mesenteric ganglion | Norepinephrine |
| Kidney | Least splanchnic nerve | T12 | Aorticorenal ganglion | Norepinephrine |
| Pelvic organs | Lumbar splanchnic nerves | L1–L2 | Inferior hypogastric plexus | Norepinephrine |
