# Anatomy Cues for Backbends (Not Pose Name Mapping)

| Cue | Body Area | Intended Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lift the sternum | chest | open the front body |
| Engage glutes lightly | hips | support lumbar spine |
| Press tops of feet down | legs/feet | stabilize pelvis |
| Draw shoulder blades down | shoulders | reduce neck tension |
