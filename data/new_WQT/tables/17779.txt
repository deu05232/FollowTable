# Cooked Foods at Room Temperature  Counter Holding Limits (Food Safety)

| Cooked / Prepared Item | Counter / Room Temp (40–90°F) | Hot Holding (≥140°F) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked rice | 2 hours max | Up to 4 hours | Cool rapidly in shallow pans |
| Cooked chicken | 2 hours max | Up to 4 hours | Discard if left out overnight |
| Cooked pasta | 2 hours max | Up to 4 hours | Toss if >2 hours at room temp |
| Pizza (cooked) | 2 hours max | N/A | Reheating doesn’t undo time abuse |
| Soup/stew (cooked) | 2 hours max | Up to 4 hours | Keep covered |
| Cooked fish | 2 hours max | Up to 4 hours | Stronger odor indicates spoilage |
| Cooked vegetables | 2 hours max | Up to 4 hours | Cool before refrigerating |
| Gravy/sauce | 2 hours max | Up to 4 hours | Danger zone guidance |
