# Carbohydrate Quality Guide  High Medium Low GI Food Groups (No Numbers)

| Food Group | Example Foods | GI Label | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fruits | Apples, oranges, pears | Low | Generally high fiber |
| Tropical fruits | Pineapple, mango | Medium | Ripeness affects response |
| Whole grains | Barley, steel-cut oats | Low | Less processed |
| Refined grains | White bread, crackers | High | More processed |
| Starchy vegetables | Potatoes, pumpkin | High | Cooking method matters |
| Legumes | Lentils, chickpeas | Low | Higher protein and fiber |
| Dairy | Milk, yogurt | Low | Varies with added sugar |
| Sugary drinks | Soda, sweetened tea | High | Rapid absorption |
