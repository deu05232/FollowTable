# U.S. Passport Expedited Processing Add-ons (Surcharges Only)

| Expedited / Speed-up Option (U.S.) | Add-on Fee (USD) | What It Speeds Up | Where Available | Notes |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| Expedited Service (standard expedite surcharge) | 60.00 | Processing time at the agency | Applicants in the U.S. | Paid in addition to regular application fees |
| 1–2 Day Delivery for Passport Book (overnight shipping add-on) | 20.66 | Return mailing speed | U.S. addresses only | Does not speed up processing; delivery add-on |
| Express Mail / Priority Express Return (alternative carrier option) | 28.75 | Return mailing speed | U.S. addresses only | Amount varies by carrier/zone; shown as a typical maximum |
| Urgent Travel Appointment (agency counter service surcharge) | 0.00 | In-person same-week processing access | U.S. passport agencies | Appointment-based; no separate expedite fee beyond expedite service when applicable |
| Expedited Service for Mailed Renewals (Form DS-82) | 60.00 | Processing time for renewals by mail | Applicants in the U.S. | Same expedite surcharge as other applications |
| Expedited Service for Children Under 16 (Form DS-11) | 60.00 | Processing time for minors | Applicants in the U.S. | Same expedite surcharge; base fees vary but are not listed here |
| Courier / Hand-carry Service (third-party) | Varies | Submission logistics | Private providers | Not a government fee; optional service cost varies widely |
