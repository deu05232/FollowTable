# Selected Battles and Operations Involving the United States (1775–1815)

| Battle / Operation | War / Conflict | Date(s) | Location | U.S. role / belligerent | Outcome / Short description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Battles of Lexington and Concord | American Revolutionary War | 19 Apr 1775 | Massachusetts (Lexington, Concord) | Colonial militia vs. British regulars | Opening fighting of the war; British forced to withdraw toward Boston |
| Battle of Bunker Hill | American Revolutionary War | 17 Jun 1775 | Charlestown, Massachusetts | Colonial militia vs. British | British tactical victory with heavy losses; boosted American morale |
| Battle of Saratoga | American Revolutionary War | 19 Sep & 7 Oct 1777 | Saratoga County, New York | Continental Army vs. British | Decisive American victory; helped secure French alliance |
| Siege of Yorktown | American Revolutionary War | 28 Sep – 19 Oct 1781 | Yorktown, Virginia | U.S. & French forces vs. British | Major British surrender; effectively ended major combat in the war |
| Battle of Fallen Timbers | Northwest Indian War | 20 Aug 1794 | Near present-day Toledo, Ohio | U.S. Army vs. Western Confederacy | U.S. victory; led to Treaty of Greenville (1795) |
| Battle of Tippecanoe | Tecumseh's War | 7 Nov 1811 | Near Lafayette, Indiana | U.S. forces vs. Native confederacy | U.S. victory; heightened tensions leading into War of 1812 |
| Battle of Lake Erie | War of 1812 | 10 Sep 1813 | Lake Erie | U.S. Navy vs. British Navy | U.S. naval victory; enabled recapture of Detroit |
| Battle of New Orleans | War of 1812 | 8 Jan 1815 | New Orleans, Louisiana | U.S. Army vs. British | Decisive U.S. victory; occurred after treaty signed but before news arrived |
