# Concerta conversion guide  switching from immediate-release methylphenidate to once-daily Concerta (example starting doses)

| Previous regimen (IR methylphenidate total daily dose) | Common prior schedule example | Suggested Concerta starting dose (mg once daily, AM) | Allowed adjustment after start | Expected target range (mg/day) | Maximum / ceiling (mg/day) | Patient groups where ceiling differs |
|---|---|---:|---|---:|---:|---|
| 5 mg twice daily (10 mg/day) | 5 mg AM + 5 mg noon | 18 | Increase by 18 mg if needed | 18–36 | 54–72 | Children 6–12: 54 max; Adolescents/Adults: 72 max (adolescents also ≤2 mg/kg/day) |
| 5 mg three times daily (15 mg/day) | 5 mg AM + noon + afternoon | 18 | +18 mg at ≥1-week intervals | 18–54 | 54–72 | Same as above |
| 10 mg twice daily (20 mg/day) | 10 mg AM + 10 mg noon | 36 | +18 mg weekly as tolerated | 36–54 | 54–72 | Same as above |
| 10 mg three times daily (30 mg/day) | 10 mg AM + noon + afternoon | 54 | +18 mg weekly if needed | 54–72 | 54–72 | For children 6–12, 54 is typically the ceiling |
| 15 mg twice daily (30 mg/day) | 15 mg AM + 15 mg noon | 54 | +18 mg weekly if needed | 54–72 | 72 | Adolescents must also respect 2 mg/kg/day ceiling |
| 20 mg twice daily (40 mg/day) | 20 mg AM + 20 mg noon | 72 | Do not exceed ceiling; consider alternative strategy if inadequate | 72 | 72 | Adolescents: also ≤2 mg/kg/day |
| 20 mg three times daily (60 mg/day) | 20 mg AM + noon + afternoon | 72 (ceiling) | No further increase; reassess treatment plan | 72 | 72 | Often requires reassessment rather than higher Concerta dosing |
