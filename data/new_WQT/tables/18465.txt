# Common inorganic ligands with formulas and net charges (coordination-chemistry quick reference)

| Ligand (common name) | Ligand species formula | Net charge of the ligand |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Chloride | Cl− | anionic (−1) |
| Hydroxide | OH− | anionic (−1) |
| Cyanide | CN− | anionic (−1) |
| Carbon monoxide | CO | neutral (0) |
| Nitrosyl (linear) | NO+ | cationic (+1) |
| Nitrite (as nitro/nitrito) | NO2− | anionic (−1) |
