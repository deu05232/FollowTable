# Mortgage Program Fees and Upfront Charges (Rates Not Provided)

| Program | Upfront Fee Type | Typical Amount | Applies When | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| FHA | Upfront Mortgage Insurance Premium (UFMIP) | 1.75% of base loan | At closing | Can be financed |
| FHA | Annual MIP (example) | 0.55%/year | Monthly | Varies by term/LTV |
| VA | Funding Fee (First use) | 2.15% | At closing | Depends on down payment |
| VA | Funding Fee (Subsequent use) | 3.30% | At closing | Reduced for some borrowers |
| Conventional | Origination Fee | 0.5%–1.0% | At closing | Lender-dependent |
| Conventional | Discount Points | 0–2 points | Optional | Buys down rate (rate not shown) |
| USDA | Guarantee Fee | 1.00% | At closing | Plus annual fee |
