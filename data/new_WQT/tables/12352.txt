# Typical Limousine Rental Rates by Vehicle Type (Hourly Price Ranges)

| Limo category | Typical hourly rate (USD) | Typical capacity (passengers) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Standard sedan limo | $70–$110/hr | 3–4 | Often billed with a minimum booking |
| Classic stretch limo | $95–$160/hr | 6–10 | Price shifts by day of week |
| Stretch SUV limo | $120–$220/hr | 10–16 | Higher fuel/maintenance costs |
| Hummer stretch limo | $160–$350/hr | 14–20 | Premium for large vehicle footprint |
| Sprinter limo van | $110–$190/hr | 10–14 | Sometimes priced like a small bus |
| Mini party bus (limo-style) | $140–$260/hr | 14–24 | Lighting/sound packages can add cost |
| Full-size party bus / motor coach | $180–$450/hr | 24–50 | Often quoted by event rather than strict hourly |
| Vintage/Exotic limo (Rolls/Bentley) | $250–$600/hr | 2–4 | Limited availability in many cities |
