# Selected Conflicts Involving the Republic of China (Taiwan) — Engagement Overview

| Conflict | ROC/Taiwan involvement (role) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Second Sino-Japanese War (1937–1945) | National Revolutionary Army (ROC) as a principal belligerent | Pre-1949 ROC government
| Chinese Civil War (1945–1949) | ROC forces against Chinese Communist forces | Mainland phase
| First Taiwan Strait Crisis (1954–1955) | ROC garrison forces involved | Artillery and naval incidents
| Second Taiwan Strait Crisis (1958) | ROC forces involved | Heavy shelling of Quemoy/Matsu
| Vietnam War (1960s–1975) | Limited ROC logistical/technical assistance (alleged) | Not always officially acknowledged
| Cold War era maritime incidents (1960s–1980s) | ROC naval/patrol involvement | A series of separate incidents (not a single war)
