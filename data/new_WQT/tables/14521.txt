# Organic Pest Control Products and Their Target Pests

| Product Category | Active Ingredient (Example) | Target Pests Repelled/Controlled | Application Method | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Botanical spray | Neem oil (azadirachtin) | Aphids, whiteflies, spider mites | Foliar spray | Works best with repeat coverage |
| Mineral dust | Diatomaceous earth | Slugs, ants, fleas, beetles | Dust around plants | Ineffective when wet |
| Soap-based control | Insecticidal soap (potassium salts) | Aphids, mealybugs, soft-bodied larvae | Foliar spray | Can burn leaves in heat |
| Physical barrier | Floating row cover | Cabbage moths, carrot fly, flea beetles | Cover beds/hoops | Must be sealed at edges |
| Bait | Iron phosphate pellets | Slugs, snails | Scatter bait | Low toxicity to pets when used correctly |
| Trap | Yellow sticky cards | Fungus gnats, whiteflies, leafminers | Hang near canopy | Monitoring + partial control |
| Repellent granules | Castor oil granules | Moles, voles (deterrence) | Broadcast and water in | Needs reapplication after rain |
