# Eye Movement Actions Mapped to Extraocular Muscles and Innervating Cranial Nerves

| Movement to test | Prime muscle(s) | Cranial nerve supplying the prime muscle(s) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Abduction (look laterally) | Lateral rectus | Abducens nerve (CN VI) |
| Adduction (look medially) | Medial rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Elevation in abducted eye | Superior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Depression in abducted eye | Inferior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Elevation in adducted eye | Inferior oblique | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Depression in adducted eye | Superior oblique | Trochlear nerve (CN IV) |
| Intorsion (top of eye rotates medially) | Superior oblique; Superior rectus | Trochlear nerve (CN IV); Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Extorsion (top of eye rotates laterally) | Inferior oblique; Inferior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III); Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
