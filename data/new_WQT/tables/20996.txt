# # Leftover Cooked Foods  Fridge vs Freezer Storage Times

| Cooked/Ready-to-eat item | Typical time it keeps well in the fridge | Typical time it keeps well in the freezer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked chicken pieces | 3–4 days | 2–6 months |
| Cooked ground beef | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked pasta (plain) | 3–5 days | 1–2 months |
| Cooked vegetable soup | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked fish (flaked/fillet) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
