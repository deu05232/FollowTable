# Antibiotic regimens used for prostatitis (example dosing schedules)

| Antibiotic option | Typical oral regimen description | Notes on when it’s commonly used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ciprofloxacin | 500 mg twice daily | Often used when a fluoroquinolone is appropriate |
| Levofloxacin | 500–750 mg once daily | Alternative fluoroquinolone option |
| Trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole | 160/800 mg twice daily | Often chosen when susceptible and tolerated |
| Doxycycline | 100 mg twice daily | Used when an atypical/STI-related cause is suspected |
