# State-by-state gubernatorial term-limit rules (selected states)

| State | Governor term limit rule | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| California | 2 terms (lifetime) | Two four-year terms total |
| Michigan | 2 terms (lifetime) | Two four-year terms total |
| New York | No limits | No constitutional/statutory limit |
| Virginia | 1 term, nonconsecutive | Cannot serve two consecutive terms |
| Washington | 2 consecutive terms | Can return after a break in service (varies by interpretation) |
