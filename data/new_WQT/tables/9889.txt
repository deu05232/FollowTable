# Zinc Intake Guide  Upper Limits, Supplements, and Food Sources (Mixed)

| Item | Amount | Unit | Comment |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Adult RDA (men) | 11 | mg/day | Recommended intake |
| Adult RDA (women) | 8 | mg/day | Recommended intake |
| Tolerable Upper Limit (adults) | 40 | mg/day | Too much can impair copper |
| Zinc gluconate supplement | 50 | mg per tablet | Not a food source |
| Multivitamin (typical) | 15 | mg per serving | Not food; varies by brand |
| Oysters, cooked | 74 | mg per 3 oz | Food example |
| Beef chuck roast, braised | 7 | mg per 3 oz | Food example |
| Pumpkin seeds, roasted | 2.2 | mg per 1 oz | Food example |
