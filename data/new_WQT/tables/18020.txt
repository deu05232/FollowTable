# Wars involving the Kingdom of Spain (selected conflicts, 18th–20th centuries)

| War/Conflict | Years | Spain and allies (dedicated column) | Opponents / Other belligerents | Main area | Result (brief) |
|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| War of the Spanish Succession | 1701–1714 | Spain (Bourbon); France | Grand Alliance (Austria, Britain, Dutch Republic, etc.) | Europe | Bourbon succession confirmed; territorial losses |
| War of Jenkins' Ear | 1739–1748 | Spain | Britain | Caribbean/Atlantic | Inconclusive; merged into wider war |
| Seven Years' War | 1756–1763 | Spain; France | Britain; Prussia and allies | Americas/Europe | Spain cedes Florida; gains Louisiana (from France) |
| American Revolutionary War (Spanish participation) | 1779–1783 | Spain; France; American rebels (aligned) | Britain | Americas/Atlantic | Spanish gains (e.g., Minorca, Florida) |
| Peninsular War | 1808–1814 | Spain; United Kingdom; Portugal | First French Empire | Iberian Peninsula | French expelled; Spanish monarchy restored |
| Spanish–American War | 1898 | Spain | United States; Cuban & Filipino revolutionaries | Caribbean/Pacific | Spain loses Cuba, Puerto Rico, Philippines |
| Rif War | 1921–1926 | Spain; France | Rif Republic (Berber forces) | Northern Morocco | Spanish/French victory |
| Spanish Civil War | 1936–1939 | Spanish Republic (with intl. volunteers) | Nationalists (supported by Italy & Germany) | Spain | Nationalist victory; Franco regime |
| War of the Oranges | 1801 | Spain; France | Portugal | Iberian Peninsula | Portuguese concessions (Treaty of Badajoz) |
| Hispano–Moroccan War | 1859–1860 | Spain | Morocco | North Africa | Spanish victory; territorial/indemnity gains |
