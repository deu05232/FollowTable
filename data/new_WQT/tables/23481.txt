# Florida Crime Levels and Penalties (Felonies and Misdemeanors Combined)

| Level | Type | Typical examples | Maximum imprisonment | Maximum fine |
|---|---|---|---:|---:|
| Capital felony | Felony | Capital murder | Death / Life | $0 |
| Life felony | Felony | Kidnapping | Life | $15,000 |
| First-degree felony | Felony | Aggravated battery | 30 years | $10,000 |
| Second-degree felony | Felony | Burglary (certain forms) | 15 years | $10,000 |
| Third-degree felony | Felony | Grand theft (certain amounts) | 5 years | $5,000 |
| First-degree misdemeanor | Misdemeanor | Battery | 1 year | $1,000 |
| Second-degree misdemeanor | Misdemeanor | Disorderly conduct | 60 days | $500 |
