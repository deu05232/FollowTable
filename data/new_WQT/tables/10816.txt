# Extraocular movement deficits by cranial nerve palsy

| Cranial nerve palsy | Typical eye position at rest | Movement most affected |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CN III palsy | "Down and out" | Adduction/elevation/depression impaired |
| CN IV palsy | Slight hypertropia | Depression in adduction impaired |
| CN VI palsy | Esotropia | Abduction impaired |
| Internuclear ophthalmoplegia | Adduction deficit | Impaired horizontal gaze coordination |
