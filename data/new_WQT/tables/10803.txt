# IV and oral antibiotic regimens with dose and frequency for severe acute bacterial prostatitis (hospital setting)

| Antibiotic | Dose | Frequency | Route | When used |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ceftriaxone | 1 g | once daily | IV | Broad empiric coverage in some settings |
| Piperacillin-tazobactam | 3.375 g | every 6 hours | IV | Severe infection/healthcare-associated risk |
| Ertapenem | 1 g | once daily | IV | ESBL risk or resistant organisms |
| Gentamicin | 5 mg/kg | once daily | IV | Often combined therapy; monitoring required |
| Ciprofloxacin | 400 mg | every 12 hours | IV | IV option then step-down to oral |
| Levofloxacin | 750 mg | once daily | IV/PO | May allow IV-to-PO transition |
