# Canon EOS DSLR Lens Mount and Adapter Compatibility Overview

| EOS body | Native mount | Accepts EF lenses | Accepts EF-S lenses | Accepts EF-M lenses | Notes about adapters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| EOS 1200D | EF/EF-S | Yes | Yes | No | EF-to-EF is native; no EF-M support |
| EOS 6D | EF | Yes | No | No | EF-only body; EF-S not compatible physically |
| EOS 7D Mark II | EF/EF-S | Yes | Yes | No | APS-C DSLR; EF-S supported |
| EOS 5D Mark III | EF | Yes | No | No | Full-frame DSLR; EF-S not supported |
| EOS 90D | EF/EF-S | Yes | Yes | No | APS-C DSLR; EF-S supported |
| EOS-1D X | EF | Yes | No | No | Pro full-frame DSLR; EF-only |
