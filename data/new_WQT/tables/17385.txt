# Prenatal Screening Markers and Associated Genetic Conditions (Marker-Based, Not Gene Locus)

| Screening test/marker | Result pattern | Associated condition(s) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Maternal serum AFP | Low | Trisomy 21 | Screening correlation only |
| Maternal serum AFP | Very low | Trisomy 18 | Non-diagnostic |
| hCG | High | Trisomy 21 | Used in combined screening |
| hCG | Low | Trisomy 18 | Used in risk calculation |
| PAPP-A | Low | Trisomy 21 | First-trimester screen |
| Inhibin A | High | Trisomy 21 | Quad screen component |
| Nuchal translucency | Increased | Trisomy 21, Trisomy 18 | Ultrasound marker |
| Cell-free DNA fraction | Low | Increased test failure risk | Not a locus assignment |
