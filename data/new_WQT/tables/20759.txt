# Autonomic Reflex Arcs and Outcomes (Baroreflex, Chemoreflex, etc.)

| Reflex | Primary stimulus | Afferent pathway | Central integration | Efferent outcome | Net physiologic effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Baroreflex | Increased arterial stretch | CN IX, CN X | Medulla | Increased vagal tone, decreased sympathetic tone | Lower HR and BP |
| Baroreflex | Decreased arterial stretch | CN IX, CN X | Medulla | Decreased vagal tone, increased sympathetic tone | Higher HR and BP |
| Chemoreflex | Low O2 / high CO2 | Carotid/aortic bodies | Medulla | Increased sympathetic vasoconstriction | Maintains perfusion pressure |
| Bainbridge reflex | Increased venous return | Atrial stretch receptors | Brainstem | Reduced vagal, increased sympathetic to SA node | Increased HR |
| Diving reflex | Cold water on face | Trigeminal afferents | Brainstem | Increased vagal to heart; sympathetic to vessels | Bradycardia + vasoconstriction |
| Micturition reflex | Bladder stretch | Pelvic afferents | Spinal cord/pons | Parasympathetic to detrusor | Urination |
| Defecation reflex | Rectal distension | Pelvic afferents | Spinal cord/pons | Parasympathetic to colon | Defecation |
