# Recommended Daily Calcium Intake (RDA) for Children and Adolescents by Age Group

| Age group | Recommended daily intake (mg/day) | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| 1–3 years | 700 | Rapid bone growth begins |
| 4–8 years | 1000 | Supports skeletal development |
| 9–13 years | 1300 | Peak bone-building years |
| 14–18 years | 1300 | Continued bone mineralization |
| 19+ years | 1000 | Included for comparison only |
| Pregnant 14–18 years | 1300 | Same as non-pregnant teens |
| Lactating 14–18 years | 1300 | Same as non-lactating teens |
