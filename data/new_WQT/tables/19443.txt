# State Tax Refund Status Portals (No Filing Deadline Information)

| State | Refund Status Website | What You Need | Typical Processing Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Georgia | Georgia Tax Center | SSN + expected refund | 2–3 weeks |
| Indiana | INTIME Refund Check | SSN + refund amount | 2–4 weeks |
| Missouri | MO Dept. of Revenue Refund Inquiry | SSN + filing status | 2–4 weeks |
| New Jersey | NJ Refund Status | SSN + refund amount | 4+ weeks |
| Ohio | Ohio ITR Refund Status | SSN + DOB | 2–3 weeks |
| Pennsylvania | PA Where’s My Refund? | SSN + refund amount | 4–6 weeks |
| Virginia | VA Refund Tracker | SSN + refund amount | 1–2 weeks |
