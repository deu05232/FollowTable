# Cooked leftovers in the refrigerator or freezer  typical storage times

| Cooked food (leftovers) | Refrigerator time | Freezer time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked pasta (plain) | 3–5 days | 2 months |
| Cooked beans or lentils | 3–5 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked chicken pieces | 3–4 days | 2–6 months |
| Cooked ground meat (leftovers) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked fish (leftovers) | 3–4 days | 2–3 months |
| Cooked vegetable sides | 3–5 days | 8–12 months |
