# Compound formulas and names used to practice oxidation states

| Chemical Formula | Compound Name | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FeCl3 | iron(III) chloride | name indicates +3 on Fe |
| Cu2O | copper(I) oxide | name indicates +1 on Cu |
| Na2SO4 | sodium sulfate | practice assigning oxidation states |
| KMnO4 | potassium permanganate | practice assigning oxidation states |
