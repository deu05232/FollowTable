# Common ions and whether they are cations or anions (with charges)

| Ion | Formula | Charge | Cation/Anion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sodium | Na+ | +1 | Cation |
| Chloride | Cl- | -1 | Anion |
| Magnesium | Mg2+ | +2 | Cation |
| Sulfate | SO4^2- | -2 | Anion |
| Ammonium | NH4+ | +1 | Cation |
