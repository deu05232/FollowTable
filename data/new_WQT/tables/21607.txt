# pH Scale Reference (Acidity vs. Alkalinity Levels)

| Category | pH Range | Typical Examples | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Battery-acid level | 0.0–1.0 | Battery acid | Extremely corrosive |
| Strong acid | 1.1–3.0 | Gastric acid | Highly acidic |
| Acidic | 3.1–6.0 | Soda, coffee | Often tastes sour |
| Near neutral | 6.1–7.9 | Pure water | Minimal taste impact |
| Mildly alkaline | 8.0–9.0 | Seawater | Can taste “slippery” |
| Alkaline | 9.1–11.0 | Baking soda solution | Noticeably basic |
| Strong base | 11.1–14.0 | Bleach, lye | Caustic |
| Measurement notes | N/A | N/A | Each 1.0 step is a 10× change in H+ |
