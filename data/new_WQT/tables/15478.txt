| Marijuana (> 1 oz) | Possession | Felony | 1–10 years | Fines possible |
| Cocaine | Possession (first offense) | Felony | 2–15 years | Fines possible |
| Cocaine | Possession (second+ offense) | Felony | 5–30 years | Fines possible |
| Methamphetamine | Possession | Felony | 2–15 years | Fines possible |
| Heroin | Trafficking (threshold quantities) | Felony | 5–30 years | Large mandatory fines |

| Any controlled substance | Possession with intent | Felony | 1–30 years | Intent inferred by quantity |
