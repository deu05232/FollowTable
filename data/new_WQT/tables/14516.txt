# # Garden pest management products and what they do

| Control approach | What it targets | Typical notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| neem oil spray | aphids, whiteflies, mites | apply to leaf surfaces |
| insecticidal soap | soft-bodied insects | repeat after rain |
| pheromone trap | moths and beetles | used for monitoring |
| copper fungicide | fungal leaf spots | avoid overuse |
