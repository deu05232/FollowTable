# Extraocular Muscles and Their Innervation (with Nuclei and Brainstem Level)

| Extraocular muscle | Primary motor cranial nerve | Motor nucleus | Brainstem level | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Superior rectus | Oculomotor (CN III, superior division) | Oculomotor nucleus | Midbrain | Also linked to levator palpebrae via CN III |
| Inferior rectus | Oculomotor (CN III, inferior division) | Oculomotor nucleus | Midbrain | Often tested with adduction + depression |
| Medial rectus | Oculomotor (CN III, inferior division) | Oculomotor nucleus | Midbrain | Primary adductor |
| Inferior oblique | Oculomotor (CN III, inferior division) | Oculomotor nucleus | Midbrain | Elevates in adduction |
| Superior oblique | Trochlear (CN IV) | Trochlear nucleus | Midbrain | Only CN emerging dorsally |
| Lateral rectus | Abducens (CN VI) | Abducens nucleus | Pons | Abducts the eye |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Oculomotor (CN III, superior division) | Oculomotor nucleus | Midbrain | Eyelid elevation (not a globe mover) |
