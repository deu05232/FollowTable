# Seasonal Deer Diet by Plant Category (Typical Browse Composition)

| Season | Primary Foods | Secondary Foods | Notes on Foraging Behavior |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Winter | Woody browse (shrubs & young trees) | Evergreen needles, bark | Deer concentrate in sheltered areas; browsing height increases on deep snow |
| Early Spring | Buds and new twigs | Early forbs | Heavy use of edge habitat; animals seek high-protein growth |
| Late Spring | Forbs and tender shoots | Shrub leaves | Frequent movement between forest and lawns |
| Summer | Forbs, garden annuals, fruit drop | Grasses (variable) | Grazing increases where irrigation keeps turf green |
| Fall | Mast (acorns, nuts) and woody browse | Forbs | Feeding peaks at dawn/dusk; diet shifts with mast availability |
| Rut (Autumn) | Woody browse near cover | Agricultural crops (where present) | Increased travel corridors and opportunistic feeding |
| Drought Periods | Any available irrigated ornamentals | Dry grasses | Deer target watered landscapes regardless of plant type |
