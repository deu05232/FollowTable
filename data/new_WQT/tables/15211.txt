# Byzantine currency and minting reference (often used during different reigns)

| Coin type | Typical metal | Approx. weight standard | Value relationship | Common mint locations |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Solidus | Gold | 4.5 g | High-value unit | Constantinople; Thessalonica |
| Histamenon | Gold | 4.4–4.5 g | Related to solidus system | Constantinople |
| Follis | Copper | varies | Low-value unit | Constantinople; Nikomedia |
| Tetarteron | Gold | lighter than solidus | Lower than histamenon in practice | Constantinople |
