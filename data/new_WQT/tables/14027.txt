# Derivatives of Special Functions (Not Differentiation Rules)

| Function Name | Expression | Derivative | Typical Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Exponential | e^x | e^x | Growth models |
| Natural log | ln(x) | 1/x | Log transforms |
| Sine | sin(x) | cos(x) | Oscillations |
| Cosine | cos(x) | -sin(x) | Oscillations |
| Tangent | tan(x) | sec^2(x) | Slopes/angles |
| Inverse tangent | arctan(x) | 1/(1+x^2) | Angle recovery |
| Hyperbolic sine | sinh(x) | cosh(x) | Relativity/catenary |
| Hyperbolic cosine | cosh(x) | sinh(x) | Relativity/catenary |
