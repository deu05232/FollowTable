# 5 GHz Wi‑Fi Band Plan (UNII Blocks and Operating Ranges)

| UNII Block | Regulatory Domain | Start Frequency (MHz) | End Frequency (MHz) | Typical Use | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| UNII-1 | FCC | 5150 | 5250 | Indoor WLAN | Low-power, no weather radar |
| UNII-2A | FCC | 5250 | 5350 | Indoor/Outdoor | DFS/TPC required |
| UNII-2C (Extended) | FCC | 5470 | 5725 | Indoor/Outdoor | DFS/TPC required |
| UNII-3 | FCC | 5725 | 5850 | Outdoor links | Higher EIRP allowed |
| UNII-4 | FCC (proposal/varies) | 5850 | 5925 | Future/adjacent uses | Rules vary by country |
| 5.9 GHz (ITS) | EU/US (varies) | 5855 | 5925 | V2X/ITS | Coexistence constraints |
| 6 GHz (Wi‑Fi 6E, adjacent) | FCC | 5925 | 7125 | WLAN expansion | Not part of 5 GHz blocks |
| 5 GHz Weather Radar (shared) | Various | 5600 | 5650 | Radar | DFS protection critical |
