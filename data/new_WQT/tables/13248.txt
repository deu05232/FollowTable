| Abbreviation | Party/Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| D | Democratic | Standard ballot label |
| R | Republican | Standard ballot label |
| I | Independent | May caucus with D or R |
| C | Conservative | Often aligned with Republicans |
| FL | Farmer–Labor | Historical third party |
| P | Progressive | Historical third party |
| V | Vacant | Seat not filled |
| Ind/D | Independent (caucusing with Democrats) | Used in some election tables |
| Ind/R | Independent (caucusing with Republicans) | Used in some election tables |
