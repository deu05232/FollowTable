# Need for Speed series — mainline release timeline (1994–2003)

| Release year | Franchise entry (title) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1994 | The Need for Speed | Series debut (Road & Track Presents) |
| 1997 | Need for Speed II | Major sequel |
| 1998 | Need for Speed III: Hot Pursuit | Introduced Hot Pursuit focus |
| 1999 | Need for Speed: High Stakes | Also known as Road Challenge in some regions |
| 2000 | Need for Speed: Porsche Unleashed | Porsche-centric entry |
| 2002 | Need for Speed: Hot Pursuit 2 | Return to Hot Pursuit concept |
| 2003 | Need for Speed: Underground | Shift toward street racing/customization |
