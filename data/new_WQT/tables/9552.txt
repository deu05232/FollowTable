| Gross-Rosen | Poland | Lower Silesia (Rogoźnica) | Concentration camp; forced labor | 1940–1945 |
| Sachsenhausen | Germany | Oranienburg, Brandenburg | Concentration camp; forced labor; detention | 1936–1945 |
| Ravensbrück | Germany | Fürstenberg/Havel, Brandenburg | Concentration camp (women); forced labor | 1939–1945 |
| Stutthof | Poland | Near Gdańsk (Sztutowo) | Concentration camp; forced labor; killing site | 1939–1945 |
| Theresienstadt (Terezín) | Czech Republic | Terezín, Ústí nad Labem Region | Ghetto and transit/holding camp | 1941–1945 |
| Westerbork | Netherlands | Drenthe (Hooghalen) | Transit camp (deportation hub) | 1942–1945 |
