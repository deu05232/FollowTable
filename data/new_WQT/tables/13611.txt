# Health effects associated with higher dietary fiber intake

| Potential effect | What it may help with |
| --- | --- |
| Increased satiety | Reduced appetite and energy intake |
| Improved bowel regularity | Less constipation |
| Lower LDL cholesterol | Reduced cardiovascular risk |
| Better glycemic control | Lower post-meal blood sugar spikes |
| Improved gut microbiome | More short-chain fatty acid production |
| Weight management support | Easier long-term adherence |
| Reduced diverticular symptoms | Fewer flare-ups in some people |
