# Limousine Service Charges (Fees and Surcharges Schedule)

| Charge Type | Typical Amount | Applies To | Details |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fuel surcharge | 5%–15% | All vehicles | Varies with local fuel index |
| Gratuity (service charge) | 15%–20% | All vehicles | Often automatically added |
| Cleaning fee | $75–$250 | SUVs / party buses | Applied for excessive mess |
| Overtime | 1.5× hourly rate | All vehicles | Charged after contracted end time |
| Peak event surcharge | 10%–30% | High-demand dates | Proms, NYE, major concerts |
| Minimum-hour requirement | 2–5 hours | Stretch/party vehicles | Higher minimums on weekends |
| Cancellation fee | $25–$200 | All vehicles | Depends on notice window |
