# U.S. Passport Fee Comparison (Routine vs Expedited) — Book Card Bundles and Special Cases

| Passport service (U.S.) | Typical form | Routine processing fee (USD) | Expedited processing fee (USD) |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| New Passport Book + Card (Form DS-11) | DS-11 | $160 | $220 |
| New Passport Book + Card (Form DS-11) | DS-11 (under 16) | $115 | $175 |
| Renewal: Passport Book + Card together (Form DS-82) | DS-82 | $160 | $220 |
| Replace (lost/stolen): Book + Card together (Form DS-11) | DS-11 | $160 | $220 |
| Change/Correct Passport (name change or data correction) | DS-5504 (if eligible) | $0 | $60 |
| Limited-validity Passport Replacement | Varies (per DOS instructions) | $0 | $60 |
| Second Passport Book (additional book) | Varies | $130 | $190 |
| Upgrade from Routine to Expedited after filing | N/A (request upgrade) | $0 | $60 |
