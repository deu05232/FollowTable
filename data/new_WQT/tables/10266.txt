# Fallout 4 Collectibles Quick Guide (Quest-Based Acquisition Notes)

| Collectible / Gear | How to Obtain (Quest/Event) | Prerequisite / Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- |
| "Charisma" Bobblehead | Rewarded after completing a key stage of a main-story investigation | Progress the main story until the related rescue is resolved |
| "Luck" Bobblehead | Found while following a pirate radio distress chain | Tune to the broadcast and finish the ship encounter |
| "Melee" Bobblehead | Picked up during a boss fight sequence inside a multi-floor dungeon | Defeat the tower boss encounter |
| "Agility" Bobblehead | Looted during a scripted set-piece after boarding an enemy vessel | Trigger the shoreline event and board the ship |
| Eddie's Peace (.44) | Awarded during companion loyalty quest conclusion | Complete the companion's personal questline |
| Kellogg's Pistol (.44) | Automatically obtained during a story mission loot step | Finish the relevant main quest objective |
| Kiloton Radium Rifle (Far Harbor) | Purchased only after aligning with a faction outcome | Advance the DLC storyline to unlock vendor inventory |
| Justice (Combat Shotgun) | Bought during a "deal" scene once a settlement dispute is settled | Resolve the settlement conflict quest |
| "Barter" Bobblehead | Collected during a cannery-related side quest finale | Start and complete the cannery quest chain |
