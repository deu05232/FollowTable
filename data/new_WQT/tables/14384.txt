# Genetic translation table for amino acids (codon-centric, not a classification list)

| Amino acid | Standard shorthand | Codon patterns in mRNA | Typical start/stop involvement |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Methionine | Met / M | AUG | Common start signal |
| Phenylalanine | Phe / F | UUU, UUC | Not start/stop |
| Leucine | Leu / L | UUA, UUG, CUU, CUC, CUA, CUG | Not start/stop |
| Tryptophan | Trp / W | UGG | Not start/stop |
