# NHL Playoff Round Labels and Descriptions (Reference Guide)

| Stage | Abbreviation | Description | Typical Number of Teams | Notes |
|---|---|---|---:|---|
| First Round | R1 | Opening round of the Stanley Cup Playoffs | 16 | Best-of-seven |
| Second Round | R2 | Division/Conference semifinals (format-dependent) | 8 | Best-of-seven |
| Conference Final | CF | Determines conference champion | 4 | Best-of-seven |
| Stanley Cup Final | SCF | League championship series | 2 | Best-of-seven |
| Stanley Cup Champion | CH | Winner of the Stanley Cup | 1 | Awarded trophy |
| Did Not Qualify | DNQ | Team missed playoffs | 0 | Regular season only |
| Play-in/Qualifying | Q | Qualifying round (special formats) | varies | Not every season |
