# Selected Capital Punishment Cases in Europe (Notable Individuals)

| Country | Executed person | Year | Place | Crime | Method | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| France | Hamida Djandoubi | 1977 | Marseille | Murder | Guillotine | Often cited as last executed in Western Europe |
| Spain | Salvador Puig Antich | 1974 | Barcelona | Murder (police officer) | Garrote | Highly publicized case under Franco |
| United Kingdom | Ruth Ellis | 1955 | London | Murder | Hanging | Last woman executed in the UK |
| Romania | Nicolae Ceaușescu | 1989 | Târgoviște | Treason | Firing squad | Summary trial after revolution |
| Belarus | Pavel Selyun | 2014 | Minsk | Murder | Shooting | Example of modern executions in Europe |
| Turkey | Erdal Eren | 1980 | Ankara | Murder | Hanging | Last executions before moratorium era |
| Russia | Andrei Chikatilo | 1994 | Novocherkassk | Serial murder | Shooting | Notable late-Soviet/post-Soviet case |
