# General Coffee Types  Caffeine by Serving Volume (Not Starbucks Menu)

| Coffee Type | Typical Serving | Caffeine (mg) | Measurement Basis |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Drip coffee | 8 fl oz | 95–165 | Average range |
| Cold brew concentrate | 2 fl oz | 100–200 | Concentrate shot |
| Espresso (single) | 1 fl oz | 60–75 | 1 shot |
| Espresso (double) | 2 fl oz | 120–150 | 2 shots |
| Cappuccino | 12 fl oz | 120–150 | Often 2 shots |
| Latte | 16 fl oz | 150–170 | Often 2 shots |
| Mocha | 16 fl oz | 150–200 | Espresso + chocolate |
| Decaf brewed coffee | 8 fl oz | 2–15 | Residual caffeine |
