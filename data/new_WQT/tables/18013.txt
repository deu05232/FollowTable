# Chronology of Major Wars and Conflicts (1900–present) by Region

| Period | Conflict | Primary theater | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1914–1918 | World War I | Europe / Global | Great-power war spreading across colonies |
| 1939–1945 | World War II | Europe / Global | Axis vs Allies worldwide |
| 1950–1953 | Korean War | East Asia | UN intervention on the peninsula |
| 1955–1975 | Vietnam War | Southeast Asia | Cold War-era prolonged conflict |
| 1979–1989 | Soviet–Afghan War | Central/South Asia | Insurgency and foreign intervention |
| 1980–1988 | Iran–Iraq War | Middle East | Interstate war over borders and influence |
| 1990–1991 | Gulf War | Middle East | Coalition campaign following invasion of Kuwait |
| 1991–2002 | Yugoslav Wars | Balkans | Series of secession and ethnic conflicts |
| 2003–2011 | Iraq War | Middle East | Regime change and subsequent insurgency |
| 2011–present | Syrian Civil War | Middle East | Multi-sided internal conflict with foreign involvement |
