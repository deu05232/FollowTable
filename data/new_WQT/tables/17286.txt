# State extension acceptance and paperwork requirements (administrative details, not deadline dates)

| State | Extension acceptance approach | Payment expectations when extending | Notes about required attachments |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Indiana | Uses federal extension for extension of time to file | Pay any expected tax by the normal due date | Attach a copy of the federal extension if requested |
| Pennsylvania | May recognize federal extension in some cases | Payment due by the normal due date | Keep proof of timely federal filing |
| Illinois | Automatic extension for filing | Payment due by the normal due date | No separate extension form for many filers |
