# Cholinergic vs Adrenergic Autonomic Signaling (Common Pairings)

| Pathway label | Autonomic context | Primary transmitter | Common receptor type | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cholinergic (preganglionic) | Sympathetic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) | Ganglionic transmission |
| Cholinergic (preganglionic) | Parasympathetic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) | Ganglionic transmission |
| Cholinergic (postganglionic) | Parasympathetic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Muscarinic (M) | Effector-organ synapse |
| Adrenergic (postganglionic) | Sympathetic | Norepinephrine (NE) | Adrenergic (α/β) | Effector-organ synapse |
| Catecholamine secretion | Sympathetic (adrenal medulla) | Epinephrine (Epi) | Adrenergic (α/β) | Hormonal route |
| Mixed catecholamines | Sympathetic (adrenal medulla) | NE + Epi | Adrenergic (α/β) | Proportions vary |
