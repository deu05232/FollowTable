# Chromosomal Regions and Representative Syndromes (Region-centered Summary)

| Chromosomal region | Representative syndromes | Typical variant type | Key clinical features (brief) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 5p | Cri-du-chat syndrome | Deletion | High-pitched cry, developmental delay |
| 4p16.3 | Wolf-Hirschhorn syndrome | Deletion | Growth restriction, seizures |
| 7q11.23 | Williams syndrome | Microdeletion | Supravalvular aortic stenosis, social personality |
| 22q11.2 | DiGeorge/VCFS | Microdeletion | Cardiac defects, immune issues |
| 15q11-q13 | Prader-Willi / Angelman syndromes | Imprinting/deletion | Hypotonia vs seizures/ataxia |
| 17p11.2 | Smith-Magenis syndrome | Deletion | Sleep disturbance, behavioral issues |
| 1p36 | 1p36 deletion syndrome | Deletion | Hypotonia, cardiomyopathy |
