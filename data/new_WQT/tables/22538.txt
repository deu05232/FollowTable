# Autonomic Neurotransmitters by Division and Ganglionic Release Site

| Autonomic division / pathway | Preganglionic neurotransmitter | Preganglionic release site | Postganglionic neurotransmitter | Postganglionic release site / target synapse |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sympathetic (typical) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Autonomic ganglia (nicotinic receptors on postganglionic neuron) | Norepinephrine (NE) | Neuroeffector junction on target tissues (adrenergic) |
| Parasympathetic (typical) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Terminal/intramural ganglia (nicotinic receptors) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Neuroeffector junction on target tissues (muscarinic) |
| Sympathetic to adrenal medulla | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Adrenal medulla chromaffin cells (nicotinic receptors) | Epinephrine (Epi) and Norepinephrine (NE) | Released to bloodstream (endocrine output) |
| Sympathetic cholinergic subset (exception) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Autonomic ganglia (nicotinic receptors) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Neuroeffector junction on target tissues (muscarinic) |
| Enteric (via autonomic inputs; intrinsic plexuses) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Enteric ganglia/plexuses (nicotinic receptors on enteric neurons) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Synapses within enteric circuitry and onto effector cells |
| Enteric (inhibitory transmitter examples) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Enteric ganglia/plexuses (nicotinic receptors) | Nitric oxide (NO) and Vasoactive intestinal peptide (VIP) | Released from enteric neurons onto smooth muscle/targets |
| Common co-transmission (sympathetic) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Autonomic ganglia (nicotinic receptors) | NE with ATP and Neuropeptide Y (NPY) | Co-released at neuroeffector junction (adrenergic terminals) |
