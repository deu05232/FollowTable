# Rotational Symmetry Orders of Common 2D Quadrilaterals

| Quadrilateral (2D shape) | Key properties (brief) | Axes of reflection symmetry | Order of rotational symmetry | Total symmetry count (reflections + rotations) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Square | 4 equal sides, 4 right angles | 4 | 4 | 8 |
| Rectangle (non-square) | opposite sides equal, 4 right angles | 2 | 2 | 4 |
| Rhombus (non-square) | 4 equal sides, angles not all right | 2 | 2 | 4 |
| Parallelogram (generic) | opposite sides parallel | 0 | 2 | 2 |
| Kite (generic) | two pairs of adjacent equal sides | 1 | 1 | 2 |
| Isosceles trapezium (isosceles trapezoid) | one pair of parallel sides, legs equal | 1 | 1 | 2 |
| Trapezium (general trapezoid) | one pair of parallel sides (no extra symmetry) | 0 | 1 | 1 |
| Dart (concave kite) | concave quadrilateral with kite-like equalities | 1 | 1 | 2 |
