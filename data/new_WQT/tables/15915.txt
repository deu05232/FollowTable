# Top Gear test track lap-time adjustments and conditions (expanded)

| Condition label | Typical track state | Stated adjustment | Applies to |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hot (H) | High temperature or humidity affecting grip | −2 seconds | Power Laps |
| Damp (D) | Light drizzle, mixed patches | −2 seconds | Power Laps |
| Moist (M) | Noticeably wet surface | −3 seconds | Power Laps |
| Wet (W) | Wet due to rain | −4 seconds | Power Laps |
| Melted snow (MS) | Standing water from melt | −4 seconds | Power Laps |
| Cold (C) | Low temperature affecting tires | 0 seconds | Power Laps |
