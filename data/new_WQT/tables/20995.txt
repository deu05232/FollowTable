# # Fresh Ingredients  How Long They Keep (Not Cooked)

| Ingredient | Typical fridge freshness window | How to store it | Signs it’s past its best |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fresh basil | 5–7 days | In a jar of water, loosely covered | Blackened leaves |
| Raw chicken (uncooked) | 1–2 days | On a tray, sealed container | Sour odor |
| Fresh mushrooms | 5–7 days | Paper bag | Slimy texture |
| Raw ground beef (uncooked) | 1–2 days | Sealed container | Gray/brown discoloration |
| Leafy greens | 3–5 days | Lined container with towel | Wilted, wet leaves |
