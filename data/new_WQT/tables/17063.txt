| --- | --- | --- |
| Popcorn, air-popped | 3 cups (24 g) | 9 |
| Potato chips | 1 oz (28 g) | 10 |
| Pretzels | 1 oz (28 g) | 13 |
| Dark chocolate (70–85%) | 1 oz (28 g) | 3 |
| Apple | 1 medium (182 g) | 6 |
| Grapes | 1 cup (151 g) | 11 |
| Raisins | 1/4 cup (40 g) | 18 |
