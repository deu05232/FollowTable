# ABO Genotype Reference Table (Alleles and Possible Phenotypes)

| Phenotype | Possible Genotypes | A Allele Present | B Allele Present | O Allele Present | Example Notation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | AA, AO | Yes | No | Optional | I^A I^A or I^A i |
| B | BB, BO | No | Yes | Optional | I^B I^B or I^B i |
| AB | AB | Yes | Yes | No | I^A I^B |
| O | OO | No | No | Yes | i i |
| Bombay (Oh) | hh (with any ABO genotype) | Variable | Variable | Variable | hh; appears as O in typing |
| Cis-AB (rare) | cis-AB variants | Yes | Yes | Variable | cis-AB / O |
| A2 subgroup (example) | A2A2, A2O | Yes | No | Optional | A2 variant |
