# Adult Blood Pressure Categories Defined by Percent of Reference Distribution (Population-Based)

| Category | Definition method | Systolic criterion | Diastolic criterion | How to apply |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Optimal | Percentile | < 50th percentile | < 50th percentile | Compare to population chart |
| Normal | Percentile | 50th–<75th percentile | 50th–<75th percentile | Requires percentile tables |
| High-normal | Percentile | 75th–<90th percentile | 75th–<90th percentile | Not fixed mm Hg |
| Elevated | Percentile | 90th–<95th percentile | or 90th–<95th percentile | Use age/sex-specific distribution |
| Stage 1 (distribution-based) | Percentile | 95th–<99th percentile | or 95th–<99th percentile | Must look up percentile |
| Stage 2 (distribution-based) | Percentile | ≥ 99th percentile | or ≥ 99th percentile | Must look up percentile |
| Special case | Mixed rule | ≥ 120/80 mm Hg | — | Still needs percentile for most cases |
| Limitations | — | Percentiles vary by cohort | Percentiles vary by cohort | Not a direct cuff cutoff chart |
