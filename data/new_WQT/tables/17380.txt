# Genetic Disorders by Clinical Category (No Specific Gene Locus Mapping)

| Disorder | Clinical category | Typical presenting age | Key clinical clue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cystic fibrosis | Metabolic/respiratory | Childhood | Recurrent lung infections |
| Duchenne muscular dystrophy | Neuromuscular | Early childhood | Progressive proximal weakness |
| Marfan syndrome | Connective tissue | Adolescence | Tall stature, lens dislocation |
| Huntington disease | Neurodegenerative | Adulthood | Chorea and cognitive decline |
| Sickle cell disease | Hematologic | Infancy/childhood | Hemolytic anemia, pain crises |
| Phenylketonuria (PKU) | Inborn error of metabolism | Newborn/infancy | Musty odor, neurodevelopmental delay |
| Neurofibromatosis type 1 | Neurocutaneous | Childhood | Café-au-lait spots, neurofibromas |
| Tay–Sachs disease | Lysosomal storage | Infancy | Cherry-red spot, neurodegeneration |
