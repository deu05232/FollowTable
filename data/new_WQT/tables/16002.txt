# Indonesia Entry Health Requirements (Non-vaccine measures included)

| Requirement Type | Name | Applies To | Required or Recommended | When/How | Proof Needed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vaccine | Yellow Fever | Travelers arriving from risk countries | Required | Before arrival | Vaccination certificate |
| Vaccine | Hepatitis A | Most travelers | Recommended | Pre-travel | No |
| Vaccine | Typhoid | Many travelers | Recommended | Pre-travel | No |
| Medication | Malaria prophylaxis | Travelers to endemic areas | Recommended | During trip | No |
| Vaccine | Tetanus booster | All travelers | Recommended | If not up to date | No |
| Document | Travel insurance | All travelers | Recommended | Before departure | Sometimes |
| Behavior | Mosquito avoidance | All travelers | Recommended | During trip | No |
| Screening | Temperature check | Some ports of entry | Possible | On arrival | No |
