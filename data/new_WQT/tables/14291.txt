# Blocked Sites in Mainland China + Workarounds Cheat Sheet

| Website / Service | Domain | Example URL | Typical Symptom | Common Workaround |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Google | google.com | https://www.google.com | Connection reset | Use VPN/HTTPS proxy |
| YouTube | youtube.com | https://www.youtube.com | Cannot load videos | Use VPN or alternate frontends |
| Facebook | facebook.com | https://www.facebook.com | DNS fails | Change DNS + VPN |
| X (Twitter) | x.com | https://x.com | Timeout | VPN / Shadowsocks |
| Wikipedia | wikipedia.org | https://www.wikipedia.org | TLS handshake fails | VPN / mirror sites |
| Instagram | instagram.com | https://www.instagram.com | App cannot refresh feed | VPN |
| WhatsApp | whatsapp.com | https://www.whatsapp.com | Messages not delivered | VPN / switch to WeChat |
| Telegram | telegram.org | https://telegram.org | Cannot connect | MTProxy / VPN |
