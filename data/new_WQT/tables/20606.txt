# Concerta dosing by patient group (starting dose and typical range)

| Patient group | Recommended starting dose | Typical dose range | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Children (6–12 years) | 18 mg once daily (morning) | 18–54 mg/day | Extended-release formulation |
| Adolescents (13–17 years) | 18 mg once daily (morning) | 18–72 mg/day | Do not exceed 2 mg/kg/day |
| Adults (18–65 years) | 18 or 36 mg once daily (morning) | 18–72 mg/day | Titrate based on response |
