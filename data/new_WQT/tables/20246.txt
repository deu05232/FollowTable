# Prenatal Screening Markers and Commonly Flagged Conditions

| Screening test/marker | Typical change | Conditions suggested | Follow-up recommended |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AFP (maternal serum) | Low | Trisomy 21 (Down syndrome) | NIPT, ultrasound, diagnostic testing |
| AFP (maternal serum) | High | Open neural tube defects | Targeted ultrasound, amniotic AFP |
| hCG (free β-hCG) | High | Trisomy 21 (Down syndrome) | NIPT or CVS/amniocentesis |
| uE3 | Low | Trisomy 18 (Edwards syndrome) | NIPT, detailed ultrasound |
| Inhibin A | High | Trisomy 21 (Down syndrome) | Risk recalculation, NIPT |
| PAPP-A (first trimester) | Low | Trisomy 13/18 risk | NIPT, ultrasound |
| Nuchal translucency | Increased | Aneuploidy risk (T21/T18/T13) | Fetal echo, diagnostic testing |
