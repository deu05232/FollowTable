# Civilian GS to Military Rank Equivalents (with Allowance Notes)

| Civilian Level | Typical GS Grade(s) | Military Rank Equivalent | Notes/Typical Allowances |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Entry | GS-1 to GS-3 | E-1 to E-3 | BAS/BAH may apply depending on status |
| Junior | GS-4 to GS-6 | E-4 to E-6 | Special duty pay varies |
| Mid | GS-7 to GS-9 | E-7 to E-9 | Leadership billets vary by unit |
| Senior | GS-10 to GS-12 | O-1 to O-3 | Officer housing allowances vary by location |
| Manager | GS-13 | O-4 | Staff role equivalency |
| Director | GS-14 | O-5 | Command/staff equivalency |
| Executive | GS-15 / SES | O-6 to O-8 | Senior leader equivalency |
