# Autonomic Reflex Triggers and Effector Outcomes (Messenger Names Included but Not Central)

| Reflex / trigger | Sensory receptor | Central integration | Efferent division | Common efferent transmitter | Typical organ outcome |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Baroreceptor reflex (↑BP) | Carotid sinus stretch | NTS (medulla) | ↑Parasympathetic, ↓Sympathetic | ACh (vagal), ↓NE | Bradycardia and reduced contractility |
| Baroreceptor reflex (↓BP) | Reduced stretch | NTS (medulla) | ↓Parasympathetic, ↑Sympathetic | ↑NE, ↓ACh | Tachycardia and vasoconstriction |
| Chemoreceptor reflex (↓O2/↑CO2) | Carotid/aortic bodies | Medullary centers | ↑Sympathetic | NE (plus Epi via adrenal) | Increased vascular tone and ventilation |
| Pupillary light reflex | Retina | Pretectal area | Parasympathetic | ACh | Pupillary constriction |
| Thermoregulatory sweating | Skin thermoreceptors | Hypothalamus | Sympathetic (sweat glands) | ACh (muscarinic) | Sweating and heat loss |
| Micturition reflex | Bladder stretch | Sacral spinal cord | Parasympathetic | ACh | Detrusor contraction and sphincter relaxation |
| Defecation reflex | Rectal stretch | Sacral spinal cord | Parasympathetic | ACh | Increased peristalsis and relaxation of internal sphincter |
