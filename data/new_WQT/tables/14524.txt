# Vegetable-Garden Companion Planting  Plant-to-Pest Repellency Mapping

| Plant (to grow nearby) | Best paired crops (examples) | Pests repelled (targets) |
|---|---|---|
| Chive | Carrot; lettuce; apple (near trees) | Carrot fly; aphids |
| Fennel (bronze) | Cabbage-family borders | Aphids; slugs |
| Thyme | Cabbage; strawberries | Cabbage worm; flea beetles |
| Oregano | Brassicas; peppers | Aphids; spider mites |
| Borage | Squash; tomatoes; strawberries | Tomato hornworm; cabbage worms |
| Lemon balm | Beans; brassicas | Mosquitoes; gnats |
| Tansy | Fruit trees; roses | Ants; flies; Japanese beetles |
| Calendula (pot marigold) | Tomatoes; cucumbers | Whiteflies; aphids; nematodes |
| Onion | Carrot; beet; cabbage | Carrot fly; cabbage worms |
| Wormwood | Perimeter plantings | Slugs; cabbage moths; flea beetles |
