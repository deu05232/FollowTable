# BBC Sports Personality of the Year Awards - Category Timeline

| Award category | Year introduced | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| BBC Sports Personality of the Year (Main Award) | 1954 | Original flagship prize |
| Overseas Sports Personality of the Year | 1960 | Recognizes non-UK athletes |
| Team of the Year | 1967 | Added to highlight team achievements |
| Coach of the Year | 1999 | Introduced for coaching impact |
| Helen Rollason Award | 1999 | For courage and achievement |
| Lifetime Achievement Award | 1996 | Honors long-term contribution |
| Young Sports Personality of the Year | 1999 | Focus on youth achievements |
