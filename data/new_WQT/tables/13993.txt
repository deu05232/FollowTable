# # Military Retirement Pay Plans — Retired Pay Base by Plan Type

| Plan Type | Retired Pay Base Used | What Is Averaged |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Final Pay | Final basic pay | Not averaged |
| High-36 | Average basic pay | Highest 36 months |
| CBS/REDUX | Average basic pay | Highest 36 months |
| BRS (Blended Retirement System) | Average basic pay | Highest 36 months |
| Disability Retirement | Final Pay or High-36 (as applicable) | Depends on member’s applicable retirement system |
