# Freezer-Only Guide for Leftovers (No Fridge Window)

| Cooked leftover item | Freezer time (best quality) | Packaging tip | Thawing note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cooked white rice | 6 months | Flat in zip bags | Thaw overnight in fridge |
| Cooked chili | 4–6 months | Portion into containers | Reheat to steaming |
| Cooked pulled pork | 2–3 months | Wrap + airtight container | Thaw in fridge |
| Cooked lasagna slices | 2–3 months | Freeze individually | Bake from frozen |
| Cooked chicken curry | 2–3 months | Leave headspace | Stir while reheating |
| Cooked meatballs (with sauce) | 3–4 months | Freeze in sauce | Simmer to reheat |
| Cooked vegetable soup | 2–3 months | Cool before freezing | Thaw in fridge |
| Cooked pancakes | 1–2 months | Separate with parchment | Toast to reheat |
