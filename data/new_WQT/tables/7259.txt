# War and Beauty
> Cast

| Cast | Role | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bowie Lam | Sun Bak Yeung 孫白颺 | Imperial Doctor Suen Loves Yuk-Ying A palace doctor who caters to the need of all concubines. He was romantically involved with Yuk-Ying, who bore his child and posed it as the Emperor's child. His relationship with his father was explored in episode 5 of Beauty at War . Died in episode 30 |
| Moses Chan | Kong-Wu 孔武 | Loves On-Seen A palace guard who gained the favor of the Emperor after saving his life. Was responsible for the safety of Yi-Sun and Yuk-Ying on their way to Beijing. |
| Chan Hung Lit | Sun Ching Wah 孫清華 | Father of Sun Bak-Yeung. He and his son share a rather cool relationship, with the father disapproving of the son's actions within the Inner Harem of the Palace. |
| Lo Hoi Pang | Chui Man Tin 徐萬田 | He is the master behind a plot to train Erchun to become a formidable, beautiful woman and send her into the Palace to actively gain the favour of the Emperor for the sake of his own life. He is murdered by his wife near the end of the series. |
| Wai Kar Hung | Chan Song 陳爽 | Fraternal brother of Kung-Wu. He was expelled from the Palace after he was caught stealing, and was discovered to have joined the rebel forces at the end of the series when they attacked the Palace. Dies at the end of the series defending his fraternal brother. |
