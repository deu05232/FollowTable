# Whether states honor a federal extension (no dates, rule-focused)

| State | How the federal extension is treated | What you may need to submit to the state |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Georgia | Automatically honored for most filers | No separate state form if federal extension was filed |
| New Jersey | Not automatically accepted for everyone | May require a state extension request depending on circumstances |
| Idaho | Automatically honored | No separate paperwork in many cases |
