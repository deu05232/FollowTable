# Nutrition Facts Reference  Macronutrient Targets for Adults (General Diet Planning)

| Metric | Recommended target | Basis | Applies to |
|---|---|---|---|
| Energy | 2000 kcal/day | Label reference diet | Adults |
| Protein | 50 g/day | 10% of energy | Adults |
| Total fat | 78 g/day | 35% of energy | Adults |
| Saturated fat | 20 g/day | <10% of energy | Adults |
| Carbohydrate | 275 g/day | 55% of energy | Adults |
| Fiber | 28 g/day | 14 g per 1000 kcal | Adults |
| Added sugars | 50 g/day | <10% of energy | Adults |
| Sodium | 2300 mg/day | Upper limit | Adults |
