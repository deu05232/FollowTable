# Venomous snakes of Texas with in-state range notes

| Scientific name | Common name | In-state distribution (general) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Crotalus atrox | Western diamondback rattlesnake | Much of west and central Texas; common in arid and semi-arid zones |
| Crotalus horridus | Timber rattlesnake | More common in eastern Texas woodlands and river corridors |
| Agkistrodon contortrix | Copperhead | Largely eastern half; often near forests and rocky creeks |
| Agkistrodon piscivorus | Cottonmouth | Mainly eastern and southeast; associated with wetlands and slow water |
| Micrurus tener | Texas coral snake | South and east portions; patchy but present in brush and woodland edges |
