# Smoke Point vs. Oxidative Stability and Shelf Life (Fat Composition Omitted)

| Oil | Smoke point (°C) | Oxidative stability (Rancimat, h @110°C) | Typical shelf life (months) | Recommended use |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Extra virgin olive oil | 190 | 10–12 | 18–24 | Medium-heat sautéing |
| High-oleic sunflower oil | 240 | 15–20 | 12–18 | High-heat frying |
| Soybean oil | 230 | 5–7 | 6–12 | Baking/frying blends |
| Canola oil | 205 | 7–9 | 9–12 | All-purpose |
| Rice bran oil | 232 | 12–16 | 12–18 | Deep frying |
| Grapeseed oil | 215 | 3–5 | 6–9 | Quick sear; avoid long storage |
| Peanut oil | 230 | 9–11 | 12–18 | Frying |
| Flaxseed oil | 107 | 0.5–1.5 | 2–3 | No-heat applications |
