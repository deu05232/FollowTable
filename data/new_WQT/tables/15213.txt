# Major Byzantine Offices — Description of Responsibilities and Rank (Reference Sheet)

| Office title | Administrative sphere | Core responsibilities | Rank/precedence | Typical insignia | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Basileus | Imperial | Supreme authority; issues edicts and commands army | Highest | Purple robes | Office definition only |
| Logothetes tou dromou | Foreign/communications | Diplomacy, posts, intelligence | High | Seal rings | Changes by period |
| Megas doux | Naval | Commander of fleet and maritime themes | High | Baton | Sometimes political role |
| Domestikos ton scholon | Military | Commander of tagmata/field army | High | Sword | East/West split occurs |
| Eparch of Constantinople | Urban administration | Markets, guilds, policing | High | Staff | Key city office |
| Protovestiarios | Court | Wardrobe/treasury management | High | Keys | Often eunuch office |
| Chartophylax | Ecclesiastical admin | Archives, synodal paperwork | Medium | Scroll case | Patriarchal staff |
| Strategos | Provincial/military | Theme governance, defense, taxes | Variable | Seal | Not always hereditary |
