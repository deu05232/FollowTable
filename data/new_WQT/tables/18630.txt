# Concerta ER dosing guide by available tablet strength and typical titration step

| Patient group | Typical starting daily amount | Common step-up pattern (interval-based) | Usual maximum daily amount |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Children (6–12) | 18 mg each morning | Increase by 18 mg at a time based on response | 54 mg/day |
| Adolescents (13–17) | 18 mg each morning | Increase by 18 mg increments as needed | 72 mg/day (do not exceed 2 mg/kg/day) |
| Adults (18–65) | 18 or 36 mg each morning | Increase by 18 mg increments based on clinical response | 72 mg/day |
