# DLNA device media support matrix (formats by category)

| Device / Profile | Video encoding families supported | Audio encoding families supported | Image formats supported | Common file packaging it can read |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| TV A (DLNA DMP) | MPEG-2 Video, H.264/AVC, MPEG-4 Part 2 | MP3, AAC-LC, WMA | JPEG, PNG | MPEG-TS, MP4, AVI |
| Blu-ray Player B (DLNA DMP) | MPEG-2 Video, H.264/AVC | MP3, AAC-LC, LPCM | JPEG | MPEG-TS, MP4 |
| Game Console C (DLNA DMP) | H.264/AVC, VC-1 | AAC-LC, MP3 | JPEG, BMP | MP4, ASF |
| NAS/Streamer D (DLNA DMS/DMP) | MPEG-2 Video, H.264/AVC, DivX/Xvid | MP3, AAC-LC, FLAC | JPEG, PNG, GIF | MP4, MKV, AVI |
