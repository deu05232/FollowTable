# P2P Lending  Borrower Risk Grades and Typical APR Ranges

| Risk grade | Typical credit score band | Typical APR (approx.) | Typical loan size | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Grade A | 760–850 | 6%–10% | $10k–$40k | Prime borrowers, lowest expected losses |
| Grade B | 720–759 | 10%–14% | $5k–$35k | Strong credit, moderate pricing |
| Grade C | 680–719 | 14%–20% | $3k–$25k | Mid-tier approvals depend on DTI |
| Grade D | 640–679 | 20%–28% | $2k–$15k | Higher pricing, tighter verification |
| Grade E | 600–639 | 28%–36% | $1k–$10k | Often requires stable income history |
| Grade F/HR | <600 | 36%+ | $1k–$5k | Many platforms decline or restrict |
| Co-signed | 620–700 | 12%–24% | $2k–$20k | Co-signer can shift grade upward |
