# Gubernatorial term limits in selected U.S. states (rule summary)

| State | Term-limit rule for governor |
| --- | --- |
| California | 2 terms total (lifetime limit) |
| Michigan | 2 terms total (lifetime limit) |
| Virginia | No consecutive terms (may serve non-consecutive terms) |
| New York | No term limits |
| Arizona | 2 consecutive terms |
| Oklahoma | 2 consecutive terms |
| Utah | No term limits |
