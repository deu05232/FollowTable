# National Social Work Salaries Grouped by Work Setting (Not by Job Title)

| Work Setting | Common Social Work Roles Included | National Average Pay | Typical Range | Pay Drivers |
| --- | --- | ---:| --- | --- |
| Hospitals & Health Systems | Medical SW, Discharge Planning, Oncology SW | $72,600 | $58,000–$92,000 | Shift differentials, specialty units |
| Outpatient Behavioral Health | LCSW, Therapist, Intake Clinician | $70,900 | $52,000–$98,000 | Billable hours, licensure |
| Schools (K-12) | School SW, Student Support Specialist | $61,700 | $47,000–$80,000 | District pay scales |
| Child Welfare Agencies | Child & Family SW, Foster Care SW | $53,800 | $40,000–$71,000 | Public funding, caseload |
| Nonprofit Community Services | Case Manager, Housing Navigator | $49,200 | $36,000–$66,000 | Grant constraints |
| Hospice & Home Health | Hospice SW, Palliative Care SW | $67,500 | $52,000–$86,000 | On-call expectations |
| Corrections & Reentry | Forensic SW, Reentry Case Manager | $63,400 | $48,000–$85,000 | Hazard pay, security clearance |
| Private Practice | LCSW (Self-employed) | $89,300 | $55,000–$140,000 | Panel rates, volume |
