# Pick-3 Lottery — Bet Types, Payouts, and Odds (mixed focus)

| Bet type | How to win | Typical payout | Odds (1 in) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Straight | Exact order | $500 | 1,000 |
| Box (6-way) | Any order (all digits different) | $80 | 167 |
| Box (3-way) | Any order (two digits same) | $160 | 333 |
| Straight/Box | Split bet | Varies | Varies |
| Front Pair | First two digits | $50 | 100 |
| Back Pair | Last two digits | $50 | 100 |
