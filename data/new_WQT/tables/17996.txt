# Felony theft (larceny) penalties by state (selected examples)

| State | Felony theft threshold (property value) | Maximum prison term | Maximum fine | Statute / source |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| California | Grand theft (generally $950+) | Up to 3 years | Up to $10,000 | Cal. Penal Code §§ 487, 489 |
| Texas | $2,500+ (varies by degree) | Up to 20 years (depending on degree) | Up to $10,000 | Tex. Penal Code § 31.03 |
| Florida | $750+ (3rd degree felony baseline) | Up to 5 years | Up to $5,000 | Fla. Stat. § 812.014 |
| New York | Grand larceny (degrees vary) | Up to 25 years (top degree) | Fines vary by degree | N.Y. Penal Law §§ 155.30–155.42 |
