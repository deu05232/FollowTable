# Federal Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT) and Other Federal Add-ons Affecting Capital Gains

| Federal Provision | Who Pays | Rate | Applies To | Threshold Example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| NIIT (Net Investment Income Tax) | Higher-income filers | 3.8% | Net investment income incl. capital gains | $200k Single / $250k MFJ |
| Additional Medicare Tax | Earned income only | 0.9% | Wages, not capital gains | $200k Single / $250k MFJ |
| AMT Interaction (simplified) | Some taxpayers | Varies | Can change effective rate | Depends on deductions |
| Qualified Dividends Alignment | Many investors | 0/15/20% | Shares LTCG rate structure | Uses LTCG brackets |
| Collectibles Rate | Certain assets | 28% max | Art, coins, etc. | Federal rule |
| Depreciation Recapture | Real estate/business | 25% max | Section 1250 gains | Federal rule |
