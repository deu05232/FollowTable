# Canada Working Income Tax Benefit (WITB)  Maximum Benefit and Net Income Limits

| Family situation | Net income must be less than (Single) | Net income must be less than (Couple) | Maximum benefit |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Single, no children | CAD 12,000 | CAD 18,000 | CAD 1,000 |
| Single, 1 child | CAD 14,500 | CAD 20,500 | CAD 1,600 |
| Single, 2 children | CAD 16,500 | CAD 22,500 | CAD 2,100 |
| Couple, no children | CAD 15,000 | CAD 21,000 | CAD 1,800 |
| Couple, 1 child | CAD 17,500 | CAD 23,500 | CAD 2,400 |
| Couple, 2+ children | CAD 20,000 | CAD 26,000 | CAD 2,800 |
