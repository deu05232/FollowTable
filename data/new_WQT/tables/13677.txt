# Battery Life by Usage Scenario (Not Model-Centric Rows)

| Usage scenario | Example earbud models | Typical battery life range (h) | What reduces it |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ANC on, music | Sony WF-1000XM5; Bose QC series | 4.5–8 | ANC strength, wind noise processing |
| ANC off, music | Samsung Galaxy Buds2 Pro; Jabra Elite series | 6–11 | higher volume, EQ boosts |
| Calls (mic active) | AirPods; Jabra; Teams-certified buds | 3–7 | sidetone, beamforming load |
| Gaming/low-latency mode | Soundcore; JBL | 3–6 | low-latency DSP profile |
| High-bitrate codec (LDAC/aptX Adaptive) | Sony; Sennheiser | 3–6 | codec bitrate, RF conditions |
| One earbud only | many true wireless models | 5–10 | relay/primary ear drain |
| Cold weather commuting | any model | 20–40% less than rated | battery chemistry temperature impact |
