# Illinois  drug possession penalties by substance schedule (offense-specific)

| Substance category | Charge label | Felony class (if charged as felony) | Possible penalty summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cannabis (above threshold) | Possession | Class 4 felony | 1–3 years; fines may apply |
| Cocaine | Possession | Class 1 felony | 4–15 years depending on amount |
| Heroin | Possession | Class X felony | 6–30 years depending on amount |
| Methamphetamine | Possession | Class 2 felony | 3–7 years depending on amount |
| Prescription opioids (no Rx) | Possession | Class 4 felony | 1–3 years |
| LSD | Possession | Class 2 felony | 3–7 years depending on amount |
