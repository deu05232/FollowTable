# Common Names vs IUPAC Names for Oxygen-Containing Compounds

| Common name | IUPAC name | Main oxygen-containing functional group | Formula | Typical use/occurrence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Rubbing alcohol | Propan-2-ol | Alcohol | C3H8O | Disinfectant/solvent |
| Acetone | Propanone | Ketone | C3H6O | Solvent |
| Vinegar (acid component) | Ethanoic acid | Carboxylic acid | C2H4O2 | Food acid |
| Formalin (solute) | Methanal | Aldehyde | CH2O | Preservative |
| Oil of wintergreen | Methyl 2-hydroxybenzoate | Ester (and phenol) | C8H8O3 | Flavor/fragrance |
| Glycerin | Propane-1,2,3-triol | Triol (alcohol) | C3H8O3 | Humectant |
| Ethylene glycol | Ethane-1,2-diol | Diol (alcohol) | C2H6O2 | Antifreeze |
