# Term Limits in State Government (Governor, Legislature, and Attorney General)

| State | Governor term limit | Legislature term limit | Attorney General term limit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Alabama | 2 consecutive terms | No limits | No term limits |
| Colorado | 2 consecutive terms | House: 4 terms; Senate: 2 terms | 2 consecutive terms |
| Delaware | 2 terms | No limits | 2 terms |
| Florida | 2 consecutive terms | 8 years | 2 consecutive terms |
| Georgia | 2 consecutive terms | No limits | 2 consecutive terms |
| Idaho | No term limits | No limits | No term limits |
| New Mexico | 2 consecutive terms | No limits | 2 consecutive terms |
| Texas | No term limits | No limits | No term limits |
