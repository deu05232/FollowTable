# Extraocular (Eye-Movement) Muscles and Their Cranial Nerve Innervation (Quick Check)

| Extraocular muscle | Primary cranial nerve (CN) innervation | Branch / note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Medial rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Inferior division (typical) |
| Inferior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Inferior division (typical) |
| Superior rectus | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Superior division (typical) |
| Inferior oblique | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Inferior division (typical) |
| Lateral rectus | Abducens nerve (CN VI) | Sole motor supply |
| Superior oblique | Trochlear nerve (CN IV) | Sole motor supply |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Superior division (raises upper eyelid) |
| Pupillary sphincter (sphincter pupillae) | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) | Parasympathetic via ciliary ganglion (pupil constriction) |
