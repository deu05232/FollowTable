# Glycemic Index Categories with Food Examples (Not a Per-Food Lookup)

| GI Category | GI Range | Example Foods (grouped in one cell) | Typical Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Very Low | 0–25 | spinach; lettuce; broccoli; mushrooms; peanuts; almonds | Mostly non-starchy foods |
| Low | 26–55 | apples; pears; kidney beans; chickpeas; milk; yogurt (plain) | Often higher fiber/protein |
| Medium | 56–69 | basmati rice; couscous; table sugar; pita bread; oatmeal (instant) | Portion size matters |
| High | 70–84 | corn flakes; white bread; jasmine rice; watermelon; pretzels | Faster glucose response |
| Very High | 85–100 | baked potato; glucose; rice cakes | Near-glucose response |
| Mixed Meals | Varies | pizza; burrito; cereal with milk | Depends on fat/protein/fiber |
| Sports Fuel | Varies | energy gels; sports drinks | Often high GI by design |
