# Italian Red Wine Varietals  Typical Body and Flavor Profile

| Varietal | Common Aromas & Flavors | Typical Body |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Barbera | Sour cherry, plum, dried herbs, licorice | Medium |
| Sangiovese | Cherry, tomato leaf, leather, earthy spice | Medium |
| Nebbiolo | Rose, tar, red cherry, anise | Medium to full |
| Montepulciano | Black cherry, cocoa, baking spice | Medium to full |
| Primitivo | Blackberry jam, pepper, sweet spice | Full |
| Dolcetto | Black fruit, almond, violets | Medium |
| Corvina (Valpolicella) | Tart cherry, cranberry, dried flowers | Light to medium |
