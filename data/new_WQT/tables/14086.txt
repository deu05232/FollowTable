# Top Gear test track  how penalties are applied to recorded laps

| Situation during the run | Why it matters | Adjustment applied |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Visible drizzle mid-lap | Grip changes across the lap | −3 seconds |
| Track drying quickly | Later sectors faster than earlier | −2 seconds |
| Very high ambient temperature | Tyre performance differs | −2 seconds |
| Light snow melt | Water reduces traction | −4 seconds |
