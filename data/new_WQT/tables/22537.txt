# Autonomic Neurotransmitters by Division, Fiber Type, and Synaptic Site

| Autonomic division / pathway | Fiber segment | Neurotransmitter released | Receptor on target cell | Main synaptic site (where released) | Notes (cholinergic vs adrenergic) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sympathetic (typical) | Preganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) | Autonomic ganglion | Cholinergic preganglionic signaling is the rule for both divisions |
| Sympathetic (typical) | Postganglionic | Norepinephrine (NE) | Adrenergic (α/β) | Effector organ (neuroeffector junction) | Adrenergic postganglionic signaling is common |
| Sympathetic to sweat glands | Postganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Muscarinic (M) | Effector organ (sweat gland) | Classic sympathetic exception: postganglionic cholinergic |
| Sympathetic to renal vasculature | Postganglionic | Dopamine | Dopamine (D1) | Effector organ (renal vessels) | Often described as a distinct sympathetic transmitter in some fibers |
| Parasympathetic | Preganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) | Terminal/intramural ganglion | Ganglia are near or within the target organ |
| Parasympathetic | Postganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Muscarinic (M) | Effector organ | Cholinergic at both ganglion and effector in most parasympathetic pathways |
| Adrenal medulla (modified sympathetic ganglion) | Preganglionic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) on chromaffin cells | Adrenal medulla | Leads to secretion into blood rather than a typical postganglionic synapse |
| Adrenal medulla output | Endocrine secretion | Epinephrine (± norepinephrine) | Adrenergic (α/β) | Circulation → distant effector tissues | Hormonal arm of sympathetic system (not a postganglionic neuron) |
