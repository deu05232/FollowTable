# United States felony charges and penalties (not broken down by state)

| Felony charge | Typical penalty range |
| --- | --- |
| Burglary | 1–20 years in prison |
| Robbery | 2–25 years in prison |
| Drug trafficking | 5–40 years in prison |
| Aggravated assault | 1–15 years in prison |
| Grand theft | 1–10 years in prison |
