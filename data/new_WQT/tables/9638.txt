# Yellow Fever Vaccination Requirement by Country (Traveler Entry Rules)

| Country | Yellow fever vaccine required for entry? | Who is affected | Documentation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ghana | Yes | All arriving travelers | International Certificate of Vaccination |
| Kenya | Yes (from risk areas) | Travelers arriving from endemic countries | Proof may be checked at border |
| South Africa | Yes (from risk areas) | Travelers arriving from yellow-fever risk countries | Certificate required |
| Indonesia | Sometimes (from risk areas) | Travelers arriving from endemic countries | Certificate may be requested |
| Brazil | No (entry), but recommended | Travelers to risk zones | Keep proof for onward travel |
| France | No | General travelers | Not applicable |
