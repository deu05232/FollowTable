# Food glycemic impact categories with examples (not item-level measured values)

| Category | Typical glycemic index range | Example foods |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Low impact | 55 or less | lentils; most non-starchy vegetables; apples |
| Moderate impact | 56–69 | quick oats; couscous; rye bread |
| High impact | 70 and above | white bread; jasmine rice; corn flakes |
