# Penalties for alcohol or drug - related violations | New York State DMV

| Violation | Mandatory Fine | Maximum Jail Term |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Second AGG DWI in 10 years (E felony) | $1,000 - $5,000 | 4 years |
| Third AGG DWI in 10 years (D felony) | $2,000 - $10,000 | 7 years |
| Driving While Intoxicated (DWI) or Driving While Impaired by a Drug (DWAI-Drug) | $500 - $1,000 | 1 year |
