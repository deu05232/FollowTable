# State-by-state felony sentencing overview (felony class format)

| State | Felony class (as labeled in the state) | Example felony charge | Maximum prison term | Maximum fine |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Oregon | Class B felony | Burglary in the first degree | Up to 10 years | Up to $250,000 |
| Ohio | Felony of the 5th degree | Theft (above threshold) | 6–12 months | Up to $2,500 |
| Virginia | Class 5 felony | Fraud (selected offenses) | Up to 10 years | Up to $2,500 |
| Georgia | Felony | Possession of Schedule I/II (first offense) | Up to 15 years | Fines possible |
| Texas | State jail felony | Credit/debit card abuse (selected cases) | 180 days–2 years | Up to $10,000 |
