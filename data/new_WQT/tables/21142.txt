# Economics careers that are explicitly about macro, finance, or regional economic planning

| Role | Main output | Common tools |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Macroeconomic Forecaster | GDP/inflation/unemployment outlooks | time-series, scenario analysis |
| Regional Economist | local economic reports, cluster studies | input-output, GIS + economic data |
| Financial Economist | asset pricing / risk studies | econometrics, modeling |
| Public Finance Analyst | tax/spending impact notes | incidence analysis, forecasting |
| Trade Policy Economist | tariff and agreement briefs | gravity models, trade statistics |
| Econometrics Specialist | model estimates and validation | regression, causal inference |
