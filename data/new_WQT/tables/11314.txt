# KJV vs NIV verse wording differences (sample verses)

| Verse | KJV | NIV |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Genesis 1:1 | In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth. | In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth. |
| Psalm 23:1 | The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want. | The LORD is my shepherd, I lack nothing. |
| Matthew 5:3 | Blessed are the poor in spirit: for theirs is the kingdom of heaven. | Blessed are the poor in spirit, for theirs is the kingdom of heaven. |
| John 3:16 | For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son... | For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son... |
