# Sanskrit Asana Glossary with Meanings (English Names Not Provided Consistently)

| Sanskrit Term (IAST) | Devanagari | Literal Meaning | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vīrabhadrāsana | वीरभद्रासन | "Warrior" (Vīra) + "seat" (āsana) | Often numbered I/II/III |
| Utkaṭāsana | उत्कटासन | "Fierce" or "powerful" seat | Commonly called Chair Pose |
| Trikoṇāsana | त्रिकोणासन | "Three-angle" seat | Standing lateral extension |
| Paśchimottānāsana | पश्चिमोत्तानासन | "West" stretch | Seated forward fold family |
| Nāvāsana | नावासन | "Boat" seat | Core-focused posture |
| Vṛkṣāsana | वृक्षासन | "Tree" seat | Balance posture |
| Bhujangāsana | भुजंगासन | "Cobra" seat | Gentle backbend |
