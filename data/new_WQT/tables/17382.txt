# Genetic disorders overview with categories and key clinical notes (no gene mapping)

| Disorder | Broad category | Typical onset | Key clinical note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cystic fibrosis | Autosomal recessive disorder | Childhood | Chronic lung infections |
| Duchenne muscular dystrophy | Muscular dystrophy | Early childhood | Progressive muscle weakness |
| Down syndrome | Chromosomal condition | Birth | Developmental delay |
| Marfan syndrome | Connective tissue disorder | Adolescence | Aortic dilation risk |
| Huntington disease | Neurodegenerative disorder | Adulthood | Chorea and cognitive decline |
| Neurofibromatosis type 1 | Tumor predisposition syndrome | Childhood | Café-au-lait spots |
| Sickle cell disease | Hemoglobinopathy | Childhood | Pain crises |
