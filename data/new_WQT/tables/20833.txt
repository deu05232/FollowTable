# Concerta dose limits and starting doses across pediatric and adult age bands

| Population | Starting dose | Maximum daily dose mentioned | How dose is expressed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Children 6–12 | 18 mg/day | 54 mg/day | fixed mg per day |
| Adolescents 13–17 | 18 mg/day | 72 mg/day | fixed mg per day |
| Adolescents 13–17 | 18 mg/day | 2 mg/kg/day | weight-based ceiling (if stated) |
| Adults 18–65 | 18–36 mg/day | 72 mg/day | fixed mg per day |
| Adults with low prior stimulant exposure | 18 mg/day | 72 mg/day | conservative start |
| Adults switching within ER products | 36 mg/day | 72 mg/day | start may vary by history |
