# Sanskrit Transliteration Glossary for Yoga Terms (Not a Pose List)

| Sanskrit (Transliteration) | English Meaning | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Asana | posture/seat | A general term, not a specific pose |
| Vinyasa | to place in a special way | Often used for flow sequences |
| Pranayama | breath regulation | Breathing practices |
| Drishti | focused gaze | Concentration point |
| Bandha | lock/seal | Energetic engagement |
| Mudra | gesture/seal | Hand/energy gestures |
| Chakra | wheel/energy center | Subtle body concept |
| Mantra | sacred sound | Repetition practice |
| Shavasana | corpse pose | This one is a pose, but the table is primarily concepts |
| Namaste | I bow to you | Greeting/closing |
