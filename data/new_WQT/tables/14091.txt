# Cranial Nerves Associated with Eye Function  Motor Sensory Classification and Example Targets

| Cranial nerve | Name | Classification (motor/sensory/both) | Main eye-related role | Example muscle (if motor) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| II | Optic | Sensory | Vision | null |
| III | Oculomotor | Mainly motor | Most eye movements; pupil constriction | Medial rectus |
| IV | Trochlear | Motor | Vertical/torsional movement | Superior oblique |
| V1 | Trigeminal (ophthalmic) | Sensory | Corneal sensation | null |
| VI | Abducens | Mainly motor | Abduction | Lateral rectus |
| VII | Facial | Both | Blink/eye closure | Orbicularis oculi |
| IX | Glossopharyngeal | Both | No direct eye movement | null |
| X | Vagus | Both | No direct eye movement | null |
