| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Nail polish remover | Propanone | Ketone | C3H6O | Consumer solvent |
| Oil of wintergreen | Methyl 2-hydroxybenzoate | Ester + phenol | C8H8O3 | Flavor/fragrance |
| Antifreeze | Ethane-1,2-diol | Diol (alcohol) | C2H6O2 | Coolant |
| Glycerin | Propane-1,2,3-triol | Triol (alcohol) | C3H8O3 | Humectant |
| Formalin | Methanal (aq.) | Aldehyde | CH2O | Preservative |
