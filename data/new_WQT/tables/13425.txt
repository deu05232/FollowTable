# Tuition & Fees by Student Type (Not School-by-School)

| Student Category | Typical Tuition & Fees Range | Example Schools Mentioned | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Community College (in-district) | $2,000–$4,500 | Temple College | Often excludes books |
| Public University (in-state) | $8,500–$12,500 | Ohio State University; University of Memphis | Fees vary widely |
| Public University (out-of-state) | $18,000–$32,000 | Ohio State University; University of Memphis | Nonresident surcharges |
| Flagship/Public (nonresident high) | $25,000–$35,000 | University of Connecticut | Program differentials |
| Private University | $30,000–$45,000 | Delaware Valley University (example) | Aid varies by student |
| Conservatory/Specialty | $0–$5,000 | Curtis Institute of Music (example) | Some are tuition-free |
| Per-credit Institutions | $140–$600 per credit | Nevada State College; SLCC | Hard to annualize |
| Online/Part-time | Highly variable | Various | Depends on credits taken |
