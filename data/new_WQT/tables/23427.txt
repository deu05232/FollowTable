# Regular and Semi-Regular Polyhedra and Their Rotation Group Orders

| Solid | Type | Rotation symmetry group | Rotational group order | Full symmetry order (incl. reflections) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Tetrahedron | Platonic solid | T | 12 | 24 | 4 faces |
| Cube | Platonic solid | O | 24 | 48 | dual to octahedron |
| Octahedron | Platonic solid | O | 24 | 48 | dual to cube |
| Dodecahedron | Platonic solid | I | 60 | 120 | dual to icosahedron |
| Icosahedron | Platonic solid | I | 60 | 120 | dual to dodecahedron |
| Cuboctahedron | Archimedean solid | O | 24 | 48 | vertex-transitive |
| Icosidodecahedron | Archimedean solid | I | 60 | 120 | vertex-transitive |
