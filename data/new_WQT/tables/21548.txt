# State Executive Term Limits (Governor and Other Offices)

| State | Governor | Lieutenant Governor | Attorney General |
|---|---|---|---|
| Alabama | 2 consecutive terms | No limit | No limit |
| Florida | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms |
| Georgia | 2 consecutive terms | No limit | No limit |
| Delaware | 2 terms | No limit | No limit |
| Colorado | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms | 2 consecutive terms |
| Texas | No limit | No limit | No limit |
| Virginia | 1 term | 1 term | No limit |
| Washington | 2 terms | 2 terms | 2 terms |
