# Effector-Organ Mapping of ANS  Receptors and Functional Effects (Transmitters Not the Focus)

| Effector organ | Sympathetic receptor (typical) | Parasympathetic receptor (typical) | Primary functional change | Example clinical correlate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Eye (pupil) | α1 | M3 | Mydriasis vs miosis | Anticholinergics cause dilation |
| Heart | β1 | M2 | ↑HR/↑contractility vs ↓HR | β-blockers reduce rate |
| Bronchi | β2 | M3 | Bronchodilation vs bronchoconstriction | β2-agonists relieve asthma |
| GI tract | α, β | M3 | ↓motility vs ↑motility | Opioids worsen constipation (indirect) |
| Bladder detrusor | β3 | M3 | Relaxation vs contraction | β3-agonists treat overactive bladder |
| Sweat glands | M3 (sympathetic cholinergic) | — | Increased sweating | Heat intolerance with antimuscarinics |
| Blood vessels (skin) | α1 | — | Vasoconstriction | Shock causes cool extremities |
| Adrenal medulla | Nn (ganglionic) | — | Catecholamine secretion | Pheochromocytoma symptoms |
