# Aneuploidy screening markers and associated conditions

| Marker | Typical finding | Associated condition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Inhibin A | High | Trisomy 21 (Down syndrome) |
| AFP | Low | Trisomy 18 (Edwards syndrome) |
| hCG | High | Trisomy 21 (Down syndrome) |
| PAPP-A | Low | Trisomy 13 (Patau syndrome) |
