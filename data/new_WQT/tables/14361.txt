# Vaccination Requirements by Entry Situation (Not by Destination Country)

| Entry Situation | Vaccine | Required/Recommended | Applies When | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Traveling from a yellow-fever risk area | Yellow fever | Required | Proof needed at border/airport | Depends on itinerary and transit countries |
| Visiting rural areas | Rabies | Recommended | High animal exposure likelihood | Consider pre-exposure series |
| Visiting during outbreak | Polio booster | Recommended/May be required | Active transmission reported | Requirements can change rapidly |
| Long-term stay (>4 weeks) | Hepatitis B | Recommended | Extended exposure risk | Consider vaccination if not immune |
| Adventure travel | Tetanus booster | Recommended | Injury risk | Ensure routine vaccines up to date |
| Food/water risk travel | Typhoid | Recommended | Street food, remote areas | Injectable or oral options |
| Healthcare/aid work | Hepatitis A | Recommended | Patient contact/field work | Often paired with HepB |
