# European Seat Belt Requirements by Country (Selected Examples)

| Country | Who Must Wear a Seat Belt | Seating Positions Covered | Notable Exemptions | Typical Monetary Penalty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| United Kingdom | Most occupants | Front & rear | Medical exemption | GBP 100 |
| France | All occupants | All seats | Taxi drivers in service (limited) | EUR 135 |
| Germany | All occupants | All seats | Medical exemption | EUR 30 |
| Spain | All occupants | All seats | Certain urban taxi cases | EUR 200 |
| Italy | All occupants | All seats | Limited medical exemption | EUR 83–332 |
| Netherlands | All occupants | All seats | Medical exemption | EUR 150 |
| Sweden | All occupants | All seats | Very limited exceptions | SEK 1500 |
