# Veterans & Military Compensation  Plan-by-Plan Payout Basis (DoD vs VA)

| Plan / Benefit | Administered by | Who it is for (short) | Based on (payout determination) | Payment type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| VA Disability Compensation | U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) | Veterans with service-connected conditions | **VA disability rating % + dependency status**, paid per VA rate schedule | Monthly, tax-free |
| VA Disability Pension (Needs-Based) | VA | Wartime veterans with limited income/assets and non-service-connected disability/age | **Countable income and net worth vs statutory pension limits** | Monthly (may be reduced by income) |
| Special Monthly Compensation (SMC) | VA | Severe disabilities (loss of use, aid & attendance, etc.) | **SMC level criteria**, paid as add-on to VA compensation | Monthly add-on |
| DoD Longevity Retirement (Active Duty) | Department of Defense (DoD) | Career members who retire after 20+ years | **Retired base pay (Final Pay or High-36) × longevity multiplier** | Monthly retired pay |
| DoD Reserve (Non-Regular) Retirement | DoD | Guard/Reserve with qualifying points; pay typically starts at retirement age | **Retired pay base × (retirement points converted to equivalent years) × multiplier** | Monthly (usually at qualifying age) |
| DoD Disability Retirement | DoD | Members found unfit with qualifying disability | **Higher of** (disability % × retired base pay) **or** (longevity formula) | Monthly retired pay |
| Combat-Related Special Compensation (CRSC) | DoD | Retirees with combat-related disabilities | **Reimburses (part of) VA offset based on combat-related disability and retired pay limits** | Monthly, special compensation |
| Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC) | VA | Eligible survivors of service members/veterans | **Statutory DIC rate + add-ons (children/aid & attendance/etc.)** | Monthly, tax-free |
