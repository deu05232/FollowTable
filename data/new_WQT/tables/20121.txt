# A level subject entry requirements (no GCSE availability indicator)

| Subject | Typical entry requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Biology | GCSE Biology grade 6 (or Combined Science 6-6) |
| Chemistry | GCSE Chemistry grade 6 (or Combined Science 6-6) |
| Physics | GCSE Physics grade 6 and Maths grade 6 |
| History | GCSE History grade 6 |
| Geography | GCSE Geography grade 6 |
| Computer Science | GCSE Maths grade 6 |
