# Companion Planting Suggestions (Benefits Without Explicit Pest Deterrence Claims)

| Main Crop | Suggested Companion Plants | Claimed Benefit | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tomatoes | Basil, parsley | Improved flavor, pollinator activity | Often used in kitchen gardens |
| Cucumbers | Dill, nasturtium | Better pollination | Provide ground cover |
| Carrots | Radish, leaf lettuce | Row marking, space efficiency | Intercrop for succession |
| Beans | Corn, squash | Structural support, weed suppression | “Three Sisters” style |
| Brassicas | Chamomile, calendula | General garden diversity | Often recommended informally |
| Strawberries | Borage, thyme | Attract beneficial insects | May improve fruit set |
| Peppers | Marigold, oregano | Dense planting canopy | Helps reduce evaporation |
| Onions | Beets, spinach | Efficient bed use | Similar watering needs |
