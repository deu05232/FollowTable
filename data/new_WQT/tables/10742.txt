| High Grade | Aa2 | AA | AA | Very low |
| Upper Medium | A1 | A+ | A+ | Low |
| Upper Medium | A2 | A | A | Low |
| Lower Medium | Baa1 | BBB+ | BBB+ | Moderate |
| Lower Medium | Baa2 | BBB | BBB | Moderate |
| Lower Medium | Baa3 | BBB- | BBB- | Moderate |
| Non-Investment | Ba1 | BB+ | BB+ | Elevated |
| Non-Investment | B1 | B+ | B+ | High |
