# Entry-level roles commonly targeted by economics majors in analysis-heavy tracks

| Job title | Field | Why an economics major fits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Economic Analyst | Public policy / government | Uses data interpretation, forecasting, and evaluation |
| Pricing Analyst | Microeconomics / strategy | Applies demand, elasticity, and market structure concepts |
| Market Research Analyst (economics focus) | Applied economics | Translates economic indicators into business insights |
| Program Evaluation Analyst | Social programs | Uses causal reasoning and quantitative methods |
| Competition/Antitrust Analyst | Regulation | Applies industrial organization and market power analysis |
| Development Economics Associate | International development | Works with poverty, growth, and impact metrics |
