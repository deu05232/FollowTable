# California  felony classes compared with misdemeanor and infraction penalties (mixed table)

| Offense level | Maximum jail/prison term | Maximum fine | Examples |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Infraction | null (no jail) | $250 | Minor traffic violations |
| Misdemeanor | Up to 1 year (county jail) | $1,000 | Simple battery |
| Wobbler (misd./fel.) | Up to 1 year or felony term | Varies | Some theft offenses |
| Felony (low-level) | 16 months–3 years | $10,000 | Certain property crimes |
| Felony (serious/violent) | 5 years to life | $10,000+ | Robbery, certain assaults |
| Felony (capital) | Life or death | Not specified | Special circumstances murder |
