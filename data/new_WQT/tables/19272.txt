# # Boiling point ranges for gasoline fractions and LPG blends

| Fuel blend / fraction | Typical components | Boiling Point Range (°C) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Gasoline (light fraction) | C5–C7 hydrocarbons (mixture) | 30–100 |
| Gasoline (heavy fraction) | C7–C10 hydrocarbons (mixture) | 90–200 |
| LPG (commercial mix) | propane + butane (blend) | -42 to -0.5 |
