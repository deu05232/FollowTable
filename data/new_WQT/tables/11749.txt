# Ancient Egyptian Deities and Their Domains (Name-to-Role Index)

| Deity | Domain / Role |
|---|---|
| Amun | God of air and hidden things; king of the gods (later syncretized as Amun-Ra) |
| Anubis | God of mummification and funerary rites |
| Bastet | Goddess of protection, cats, and the home |
| Geb | God of the earth |
| Hathor | Goddess of love, music, and joy |
| Horus | God of kingship and the sky |
| Isis | Goddess of magic and motherhood |
| Khnum | God of the Nile’s source and creator who fashions beings on a potter’s wheel |
| Ma'at | Goddess of truth, justice, and cosmic order |
| Nephthys | Goddess of mourning and funerary protection |
| Nut | Goddess of the sky |
| Osiris | God of the dead and the afterlife |
| Ptah | God of craftsmen and creation |
| Ra | God of the sun |
| Sekhmet | Goddess of war and healing |
| Thoth | God of writing, knowledge, and wisdom |
