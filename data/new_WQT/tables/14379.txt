# Amino acids list emphasizing acid–base constants (not classification-focused)

| Amino acid | Acid dissociation values (carboxyl and amino groups) | Typical isoelectric point range | Side-chain ionization behavior |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Aspartic acid | Low pH deprotonation of carboxyl; higher pH deprotonation of amino | Acidic range | Side chain behaves as an additional acid |
| Lysine | Low pH deprotonation of carboxyl; higher pH deprotonation of amino | Basic range | Side chain behaves as an additional base |
| Cysteine | Low pH deprotonation of carboxyl; higher pH deprotonation of amino | Near-neutral range | Thiol can ionize under basic conditions |
| Histidine | Low pH deprotonation of carboxyl; higher pH deprotonation of amino | Near-neutral range | Imidazole can be protonated/deprotonated |
