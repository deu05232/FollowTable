# UK Working Tax Credit  Hours-Based Eligibility and Examples (Includes Non-US Program)

| Household type | Minimum weekly work hours | Age requirement | Childcare element available | Disability element | Example weekly hours pattern | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Single (no children) | 30+ | 25+ | No | No | 5 days × 6 hours | UK program; separate from US credits |
| Single (with children) | 16+ | 16+ | Yes | No | 4 days × 4 hours | Eligibility changes with child age |
| Couple (no children) | 30+ combined | 25+ | No | No | 3 days × 10 hours (combined) | Either partner can meet hours |
| Couple (with children) | 24+ combined | 16+ | Yes | No | 2 days × 12 hours (combined) | Childcare receipts required |
| Disabled worker | 16+ | 16+ | Possible | Yes | Flexible schedule allowed | Requires disability evidence |
| Over-50 return-to-work | 16+ | 50+ | No | No | 5 days × 3.5 hours | Time-limited element in some years |
