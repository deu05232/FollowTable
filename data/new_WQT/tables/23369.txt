# Blood Pressure Interpretation by Measurement Context (Office vs Home vs Ambulatory)

| Measurement setting | Clinical label | Systolic range (mm Hg) | Diastolic range (mm Hg) | How thresholds are used |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Office (clinic) | Normal | <120 | <80 | Standard office cutoffs |
| Office (clinic) | Elevated | 120–129 | <80 | Office-only definition |
| Home monitoring | Hypertension | ≥135 | ≥85 | Lower threshold than office |
| Home monitoring | Severe hypertension | ≥160 | ≥100 | Context-dependent |
| Ambulatory (24h avg) | Hypertension | ≥130 | ≥80 | Average-based cutoff |
| Ambulatory (nighttime) | Hypertension | ≥120 | ≥70 | Nighttime-specific baseline |
