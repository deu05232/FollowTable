# Adult blood pressure categories with age-specific notes (mixed content)

| Category | Systolic/Diastolic threshold (mm Hg) | Typical age group note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Normal | < 120 / < 80 | Often targeted for adults under 65 |
| Prehypertension | 120–139 / 80–89 | Common in middle-aged adults |
| Stage 1 hypertension | 140–159 / 90–99 | Risk increases with age |
| Stage 2 hypertension | ≥ 160 / ≥ 100 | More frequent in older adults |
| Older-adult target (context note) | < 130 / < 80 | Sometimes used as a goal in seniors |
| Pediatric reminder (context note) | Use percentiles | Not classified by fixed cutoffs |
