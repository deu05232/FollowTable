# Typical chemical composition ranges for stainless steel 304 vs 310 (not focused on density)

| Alloying element | 304 range (wt.%) | 310 range (wt.%) | Why it matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chromium | 18–20 | 24–26 | Corrosion/oxidation resistance |
| Nickel | 8–10.5 | 19–22 | Austenite stability |
| Carbon | ≤0.08 | ≤0.25 | Strength/sensitization |
| Manganese | ≤2.0 | ≤2.0 | Deoxidation |
| Silicon | ≤1.0 | ≤1.5 | Oxidation resistance |
