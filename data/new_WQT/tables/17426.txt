# Rotational symmetry of common quadrilaterals (comparison-focused)

| Shape | Brief identifying feature | Rotational symmetry (how many matches in one full turn) | Typical turn angle that maps it onto itself |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Square | all sides equal, all angles right | 4 | 90° |
| Rectangle (non-square) | opposite sides equal, all angles right | 2 | 180° |
| Rhombus (non-square) | all sides equal, angles not all right | 2 | 180° |
| Parallelogram (non-rectangle) | opposite sides parallel | 2 | 180° |
| Kite (generic) | two pairs of adjacent equal sides | 1 | 360° |
| Trapezium (generic) | one pair of parallel sides | 1 | 360° |
