# Chronological dating of biblical books (includes book names but no version comparison)

| Approx. period | Books commonly placed in this period |
|---|---|
| 10th–9th centuries BCE | Early royal traditions; some Psalms (traditional attributions) |
| 8th century BCE | Amos; Hosea; Micah (parts); Isaiah (parts) |
| 7th century BCE | Nahum; Zephaniah; Habakkuk; Deuteronomy (core) |
| 6th century BCE | Jeremiah; Ezekiel; Obadiah (core) |
| 5th–4th centuries BCE | Haggai; Zechariah (parts); Malachi; Chronicles |
| 3rd–2nd centuries BCE | Ecclesiastes; Song of Songs; Jonah (late dating views) |
