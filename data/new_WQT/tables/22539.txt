# Autonomic Neurotransmitters by Synapse Location (Ganglion vs Effector)

| Autonomic division | Synapse location | Typical neurotransmitter released | Receptor family (typical) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sympathetic | Preganglionic neuron → autonomic ganglion | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) |
| Sympathetic | Postganglionic neuron → effector organ | Norepinephrine (NE) | Adrenergic (α/β) |
| Parasympathetic | Preganglionic neuron → autonomic ganglion | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) |
| Parasympathetic | Postganglionic neuron → effector organ | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Muscarinic (M) |
| Sympathetic (adrenal medulla pathway) | Preganglionic neuron → chromaffin cells | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Nicotinic (Nn) |
| Sympathetic (adrenal medulla secretion) | Chromaffin cells → bloodstream | Epinephrine (Epi) / Norepinephrine (NE) | Adrenergic (α/β) |
