# Selected animal phyla and their common names

| Phylum | Common name | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Chordata | Vertebrates and relatives | Includes subphyla such as Urochordata |
| Arthropoda | Insects, spiders, crustaceans | Largest animal phylum |
| Mollusca | Snails, clams, squid | Soft-bodied animals |
| Annelida | Segmented worms | Includes earthworms and leeches |
| Cnidaria | Jellyfish, corals | Stinging cells (cnidocytes) |
| Echinodermata | Starfish, sea urchins | Marine, radial symmetry in adults |
