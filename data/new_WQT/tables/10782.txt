# Reactivity and Leaving-Group Trends for Oxygen-Containing Acyl Compounds

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Acetic anhydride | Anhydride | RCOO− | ~4.8 | 2 | Substitution favored |
| Methyl acetate | Ester | RO− | ~16 | 3 | Slow substitution |
| Acetamide | Amide | NH2− | ~38 | 4 | Very slow |
| p-Nitrophenyl acetate | Activated ester | p-Nitrophenoxide | ~7.2 | 2 | Fast acyl transfer |
| Acetyl imidazole | Acyl imidazole | Imidazole | ~7 | 2 | Efficient acylation |
