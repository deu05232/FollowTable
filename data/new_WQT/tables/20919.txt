# # A Level Subjects and Common Prerequisites

| Subject | Common GCSE/background expectation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mathematics | Strong GCSE Maths grade | Often required for Physics/Economics |
| Chemistry | GCSE Chemistry/Combined Science | Practical endorsement in many schools |
| Geography | GCSE Geography helpful but not always required | Mix of physical and human units |
| Economics | GCSE Maths helpful | Essay + data response |
| Computer Science | GCSE Maths helpful | Programming and theory |
