# U.S. Coast Guard Officer Ranks vs Pay-Grade Codes (with Common Short Forms)

| Pay Grade | Rank Title (USCG) | Common Abbreviation | Common Reference / Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| O-1 | Ensign | ENS | Often written as "ENS" in rosters and messages |
| O-2 | Lieutenant Junior Grade | LTJG | Frequently said/written "Lieutenant JG" or "LTJG" |
| O-3 | Lieutenant | LT | Commonly "LT"; spoken as "Lieutenant" |
| O-4 | Lieutenant Commander | LCDR | Often "LCDR"; spoken "Lieutenant Commander" |
| O-5 | Commander | CDR | Typically "CDR" in correspondence |
| O-6 | Captain | CAPT | Written "CAPT" to avoid confusion with Army/Air Force CPT |
| O-7 | Rear Admiral (Lower Half) | RDML | Commonly "Rear Admiral"; abbreviation clarifies grade |
| O-8 | Rear Admiral (Upper Half) | RADM | Often "RADM"; sometimes just "Rear Admiral" |
| O-9 | Vice Admiral | VADM | Standard short form in official documents |
| O-10 | Admiral | ADM | Highest regular officer grade; "ADM" in writing |
