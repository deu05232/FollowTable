# Pupil exam patterns and associated cranial nerve considerations

| Pupil finding | Light reaction | Possible nerve-related interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Unilateral dilated pupil | Poor/none | May suggest CN III compression |
| Bilateral pinpoint pupils | Preserved | Often drug-related; not focal EOM weakness |
| Bilateral fixed dilation | null | Severe anoxia or medication effect |
| Anisocoria | Variable | Could be physiologic or neurologic |
