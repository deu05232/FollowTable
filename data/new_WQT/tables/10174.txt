# Iron Deficiency  Treatment Targets and High-Iron Foods (Clinical Guidance)

| Population | Clinical goal | Suggested iron supplement (elemental iron/day) | Duration | Example high-iron foods |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Children (general) | Correct anemia | 3 mg/kg/day | 3 months | Fortified cereal, beans |
| Adolescents (general) | Replete stores | 60 mg/day | 3–6 months | Red meat, lentils |
| Athletes | Maintain ferritin | 18–36 mg/day | As needed | Spinach, clams |
| Menstruating teens | Reduce fatigue | 45–65 mg/day | 8–12 weeks | Beef, pumpkin seeds |
| Vegetarian teens | Improve intake | 30–60 mg/day | 3 months | Tofu, chickpeas |
| Follow-up | Lab monitoring | N/A | Every 4–8 weeks | N/A |
| Safety | Avoid overdose | Max 45 mg/day unless supervised | Ongoing | N/A |
