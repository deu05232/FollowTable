# Rotational symmetry of common 2D shapes (described in words)

| 2D shape | Rotational symmetry (descriptive) | Example rotation |
|---|---|---|
| Parallelogram | two-fold rotation | matches after a half-turn |
| Rectangle | two-fold rotation | matches after a half-turn |
| Equilateral triangle | three-fold rotation | matches every one-third turn |
| Regular hexagon | six-fold rotation | matches every one-sixth turn |
