# UNCLOS thematic table summarizing maritime zones (no country participation actions)

| Maritime zone concept | Typical maximum distance described | What the zone generally covers |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Territorial sea | up to 12 nautical miles | Full sovereignty, subject to innocent passage |
| Contiguous zone | up to 24 nautical miles | Limited enforcement to prevent infringements |
| Exclusive economic zone | up to 200 nautical miles | Resource rights and certain jurisdiction |
