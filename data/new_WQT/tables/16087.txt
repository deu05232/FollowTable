# Rotation Centers and Symmetry Symbols for Plane Figures

| 2D Figure / Pattern | Symmetry symbol | Rotation center notation | Typical rotation present |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Square motif | C4 | one C4 at center | 90° turns about center |
| Rectangle motif | C2 | one C2 at center | 180° turns about center |
| Equilateral triangle motif | C3 | one C3 at centroid | 120° turns about centroid |
| Regular hexagon motif | C6 | one C6 at center | 60° turns about center |
| Parallelogram tile | C2 | one C2 at intersection of diagonals | 180° turns |
| Rhombus tile | C2 | one C2 at intersection of diagonals | 180° turns |
| Regular pentagon motif | C5 | one C5 at center | 72° turns |
| Regular octagon motif | C8 | one C8 at center | 45° turns |
| Circle | SO(2) | continuum of centers (any point) | arbitrary rotation |
| Generic trapezoid | C1 | no special center | only 360° |
