# Stainless spring wire (AISI 302) — typical mechanical properties

| Property | Typical value | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ultimate tensile strength | 1600–2000 MPa | Cold drawn wire |
| Yield strength | 1200–1600 MPa | Approximate range |
| Modulus of elasticity (E) | ~193 GPa | Austenitic stainless |
| Shear modulus (G) | ~74 GPa | For torsional response |
| Hardness | 40–50 HRC | Depends on temper |
| Elongation | 3–8% | Varies with temper |
