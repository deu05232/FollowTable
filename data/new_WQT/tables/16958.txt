# ABO Blood Group Inheritance (Punnett Squares Summary)

| Parent Genotypes | Possible Child Genotypes | Possible Child Phenotypes | Typical Teaching Example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AO × AO | AA, AO, OO | A or O | Two type-A parents can have type-O child |
| AO × BO | AB, AO, BO, OO | AB, A, B, O | Classic four-outcome cross |
| AA × OO | AO | A only | All children are type A |
| BB × OO | BO | B only | All children are type B |
| AB × OO | AO, BO | A or B | No AB child possible |
| AB × AB | AA, AB, BB | A, AB, B | No O child possible |
| AA × AB | AA, AB | A or AB |  |
| BO × OO | BO, OO | B or O |  |
| Rh: Dd × dd | Dd, dd | Rh+ or Rh- | Rh factor inheritance example |
