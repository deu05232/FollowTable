# U.S. Army Enlisted Insignia Guide (No Pay-Grade Column)

| Rank Title | Common Name | Insignia Description | Typical Role | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Private | Private | No insignia | Entry-level trainee | Often addressed as "Private" |
| Private First Class | PFC | One chevron | Junior soldier | Informally "P-F-C" |
| Specialist | SPC | Eagle shield | Technical specialist | Not called "Corporal" |
| Corporal | CPL | Two chevrons | Small-team leader | Addressed as "Corporal" |
| Sergeant | SGT | Three chevrons | Squad NCO | Addressed as "Sergeant" |
| Staff Sergeant | SSG | Three chevrons + rocker | Senior squad NCO | Addressed as "Staff Sergeant" |
| Sergeant First Class | SFC | Three chevrons + two rockers | Platoon sergeant | Addressed as "Sergeant" |
| Master Sergeant | MSG | Three chevrons + three rockers | Senior NCO | Addressed as "Sergeant" |
