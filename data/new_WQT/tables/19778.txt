# Common Molecules and Their VSEPR Shapes (Examples-First Lookup)

| Molecule | Central atom | Bonding groups (around central) | Lone pairs on central | VSEPR molecular shape | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| H2O | O | 2 | 2 | Bent | polar, typical textbook example |
| NH3 | N | 3 | 1 | Trigonal pyramidal | one lone pair on N |
| CO2 | C | 2 | 0 | Linear | two double bonds count as 2 groups |
| SO2 | S | 2 | 1 | Bent | resonance; counts as 3 electron groups |
| PCl5 | P | 5 | 0 | Trigonal bipyramidal | expanded octet |
| SF6 | S | 6 | 0 | Octahedral | hypervalent |
| XeF4 | Xe | 4 | 2 | Square planar | AX4E2 case |
| BrF5 | Br | 5 | 1 | Square pyramidal | AX5E1 case |
