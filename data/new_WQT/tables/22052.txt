# # Windows Alt codes for common punctuation and typographic symbols

| Key input sequence | Produced symbol | Typical meaning/use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Alt+0150 | – | en dash |
| Alt+0151 | — | em dash |
| Alt+0169 | © | copyright sign |
| Alt+0174 | ® | registered sign |
| Alt+0176 | ° | degree sign |
