| English-form surname | Irish (Gaelic) surname | Variant / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Murphy | Ó Murchadha | also seen as Ó Murchú (regional) |
| Kelly | Ó Ceallaigh | sometimes anglicized as O’Kelly |
| O'Sullivan | Ó Súilleabháin | also O’Sullivan Beare / Mór (historical branches) |
| Walsh | Breathnach | literally “Welshman/foreigner” |
| Smith | Mac Gabhann | also rendered as Mac an Ghobhainn |
| O'Brien | Ó Briain | from Brian/Briain lineage |
| Byrne | Ó Broin | also written Ó Bruin in some sources |
| Ryan | Ó Maoilriain | also Mulryan (older anglicization) |
| Doyle | Ó Dubhghaill | sometimes O’Dowell (confused forms) |
| Gallagher | Ó Gallchobhair | also spelled Ó Gallchóir |
| Nolan | Ó Nualláin | sometimes O’Nolan |
| Maguire | Mag Uidhir | also McGuire (anglicized) |
