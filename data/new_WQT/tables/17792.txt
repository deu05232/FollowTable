# Meal Prep Storage  Cooked vs Raw Proteins (Fridge Freezer)

| Food | State | Refrigerator Time | Freezer Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Chicken breast | Cooked | 3–4 days | 2–6 months | Store within 2 hours |
| Chicken breast | Raw | 1–2 days | 9 months | USDA-style guidance |
| Ground beef | Cooked | 3–4 days | 2–3 months | Reheat to safe temp |
| Ground beef | Raw | 1–2 days | 3–4 months | Keep sealed |
| Salmon | Cooked | 3–4 days | 2–3 months | Cool quickly |
| Salmon | Raw | 1–2 days | 2–3 months | Thaw in fridge |
| Pork chops | Cooked | 3–4 days | 2–3 months | Label dates |
| Pork chops | Raw | 3–5 days | 4–6 months | — |
