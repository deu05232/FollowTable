# MRI Spine Scheduling Guide (no CPT codes, focuses on prep)

| Spine MRI Type | Typical Duration | Patient Prep | Contrast Mention |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cervical spine MRI | 30–45 min | Remove metal, screening form | Sometimes |
| Thoracic spine MRI | 30–45 min | Arrive early for check-in | Sometimes |
| Lumbar spine MRI | 30–45 min | Bring prior images if available | Sometimes |
