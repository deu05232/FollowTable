# Vaccinations for Brazil (Travel Clinic Summary) — Yellow Fever entry and travel guidance

| Destination area (Brazil) | Yellow fever vaccine status | Notes for travelers |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Amazon region states | Recommended | Higher transmission risk; consider proof of vaccination |
| Rio de Janeiro state | Recommended | Often advised for outdoor travel |
| São Paulo state | Recommended | Risk varies by itinerary |
| Northeast coastal cities | Usually not recommended | Typically lower risk in urban coastal areas |
| Border areas with endemic countries | Recommended | May be requested when crossing borders |
| Transit through endemic region airports | May be required by next country | Check onward destination rules |
