# New York limitation periods by type of civil claim (not focused on contract debt)

| Claim type | Time limit | Legal reference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Personal injury | 3 years | CPLR 214 |
| Medical malpractice | 2.5 years | CPLR 214-a |
| Libel / slander | 1 year | CPLR 215 |
| Legal malpractice | 3 years | CPLR 214(6) |
