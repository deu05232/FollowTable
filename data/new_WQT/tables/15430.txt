# Classical Yoga Terms and Concepts (Not Individual Pose Names)

| Sanskrit Term | Common English Rendering | Domain | Short Definition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Yama | ethical restraints | Philosophy | Guidelines for conduct |
| Niyama | personal observances | Philosophy | Practices for self-discipline |
| Pratyahara | sense withdrawal | Philosophy | Turning attention inward |
| Dharana | concentration | Meditation | Single-point focus |
| Dhyana | meditation | Meditation | Sustained awareness |
| Samadhi | absorption | Meditation | Non-dual integration |
| Prana | life force | Subtle body | Vital energy concept |
| Nadis | energy channels | Subtle body | Pathways for prana |
