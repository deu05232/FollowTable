# Military Retirement Benefit Calculator Inputs by Pay Grade (Reference Sheet)

| Input Category | Example Field | Common Values | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pay Grade | Rank/grade at retirement | E-7, O-3, O-5 | Sets the base pay line used |
| Years of Service | Creditable service | 10, 20, 30 | Drives multiplier eligibility |
| Pay Table Year | Effective pay chart | 2022, 2023, 2024 | Selects the basic pay amount |
| Component | Active / Reserve | Active, Guard, Reserve | Affects points vs years |
| Retirement System | Legacy / BRS | High-36, BRS | Determines formula type |
| Civilian Equivalent | GS band | GS-11, GS-13 | Used for HR comparisons |
| Rank Category | Enlisted/Officer | Enlisted, Officer | Organizes pay lookup |
| Promotion Date | TIG/TIS indicator | Month/Year | Impacts which pay step applies |
