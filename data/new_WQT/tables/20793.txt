# Stainless Steel Density Reference by Product Form (Not by Grade)

| Product Form | Typical Density (g/cm³) | Grade Examples | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Austenitic sheet/plate | 7.90–8.05 | 304, 304L, 316, 310 | Range depends on chemistry |
| Austenitic bar | 7.88–8.03 | 304, 316, 321, 347 | Cold work can affect apparent density |
| Ferritic sheet | 7.70–7.75 | 430, 409 | Lower Ni content |
| Martensitic bar | 7.70–7.80 | 410, 420 | Heat treatment dependent |
| Duplex plate | 7.75–7.85 | 2205, 2507 | Mixed microstructure |
| Precipitation-hardening bar | 7.75–7.85 | 17-4PH, 15-5PH | Condition dependent |
| Wire | 7.75–8.05 | 302, 304, 316 | Manufacturing variation |
| Tube/pipe | 7.75–8.05 | 304, 316, 310 | Depends on alloy family |
