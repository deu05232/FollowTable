# Baby Growth Reference (with Notes and Milestone Anecdotes)

| Age | Typical weight range (lb) | Typical length range (in) | Notes / anecdotes | Comparison to older age |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Newborn (0–1 mo) | 6.5–9.0 | 19.0–21.5 | Feeding frequency often drives day-to-day fluctuations | Smaller than 3 months |
| 2 months | 9.0–12.5 | 21.5–24.0 | Many babies start smiling; growth spurts common | About half of 12 months |
| 4 months | 12.0–16.0 | 23.0–26.0 | Sleep regressions may coincide with appetite changes | Noticeably shorter than 1 year |
| 6 months | 14.0–18.5 | 25.0–27.5 | Solids may begin; temporary plateaus can occur | Roughly 2–3 inches less than 12 months |
| 9 months | 16.0–22.0 | 26.5–29.5 | Crawling can change body composition | Similar length to 12 months (varies) |
| 12 months | 18.0–25.0 | 28.0–31.5 | First steps sometimes reported around this time | Baseline for toddler comparisons |
| 15 months | 20.0–27.0 | 29.5–33.0 | Some toddlers slim down after walking begins | Closer to 18 months than 12 months |
