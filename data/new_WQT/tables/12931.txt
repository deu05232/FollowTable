# New York Statute of Limitations — General Civil Actions (No Contract Debt Breakdown)

| Cause of Action (NY) | Limitations Period | Primary Citation | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Personal injury (negligence) | 3 years | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214(5) | From date of injury/accident |
| Property damage | 3 years | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214(4) | Injury to property |
| Defamation (libel/slander) | 1 year | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 215(3) | Publication/utterance based |
| Medical malpractice | 2 years 6 months | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214-a | Special tolling rules may apply |
| Legal malpractice | 3 years | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214(6) | Regardless of theory |
| Fraud | 6 years (or 2 from discovery) | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 213(8) | Discovery rule alternative |
| Article 78 proceeding | 4 months | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 217(1) | Against a body/officer |
| Wrongful death | 2 years | EPTL § 5-4.1 | From death |
| Intentional tort (assault/battery) | 1 year | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 215(3) | Short limitations |
| Employment discrimination (NYSHRL) | 3 years | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 214(2) (often applied) | Administrative paths differ |
| Breach of contract (general) | 6 years | N.Y. C.P.L.R. § 213(2) | Not separated by oral vs written |
