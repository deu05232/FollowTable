# Comparison of Seat Belt Enforcement Types by U.S. State (Primary vs Secondary vs Other)

| State | Enforcement type label used by state summaries | Standardized category | Brief description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| California | Primary Enforcement | Primary | Seat belt violation can be the sole reason for a stop |
| Washington | Primary Enforcement | Primary | Standalone seat belt stop permitted |
| Nevada | Primary Enforcement | Primary | Direct enforcement of seat belt requirements |
| Colorado | Secondary Enforcement | Secondary | Usually requires another citable offense to initiate a stop |
| Iowa | Secondary Enforcement | Secondary | Seat belt ticket generally issued after another stop reason |
| Pennsylvania | Secondary Enforcement | Secondary | Enforcement treated as secondary in statewide summaries |
| New Hampshire | No adult seat belt law | Other / N/A | No adult seat belt mandate; enforcement type not applicable |
| District of Columbia | Primary Enforcement | Primary | Primary enforcement within the district |
