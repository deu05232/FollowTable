# Common Antihypertensive Drug Classes and Representative Medications (Generic and Brand Names)

| Drug Class | Generic Name (Medication) | Common Brand Name(s) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ACE inhibitor | lisinopril | Prinivil, Zestril | Oral; hypertension, heart failure |
| ACE inhibitor | enalapril | Vasotec | Oral; hypertension |
| ARB (angiotensin II receptor blocker) | losartan | Cozaar | Oral; hypertension |
| ARB (angiotensin II receptor blocker) | valsartan | Diovan | Oral; hypertension, heart failure |
| Thiazide diuretic | hydrochlorothiazide | Microzide | Oral; often combined with other agents |
| Thiazide-like diuretic | chlorthalidone | Thalitone | Oral; longer-acting than HCTZ |
| Dihydropyridine calcium channel blocker | amlodipine | Norvasc | Oral; edema is a common adverse effect |
| Non-dihydropyridine calcium channel blocker | diltiazem | Cardizem | Oral/IV formulations exist |
| Beta blocker (cardioselective) | metoprolol | Lopressor, Toprol XL | Oral/IV; rate control and BP |
| Loop diuretic | furosemide | Lasix | Oral/IV; edema/CHF with BP effect |
| Alpha-1 blocker | doxazosin | Cardura | Oral; also used in BPH |
| Central alpha-2 agonist | clonidine | Catapres | Oral/transdermal; rebound HTN if stopped abruptly |
