# TeX and ASCII Approximations for Mathematical Symbols

| Symbol | Meaning | TeX | Plain ASCII Approximation | Unicode (U+) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ≤ | Less-than or equal | \\leq | <= | U+2264 |
| ≥ | Greater-than or equal | \\geq | >= | U+2265 |
| ≠ | Not equal | \\neq | != | U+2260 |
| ± | Plus-minus | \\pm | +/- | U+00B1 |
| × | Multiplication sign | \\times | x | U+00D7 |
| ÷ | Division sign | \\div | / | U+00F7 |
| ∑ | Summation | \\sum | sum() | U+2211 |
