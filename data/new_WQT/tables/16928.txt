# Teen Athlete Supplement Plan (Recommendations Not Age-Banded)

| Nutrient/Supplement | Suggested daily amount | Unit | Intended user | Timing |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Protein (total) | 110 | g/day | Active teens | Spread across meals |
| Calcium | 1200 | mg/day | Adolescents | With meals |
| Vitamin D | 25 | µg/day | Adolescents | Morning |
| Iron | 18 | mg/day | Endurance athletes | With vitamin C |
| Omega-3 (EPA+DHA) | 1000 | mg/day | Teen athletes | With dinner |
| Magnesium | 400 | mg/day | Teen athletes | Evening |
| Zinc | 12 | mg/day | Teen athletes | With food |
| Electrolytes (sodium) | 1500 | mg/day | Heavy sweaters | During training |
