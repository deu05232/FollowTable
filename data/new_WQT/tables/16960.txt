| A+ | A+, AB+ | A+, A-, O+, O- | Common hospital stock |
| B- | B-, B+, AB-, AB+ | B-, O- | Compatible with AB |
| B+ | B+, AB+ | B+, B-, O+, O- |  |
| AB- | AB-, AB+ | AB-, A-, B-, O- | Universal plasma donor (not RBC) |
| AB+ | AB+ | All types | Universal RBC recipient |
