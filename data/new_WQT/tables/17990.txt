# Failure-to-appear penalties by offense severity (general, not state-specific)

| Offense severity | Maximum additional prison term | Offense level |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Punishable by death or life | 10 years | 14 |
| Punishable by 15+ years | 10 years | 14 |
| Any other felony | 2 years | 8 |
| Misdemeanor | 1 year | 5 |
