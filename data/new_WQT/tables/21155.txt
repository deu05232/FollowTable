# Books of the Old Testament across major Christian versions (sample list)

| Version/Tradition | Book names as listed in that version | Notes on inclusion |
| --- | --- | --- |
| King James Bible (Protestant) | Genesis; Exodus; Leviticus; Numbers; Deuteronomy; Joshua; Judges; Ruth; 1 Samuel; 2 Samuel; 1 Kings; 2 Kings | Typically does not include deuterocanonical books in the main OT |
| Douay–Rheims (Catholic) | Genesis; Exodus; Leviticus; Numbers; Deuteronomy; Joshua; Judges; Ruth; 1 Kings; 2 Kings; 3 Kings; 4 Kings | Often follows Vulgate-style naming for some historical books |
| Vulgate (Latin) | Genesis; Exodus; Leviticus; Numeri; Deuteronomium; Iosue; Iudicum; Ruth; I Regum; II Regum; III Regum; IV Regum | Latin titles; ordering/naming may differ |
| Septuagint tradition (Greek) | Genesis; Exodus; Leviticus; Numbers; Deuteronomy; Joshua; Judges; Ruth; 1 Kingdoms; 2 Kingdoms; 3 Kingdoms; 4 Kingdoms | Greek naming often uses “Kingdoms” for Samuel/Kings group |
