# Transmitter Identity in Autonomic Signaling  Neuron Type vs Released Chemical Messenger

| Neuron / cell type in autonomic pathway | Autonomic division (typical association) | Primary chemical messenger released | Release context (where in the pathway) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Preganglionic sympathetic neuron | Sympathetic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Synapse within sympathetic ganglion (onto postganglionic neuron) |
| Preganglionic parasympathetic neuron | Parasympathetic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Synapse within terminal/intramural ganglion (onto postganglionic neuron) |
| Postganglionic sympathetic neuron (adrenergic) | Sympathetic | Norepinephrine (NE) | Neuroeffector junction on target tissues |
| Postganglionic parasympathetic neuron (cholinergic) | Parasympathetic | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Neuroeffector junction on target tissues |
| Sympathetic preganglionic neuron to adrenal medulla | Sympathetic (adrenomedullary pathway) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Synapse onto chromaffin cells (acts like a modified ganglion) |
| Adrenal medulla chromaffin cell | Sympathetic-related endocrine output | Epinephrine (Epi) (major) and Norepinephrine (NE) | Secreted into bloodstream |
| Postganglionic sympathetic neuron (cholinergic exception) | Sympathetic (subset) | Acetylcholine (ACh) | Neuroeffector junction (cholinergic sympathetic terminals) |
| Postganglionic autonomic neuron (peptidergic co-transmitter example) | Sympathetic or Parasympathetic (varies by site) | Neuropeptides (e.g., NPY, VIP) | Co-released with ACh or NE at autonomic terminals |
