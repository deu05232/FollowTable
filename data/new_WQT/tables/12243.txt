# Resistivity of insulating polymers (volume resistivity) at room temperature

| Polymer | Volume resistivity (Ω·cm) |
|---|---|
| PTFE (Teflon) | 1×10^18 |
| Polyethylene (PE) | 1×10^16 |
| Polypropylene (PP) | 1×10^16 |
| Polycarbonate (PC) | 1×10^15 |
| PVC (rigid) | 1×10^14 |
| Nylon 6 | 1×10^12 |
