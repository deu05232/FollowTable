# Iran — parental leave entitlements by beneficiary and condition (duration and payment level)

| Beneficiary | Leave type | Statutory length | Paid? | Wage replacement | Eligibility / conditions | Administering payer |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mother | Maternity leave (standard) | 26 weeks | Yes | 100% of wages | Typically tied to social insurance coverage and medical certification | Social insurance / employer via scheme |

| Mother | Postnatal leave (part of maternity) | Remainder of 26 weeks | Yes | 100% | Taken after birth; counted within total maternity entitlement | Social insurance |

| Parent (either) | Unpaid parental care leave | Not specified in this summary | No | 0% | May depend on employer policy/sector; not a universal statutory entitlement in many summaries | Employer |

| Parent | Adoption-related leave | Not specified in this summary | Varies | Varies | Dependent on applicable labor/social insurance provisions | Varies |
