# Comparing Oxygen-Bearing Functional Groups by Key Substructure and IUPAC Naming Pattern

| Oxygen-containing group | Key substructure to spot | General formula | Typical IUPAC suffix | Typical IUPAC prefix (when not principal) | Example (IUPAC name) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Alcohol | –OH (no carbonyl) | R–OH | -ol | hydroxy- | propan-2-ol |
| Phenol | Ar–OH | Ar–OH | -ol (often “phenol” retained) | hydroxy- | benzenol (phenol) |
| Aldehyde | terminal –CHO | R–CHO | -al | formyl- / oxo- | ethanal |
| Ketone | internal >C=O | R–CO–R′ | -one | oxo- | butan-2-one |
| Carboxylic acid | –C(=O)OH | R–CO2H | -oic acid | carboxy- | propanoic acid |
| Ester | –C(=O)OR′ | R–COOR′ | -oate (alkyl alkanoate) | alkoxycarbonyl- | methyl ethanoate |
| Acid anhydride | –C(=O)–O–C(=O)– | (RCO)2O | -oic anhydride | (rare as prefix) | ethanoic anhydride |
| Acyl halide | –C(=O)X | R–COX | -oyl halide | halocarbonyl- | ethanoyl chloride |
| Amide | –C(=O)NR2 | R–CONH2 / R–CONR2 | -amide | carbamoyl- | ethanamide |
| Ether | –O– between carbons | R–O–R′ | (no single suffix; named as alkoxyalkane) | alkoxy- | ethoxyethane |
| Epoxide (oxirane ring) | three-membered C–O–C ring | cyclic ether | (often “oxirane”) | epoxy- | 1,2-epoxypropane |
