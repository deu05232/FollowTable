# European Adult Blood Pressure Classification (Office BP, mm Hg)

| Category | Systolic range (mm Hg) | Diastolic range (mm Hg) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Optimal | < 120 | < 80 |
| Normal | 120–129 | 80–84 |
| High normal | 130–139 | 85–89 |
| Hypertension Grade 1 | 140–159 | 90–99 |
| Hypertension Grade 2 | 160–179 | 100–109 |
| Hypertension Grade 3 | ≥ 180 | ≥ 110 |
| Isolated systolic hypertension | ≥ 140 | < 90 |
