# Africa Travel Immunizations (Regional Summary) — Diseases and Vaccines

| Disease | Vaccine/Prevention | Typical Traveler Groups | Comments |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Yellow Fever | Yellow fever vaccine | Some travelers to endemic areas | Check local health guidance |
| Typhoid Fever | Typhoid vaccine | Many travelers | Food and water risk |
| Hepatitis A | Hepatitis A vaccine | Most travelers | Common recommendation |
| Meningococcal Disease | Meningitis vaccine | Hajj/meningitis belt travel | Seasonal considerations |
| Rabies | Rabies vaccine | Long stays/animal contact | Pre-exposure option |
| Cholera | Oral cholera vaccine | Limited situations | Depends on outbreak |
| Polio | Polio booster | Some adults | Based on history |
| Malaria | Chemoprophylaxis | Many travelers | Not a vaccine |
