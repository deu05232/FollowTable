# Dietary Fiber  Definitions, Recommended Intake, and Common Health Effects

| Section | Key point | Source/Authority |
|---|---|---|
| Definition | Dietary fiber includes nondigestible carbohydrates intrinsic to plants | National nutrition guideline |
| Measurement | Total fiber may include dietary + functional fiber | Scientific committee summary |
| Recommended intake | Adults: 25–38 g/day (varies by age/sex) | Dietary reference intake |
| Health effect | Improves bowel regularity and stool bulk | Clinical review |
| Health effect | Helps lower LDL cholesterol | Meta-analysis |
| Health effect | May improve glycemic control | Diabetes guideline |
| Labeling | Some labels list fiber per 100 g or per serving depending on region | Food labeling regulation |
