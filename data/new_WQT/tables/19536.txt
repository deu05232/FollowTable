# Zinc-Rich Foods With Cooking Tips and Meal Ideas (Not a Zinc Amount Comparison Table)

| Oysters | Often cited as zinc-rich | Steam lightly; avoid overcooking | 6 oysters with lemon |
| Beef | Supports overall mineral intake | Pair with vitamin C foods | 1 steak salad |
| Pumpkin seeds | Convenient snack | Add to yogurt or oatmeal | 2 tbsp topping |
| Chickpeas | Plant-based option | Use in hummus | 1 cup chickpea salad |

| Cashews | Easy to portion | Choose unsalted | 1 handful snack |
| Greek yogurt | Dairy option | Add berries | 1 cup breakfast |
| Fortified cereal | Convenient | Check label | 1 bowl with milk |
