# Deer Resistance by Plant Type (Grades Given for Categories, Not Individual Plants)

| Plant Type | Examples Included | Deer Resistance Grade | Typical Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Shrubs | Kerria japonica; Viburnum dentatum; Berberis spp. | B | Browsing varies by cultivar and pressure |
| Perennials | Delphinium spp.; Penstemon spp. | C | Tender new growth often targeted |
| Annuals | Coleus spp.; Ageratum houstonianum | C | Often treated as seasonal browse |
| Bulbs | Gladiolus spp.; Colchicum spp. | B | Some are ignored after flowering |
| Groundcovers | Epimedium spp.; Pachysandra procumbens | A | Texture/chemistry tends to deter deer |
| Trees | Ilex opaca | A | Established plants fare better |
