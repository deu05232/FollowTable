# Union Territories of India and Their Capital Cities (With Notes on Administrative HQ)

| Union Territory | Capital City | Administrative Headquarters / Seat of Government | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Andaman and Nicobar Islands | Port Blair | Port Blair | — |
| Chandigarh | Chandigarh | Chandigarh | Serves as the capital of Punjab and Haryana as well |
| Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu | Daman | Daman | Formed by merger; administrative seat listed as Daman |
| Delhi (NCT) | New Delhi | New Delhi | National capital territory; seat of Union Government |
| Jammu and Kashmir | Srinagar (summer), Jammu (winter) | Srinagar / Jammu | Seasonal capitals used for administration |
| Ladakh | Leh | Leh | UT formed in 2019; administrative seat at Leh |
| Lakshadweep | Kavaratti | Kavaratti | — |
| Puducherry | Puducherry | Puducherry | Includes enclaves such as Karaikal, Mahe, Yanam |
| Lakshadweep (Agatti) | Kavaratti | Kavaratti | Agatti is a key entry point, but capital remains Kavaratti |
| Delhi (NCT) (Old Delhi) | New Delhi | New Delhi | Old Delhi is historic; capital is New Delhi |
| Andaman and Nicobar Islands (Proposed) | Port Blair | Port Blair | No separate proposed capital; Port Blair remains the seat |
| Chandigarh (Administrative) | Chandigarh | Chandigarh | Single-city UT; capital and HQ are the same |
