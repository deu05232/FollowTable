# Typical Limousine Rental Rates by Vehicle Type (Hourly Price Ranges)

| Limousine / Rental Vehicle Type | Typical Hourly Rate (USD) | Passenger Capacity (comfortable) | Common Booking Minimum | Notes / Typical Use |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Classic Stretch Sedan (Lincoln-style) | $90–$150/hr | 6–8 | 2–4 hours | Weddings, airport transfers, nights out |
| Luxury Sedan (Executive car service) | $60–$110/hr | 2–3 | 1–2 hours | Business rides; not a "stretch" limo |
| Stretch SUV (Escalade/Suburban style) | $110–$200/hr | 10–14 | 3–4 hours | Popular for groups; higher fuel/insurance |
| Hummer Stretch Limo | $150–$325/hr | 14–20 | 3–5 hours | Often priced higher; flashy option |
| Sprinter Limo Van (upscale van) | $120–$220/hr | 10–14 | 3–4 hours | Good headroom; easier city parking |
| Party Bus (small) | $140–$260/hr | 14–20 | 4–5 hours | LED/sound system; priced by bus size |
| Party Bus (large) | $180–$350/hr | 22–40 | 4–6 hours | Event nights may require longer minimum |
| Limo Coach / Mini Coach (limo-style shuttle) | $130–$240/hr | 18–28 | 4–5 hours | Group transport with a more "coach" feel |
| Motor Coach (full-size coach) | $175–$325/hr | 40–56 | 5–8 hours | Best for large groups; sometimes day-rate |
