# Felony penalties by offense type (multi-offense, no state column)

| Offense type | Felony/Misdemeanor | Maximum penalty |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drug possession (certain substances) | Felony | Up to 15 years |
| Identity theft | Felony | Up to 10 years |
| Arson | Felony | Up to 20 years |
| Forgery | Felony | Up to 5 years |
| Embezzlement | Felony | Up to 10 years |
