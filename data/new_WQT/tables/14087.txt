# Top Gear test track  lap-time correction terms and when to use them

| Term used on the board | When it applies | Typical correction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cold run | Low track temperature affecting tyres | −1 second |
| Damp patches | Mixed grip across the lap | −2 seconds |
| Standing water | Significant water on racing line | −4 seconds |
| Dusty surface | Reduced grip from debris | −1 second |
