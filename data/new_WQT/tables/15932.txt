# Rotational symmetry order of 2D triangles and quadrilaterals (order only)

| 2D shape | Order of rotational symmetry |
| --- | --- |
| Equilateral triangle | 3 |
| Isosceles triangle (non-equilateral) | 1 |
| Scalene triangle | 1 |
| Parallelogram (generic) | 2 |
| Rhombus (non-square) | 2 |
| Trapezium (generic) | 1 |
| Kite (generic) | 1 |
