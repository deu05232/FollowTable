# Rotation Symmetry Order for Typical Plane Shapes (Non-regular and Curved)

| Shape (2D) | Description | Order of rotational symmetry | Rotation angles that work (degrees) | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Rectangle (non-square) | Opposite sides equal, right angles | 2 | 0, 180 | Matches after a half-turn only |
| Square | Equal sides, right angles | 4 | 0, 90, 180, 270 | Quarter-turn symmetry |
| Circle | All points equidistant from center | Infinite | Any angle | Continuous rotational symmetry |
| Ellipse (non-circle) | Stretched circle | 2 | 0, 180 | Only half-turn symmetry |
| Semicircle | Half of a circle | 1 | 0 | No non-trivial rotation matches |
| Isosceles trapezoid | One pair of parallel sides, equal legs | 1 | 0 | Has reflection symmetry but no rotational symmetry beyond identity |
| Parallelogram (non-rectangle) | Opposite sides parallel | 2 | 0, 180 | Half-turn symmetry about intersection of diagonals |
| Regular 5-point star (pentagram) | Star polygon with equal points | 5 | 0, 72, 144, 216, 288 | Same order as regular pentagon |
