# Travel Immunizations by Continent (Summary Guide)

| Continent | Typical Vaccines Mentioned | Required vs Recommended Notes | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Africa | Yellow fever, Typhoid, Hepatitis A, Meningococcal, Rabies | Yellow fever sometimes required for entry to some countries; others usually recommended | Requirements vary widely by country and itinerary |
| Asia | Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Japanese encephalitis, Rabies | Mostly recommended; requirements depend on country and transit | Rural travel may change recommendations |
| Europe | Routine vaccines, Tick-borne encephalitis | Generally recommended; few entry requirements | Depends on outdoor activities |
| South America | Yellow fever, Hepatitis A, Typhoid | Yellow fever required for some itineraries/entries; others recommended | Jungle regions often drive guidance |
| North America | Routine vaccines, Hepatitis A (some areas) | Mostly recommended | Food/water risk varies |
| Oceania | Hepatitis A, Typhoid (some islands), Rabies (rare) | Mostly recommended | Limited disease risk in many destinations |
