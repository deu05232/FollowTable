# Powerball-Style Game — Aggregate Odds by Win Type (Not by Tier)

| Win type | Definition | Odds per play | Includes tiers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jackpot win | Highest category only | 1 in 290,000,000 | Tier 1 |
| Major prize win | Any prize ≥ $50,000 | 1 in 700,000 | Tiers 2–3 combined |
| Medium prize win | Any prize $500–$49,999 | 1 in 12,000 | Multiple tiers combined |
| Small cash win | Any prize $10–$499 | 1 in 38 | Multiple tiers combined |
| Any prize | Any payout including ticket refund | 1 in 25 | All tiers combined |
| Any profit | Prize greater than ticket cost | 1 in 90 | Cross-tier aggregation |
| Any free play | Non-cash prize like free ticket | 1 in 55 | Cross-tier aggregation |
