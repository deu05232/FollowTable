# # Antibiotic dosing regimens used for prostatitis (adult)

| Antibiotic | Typical oral dosage | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ciprofloxacin | 500 mg twice daily | Fluoroquinolone option often listed for prostatitis | 
| Levofloxacin | 500 mg once daily | Alternative fluoroquinolone regimen | 
| Trimethoprim/sulfamethoxazole (TMP-SMX DS) | 160 mg/800 mg twice daily | Common oral regimen | 
| Doxycycline | 100 mg twice daily | Often used when atypical pathogens are suspected |
