# Geneva Conventions — Summary of resolutions and interpretive instruments (no participating states)

| Instrument/Resolution | Adopted by | Year | What it does |
|---|---|---:|---|
| UNGA Resolution 2444 | UN General Assembly | 1968 | Affirms basic humanitarian principles |
| ICRC Interpretive Guidance on Direct Participation | ICRC | 2009 | Provides interpretive guidance |
| UN Security Council Resolution 1265 | UNSC | 1999 | Protection of civilians in armed conflict |
| UN Security Council Resolution 1674 | UNSC | 2006 | Reaffirms protection of civilians |
| UNGA Resolution 73/204 | UNGA | 2018 | Calls for compliance with IHL |
| ICRC Commentary (Updated) | ICRC | 2016 | Updates commentary on Geneva Conventions |
| UNGA Resolution 75/29 | UNGA | 2020 | Notes on IHL and new technologies |
