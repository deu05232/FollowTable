# Autonomic Fiber Types and Ganglia Locations (Structural Summary)

| Component | Fiber class (common) | Myelination | Typical conduction speed | Typical ganglia location | Example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sympathetic preganglionic | B fibers | Myelinated | Moderate (approx. 3–15 m/s) | Paravertebral chain | White rami communicantes |
| Sympathetic postganglionic | C fibers | Unmyelinated | Slow (approx. 0.5–2 m/s) | Near spinal cord | Gray rami communicantes |
| Parasympathetic preganglionic | B fibers | Myelinated | Moderate | Near/in target organ | Vagus to thoracic viscera |
| Parasympathetic postganglionic | C fibers | Unmyelinated | Slow | Intramural ganglia | Enteric plexuses |
| Visceral afferent (pain) | Aδ/C fibers | Variable | Variable | DRG (sensory) | Referred pain pathways |
| Adrenal medulla pathway | Preganglionic-like | Myelinated | Moderate | Chromaffin cells act as “postganglionic” | Epi/NE release to blood |
| Enteric interneurons | Local circuits | Unmyelinated | Local | Myenteric/submucosal plexus | Peristaltic reflex |
| Somatic motor (contrast) | Aα fibers | Heavily myelinated | Fast | No peripheral ganglion | Neuromuscular junction |
