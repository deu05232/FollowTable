# Adult Blood Pressure Classification with Measurement Notes and Follow-up Actions

| Category | Systolic (mm Hg) | Diastolic (mm Hg) | Confirmation method | Recommended follow-up |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Normal | <120 | <80 | Repeat annually | Lifestyle maintenance |
| Elevated (High-normal) | 120–129 | <80 | Home BP monitoring x 1–2 weeks | Diet/exercise, recheck 3–6 mo |
| Stage 1 hypertension | 130–139 | or 80–89 | Average of ≥2 readings on ≥2 visits | Consider meds if ASCVD risk high |
| Stage 2 hypertension | ≥140 | or ≥90 | Confirm with ambulatory BP | Start meds, follow up 1 mo |
| Hypertensive urgency | ≥180 | and/or ≥120 | Evaluate for symptoms | Same-day assessment |
| White-coat hypertension | Office ≥130 | Home <130/<80 | ABPM/HBPM comparison | Ongoing monitoring |
| Masked hypertension | Office <130/<80 | Home ≥130/≥80 | ABPM/HBPM | Risk reduction, consider meds |
