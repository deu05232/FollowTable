# North Carolina Sales Tax Breakdown (State + Special Districts, Not County Rows)

| Tax Component | Rate | Applies To | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| State General Rate | 4.75% | All taxable sales | Base statewide rate |
| Local Add-on (Typical) | 2.00% | Many local areas | Varies by locality |
| Transit District Surcharge | 0.50% | Transit districts | Only in designated districts |
| Stadium/Facility Surcharge | 0.25% | Special zones | Limited areas |
| Prepared Food Surcharge | 0.50% | Certain prepared foods | Category-based |
| Lodging Surcharge | 3.00% | Accommodations | Additional to general rate |
| Alcohol Surcharge | 1.00% | Alcohol sales | Additional levy |
