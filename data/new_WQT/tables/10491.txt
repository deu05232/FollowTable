| Property/Ratio | Expression involving R | Typical use | Example (dimensionless where applicable) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---:|---|
| Reduced temperature | T_r = T / T_c | corresponding states | — | does not provide R |
| Compressibility factor | Z = PV/(nRT) | real-gas deviation | — | R not numerically tabulated |
| Mayer relation | C_p,m − C_v,m = R | heat capacities | — | R appears symbolically |
| Adiabatic index | \gamma = C_p,m / C_v,m | sound speed, adiabats | 1.40 (air approx.) | depends on gas |

| Isentropic relation | PV^\gamma = const | ideal gas process | — | no unit conversions for R |
| Sackur–Tetrode (partial) | S_m = R[\ln(\cdots)+5/2] | statistical thermodynamics | — | R only symbolic |
