# Checked Baggage Allowance by Cabin Class (Total Weight Across All Pieces)   Example Airline A

| Cabin class | Allowance type | Total checked baggage allowance (combined) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Basic Economy | Weight concept | 20 kg total | Any number of pieces as long as total weight is within limit |
| Economy | Weight concept | 25 kg total | May be split across multiple bags |
| Premium Economy | Weight concept | 30 kg total | Pooling across passengers not permitted |
| Business | Weight concept | 40 kg total | Sports equipment counts toward total |
| First | Weight concept | 50 kg total | Oversize charges may still apply |
| Infant (no seat) | Weight concept | 10 kg total | Plus stroller allowed |
| Elite member bonus | Add-on | +10 kg total | Applies on eligible flights only |
