# Vaccinations for Brazil - Traveler Health Summary (Entry & Routine)

| Vaccine | Required/Recommended for Entry | Who Should Consider It | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Yellow Fever | Required (proof may be checked if arriving from risk countries) | Travelers to Amazon region | Certificate validity rules apply |
| Hepatitis A | Recommended | Most travelers | Food/water risk |
| Typhoid | Recommended | Rural/long-stay travelers | Food/water risk |
| Tetanus-Diphtheria | Recommended | All travelers | Booster if overdue |
| Rabies | Consider | Adventure/animal exposure | Pre-exposure series |
| Measles (MMR) | Recommended | All travelers | Ensure up to date |
| COVID-19 | Recommended | All travelers | Per local guidance |
