# Deer-Preferred Plant Parts and Typical Browsing Signs on Ornamentals

| Plant Example | Plant Category | Part Most Often Eaten | Typical Browsing Sign | Time of Year Most Common |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Roses | Shrub | Buds and tender shoots | Clean, angled cuts on stems | Spring–summer |
| Sunflowers | Annual | Leaves and flower heads | Ragged leaf edges; stalk damage | Summer |
| Rhododendron | Shrub | Leaves (sometimes) | Stripped lower foliage | Winter |
| Daylilies | Perennial | Leaves | Missing leaf fans near ground | Spring |
| Hostas | Perennial | Leaves | Complete defoliation overnight | Spring–summer |
| Arborvitae | Evergreen | Foliage tips | Browsed hedges up to shoulder height | Winter |
| Tulips | Bulb | Flower buds | Buds clipped before opening | Spring |
