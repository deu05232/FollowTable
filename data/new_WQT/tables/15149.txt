# U.S. Army Enlisted Ranks and Pay-Grade Codes (with Common Abbreviations)

| Pay Grade | Rank Title (Army) | Common Abbreviation / Spoken Form | Notes (how it’s referred to) |
|---|---|---|---|
| E-1 | Private (PV1) | PV1 / "Private" | Entry-level enlisted; sometimes just "Private" |
| E-2 | Private (PV2) | PV2 / "Private" | Often called "Private" unless specificity is needed |
| E-3 | Private First Class | PFC / "PFC" | Usually addressed as "Private" or "PFC" depending on unit |
| E-4 | Specialist | SPC / "Specialist" | Commonly "Specialist"; not a NCO rank |
| E-4 | Corporal | CPL / "Corporal" | NCO at E-4; addressed as "Corporal" |
| E-5 | Sergeant | SGT / "Sergeant" | Standard junior NCO; addressed as "Sergeant" |
| E-6 | Staff Sergeant | SSG / "Staff Sergeant" | Often shortened to "Staff" in informal contexts |
| E-7 | Sergeant First Class | SFC / "Sergeant First Class" | Sometimes said as "SFC" |
| E-8 | Master Sergeant | MSG / "Master Sergeant" | Duty-position dependent; not the same as 1SG |
| E-8 | First Sergeant | 1SG / "First Sergeant" | Typically addressed as "First Sergeant" regardless of name |
| E-9 | Sergeant Major | SGM / "Sergeant Major" | Senior enlisted; addressed as "Sergeant Major" |
| E-9 | Command Sergeant Major | CSM / "Command Sergeant Major" | Often "CSM"; senior enlisted advisor at command level |
| E-9 | Sergeant Major of the Army | SMA / "Sergeant Major of the Army" | Unique appointment; often referenced as "SMA" |
