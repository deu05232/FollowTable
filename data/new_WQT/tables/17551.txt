# # Social Security normal retirement age and delayed retirement credits

| Year of birth | Normal retirement age (NRA) | Delayed retirement credit per year after NRA |
|---|---|---|
| 1943-1954 | 66 years | 8% |
| 1955 | 66 years, 2 months | 8% |
