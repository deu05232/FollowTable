# Venomous snakes of Arizona (selected) with within-state distribution notes

| State | Venomous snake (common name) | Scientific name | Typical in-state distribution / where encountered |
|---|---|---|---|
| Arizona | Western diamondback rattlesnake | Crotalus atrox | Southern & central AZ deserts (Sonoran Desert); desert scrub, rocky washes |
| Arizona | Sidewinder | Crotalus cerastes | Western/southwestern AZ sandy deserts; dunes and sandy flats |
| Arizona | Mojave rattlesnake | Crotalus scutulatus | West and south AZ deserts; creosote flats and open desert |
| Arizona | Speckled rattlesnake | Crotalus mitchellii | Western AZ rocky deserts; boulder fields and canyon slopes |
| Arizona | Black-tailed rattlesnake | Crotalus molossus | Central/southern AZ uplands; oak woodland, rocky hills, canyon country |
| Arizona | Prairie rattlesnake | Crotalus viridis | Northern/eastern AZ high plains; grasslands and open mesas |
| Arizona | Tiger rattlesnake | Crotalus tigris | South-central AZ; rocky desert foothills (often around desert mountains) |
| Arizona | Arizona black rattlesnake | Crotalus cerberus | Central AZ (Mogollon Rim / high-elevation forests); pine-oak woods, rocky outcrops |
| Arizona | Ridge-nosed rattlesnake | Crotalus willardi | Southeastern AZ “Sky Islands”; higher-elevation canyons/woodlands |
| Arizona | Arizona coral snake | Micruroides euryxanthus | Southern & central AZ; desert/grassland edges, often in leaf litter or under cover |
