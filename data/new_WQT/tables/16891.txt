# Fuel products and boiling ranges (mixture product oriented, not individual alkanes)

| Fuel/product | Typical boiling range (°C) | Typical use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Petroleum ether | 30–60 | solvent/extraction |
| Naphtha | 30–200 | petrochemical feed |
| Gasoline | 40–205 | motor fuel |
| Kerosene | 150–300 | jet/heating fuel |
