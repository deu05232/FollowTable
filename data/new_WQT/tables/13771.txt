# Wine Sweetness & Body Quick Guide (by Style Category)

| Style Category | Typical Body (Light–Full) | Residual Sugar (g/L) | Common Flavor Impressions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Brut Sparkling | Light | 0–12 | Citrus, green apple, brioche |
| Extra Dry Sparkling | Light | 12–17 | Pear, peach, floral |
| Dry Riesling | Light | 4–9 | Lime, slate, green apple |
| Off-Dry Riesling | Light–Medium | 10–25 | Peach, honey, jasmine |
| Late Harvest White | Medium | 45–120 | Apricot jam, honey, marmalade |
| Dessert Wine | Full | 120–250 | Caramel, dried fig, toffee |
| Fortified (Port/Sherry) | Full | 60–150 | Raisin, walnut, caramel |
| Rosé (Dry) | Light | 0–6 | Strawberry, watermelon, citrus |
