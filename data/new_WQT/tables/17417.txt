# Economics Major Job Title Ideas by Skill Focus (Quant, Policy, Research, Business)

| Skill focus | Specific job title | Common tools/knowledge | Example work outputs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Econometrics & stats | Econometrician (Entry-Level) | Regression, causal inference, R/Stata | Model estimates, technical notes |
| Econometrics & stats | Quantitative Analyst (Economic Data) | Python, SQL, time-series | Forecast models, automated reports |
| Public policy | Public Finance Analyst | Fiscal policy, tax incidence | Revenue estimates, policy briefs |
| Public policy | Regulatory Policy Analyst | Regulatory impact analysis | RIA write-ups, stakeholder summaries |
| Labor & social | Labor Market Analyst | Labor econ, wage/occupation data | Labor trend reports, wage benchmarks |
| Research & evaluation | Program Evaluation Analyst | Experimental/quasi-experimental methods | Impact evaluations, KPI frameworks |
| Business analytics | Demand Planning Analyst | Forecasting, seasonality | Demand forecasts, inventory recommendations |
| Business analytics | Revenue Management Analyst | Pricing, optimization | Fare/price recommendations, A/B results |
| Banking & risk | Macro Research Associate | Macroeconomic indicators | Weekly macro notes, scenario analysis |
| Banking & risk | Economic Capital Analyst | Risk modeling, stress testing | Stress test results, capital reports |
| Real estate & urban | Real Estate Market Analyst | Local econ, comps, absorption | Market reports, rent/price projections |
| Trade & international | Trade Compliance Analyst (Economics/Policy) | Tariffs, trade policy basics | Country briefs, compliance summaries |
