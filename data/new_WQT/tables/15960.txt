# United States DST Rules (General Schedule, Not City-Specific)

| Period | Rule Type | DST Start Rule | DST End Rule | Change Time (Local) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1987–2006 | Federal rule | First Sunday in April | Last Sunday in October | 2:00 AM | Most states observed |
| 2007–Present | Federal rule | Second Sunday in March | First Sunday in November | 2:00 AM | Current standard rule |
| Arizona (most areas) | Exception | Not observed | Not observed | N/A | Navajo Nation differs |
| Hawaii | Exception | Not observed | Not observed | N/A | No DST |
| U.S. territories | Exception | Not observed | Not observed | N/A | e.g., Puerto Rico |
| Indiana (pre-2006) | Historical exception | Mixed observance | Mixed observance | Varies | County-by-county history |
| Local ordinances (historical) | Historical | Varies | Varies | Varies | Prior to uniform rules |
