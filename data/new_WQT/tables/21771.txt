# Concerta (methylphenidate ER) age-stratified dosing overview for ADHD (starting dose and maintenance range)

| Patient group (age) | Starting dose | Maintenance / effective range | Dose escalation frequency | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pediatrics: 6–7 years | 18 mg once daily | 18–54 mg/day | Adjust in 18 mg increments | Swallow whole; once-daily morning dosing |
| Pediatrics: 8–10 years | 18 mg once daily | 18–54 mg/day | Adjust in 18 mg increments | Monitor appetite, sleep, BP/HR |
| Pediatrics: 11–12 years | 18 mg once daily | 18–54 mg/day | Adjust in 18 mg increments | Reassess benefit vs adverse effects during titration |
| Teens: 13–14 years | 18 mg once daily | 18–72 mg/day | Adjust in 18 mg increments | Do not exceed 2 mg/kg/day |
| Teens: 15–17 years | 18 mg once daily | 18–72 mg/day | Adjust in 18 mg increments | Max 72 mg/day in typical guidance |
| Adults: 18–65 years | 18 or 36 mg once daily | 18–72 mg/day | Adjust in 18 mg increments | Use lowest effective dose; individualize |
