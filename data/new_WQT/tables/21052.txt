# Federal Capital Gains Rates Timeline (Historical Changes Over Time)

| Period | Short-Term Capital Gains | Long-Term Capital Gains | Notable Policy Context | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| 1998–2002 | Ordinary income rates | 20% (most taxpayers) | Late-1990s structure | Simplified headline rate |
| 2003–2007 | Ordinary income rates | 15% (most taxpayers) | JGTRRA reductions | Lowered LT rate |
| 2008–2012 | Ordinary income rates | 15% (0% for some) | Expansion of 0% tier | Income-dependent |
| 2013–2017 | Ordinary income rates | 20% top (0/15/20 tiers) | ATRA + NIIT introduced | NIIT not shown |
| 2018–2020 | Ordinary income rates | 20% top (separate thresholds) | TCJA era thresholds | Filing-status thresholds omitted |
| 2021–2023 | Ordinary income rates | 20% top (0/15/20 tiers) | Inflation indexing | Headline rates only |
| 2024–present | Ordinary income rates | 20% top (0/15/20 tiers) | Ongoing framework | Subject to law changes |
