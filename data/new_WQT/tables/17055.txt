# Glycemic Load (GL) Categories and Typical Examples (Range Classification)

| Category | GL range (per serving) | Typical examples | Guidance |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Very low GL | 0–5 | leafy greens, nuts, most non-starchy vegetables | minimal post-meal impact |
| Low GL | 6–10 | berries, milk, lentils | generally favorable |
| Moderate GL | 11–19 | oatmeal, brown rice, banana | watch portion size |
| High GL | 20–29 | white rice (large serving), fries | limit frequency |
| Very high GL | 30+ | large bagel, sugary drinks | avoid or reduce portion |
| Unknown/variable | depends | mixed dishes (pizza, curry) | calculate from carbs and GI |
