# Canada Post parcel services (online vs post office)

| Canada Post service | Online price | Post office price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Regular Parcel (up to 1 kg) | $14.50 | $16.00 |
| Expedited Parcel (up to 1 kg) | $18.75 | $20.25 |
| Xpresspost (up to 1 kg) | $27.40 | $29.90 |
| Priority (up to 1 kg) | $58.00 | $61.00 |
