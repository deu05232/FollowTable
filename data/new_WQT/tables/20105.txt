|---|---|---|---|---|
| Entry-level DSLR | EOS 4000D, EOS 2000D, EOS 250D | Polycarbonate | 430–520 g | Lightest options |
| Upper entry-level | EOS 850D, EOS 90D | Polycarbonate + metal chassis | 500–730 g | Moderate |
| Enthusiast / Prosumer APS-C | EOS 7D Mark II | Magnesium alloy | 800–950 g | Heavier APS-C |
| Prosumer Full-frame | EOS 6D, EOS 6D Mark II | Mixed | 730–820 g | Full-frame but portable |
| Professional Full-frame | EOS 5D series | Magnesium alloy | 850–1000 g | Needs good strap |
| Flagship / Integrated grip | EOS-1D series | Sealed magnesium alloy | 1300–1550 g | Very heavy |
