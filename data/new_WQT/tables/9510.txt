# Deer Resistance Ratings by Plant Category (Not Per Latin Species)

| Plant Category | Examples (Latin) | Typical Deer Resistance Rating | Comments |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ornamental grasses | Miscanthus sinensis; Panicum virgatum | A | Usually avoided once established |
| Spring bulbs | Tulipa gesneriana; Crocus vernus | D | Frequently browsed in spring |
| Aromatic herbs | Lavandula angustifolia; Salvia officinalis | A | Scent reduces browsing |
| Evergreen shrubs | Buxus sempervirens; Ilex crenata | B | Variable by cultivar and site |
| Tender annuals | Impatiens walleriana; Begonia semperflorens | D | Often heavily eaten |
| Spiny shrubs | Berberis thunbergii; Rosa rugosa | B | Thorns deter but not always |
| Ferns | Athyrium filix-femina; Dryopteris marginalis | B | Browsing varies with pressure |
| Wetland perennials | Iris versicolor; Lobelia cardinalis | C | Palatability varies seasonally |
