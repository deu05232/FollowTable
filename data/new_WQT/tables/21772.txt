# Concerta (methylphenidate ER) dosing recommendations by age group (general guideline)

| Age band | Typical starting dose (once daily, AM) | Typical titration step | Usual dose range | Maximum daily dose / limit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Children 6–8 years | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at ≥ weekly intervals | 18–54 mg/day | 54 mg/day |
| Children 9–12 years | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at ≥ weekly intervals | 18–54 mg/day | 54 mg/day |
| Adolescents 13–15 years | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at ≥ weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | 72 mg/day (also not to exceed 2 mg/kg/day) |
| Adolescents 16–17 years | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at ≥ weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | 72 mg/day (also not to exceed 2 mg/kg/day) |
| Adults 18–64 years | 18 or 36 mg/day | +18 mg at ≥ weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | 72 mg/day |
| Older adults ≥65 years | Not established (use caution) | Individualize | Not established | Not established |
