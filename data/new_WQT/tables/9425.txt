# South Africa Food-Service Fees and Taxes That Affect New Fast-Food Outlets (Not Brand-Specific)

| Cost Category | Typical Amount/Rate | Applies To | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| VAT (Value-Added Tax) | 15% | Most taxable sales | Output VAT vs input VAT |
| Company income tax | 27% | Companies | Subject to taxable profit |
| UIF (Unemployment Insurance Fund) | 1% employer + 1% employee | Payroll | Capped monthly earnings apply |
| SDL (Skills Development Levy) | 1% of payroll | Payroll (if threshold met) | Training levy |
| Municipal trading permit | R500 – R5,000 | Local authority | Varies by municipality |
| Food premises certificate | R300 – R2,500 | Health department | Inspection required |
| Fire safety compliance | R1,000 – R25,000 | Premises | Depends on upgrades needed |
| Liquor license (if applicable) | R15,000 – R120,000 | Restaurants selling alcohol | Province-dependent |
