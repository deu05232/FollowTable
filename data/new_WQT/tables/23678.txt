# Selected Monogenic Disorders and Their Causative Genes

| Disorder | Gene | Typical Inheritance | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cystic fibrosis | CFTR | Autosomal recessive | Classic CF due to pathogenic variants in CFTR |
| Duchenne muscular dystrophy | DMD | X-linked recessive | Usually truncating variants causing absent dystrophin |
| Spinal muscular atrophy | SMN1 | Autosomal recessive | Most cases involve SMN1 deletion/conversion |
| Huntington disease | HTT | Autosomal dominant | CAG repeat expansion in HTT |
| Marfan syndrome | FBN1 | Autosomal dominant | Fibrillin-1 defects affecting connective tissue |
| Neurofibromatosis type 1 | NF1 | Autosomal dominant | Tumor suppressor gene NF1 |
| Sickle cell disease | HBB | Autosomal recessive | Missense variant (p.Glu6Val) in beta-globin |
| Achondroplasia | FGFR3 | Autosomal dominant | Commonly p.Gly380Arg variant in FGFR3 |
