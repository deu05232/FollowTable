# Common White Wine Styles Compared by Body and Typical Flavor Profile

| Wine style | Typical aromas/flavors | Body feel |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sauvignon Blanc | Citrus, gooseberry, cut grass, herbal notes | Light |
| Pinot Grigio | Lemon, green apple, subtle pear | Light |
| Riesling (dry) | Lime, stone fruit, floral notes, petrol hint | Light to medium |
| Chardonnay (unoaked) | Green apple, citrus, minerality | Medium |
| Chardonnay (oaked) | Vanilla, butter, toast, baked apple | Medium to full |
| Viognier | Peach, apricot, honeysuckle | Full |
