# BMI Categories and Healthy Weight Status (Adults)

| BMI Range | Weight Status | Risk Note | Typical Guidance |
|---|---|---|---|
| < 18.5 | Underweight | Nutrient deficiency risk | Consider medical evaluation |
| 18.5–24.9 | Normal weight | Lower chronic disease risk | Maintain lifestyle |
| 25.0–29.9 | Overweight | Elevated risk | Consider weight reduction |
| 30.0–34.9 | Obesity Class I | High risk | Structured weight-loss plan |
| 35.0–39.9 | Obesity Class II | Very high risk | Clinical support recommended |
| ≥ 40.0 | Obesity Class III | Extremely high risk | Specialist management |
| Note | — | BMI does not measure body fat directly | Use with other measures |
