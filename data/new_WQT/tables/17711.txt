# Extraocular muscle-related clinical signs in cranial nerve palsies

| Finding | Possible cranial nerve involved |
| --- | --- |
| Ptosis with a "down and out" eye | Oculomotor nerve (CN III) |
| Vertical diplopia worse on looking down | Trochlear nerve (CN IV) |
| Inability to abduct the eye | Abducens nerve (CN VI) |
