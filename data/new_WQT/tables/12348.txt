# Limousine Rental Rates by Occasion (Package Pricing and Ranges)

| Occasion | Typical limo type | Pricing format | Typical cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Prom night | Stretch limo | 4-hour package | $450–$750 per package |
| Wedding | Luxury sedan + stretch limo | Hourly range | $110–$220/hr |
| Airport transfer | Executive sedan | Flat trip fee | $95–$180 per trip |
| Bachelor/Bachelorette | SUV limo or party bus | Hourly range | $150–$350/hr |
| Corporate roadshow | Executive sedan/SUV | Day rate | $650–$1,200 per day |
| Concert / sporting event | Party bus | Hourly range | $180–$450/hr |
| Wine tour | Sprinter limo van | 6-hour package | $720–$1,200 per package |
