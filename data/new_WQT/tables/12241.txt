# Qualitative electrical conductivity of states of matter

| Medium | Electrical conductivity (qualitative) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Dry air | Very low | Insulating until breakdown |
| Seawater | High | Ionic conduction dominates |
| Copper | Very high | Metallic conduction |
| Glass | Very low | Insulator |
| Plasma | Extremely high | Often treated as near-infinite |
| Silicon (intrinsic) | Moderate | Strongly temperature dependent |
