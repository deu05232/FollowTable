# Earth's crust composition reported as oxides (major components)

| Oxide component | Mass percent in crust | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| SiO2 | 60.6 | silica in silicate minerals |
| Al2O3 | 15.9 | alumina-bearing minerals |
| CaO | 6.4 | calcium in carbonates/silicates |
| MgO | 4.7 | magnesium in mafic minerals |
| FeO | 6.7 | iron in ferromagnesian minerals |
