# Temples and Cult Centers of Major Egyptian Deities

| Deity | Major temple/cult site | City/Region | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Amun | Karnak Temple Complex | Thebes (Luxor) | State god in the New Kingdom; major priesthood center |
| Isis | Temple of Isis | Philae (Aswan) | Late-period and Ptolemaic focus; pilgrimage destination |
| Horus | Temple of Horus | Edfu | Well-preserved Ptolemaic temple |
| Hathor | Temple of Hathor | Dendera | Associated with festivals and music |
| Ptah | Great Temple of Ptah | Memphis | Ancient cult center in Lower Egypt |
| Osiris | Osireion | Abydos | Linked to funerary rites and royal pilgrimage |
| Sobek | Temple of Sobek | Kom Ombo | Shared complex; crocodile cult |
| Ra | Sun temples | Heliopolis | Early theological center of solar worship |
