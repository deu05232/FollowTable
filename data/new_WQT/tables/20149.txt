# Typical Antibiotic Dosing Schedules for Chronic Bacterial Prostatitis (Adults)

| Antibiotic | Typical dose | Frequency | Typical duration |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ciprofloxacin | 500 mg | twice daily | 4–6 weeks |
| Levofloxacin | 500–750 mg | once daily | 4–6 weeks |
| Ofloxacin | 300 mg | twice daily | 4–6 weeks |
| Trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole (TMP/SMX DS) | 160/800 mg | twice daily | 4–6 weeks |
| Doxycycline | 100 mg | twice daily | 2–4 weeks (pathogen-dependent) |
| Azithromycin | 500 mg | once daily | 3–7 days (pathogen-dependent) |
| Fosfomycin tromethamine | 3 g | every 48–72 hours | 2–6 weeks (selected cases) |
| Amoxicillin-clavulanate | 875/125 mg | twice daily | 2–4 weeks (selected cases) |
