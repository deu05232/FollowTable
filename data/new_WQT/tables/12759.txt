# Estimated Victim Totals by Camp (Selected Sites)

| Camp | Estimated Victims | Primary Victim Groups | Reference Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Auschwitz-Birkenau | 1,000,000+ | Jews, Poles, Roma, Soviet POWs | Aggregate scholarly estimates |
| Treblinka | 700,000–900,000 | Jews | Operation Reinhard camp |
| Bełżec | 430,000–600,000 | Jews | Estimates vary by source |
| Sobibór | 170,000–250,000 | Jews | Included death march victims |
| Chełmno | 150,000–320,000 | Jews, Roma | Includes gassing vans period |
| Majdanek | 79,000–235,000 | Jews, Poles, Soviet POWs | Wide estimate range |
| Jasenovac | 80,000–100,000 | Serbs, Jews, Roma | Not a Nazi camp (often compared) |
