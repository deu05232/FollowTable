# Plants reported to deter rodents and other small mammals

| Plant | Pests/animals deterred |
| --- | --- |
| Daffodil | squirrels, voles |
| Mint | mice, rats |
| Marigold | rabbits, gophers |
| Allium (ornamental onion) | rabbits, deer |
| Castor bean plant | moles |
| Fritillaria imperialis | moles, voles |
