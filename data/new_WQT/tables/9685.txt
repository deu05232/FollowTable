# LINDOR Assorted Bag Guide  Wrapper Colors and Multiple Possible Fillings

| Wrapper color / design | Possible flavour(s) in mixed bags | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Red | Milk Chocolate; Milk Hazelnut | Same base red tone appears on multiple variants in some assortments |
| Blue | Dark Chocolate; 70% Dark | Shade varies by region; not always one-to-one |
| Gold | White Chocolate; Caramel | Gold can indicate more than one “light” flavour depending on print |
| Green | Pistachio; Mint | Seasonal and limited runs reuse green wrappers |
| Pink | Strawberry; Neapolitan | Pink used for several fruit-themed varieties |
| Silver | Cookies & Cream; Vanilla | Silver/white wrappers overlap across “cream” flavours |
| Brown | Milk Truffle; Peanut Butter | Brown may include text print or be plain |
| Black | Extra Dark; Dark Sea Salt | Black wrappers sometimes differentiate only via small text |
| Purple | Blueberry; Açai | Often appears as a berry category rather than a single flavour |
| Orange | Orange; Mango | Citrus/tropical flavours share orange family wrappers |
