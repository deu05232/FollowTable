# Concerta (methylphenidate ER) initiation and weekly titration by patient group (once-daily dosing)

| Patient group | Prior stimulant exposure | Recommended starting dose (mg once daily, AM) | Titration step | Titration interval | Typical maintenance range (mg/day) | Maximum / ceiling (mg/day) | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|---:|---:|---|
| Children 6–12 years | Stimulant-naïve | 18 | +18 | Weekly | 18–54 | 54 | Increase only if needed for symptom control; assess appetite/sleep each step |
| Children 6–12 years | Switching from IR methylphenidate | 18 or 36 | +18 | Weekly | 18–54 | 54 | Choose start based on prior total daily IR dose and tolerability |
| Adolescents 13–17 years | Stimulant-naïve | 18 | +18 | Weekly | 18–72 | 72 (and do not exceed 2 mg/kg/day) | Weight-based ceiling may be lower than 72 mg/day |
| Adolescents 13–17 years | Previously on methylphenidate products | 36 | +18 | Weekly | 36–72 | 72 (and do not exceed 2 mg/kg/day) | Start higher if already tolerating methylphenidate |
| Adults 18–65 years | Stimulant-naïve | 18 or 36 | +18 | Weekly | 18–72 | 72 | Select 36 mg start when clinically appropriate (e.g., prior stimulant exposure) |
| Adults 18–65 years | Switching from another stimulant | 36 | +18 | Weekly | 36–72 | 72 | Verify equivalence/conversion; monitor BP/HR during upward titration |
| Any age (within approved groups) | Sensitive to stimulants / adverse effects at baseline | 18 | Hold or +18 | ≥1 week | 18–36 | Per age ceiling | Consider slower titration; dose reductions allowed if side effects |
| Any age (within approved groups) | Inadequate response at current dose | — | +18 | Weekly | Within age range | Per age ceiling | Do not exceed ceiling; reassess diagnosis, adherence, comorbidities if no response |
