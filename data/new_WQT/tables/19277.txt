| Solvent category | Typical composition | Boiling range (°C) | Density (g/mL, 20°C) | Use case |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Petroleum ether (light) | mixture of C5–C6 alkanes | 30–60 | 0.64–0.66 | Extraction solvent |
| Petroleum ether (heavy) | mixture of C6–C7 alkanes | 60–90 | 0.66–0.69 | Cleaning, extraction |
| Hexanes (commercial) | mixture (n-hexane + methylpentanes) | 64–70 | 0.66 | Industrial solvent |
| Heptanes (commercial) | mixture (n-heptane + isoheptanes) | 90–100 | 0.68 | Adhesives, lab solvent |
| Ligroin | mixed aliphatic hydrocarbons | 60–140 | 0.67–0.72 | Recrystallization |
| Mineral spirits | mixed C7–C12 hydrocarbons | 150–200 | 0.76–0.80 | Paint thinner |
| Naphtha (general) | mixed C5–C10 hydrocarbons | 30–200 | 0.70–0.79 | Feedstock/solvent |
| White spirit | mixed aliphatic/aromatic hydrocarbons | 130–230 | 0.77–0.81 | Degreasing |
