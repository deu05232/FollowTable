# Element valences and common ion charges (mixed reference)

| Element | Common Valence States | Example Ion | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Iron | +2, +3 | Fe2+ | Transition metal |
| Copper | +1, +2 | Cu+ | Transition metal |
| Sulfur | -2, +4, +6 | S2- | Also forms oxyanions |
| Nitrogen | -3, +3, +5 | N3- | Multiple oxidation states |
| Chlorine | -1, +1, +5, +7 | Cl- | Also forms oxyanions |
| Carbon | -4, +2, +4 | (none) | Often covalent |
| Silver | +1 | Ag+ | Common in salts |
