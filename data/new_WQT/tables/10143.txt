# Extraocular Muscles  Innervation and Primary Actions (Quick Review)

| Extraocular muscle | Cranial nerve (branch) | Primary action | Secondary/tertiary actions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Superior rectus | Oculomotor (CN III, superior division) | Elevation | Intorsion; adduction |
| Inferior rectus | Oculomotor (CN III, inferior division) | Depression | Extorsion; adduction |
| Medial rectus | Oculomotor (CN III, inferior division) | Adduction | — |
| Lateral rectus | Abducens (CN VI) | Abduction | — |
| Superior oblique | Trochlear (CN IV) | Intorsion | Depression; abduction |
| Inferior oblique | Oculomotor (CN III, inferior division) | Extorsion | Elevation; abduction |
| Levator palpebrae superioris | Oculomotor (CN III, superior division) | Upper eyelid elevation | — |
