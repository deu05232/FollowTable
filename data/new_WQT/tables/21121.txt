# Köppen Climate Threshold Ranges Used to Classify Cities (Reference Table)

| Köppen Group | Subtype | Classification Rule (Range-Based) | Temperature Thresholds | Precipitation Thresholds | Example Cities |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A | Af | Tropical rainforest | All months ≥ 18°C | Driest month ≥ 60 mm | Singapore, Manaus |
| A | Am | Tropical monsoon | All months ≥ 18°C | Driest month < 60 mm but monsoon satisfied | Mumbai, Cairns |
| A | Aw/As | Tropical savanna | All months ≥ 18°C | Pronounced dry season | Miami, Darwin |
| B | BWh | Hot desert | Mean annual temp high | Precip < aridity threshold | Phoenix, Riyadh |
| B | BSk | Cold semi-arid | Mean annual temp lower | Precip < aridity threshold | Denver, Ulaanbaatar |
| C | Csa/Csb | Mediterranean | Warmest month > 22°C (Csa) or < 22°C (Csb) | Dry summer criteria | Los Angeles, Lisbon |
| D | Dfa/Dfb | Humid continental | Coldest month ≤ 0°C (or -3°C) | No dry season requirement | Boston, Warsaw |
| E | ET | Tundra | Warmest month 0–10°C | N/A | Reykjavik, Nome |
