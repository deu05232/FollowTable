# Oregon Felony Classes  Max Prison and Fine (No Specific Felony Charges Listed)

| Felony class | Maximum prison term | Maximum fine | Sentencing notes | Common examples (non-exhaustive) |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Class A Felony | 20 years | $375,000 | Often used for the most serious non-murder offenses | Kidnapping, major assault |
| Class B Felony | 10 years | $250,000 | May involve presumptive prison depending on grid | Burglary I, drug delivery |
| Class C Felony | 5 years | $125,000 | Frequently probation-eligible depending on history | Theft I, DUII felony |
| Unclassified Felony | Varies | Varies | Set by the specific statute | Certain sex offenses |
| Measure/Drug Felony Level (legacy) | Varies | Varies | Depends on offense date and statute | Controlled substances |
| Attempt/Conspiracy (felony) | Reduced from base | Reduced from base | Generally one level lower than completed offense | Attempted burglary |
| Repeat offender enhancement | Adds time | Adds fines | Can increase prison exposure substantially | Persistent felony offender |
