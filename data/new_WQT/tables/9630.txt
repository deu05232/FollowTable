| TT3 | Postpartum or next pregnancy | 6 months after TT2 | Longer protection | Follow-up dependent |
| TT4 | Subsequent visit | 1 year after TT3 | Extended protection | Booster program |
| TT5 | Later booster | 1 year after TT4 | Long-term protection | Lifetime course completion |
| Catch-up | If unknown history | As per clinician | Varies | Programmatic guidance |
