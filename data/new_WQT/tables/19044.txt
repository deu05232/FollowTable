# Adult Blood Pressure Categories (mm Hg)  Systolic Diastolic Ranges

| Blood pressure category | Systolic (mm Hg) | Diastolic (mm Hg) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hypotension (low blood pressure) | < 90 | < 60 |
| Normal | 90–119 | 60–79 |
| Elevated (formerly “prehypertension”) | 120–129 | < 80 |
| Hypertension — Stage 1 | 130–139 | 80–89 |
| Hypertension — Stage 2 | 140–179 | 90–119 |
| Hypertensive crisis (seek urgent care) | ≥ 180 | ≥ 120 |
