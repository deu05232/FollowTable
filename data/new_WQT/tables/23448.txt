# Chicago Ground Transport Rate Card (Limo Hourly + Taxi Per-Mile + Airport Shuttles)

| Service Category | Pricing Basis | Rate | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sedan (non-limo) | Per mile | $3.25/mi | Standard curb-to-curb |
| Stretch Limousine | Per hour | $135–$220/hr | 8–10 passengers, 3-hr minimum |
| SUV Limousine | Per hour | $160–$260/hr | 12–14 passengers |
| Party Bus | Per hour | $190–$325/hr | 18–30 passengers |
| Taxi (city meter) | Flag drop + per mile | $4.00 + $2.70/mi | Excludes tolls |
| Airport Shuttle (shared) | Per person | $22–$35 | Depends on zone |
| Black Car (executive) | Per hour | $95–$140/hr | 4–6 passengers |
| Gratuity & fees | Percent | 18–22% | Typical service charge |
| Fuel surcharge | Percent | 5–10% | When applicable |
