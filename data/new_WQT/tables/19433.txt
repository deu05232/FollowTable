# Federal Income Tax Extension Overview (Not State-Specific)

| Topic | Description | Date/Timeframe | Form |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Standard federal filing deadline | Normal due date to file Form 1040 | April (varies by year) | Form 1040 |
| Extension request due date | Deadline to submit an extension request | Same as standard federal deadline | Form 4868 |
| Extended federal filing deadline | Last day to file after extension is approved | October (varies by year) | Form 1040 |
| Payment with extension | Extension to file is not an extension to pay | Pay by April deadline | Form 4868/Payment voucher |
| Penalty basics | Failure-to-file vs failure-to-pay | Accrues after April deadline | N/A |
| E-file availability | Online submission of extension request | Prior to April deadline | Form 4868 (e-file) |
