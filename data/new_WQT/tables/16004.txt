# Travel Vaccinations Overview by Disease (Indonesia) — General Guidance

| Disease | Typical Vaccine | Who Should Consider It | Notes for Travelers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hepatitis A | Hepatitis A vaccine | Most travelers | Food/water exposure risk |
| Typhoid Fever | Typhoid vaccine | Many travelers | Higher risk outside major hotels |
| Rabies | Rabies vaccine | Long stays/animal contact | Pre-exposure series may be considered |
| Japanese Encephalitis | JE vaccine | Rural/extended travel | Seasonal and location-dependent |
| Malaria | Antimalarial tablets | Many travelers | Not a vaccine; chemoprophylaxis |
| Cholera | Oral cholera vaccine | Limited situations | Outbreak/aid-worker scenarios |
| Tetanus | Tdap/Td booster | All travelers | Routine booster recommended |
| Measles | MMR | Unvaccinated/unsure | Outbreaks occur worldwide |
