# Vaccinations for Kenya — Required and Recommended Vaccines for Travelers (Mixed list)

| Vaccine | Required/Recommended | Typical reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Yellow fever | Required (from risk areas) | Entry requirement depending on origin |
| Hepatitis A | Recommended | Food and water exposure |
| Typhoid | Recommended | Food and water exposure |
| Tetanus | Recommended | Routine booster |
| Rabies | Consider | Animal exposure |
| Malaria | Not a vaccine (chemoprophylaxis) | Bite prevention/medication |
