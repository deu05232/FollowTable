# Adult Blood Pressure Classification (Office Clinic, mm Hg) — Systolic and Diastolic Thresholds

| Category | Systolic (mm Hg) | Diastolic (mm Hg) |
|---|---:|---:|
| Normal | <120 | and <80 |
| Elevated (High-Normal) | 120–129 | and <80 |
| Hypertension — Stage 1 | 130–139 | or 80–89 |
| Hypertension — Stage 2 | ≥140 | or ≥90 |
| Hypertensive Crisis* | >180 | and/or >120 |
| Isolated Systolic Hypertension (ISH) | ≥140 | and <90 |
| Isolated Diastolic Hypertension (IDH) | <140 | and ≥90 |
