| Person | Position (1945) | Organization | Responsibility | Related Decision/Task |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Harry S. Truman | President | U.S. Government | Final executive authority | Authorized use under wartime policy |
| Henry L. Stimson | Secretary of War | War Department | Strategic oversight | Chaired high-level deliberations |
| Leslie R. Groves | Major General | Manhattan Engineer District | Program leadership | Directed production and deployment planning |
| J. Robert Oppenheimer | Scientific Director | Los Alamos Laboratory | Technical leadership | Oversaw weapon development |
| Paul W. Tibbets | Colonel | 509th Composite Group | Mission command | Led delivery aircraft |
| Thomas Ferebee | Major | USAAF | Bombardment | Aimed and released weapon |
| William S. Parsons | Captain | US Navy | Ordnance/weaponeering | In-flight arming responsibility |
| Curtis LeMay | Major General | XXI Bomber Command | Strategic air operations | Oversaw broader bombing campaign |
