# NPT Tap Drill Size Chart (Pipe Taper Threads) — Common Sizes

| NPT tap size | Threads per inch (TPI) | Recommended drill size (inch) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1/16-27 NPT | 27 | 11/32 | Pilot hole before tapping |
| 1/8-27 NPT | 27 | 21/64 | Common for small fittings |
| 1/4-18 NPT | 18 | 7/16 | Use cutting fluid for steel |
| 3/8-18 NPT | 18 | 37/64 | Tapered thread engagement |
| 1/2-14 NPT | 14 | 23/32 | Keep tap square to hole |
| 3/4-14 NPT | 14 | 59/64 | Larger pipe sizes |
| 1-11.5 NPT | 11.5 | 1 5/32 | Typical shop reference |
| 1 1/4-11.5 NPT | 11.5 | 1 1/2 | For heavier fittings |
