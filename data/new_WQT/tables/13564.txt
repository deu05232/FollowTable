# U.S. Army Enlisted Pay Grades (E-3 to E-9) and Rank Titles

| Pay Grade | Army Enlisted Rank Title | Abbreviation | Typical Leadership/Role (brief) |
|---|---|---|---|
| E-3 | Private First Class | PFC | Junior enlisted; team member |
| E-4 | Specialist | SPC | Technical/individual contributor |
| E-4 | Corporal | CPL | Junior NCO; small team leader |
| E-5 | Sergeant | SGT | Team leader |
| E-6 | Staff Sergeant | SSG | Squad leader/section leader |
| E-7 | Sergeant First Class | SFC | Platoon sergeant (assistant)/senior NCO |
| E-8 | Master Sergeant | MSG | Senior NCO; operations/leadership |
| E-8 | First Sergeant | 1SG | Company-level senior enlisted advisor |
| E-9 | Sergeant Major | SGM | Senior NCO; battalion/brigade staff |
| E-9 | Command Sergeant Major | CSM | Command-level senior enlisted advisor |
| E-9 | Sergeant Major of the Army | SMA | Service senior enlisted advisor |
