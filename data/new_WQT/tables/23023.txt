# Aromatic and Flowering Plants Linked to Specific Repelled Pests (Quick Lookup)

| Plant | Scientific name | Repels/deters (pest) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Lemongrass | Cymbopogon citratus | Mosquitoes | Often grown in containers near patios |
| Fennel | Foeniculum vulgare | Aphids, slugs | Keep some distance from certain vegetables |
| Dill | Anethum graveolens | Aphids, spider mites | Commonly interplanted in herb gardens |
| Thyme (common thyme) | Thymus vulgaris | Cabbage worm, whiteflies | Low edging plant for beds |
| Oregano | Origanum vulgare | Ants, aphids | Groundcover; also works in pots |
| Tansy | Tanacetum vulgare | Ants, flies, moths | Strong-scented; can spread if unmanaged |
| Bay laurel | Laurus nobilis | Pantry moths, weevils | Dried leaves used in storage areas |
| Chervil | Anthriscus cerefolium | Slugs, aphids | Prefers cooler conditions |
| Borage | Borago officinalis | Tomato hornworm, cabbage worm | Often used near tomatoes/brassicas |
| Geranium (scented) | Pelargonium graveolens | Mosquitoes, flies | Grown near windows or seating |
| Wormwood | Artemisia absinthium | Moths, flea beetles | Aromatic foliage; avoid overuse near some crops |
| Calendula (pot marigold) | Calendula officinalis | Aphids, whiteflies | Common companion flower in veggie beds |
