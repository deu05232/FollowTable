# Elephant Development Stages and Calf Terminology

| Species | Stage | Age | Term Used | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Elephant | Newborn | 0–1 month | Calf | Nursing, stays close to mother |
| Elephant | Infant | 1–12 months | Calf | Learns to use trunk |
| Elephant | Juvenile | 1–5 years | Juvenile | Increasing independence |
| Elephant | Subadult | 5–10 years | Subadult | Growth continues |
| Elephant | Adult | 10–50 years | Adult | Fully mature |
| Elephant | Mature adult | 50+ years | Mature adult | Elder in herd |
