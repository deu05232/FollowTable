# Cranial nerves involved in eye movement  basic attributes

| Cranial nerve relevant to eye movement | Main modality profile | General role in eye/face function |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Oculomotor (CN III) | Mainly motor | Moves most extraocular muscles, eyelid elevation, parasympathetic to pupil |
| Trochlear (CN IV) | Motor | Controls one extraocular muscle involved in downward/inward gaze |
| Abducens (CN VI) | Mainly motor | Controls abduction of the eye |
| Trigeminal (V1) | Sensory | Corneal sensation important for ocular reflexes |
