| Shopfitting / Build-Out | R450,000 | R1,200,000 | R3,500,000 | Construction, finishes, counters, signage |
| Kitchen Equipment | R250,000 | R750,000 | R1,800,000 | Ovens, fryers, refrigeration, POS hardware |
| Training & Travel | R10,000 | R35,000 | R120,000 | Staff training, travel, accommodation |
| Initial Inventory | R20,000 | R90,000 | R250,000 | Food, packaging, smallwares |
| Pre-Opening Marketing | R5,000 | R25,000 | R100,000 | Launch campaign and promotions |
| Working Capital (3 months) | R60,000 | R220,000 | R800,000 | Cash buffer for wages, utilities, contingencies |
