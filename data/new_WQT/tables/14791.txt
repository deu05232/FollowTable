# Wine Sweetness Terms and Residual Sugar Ranges (Selection Guide)

| Sweetness level | Residual sugar range (g/L) | Typical context |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bone dry | 0–3 | Very dry still wines |
| Dry | 4–9 | Many table wines |
| Off-dry | 10–18 | Some Riesling styles |
| Medium-sweet | 19–45 | Dessert-leaning wines |
| Sweet | 46+ | Dessert wines |
