# Top Gear Test Track — Rulebook and Eligibility for the Power Lap Board

| Rule area | Requirement | Allowed examples | Not allowed examples | Rationale | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tires | Road-legal tires only | OE performance tires | Slicks / racing compounds | Keep comparison fair | Exceptions rarely discussed |
| Vehicle type | Production road cars | Supercars, hot hatches | One-off prototypes | Board is for consumer cars | Limited-run gray area |
| Setup | Standard factory spec | Stock aero, stock power | Aftermarket tuning | Prevents "modded" advantages | Dealer options debated |
| Conditions | Must be recorded with condition code | Dry, Hot, Moist | Unlogged conditions | Transparency | Some older laps lack detail |
| Timing | Official Top Gear timing only | Stig timed lap | External stopwatch | Consistency | Retests sometimes occur |
| Safety | Track marshal clearance | Full run permitted | Red-flagged lap | Avoid compromised laps | Partial laps excluded |
| Publication | Must be aired / published | Episode feature | Unbroadcast tests | Verifiability | Clips may be cut |
| Classification | Adjustments policy | Use published deductions | Private recalculations | Avoid disputes | "Est." discouraged |
