# Concerta ER Dosing by Clinical Scenario (Including Prior Regimen Considerations)

| Scenario | Typical starting dose (mg qAM) | Typical dose range (mg/day) | Prior-regimen factor included | Example |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- | --- |
| Stimulant-naïve patient | 18 | 18–54 | No | New diagnosis, no prior MPH |
| Switching from IR MPH | 36–54 | 36–72 | Yes | From 10 mg TID → 54 mg |
| Switching from another ER MPH | 36–72 | 36–72 | Yes | From ER 40 mg → 72 mg |
| Partial response on 36 mg | 54 | 54–72 | Indirect | Increase one strength |
| Late-day rebound symptoms | 36 | 36–72 | Indirect | Consider timing/adjunct IR |
| Sensitive to side effects | 18 | 18–36 | No | Anxiety/insomnia risk |
| Need longer coverage | 36 | 36–72 | No | Full school/work day |
| High-risk for misuse | 18–36 | 18–54 | No | Prefer monitored titration |
