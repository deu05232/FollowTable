# Spine MRI CPT Code Matrix (Region × Contrast Pattern)

| Spinal Region | CPT (No Contrast) | CPT (With Contrast) | CPT (With & Without Contrast) | Typical Use Case |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Cervical (C-Spine) | 72141 | 72142 | 72156 | Neck pain, radiculopathy; add contrast for suspected infection/tumor |
| Thoracic (T-Spine) | 72146 | 72147 | 72157 | Mid-back pain; contrast often for neoplasm/inflammation |
| Lumbar (L-Spine) | 72148 | 72149 | 72158 | Low-back pain/sciatica; contrast for post-op or mass evaluation |
| Sacrum/Coccyx | 72195 | 72196 | 72197 | Sacral pain, tumor/infection workup; contrast as indicated |
| Multi-region spine (C/T/L) | 72141 + 72146 + 72148 | 72142 + 72147 + 72149 | 72156 + 72157 + 72158 | When separate regions are imaged and billed individually |
| Multi-region including sacrum | 72141 + 72146 + 72148 + 72195 | 72142 + 72147 + 72149 + 72196 | 72156 + 72157 + 72158 + 72197 | Extensive coverage; sacrum/coccyx coded separately when performed |
