# Florida Driving-Related Offenses and Penalties (Mixed Felony Misdemeanor)

| Offense | Level | Maximum Incarceration | Maximum Fine | License Consequences |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Reckless driving | Misdemeanor | 90 days | $500 | Possible suspension |
| Reckless driving (repeat) | Misdemeanor | 6 months | $1,000 | Higher suspension risk |
| DUI (first) | Misdemeanor | 6 months | $1,000 | Mandatory revocation period |
| DUI with property damage | Misdemeanor | 1 year | $1,000 | Restitution common |
| Leaving the scene (property damage) | Misdemeanor | 60 days | $500 | Points/suspension |
| Leaving the scene (injury) | Felony | 5 years | $5,000 | Mandatory revocation |
| Fleeing/eluding (basic) | Felony | 5 years | $5,000 | Vehicle forfeiture possible |
| Vehicular homicide | Felony | 15 years | $10,000 | Serious felony charge |
