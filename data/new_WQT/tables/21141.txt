# Economics-major career paths focused on economic analysis and public economic development work

| Career role | Typical employers | What you do (economics-centered) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Economist (public sector) | Central bank, treasury, statistical agency | Forecast macro indicators, evaluate policy impacts |
| Economic Development Specialist | City/county development office, regional councils | Analyze local industries, incentives, and growth strategies |
| Policy Analyst (economic policy) | Think tanks, government ministries, NGOs | Cost-benefit analysis, policy research, program evaluation |
| Research Analyst (economics) | Universities, research institutes | Build models, run regressions, publish findings |
| Labor Market Analyst | Workforce agencies, consulting | Study wages, employment trends, and labor policy |
| International Trade Analyst | Trade commissions, customs agencies, firms | Analyze tariffs, trade flows, and competitiveness |
