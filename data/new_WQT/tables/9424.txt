# South Africa Fast-Food Franchise Start-Up Budget Breakdown (Example  KFC)

| Cost Line Item | Notes | Typical Range (ZAR) |
|---|---|---|
| Initial franchise fee | Once-off fee to join the system | R350,000 – R650,000 |
| Shopfitting & kitchen equipment | Counters, fryers, cold rooms, signage | R2,200,000 – R4,800,000 |
| Lease deposit | Varies by landlord and location | R120,000 – R450,000 |
| Opening inventory | First stock order | R80,000 – R220,000 |
| Training & travel | Manager training, accommodation | R40,000 – R140,000 |
| Professional fees | Legal, accounting, plans | R30,000 – R120,000 |
| Working capital (3 months) | Payroll, utilities, local marketing | R300,000 – R900,000 |
| Total estimated start-up | Summed estimate for one store | R3,120,000 – R7,280,000 |
