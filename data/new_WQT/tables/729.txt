# List of Buffy the Vampire Slayer and Angel cast and crew
> Key

| Letter by name | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| None | Indicates that this person worked on Buffy |
| (guest starred on Angel ) | Indicates that this person acted on Buffy , but also appeared in a small number of Angel episodes playing the same role |
| (A) | Indicates that this person worked on Angel |
| (B/A) | Indicates that this person worked on Buffy & Angel |
