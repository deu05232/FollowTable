# Concerta ER dosing by age group (starting dose and usual titration range)

| Age group | Starting dose (once daily, morning) | Typical dose range | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Children 6–8 years | 18 mg/day | 18–36 mg/day | Increase in 18 mg steps if needed |
| Children 9–12 years | 18 mg/day | 18–54 mg/day | Do not exceed labeled pediatric max |
| Adolescents 13–15 years | 18 mg/day | 18–72 mg/day | May require higher doses than younger children |
| Adolescents 16–17 years | 18 mg/day | 18–72 mg/day | Max may be limited by mg/kg guidance |
| Adults 18–65 years | 18 or 36 mg/day | 18–72 mg/day | Choose start based on prior stimulant exposure |
| Older adults (65+) | Not established | Not established | Use only if specifically directed; limited data |
