# Adobe font family size guidance (non-software item list)

| Typeface family | Recommended use | Suggested size range | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adobe Jenson | Book text | 9–13 pt | Optimized for readability |
| Adobe Garamond | Editorial | 10–14 pt | Classic old-style serif |
| Adobe Caslon | Long-form print | 10–14 pt | Traditional proportions |
| Adobe Myungjo | CJK typography | 9–12 pt | For Korean text settings |
