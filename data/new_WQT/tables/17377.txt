# Common Aneuploidy Types and Phenotypic Summaries (No Gene-Specific Causation)

| Aneuploidy type | Karyotype example | Common name | Typical phenotype summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trisomy 21 | 47,XX,+21 | Down syndrome | Intellectual disability, characteristic facies |
| Trisomy 18 | 47,XY,+18 | Edwards syndrome | Severe developmental delay, clenched fists |
| Trisomy 13 | 47,XX,+13 | Patau syndrome | Midline defects, polydactyly |
| Monosomy X | 45,X | Turner syndrome | Short stature, gonadal dysgenesis |
| 47,XXY | 47,XXY | Klinefelter syndrome | Hypogonadism, infertility |
| 47,XYY | 47,XYY | XYY syndrome | Tall stature, variable learning issues |
| 47,XXX | 47,XXX | Triple X syndrome | Often mild phenotype |
| Trisomy 16 (mosaic) | 47,XX,+16/46,XX | Mosaic trisomy 16 | Growth restriction, variable anomalies |
