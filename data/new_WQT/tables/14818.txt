| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Web Access | Outlook.com Web | Browser-based mailbox access | https://outlook.live.com | 443 | TLS |
| Mobile Sync | Exchange ActiveSync | Sync mail/contacts/calendar | eas.outlook.com | 443 | TLS |
| Mail Retrieval | IMAP | Standard mailbox access | imap-mail.outlook.com | 993 | SSL/TLS |
| Mail Retrieval | POP3 | Download-only access | pop-mail.outlook.com | 995 | SSL/TLS |
| Mail Sending | SMTP Submission | Authenticated mail submission | smtp-mail.outlook.com | 587 | STARTTLS |
| Account | Microsoft Account | Identity and sign-in | login.live.com | 443 | TLS |
| Autodiscover | Autodiscover | Client auto-configuration | autodiscover.outlook.com | 443 | TLS |
