# Economics Major Job Options by Work Setting  Specific Roles, Daily Tasks, and Location Patterns

| Work Setting | Job Title | Typical Day-to-Day Tasks | Location Pattern / Where These Jobs Cluster |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Government (Local/State) | Economic Development Coordinator | Tracks local business metrics, supports incentive programs, prepares council presentations | City halls and regional development offices; tied to specific municipalities; mostly on-site |
| Government (Federal/State) | Labor Market Analyst | Monitors employment/wage data, builds reports, and briefs leadership on labor trends | State capitals and major metros; may coordinate with regional field offices |
| Central Bank / Financial Regulator | Bank Examiner (Entry-Level) | Reviews bank portfolios, checks compliance, and writes examination findings | Often home-office city plus travel to banks within a region |
| Private Sector (Corporate) | Business Intelligence Analyst | Queries databases, defines KPIs, builds dashboards, and presents insights to teams | Tech/corporate hubs; hybrid common; sometimes fully remote |
| Private Sector (Insurance) | Actuarial Analyst (Early Career) | Assists with risk modeling, pricing, and reserving analyses (often with exam track) | Insurance hubs in certain cities; mostly office-based, increasing hybrid options |
| Private Sector (Retail/CPG) | Demand Forecasting Analyst | Forecasts sales, evaluates seasonality/promotions, and improves forecast accuracy | HQ-focused in major metros; occasional travel to distribution/retail sites |
| Consulting (Management) | Analyst, Strategy & Operations | Builds models, researches markets, sizes opportunities, and supports client workshops | Large metro offices; frequent client travel; location depends on staffing |
| Consulting (Economic/Litigation) | Research Analyst (Antitrust/Litigation Support) | Collects data, runs econometric tests, and drafts memos for expert witnesses | Concentrated in big cities with law/consulting markets; deadline-driven |
| Nonprofit / NGO | Grants & Impact Analyst | Tracks budgets/outcomes, writes impact summaries, and supports reporting to funders | Often in capitals/major metros; some roles remote; field work depends on program |
| Real Estate (Commercial) | Portfolio/Asset Management Analyst | Monitors property performance, updates cash-flow models, and supports refinancing decisions | Real estate centers; office-based with occasional site visits |
| Healthcare | Healthcare Policy/Analytics Associate | Analyzes claims/utilization data and supports policy or payer-provider strategy | Strong in healthcare hubs; hybrid common for analytics roles |
| Energy & Utilities | Energy Market Analyst (Junior) | Tracks power/gas prices, regulatory changes, and supports procurement/hedging decisions | Energy hubs and utility HQ cities; some shift work depending on trading/ops |
| Logistics / Transportation | Transportation Economist (Assistant/Analyst) | Evaluates tolling/pricing, ridership demand, and cost-benefit analyses for projects | Major metros with transit agencies; project meetings may require local travel |
| Education/Research (Applied) | Research Coordinator (Economics/Policy Lab) | Manages surveys/fieldwork logistics, cleans data, and coordinates with partners | University towns and partner regions; may require travel to study sites |
