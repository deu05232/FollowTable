# pH Ranges by Water Type and Treatment (not brand-specific, range-based)

| Water Category | Typical pH Range | Why It Varies | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Reverse osmosis water | 4.5–6.0 | depends on remineralization | home RO systems |
| Distilled water | 5.0–6.5 | absorbs CO2 from air | lab distilled |
| Spring water | 6.5–8.5 | geology and mineral content | general category |
| Mineral water (sparkling) | 4.0–6.0 | dissolved CO2 | general category |
| Alkaline-enhanced water | 8.5–10.0 | additives/ionization | general category |
| Artesian water | 6.8–8.2 | aquifer differences | general category |
