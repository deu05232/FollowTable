# Common tomato pests and typical symptoms

| Pest | Plant part affected | Typical signs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Aphids | leaves | curling leaves, sticky honeydew |
| Whiteflies | leaves | yellowing, sooty mold |
| Hornworms | leaves/fruit | large chewed holes |
| Cutworms | stems | seedlings severed at soil line |
| Spider mites | leaves | stippling, fine webbing |
| Slugs | fruit | irregular holes and slime trails |
