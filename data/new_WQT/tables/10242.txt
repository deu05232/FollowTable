# Vasopressor and Inotrope Agents Grouped by Hemodynamic Effect (Mechanism Mix)

| Primary Hemodynamic Effect | Agent (Brand) | Generic | Comment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Predominantly alpha-1 vasoconstriction | Neo-Synephrine | phenylephrine | raises SVR; reflex bradycardia possible |
| Mixed alpha/beta support | Levophed | norepinephrine | common first-line in septic shock |
| Beta-1 inotropy (increased contractility) | Dobutrex | dobutamine | increases cardiac output |
| Beta agonist bronchodilation + some pressor effect | Primatene Mist | epinephrine | not a standard ICU pressor formulation |
| Vasopressin receptor vasoconstriction | Vasostrict | vasopressin | adjunct in vasodilatory shock |
| Dopaminergic/adrenergic dose-dependent | Intropin | dopamine | variable effects by dose |
| Alpha/beta agonist with strong inotropy | Adrenalin | epinephrine | increases CO and SVR |
