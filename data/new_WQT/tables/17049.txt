# Glycemic Index Classification Ranges and Example Foods

| GI Class | GI Range | Typical interpretation | Example foods |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Low | 0–55 | Slower rise in blood glucose | lentils, chickpeas, most non-starchy vegetables |
| Low | 0–55 | Often recommended for steady energy | apples, pears, oranges |
| Medium | 56–69 | Moderate impact | brown rice, quick oats, rye bread |
| Medium | 56–69 | Mixed depending on preparation | pineapple, raisins |
| High | 70+ | Faster rise in blood glucose | white bread, corn flakes, instant rice |
| High | 70+ | Often spikes more quickly | baked potato, pretzels, glucose |
