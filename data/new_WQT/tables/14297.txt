# Common internet censorship methods used in China and what they affect

| Method | What it targets | What users typically observe | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| DNS poisoning | Domain lookups | Wrong IP / failed resolution | Can vary by resolver |
| IP blocking | IP ranges | Timeout | May affect shared hosting |
| URL keyword filtering | URL strings | Specific pages fail | Can be selective |
| SNI/HTTPS filtering | TLS handshake metadata | Connection reset | Often intermittent |
| Packet injection/reset | TCP streams | Reset packets | Depends on keywords |
| Traffic throttling | Bandwidth | Very slow access | Not always a full block |
