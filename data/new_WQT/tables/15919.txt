# 3D Point Groups  Rotation Orders and Symmetry Elements

| Schoenflies symbol | Common name | Rotational axes present (orders) | Typical 3D example |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | trivial | 1 | asymmetric molecule |
| C2 | twofold cyclic | 2 | twisted biphenyl (idealized) |
| C3 | threefold cyclic | 3 | ammonia (idealized rotational part) |
| D2 | dihedral | 2, 2, 2 | rectangular box (rotation-only) |
| D3 | trigonal dihedral | 3 and three 2-fold axes | trigonal prism |
| T | chiral tetrahedral | 3, 2 | regular tetrahedron (rotations) |
| O | chiral octahedral | 4, 3, 2 | cube/octahedron (rotations) |
| I | chiral icosahedral | 5, 3, 2 | icosahedron/dodecahedron (rotations) |
