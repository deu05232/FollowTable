# Wallpaper Groups and Rotation Center Orders in Periodic Tilings

| Wallpaper group | Example tiling motif | Rotation centers present (orders) | Notes on rotation centers |
|---|---|---|---|
| p1 | generic parallelogram lattice | 1 only | no non-trivial rotation centers |
| p2 | brick-like translation pattern | 2 | 180° rotocenters at lattice half-turns |
| p3 | triangular lattice | 3 | 120° centers at triangle vertices |
| p4 | square grid | 4, 2 | 90° centers at grid points; 180° at edge midpoints |
| p6 | hexagonal lattice | 6, 3, 2 | 60° at hexagon centers; 120° and 180° also occur |
| p4m | square tiling with mirrors | 4, 2 | rotation centers combined with reflection axes |
| p6m | hexagon/triangle tiling with mirrors | 6, 3, 2 | rotocenters plus mirror lines through them |
