# Common aspect ratios for photos and screens

| Aspect Ratio | Ratio (W:H) | Common Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 4:3 | 1.33:1 | Compact cameras, some sensors |
| 3:2 | 1.50:1 | DSLR / APS-C / full-frame photos |
| 16:9 | 1.78:1 | Video and widescreen displays |
| 1:1 | 1.00:1 | Square crops / social media |
