# Concerta (methylphenidate ER) dosing by patient age group (starting dose and usual max limits)

| Patient group | Typical starting dose (once daily, morning) | Typical titration step | Usual dose range | Maximum / limit notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Children (6–12 years) | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at weekly intervals | 18–54 mg/day | Do not exceed 54 mg/day in this age group |
| Adolescents (13–17 years) | 18 mg/day | +18 mg at weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | Max 72 mg/day; may also be limited by 2 mg/kg/day |
| Adults (18–65 years) | 18–36 mg/day | +18 mg at weekly intervals | 18–72 mg/day | Max 72 mg/day |
| Older adults (≥65 years) | Not established | — | Not established | Use only with careful risk/benefit assessment; limited data |
| Patients sensitive to stimulants (any age) | 18 mg/day | +18 mg more slowly (e.g., every 1–2 weeks) | 18–54 mg/day (individualized) | Consider lower ceiling if adverse effects occur |
| Patients with comorbid anxiety or insomnia risk (any age) | 18 mg/day | +18 mg cautiously | 18–54 mg/day (individualized) | Prefer morning dosing; avoid late dosing; adjust based on tolerability |
