# Capital Gains Rates Tied to Ordinary Income Brackets (Federal Overview)

| Ordinary Income Bracket | Short-Term Capital Gains Rate | Long-Term Capital Gains Rate | Collectibles Long-Term Rate | Depreciation Recapture (Real Estate) | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 10% bracket | 10% | 0% | 28% | 25% | ST treated as ordinary income |
| 12% bracket | 12% | 0% | 28% | 25% | Threshold-based LT rates |
| 22% bracket | 22% | 15% | 28% | 25% | LT often 15% in mid brackets |
| 24% bracket | 24% | 15% | 28% | 25% | NIIT may apply at higher incomes |
| 32% bracket | 32% | 15% | 28% | 25% | LT can remain 15% |
| 35% bracket | 35% | 20% | 28% | 25% | LT top rate typically 20% |
| 37% bracket | 37% | 20% | 28% | 25% | Additional surtaxes possible |
