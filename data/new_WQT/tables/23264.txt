| Rosy boa | Lichanura trivirgata | deserts, scrub |
| Rubber boa | Charina bottae | forests, mountains |
| Coachwhip | Masticophis flagellum | open areas |
| Ring-necked snake | Diadophis punctatus | moist woodlands |
| Gartersnake | Thamnophis spp. | near water |
