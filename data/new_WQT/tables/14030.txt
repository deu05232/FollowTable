# Financial Derivatives Overview (Options Forwards) and Key Sensitivities

| Instrument Name | Payoff Pattern | Key Rate/Greek (Sensitivity) | 'Derivative' Mentioned As | Typical Underlying |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Forward | V_T = S_T - K | Delta ≈ 1 | Financial derivative contract | Commodity/FX |
| Futures | V_T = S_T - K (marked-to-market) | Delta ≈ 1 | Financial derivative contract | Index/commodity |
| Call Option | V_T = max(S_T-K,0) | Delta, Gamma, Vega | Derivative security | Equity |
| Put Option | V_T = max(K-S_T,0) | Delta, Gamma, Vega | Derivative security | Equity |
| Swap | Net PV of cashflow legs | DV01 | Interest-rate derivative | Rates |
| CDS | Protection leg vs premium leg | Credit spread sensitivity | Credit derivative | Credit |
| Barrier Option | Depends on hitting a barrier | Path-dependent Greeks | Exotic derivative | FX/equity |
