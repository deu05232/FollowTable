# Children’s Calcium Target with Serving-Based Equivalents

| Age group | Recommended calcium intake | Equivalent servings (approx.) | Example items counted as 1 serving |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| 4–6 years | 1000 mg/day | 3 servings/day | 1 cup milk / 200 g yogurt |
| 7–9 years | 1200 mg/day | 3–4 servings/day | 30 g cheese / fortified soy drink |
| 10–12 years | 1300 mg/day | 4 servings/day | calcium-set tofu / canned salmon |
| 13–15 years | 1300 mg/day | 4 servings/day | milk / yogurt / cheese |
| 16–18 years | 1300 mg/day | 4 servings/day | fortified cereal + milk |
| Notes | — | — | Serving estimates vary by product and fortification |
| Label crosswalk | 1300 mg/day | 100% DV basis | Uses adult %DV conventions on many labels |
