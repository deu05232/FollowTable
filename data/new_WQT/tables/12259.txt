# Credit score ranges and what they mean for borrowers (not platform minimums)

| Score band | Label | Typical access to credit | Common borrower characteristics |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 800–850 | Exceptional | Easiest approval | Long credit history, low utilization |
| 740–799 | Very good | Broad approval | Few late payments |
| 670–739 | Good | Many approvals | Moderate utilization |
| 580–669 | Fair | Limited approval | Thin file or past delinquencies |
| 300–579 | Poor | Hard to approve | Recent defaults or collections |
| N/A | No score | Usually requires manual review | New-to-credit borrowers |
