# Adult blood pressure classification (category-first layout)

| Category | Systolic range (mm Hg) | Diastolic range (mm Hg) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hypotension | <90 | <60 |
| Normal | 90–119 | 60–79 |
| Prehypertension | 120–139 | 80–89 |
| Hypertension (Stage 1) | 140–159 | 90–99 |
| Hypertension (Stage 2) | ≥160 | ≥100 |
