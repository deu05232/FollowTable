|---|---|---:|---:|---:|
| ! | Exclamation mark | 33 | 41 | 21 |
| @ | At sign | 64 | 100 | 40 |
| # | Number sign | 35 | 43 | 23 |
| $ | Dollar sign | 36 | 44 | 24 |
| % | Percent sign | 37 | 45 | 25 |
| & | Ampersand | 38 | 46 | 26 |
| * | Asterisk | 42 | 52 | 2A |
| ~ | Tilde | 126 | 176 | 7E |
