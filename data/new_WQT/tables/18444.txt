# ESC ESH-Style Office Blood Pressure Categories with Additional Cardiovascular Measures

| Category | Office SBP (mm Hg) | Office DBP (mm Hg) | Typical LVH finding | Suggested next test | Example ASCVD 10-yr risk |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|---:|
| Optimal | < 120 | < 80 | uncommon | none | 2% |
| Normal | 120–129 | 80–84 | rare | lifestyle review | 4% |
| High-normal | 130–139 | 85–89 | possible | ambulatory BP + lipid panel | 7% |
| Grade 1 hypertension | 140–159 | 90–99 | more frequent | echo + microalbumin | 12% |
| Grade 2 hypertension | 160–179 | 100–109 | common | echo + renal function | 18% |
| Grade 3 hypertension | ≥ 180 | ≥ 110 | very common | urgent evaluation | 25% |
| Isolated systolic hypertension | ≥ 140 | < 90 | age-related | home BP monitoring | 10% |
