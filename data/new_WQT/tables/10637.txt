# VA Education and Housing Benefits (GI Bill) Monthly Payment Overview

| Benefit Program | Eligibility Driver | Typical Monthly Amount | Family Status Adjustment | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Post-9/11 GI Bill MHA | Enrollment (full-time) | $1,600 | Not dependent-based | Housing allowance varies by ZIP code |
| Post-9/11 GI Bill Book Stipend | Credits taken | Up to $83 | Not dependent-based | Paid per credit hour |
| Montgomery GI Bill (MGIB) | Enrollment (full-time) | $2,200 | Not dependent-based | Flat rate, no dependents factor |
| Yellow Ribbon | School participation | Varies | Not dependent-based | Supplement for tuition |
| Tutorial Assistance | Academic need | Up to $100 | Not dependent-based | Limited reimbursement |
| STEM Scholarship | Program approval | $2,000 | Not dependent-based | Additional entitlement months |
| Work-Study | Hours worked | $300–$700 | Not dependent-based | Paid based on time worked |
