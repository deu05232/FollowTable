# Raw Meat and Seafood Storage Times (Uncooked) — Refrigerator vs Freezer

| Category | Item (Raw/Uncooked) | Refrigerator (32–40°F) | Freezer (0°F) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Poultry | Chicken breasts (raw) | 1–2 days | 9 months |
| Poultry | Ground turkey (raw) | 1–2 days | 3–4 months |
| Beef | Ground beef (raw) | 1–2 days | 3–4 months |
| Beef | Steak (raw) | 3–5 days | 6–12 months |
| Pork | Pork chops (raw) | 3–5 days | 4–6 months |
| Seafood | Salmon fillet (raw) | 1–2 days | 2–3 months |
| Seafood | Shrimp (raw) | 1–2 days | 3–6 months |
| Eggs | Shell eggs (raw) | 3–5 weeks | Not recommended |
