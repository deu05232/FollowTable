# Baby size guide by age and clothing fit notes (includes height weight but focuses on sizing advice)

| Age band | Recommended clothing size | Fit guidance notes | Height/weight guidance |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0–3 months | 0–3 mo | If between sizes, size up for diapers | About 21–24 in, 8–12 lb |
| 3–6 months | 3–6 mo | Roomy fit for layering in winter | About 24–26.5 in, 12–16.5 lb |
| 6–9 months | 6–9 mo | Slim brands may run short in the torso | About 26.5–28.5 in, 16.5–20.5 lb |
| 9–12 months | 9–12 mo | Consider footed pajamas length first | About 28.5–30.5 in, 20.5–24.5 lb |
