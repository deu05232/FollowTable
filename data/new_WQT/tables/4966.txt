| District of Columbia | $10.50 | As of June 2016, the tipped wage in Washington, D.C., is $2.77 per hour. [75] A law enacted in January 2014 will have annual increases every July; $10.50 in 2015, and $11.50 in 2016. Afterwards the increases will be based on the region's cost of living. [169] After unanimous approval for the first round vote, a 2016 bill is expected to pass that would further increase the minimum to $15/hour by 2020 ($5/hour for tipped workers) and thereafter index it to inflation.
