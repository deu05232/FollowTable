# Major Bible Translations and Edition Notes (Overview by Version)

| Version | Language | First complete publication | Primary source text | Notable features |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| King James Version (KJV) | English | 1611 | Masoretic Text & Textus Receptus | Classic Early Modern English style |
| New International Version (NIV) | English | 1978 | Eclectic critical editions | Dynamic equivalence tendency |
| English Standard Version (ESV) | English | 2001 | Critical editions | Essentially literal; evangelical usage |
| Douay-Rheims | English | 1609–1610 (NT/OT) | Latin Vulgate | Traditional Catholic translation |
| Nova Vulgata | Latin | 1979 | Critical editions | Modern official Latin Catholic text |
| Septuagint (LXX) | Greek | 3rd–1st c. BCE | Hebrew/Aramaic traditions | Ancient Jewish Greek translation |
| Peshitta | Syriac | 2nd–5th c. CE | Syriac tradition | Key Eastern Christian Bible |
| Luther Bible | German | 1534 | Hebrew & Greek | Influential Reformation-era translation |
