# Differences between Gram Positive and Gram Negative Bacteria

| S.N. | Characteristics | Gram Positive |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Gram Reaction | Retain crystal violet dye and stain blue or purple |
| 2 | Cell Wall | Cell Wall is 20-30 nm thick. |
| 3 | Cell Wall | The wall is Smooth. |
| 4 | Peptidoglycan Layer | Thick (multilayered) |
