# Bread - How Long Does Bread Last ? Shelf Life Storage Expiration

| Opened/Unopened | Pantry | Refrigerator |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Packaged Bread (Soft) lasts for | 5-7 Days | Not Recommended |
| Refrigerator Biscuit Dough (Pillsbury biscuits & rolls) lasts for | 2 Hours | 1-2 Weeks |
| Frozen Bread Dough (Soft) lasts for | 1 Day | 2-3 Days |
| Cooked French Toast lasts for | 2 Hours | 5-7 Days |
