# EITC Claim Requirements Checklist by Filing Status (Focus on Documentation and Eligibility Tests)

| Filing status | Qualifying child required? | Relationship test focus | Residency test (months) | Due diligence forms | Common audit trigger | Recommended records |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Single | Optional | Child/relative relationship | 6+ if claiming child | Form 8867 (preparer) | Inconsistent child address | School/medical records |
| Head of Household | Usually | Support & household tests | 6+ if claiming child | Form 8867 (preparer) | Multiple taxpayers claim same child | Lease, utility bills |
| Married Filing Jointly | Optional | Same as above | 6+ if claiming child | Form 8867 (preparer) | Missing SSNs | SSN cards, birth certificates |
| Married Filing Separately | Generally not allowed | N/A | N/A | N/A | Improper filing status | Separation agreement |
| Qualifying Widow(er) | Usually | Child dependency linkage | 6+ | Form 8867 (preparer) | Prior-year dependency conflicts | Prior returns, death certificate |
| Nonresident alien | Typically ineligible | Residency status | N/A | N/A | Incorrect residency claim | Immigration paperwork |
