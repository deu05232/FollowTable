# Recommended Daily Iron Intake (RDA) for Children and Adolescents (Male Female)

| Age group | Sex | Recommended daily iron (mg/day) |
|---|---|---:|
| 4–8 years | All children | 10 |
| 9–13 years | All children | 8 |
| 14–18 years | Male | 11 |
| 14–18 years | Female | 15 |
| 19–50 years | Female | 18 |
| 19+ years | Male | 8 |
