# Distillation Range of Common Petroleum Fuels and Light Hydrocarbon Cuts

| Material | Type | Approx. boiling / distillation range (°C) |
|---|---|---|
| Natural gas liquids (NGL) | mixture | -160 to 30 |
| LPG (propane/butane blend) | mixture | -45 to 5 |
| Petroleum ether | mixture | 30 to 60 |
| Gasoline | mixture | 30 to 210 |
| Kerosene | mixture | 150 to 300 |
| Diesel fuel | mixture | 180 to 360 |
| Jet fuel (Jet A) | mixture | 150 to 300 |
