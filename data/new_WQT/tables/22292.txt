# ANS Pathway Wiring vs. Chemical Signaling (Fiber Classes Emphasized)

| Pathway segment | Typical fiber type / myelination | Conduction velocity (approx.) | Dominant transmitter at synapse | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sympathetic preganglionic | B fibers, lightly myelinated | 3–15 m/s | Acetylcholine | Cell bodies in intermediolateral column |
| Sympathetic postganglionic | C fibers, unmyelinated | 0.5–2 m/s | Norepinephrine | Long vs short axon length varies by organ |
| Parasympathetic preganglionic | B fibers, myelinated | 3–15 m/s | Acetylcholine | Long preganglionic projections |
| Parasympathetic postganglionic | C fibers, unmyelinated | 0.5–2 m/s | Acetylcholine | Short postganglionic projections |
| Adrenal medulla (modified ganglion) | Preganglionic B fibers | 3–15 m/s | Acetylcholine | Chromaffin cells secrete Epi/NE |
| Visceral afferents (reflex arc) | Aδ/C mixed | variable | Glutamate (central) | Carries baro/chemo signals to CNS |
| Enteric intrinsic circuits | local interneurons | variable | ACh, NO, VIP | Complex local wiring dominates function |
