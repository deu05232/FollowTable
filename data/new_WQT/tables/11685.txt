# Oxygen-containing functional groups  structure and quick identification cues

| Functional group | Key atom pattern | Shorthand | What to look for in a structure | Example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Phenol | Ar–OH | ArOH | hydroxyl directly on an aromatic ring | phenol |
| Acyl halide (oxygen-containing) | R–C(=O)–X | RCOX | carbonyl carbon attached to halogen | acetyl chloride |
| Acid anhydride | R–C(=O)–O–C(=O)–R' | (RCO)2O | two carbonyls linked by an oxygen | acetic anhydride |
| Amide (oxygen-containing) | R–C(=O)–NR2 | RCONR2 | carbonyl carbon attached to nitrogen | acetamide |
| Carboxylate (salt) | R–C(=O)O− | RCOO− | deprotonated carboxylic acid | sodium acetate |
