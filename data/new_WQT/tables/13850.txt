# Public Holidays in India — Festival Season (Approximate Dates)

| Holiday | Approx. Month/Season | Region | Why it is observed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Makar Sankranti | Mid-January | Several states | Harvest festival (date varies by local calendar) |
| Holi | March (full moon) | Many states | Spring festival; depends on lunar calendar |
| Eid al-Fitr | April or May | Nationwide (varies) | End of Ramadan; moon sighting dependent |
| Eid al-Adha | June or July | Nationwide (varies) | Based on lunar calendar |
| Raksha Bandhan | August (full moon) | Many states | Sibling celebration; lunar date |
| Dussehra | September or October | Many states | Movable festival period |
| Diwali | October or November | Many states | Festival window across multiple days |
| Guru Nanak Jayanti | November (full moon) | Punjab and others | Lunar calendar |
| Christmas | Late December | Nationwide | Fixed in Gregorian calendar |
