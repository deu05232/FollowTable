# Pest Control Product Categories and Typical Targets (Not Plant-Specific)

| Product / Chemical Category | Example Active Ingredients | Typical Target Pests | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Insect repellents | DEET, picaridin | Mosquitoes, biting flies | Personal protection rather than planting |
| Pyrethroids | permethrin, cypermethrin | Ants, beetles, roaches | Broad-spectrum insecticides |
| Neonicotinoids | imidacloprid, thiamethoxam | Aphids, whiteflies | Systemic; pollinator concerns |
| Insecticidal soaps | potassium salts of fatty acids | Aphids, mites, whiteflies | Contact-only; needs coverage |
| Horticultural oils | mineral oil, neem oil | Scale insects, mites | Can smother soft-bodied pests |
| Molluscicides | iron phosphate | Slugs, snails | Bait formulations common |
| Rodenticides | bromadiolone | Rats, mice | Use according to local regulations |
| Nematicides | fluopyram | Root-knot nematodes | Often restricted / specialized |
| Fungicides | copper hydroxide | Powdery mildew, leaf spots | Disease control, not pests |
| Herbicides | glyphosate | Weeds | Not pest-repelling plants |
| Attractants | pheromone lures | Moths, beetles | Used for trapping/monitoring |
