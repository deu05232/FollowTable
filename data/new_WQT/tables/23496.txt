# Texas felony classifications and standard punishment ranges

| Felony level | Maximum prison term | Maximum fine | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Capital felony | Death or life imprisonment | Up to $10,000 | Certain cases allow life without parole |
| First-degree felony | Up to 99 years or life | Up to $10,000 | Minimum terms can apply |
| Second-degree felony | Up to 20 years | Up to $10,000 |  |
| Third-degree felony | Up to 10 years | Up to $10,000 |  |
| State jail felony | Up to 2 years (state jail) | Up to $10,000 | Often has alternative sentencing options |
| Enhanced felony (habitual) | Up to life | Up to $10,000 | Enhancement depends on prior convictions |
