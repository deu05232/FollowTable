# Blood pressure percentiles for adolescents (classification thresholds)

| Classification | Systolic threshold | Diastolic threshold |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Normal | <90th percentile | <90th percentile |
| Elevated | 90th to <95th percentile | 90th to <95th percentile |
| Stage 1 hypertension | 95th to <99th percentile + 5 mm Hg | 95th to <99th percentile + 5 mm Hg |
| Stage 2 hypertension | ≥99th percentile + 5 mm Hg | ≥99th percentile + 5 mm Hg |
