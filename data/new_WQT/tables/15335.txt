# Carbonyl-containing oxygen functional groups (focused comparison)

| Carbonyl-related group | Generic formula | Key structural cue | Example | Example formula |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Aldehyde | R–CHO | Terminal C=O with H | Benzaldehyde | C6H5CHO |
| Ketone | R–CO–R' | Internal C=O | 2-Butanone | C4H8O |
| Carboxylic acid | R–COOH | C=O plus –OH | Propionic acid | C2H5COOH |
| Ester | R–COOR' | C=O plus –OR | Methyl benzoate | C6H5COOCH3 |
| Anhydride | (RCO)2O | Two carbonyls linked by O | Succinic anhydride | C4H4O3 |
| Acyl halide | R–COX | C=O plus halogen | Benzoyl chloride | C6H5COCl |
| Amide | R–CONH2 | C=O plus amino | Benzamide | C6H5CONH2 |
