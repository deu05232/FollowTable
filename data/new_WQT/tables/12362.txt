# United Kingdom State Pension  Pension Age and Deferral Increase Rules

| Birth cohort | State Pension age | Earliest claim | Deferral increase rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Born before 6 Apr 1950 | 65 | At pension age | ~1% per 9 weeks | Legacy schedule |
| 6 Apr 1950–5 Apr 1953 | 65–66 (phased) | At pension age | ~1% per 9 weeks | Transition period |
| 6 Apr 1953–5 Apr 1960 | 66 | At pension age | ~1% per 9 weeks | Standard age |
| 6 Apr 1960–5 Apr 1977 | 67 (scheduled) | At pension age | ~1% per 9 weeks | Subject to legislation |
| 6 Apr 1977–5 Apr 1978 | 67–68 (phased) | At pension age | ~1% per 9 weeks | Phase-in |
| From 6 Apr 1978 | 68 | At pension age | ~1% per 9 weeks | Future schedule |
| Mixed cohorts | Varies | — | — | Country-specific |
