# Concerta Titration Schedule and Switching Guide (Including Age Mentions)

| Clinical situation | Day 1 dose | Week 1–2 adjustment | Week 3+ | Maximum | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| New start (age ≥6) | 18 mg QAM | Increase by 18 mg/week if needed | Maintain effective dose | 72 mg/day | Use lowest effective dose |
| Switching from methylphenidate IR 5 mg BID | 18 mg QAM | Reassess in 7 days | Adjust by 18 mg | 54–72 mg/day | Not a mg-to-mg conversion |
| Switching from methylphenidate IR 10 mg BID | 36 mg QAM | Reassess in 7 days | Adjust by 18 mg | 72 mg/day | Monitor for rebound |
| Switching from Ritalin LA 20 mg | 36 mg QAM | Increase by 18 mg/week | — | 72 mg/day | Consider afternoon symptom control |
| Switching from Metadate CD 30 mg | 54 mg QAM | Adjust weekly | — | 72 mg/day | Individualize based on response |
| Partial response | Current dose | Add 18 mg/day | Continue titration | 72 mg/day | Evaluate adherence |
| Intolerable adverse effects | Current dose | Decrease by 18 mg | Consider alternative | — | Consider non-stimulant |
