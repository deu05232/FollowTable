# Melee Unlockable Fighters and Their Related Extras

| Fighter (Unlocked) | Primary Unlock Requirement | Alternate Requirement | Also Unlocks / Related Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Luigi | 800 VS matches | Complete Adventure Mode with certain completion criteria | Luigi Trophy appears in Trophy Gallery |
| Falco | 300 VS matches | Clear 100-Man Melee | Adds "Corneria"-related music entry in Sound Test |
| Dr. Mario | 100 VS matches | Clear Classic Mode with Mario | Dr. Mario Trophy becomes available |
| Ganondorf | 600 VS matches | Clear Event Match chain culminating in a later event | Unlocks a bonus event set |
| Jigglypuff | Clear Classic Mode once | Clear Adventure Mode once | Jigglypuff Trophy/collectible entry |
| Mewtwo | 700 VS matches | Long play-time requirement | Adds a Poké Ball-related trophy chance |
